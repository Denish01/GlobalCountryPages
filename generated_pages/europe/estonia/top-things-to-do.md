[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Estonian Open-Air Museum** to learn about traditional Estonian architecture and way of life
- Take a **bog hiking tour** in Lahemaa National Park to explore the country's unique natural environment
- Attend a **traditional Estonian folk music performance** to experience the country's rich cultural heritage
- Go on a **hiking tour in the Soomaa National Park** to see the beautiful landscapes and wildlife of western Estonia
- Visit the **Telliskivi Creative City** in Tallinn to explore the city's vibrant arts and culture scene
- Take a **day trip to the island of Saaremaa** to experience the unique culture and natural beauty of Estonia's largest island

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Lahemaa National Park**, one of the largest and most diverse national parks in Europe
- Visit the **Soomaa National Park**, known for its beautiful landscapes and diverse wildlife
- Go **hiking in the Otepää Hills**, a scenic region with beautiful forests and lakes
- Relax on the **Pärnu Beach**, a popular summer destination with beautiful sand and clear water
- Take a **boat tour of the Vilsandi National Park**, a protected area with diverse wildlife and scenic landscapes
- Explore the **Kõrvemaa Nature Reserve**, a unique area with diverse wildlife and natural beauty
