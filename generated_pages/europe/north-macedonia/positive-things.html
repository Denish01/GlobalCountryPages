  • The Stobi Archaeological Site is a well-preserved ancient city with impressive ruins and artifacts from the Roman and Byzantine periods.

  • The Macedonian folk music and dance traditions are an integral part of the country's cultural heritage, with various festivals and events celebrating these art forms.
