[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a boat tour of **Lake Ohrid** to enjoy its stunning natural beauty and learn about its unique ecosystem
- Visit a **traditional Macedonian village** to experience the local culture and try traditional cuisine
- Attend a **folk festival** to enjoy traditional music and dance performances
- Take a **wine tour** to learn about Macedonia's rich wine-making tradition and sample some of the best wines
- Go **hiking in the Shar Mountains** to enjoy stunning scenery and spot unique wildlife
- Visit a **local market** to try traditional food and buy unique handicrafts
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Macedonian cuisine and cook a meal with a local family

- Take a day trip from **Ohrid to the Bay of Bones** (10 km, 20 minutes) to learn about ancient history and enjoy stunning lake views
- Explore the **Kruševo** town from Prilep (30 km, 45 minutes) to learn about the country's rich history and culture
