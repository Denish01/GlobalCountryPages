Cultural Treasures

  • The Acropolis of Athens is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most iconic ancient monuments in Greece, attracting millions of visitors each year.
  • The Monastery of Hosios Loukas is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a significant example of Byzantine architecture and art.
  • The traditional Syrtaki dance is a popular folk dance in Greece, known for its energetic and lively rhythms.
  • The Delphi Archaeological Site is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a significant cultural and historical landmark in Greece, featuring ancient ruins and artifacts.
  • The National Gallery of Greece is a prominent art museum in Athens, featuring a collection of Greek and European art from the 19th and 20th centuries.

People & Global Impact

  • Aristotle Onassis was a Greek shipping magnate and businessman who played a significant role in the development of the global shipping industry.
  • Melina Mercouri was a Greek actress, singer, and politician who served as the Minister of Culture and Sports and was a strong advocate for the arts and culture.
  • The Greek diaspora has made significant contributions to the global community, with notable figures such as Elia Kazan, a Greek-American film director, and Peter Diamandis, a Greek-American entrepreneur and engineer.
  • Giorgos Seferis was a Greek poet and diplomat who was awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1963 and served as the Greek Ambassador to the United Kingdom.

What Visitors Love

  • The Greek cuisine is known for its rich flavors and aromas, with popular dishes such as moussaka, souvlaki, and Greek salad.
  • The Greek islands are a popular tourist destination, with beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and picturesque villages.
  • The hospitality of the Greek people is renowned for its warmth and generosity, making visitors feel welcome and at home.
  • The rich history and culture of Greece is a major draw for tourists, with numerous ancient monuments, museums, and historical sites to explore.
  • The traditional Greek festivals and celebrations, such as the Patras Carnival and the Athens Festival, are a unique and exciting experience for visitors.
  • The stunning natural scenery of Greece, including the mountains, forests, and coastline, offers a range of outdoor activities and breathtaking views.

Hidden Gems

  • The Vikos-Aoos National Park is a lesser-known national park in northern Greece, featuring stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and traditional villages.
  • The town of Plovdiv is a charming and historic town in northern Greece, known for its well-preserved ancient architecture and vibrant cultural scene.
  • The island of Karpathos is a hidden gem in the Dodecanese islands, featuring beautiful beaches, traditional villages, and a unique cultural heritage.
  • The Lake Kerkini is a natural wetland in northern Greece, known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse birdlife, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and birdwatchers.
