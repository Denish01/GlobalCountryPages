Albania and North Macedonia are two neighboring countries in the Balkan region, both offering a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. In terms of cost of living, Albania has a slight edge over North Macedonia, with lower prices for accommodation, food, and transportation. However, Albania's economy has been growing at a faster pace in recent years, with a GDP growth rate of around 4% compared to North Macedonia's 3%. Overall, Albania is a more affordable destination for travelers and expats, with a cost of living that is around 10-20% lower than North Macedonia. Additionally, the cost of living in both countries can fluctuate over time, so it's always a good idea to research current prices and exchange rates before making any decisions.
