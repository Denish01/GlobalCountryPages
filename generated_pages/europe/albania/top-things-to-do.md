# Top Things to Do in Albania — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Albania, visit its ancient cities, stunning beaches, and vibrant culture, which offer a unique blend of history, nature, and entertainment that sets it apart from other European destinations.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a traditional **Albanian cooking class** to learn how to prepare local dishes like qofte and fërgesë
- Explore the **Accursed Mountains** on a guided trek, known for their rugged beauty and traditional villages
- Visit a **local winery** to taste some of Albania's excellent wines, such as the renowned Kallmet and Shesh i Bardhë varieties
- Attend a **traditional folk festival**, like the Gjirokastër National Folklore Festival, to experience Albanian music and dance
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the countryside, offering a unique perspective on rural Albanian life
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Karaburun-Sazan Marine Park**, exploring the stunning coastline and marine life of Albania's first marine park
- Experience the **traditional Albanian hospitality** by staying with a local family, learning about customs and way of life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Valbona Valley National Park**, known for its stunning mountain scenery and traditional villages
- Visit the **Llogara National Park**, featuring beautiful beaches, forests, and wildlife
- Relax on the **Vlorë Beach**, a popular destination for sunbathing and swimming
- Take a **hike** in the **Accursed Mountains**, offering breathtaking views and challenging trails
- Go **rafting** on the **Vjosa River**, one of Europe's last wild rivers, for an exhilarating adventure experience

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Medieval Art** in Korça, featuring a collection of medieval art and artifacts
- Explore the **Et'hem Bey Mosque** in Tirana, a beautiful example of Ottoman architecture
- Attend a **performance** at the **Tirana Opera House**, showcasing Albanian music and dance
- Learn about Albanian history at the **National Historical Museum** in Tirana
- Discover the **traditional Albanian architecture** of the **Gjirokastër Old Town**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Krujë Castle**, a historic fortress and museum showcasing Albanian history and culture
- Explore the **Tirana's Blloku neighborhood**, known for its vibrant nightlife and trendy cafes
- Relax in the **Thermal Baths of Benja**, a natural hot spring resort in the mountains
- Discover the **Albanian Riviera**, a stunning coastline with beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters
- Take a **day trip** to the **Korça region**, known for its traditional villages, stunning scenery, and delicious cuisine

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip** from **Tirana** to **Krujë** (30 km, 30 minutes), visiting the historic castle and old town
- Explore the **Apollonia Archaeological Park** from **Fier** (15 km, 20 minutes), discovering ancient ruins and stunning views
- Visit the **Lekuresi Castle** from **Saranda** (5 km, 10 minutes), enjoying breathtaking views of the Albanian Riviera
- Go on a **day trip** from **Vlorë** to **Llogara National Park** (30 km, 45 minutes), hiking and relaxing in the beautiful scenery
- Take a **day trip** from **Shkodra** to **Rozafa Castle** (5 km, 10 minutes), exploring the historic fortress and stunning views
