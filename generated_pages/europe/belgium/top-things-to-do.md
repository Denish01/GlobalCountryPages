# Top Things to Do in Belgium — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Belgium, be sure to explore its rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes, which offer a wide range of activities and experiences that cater to all interests and preferences.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **brewery tour** to learn about the production of Belgian beer, which is famous worldwide for its quality and variety
- Visit a **chocolate workshop** to learn about the art of Belgian chocolate-making and create your own treats
- Explore the ** Comics Art Museum** in Brussels, which showcases the history and significance of Belgian comics
- Attend a **Flanders Festival** to experience the rich cultural heritage of the region through music, theater, and dance performances
- Go on a **bike tour** along the Belgian coast, which offers stunning views of the North Sea and the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Diamond District** in Antwerp, which is one of the largest diamond trading centers in the world
- Take a **canal boat tour** in Bruges, which offers a unique perspective on the city's stunning architecture and history

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **High Fens** nature reserve, which is one of the largest and most pristine nature reserves in Europe
- Explore the **Ardennes Forest**, which offers stunning scenery, hiking trails, and a wide range of wildlife
- Go hiking in the **Hautes Fagnes** national park, which features unique landscapes and ecosystems
- Relax on the **Belgian coast**, which offers a range of beaches, dunes, and scenic coastal walks
- Visit the **Zwin** nature reserve, which is a protected area for birds and other wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Royal Museums of Fine Arts** in Brussels, which features an impressive collection of art from around the world
- Explore the **Flanders Fields Museum** in Ypres, which offers a poignant insight into the history of World War I
- Attend a **concert** at the **Palais des Beaux-Arts** in Brussels, which is one of the most famous concert halls in Europe
- Visit the **Cathedral of St. Michael and St. Gudula** in Brussels, which features stunning Gothic architecture and an impressive collection of art
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Belgian cuisine, which is famous for its waffles, chocolate, and beer

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Tournai**, which is a hidden gem with a rich history, stunning architecture, and a unique cultural heritage
- Explore the **castle of Gaasbeek**, which is a medieval fortress with a rich history and stunning architecture
- Go to the **village of Dinant**, which is a picturesque town with a rich history, stunning scenery, and a unique cultural heritage
- Visit the **museum of De Panne**, which showcases the history and production of Belgian lace

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Bruges** from Brussels (100 km, 1 hour), which offers stunning architecture, a rich history, and a unique cultural heritage
- Visit **Ghent** from Brussels (55 km, 30 minutes), which features stunning architecture, a rich history, and a vibrant cultural scene
- Explore **Antwerp** from Brussels (45 km, 30 minutes), which is famous for its diamond trading, fashion, and cultural heritage
- Go to **Ypres** from Brussels (120 km, 1.5 hours), which offers a poignant insight into the history of World War I and features stunning scenery
- Take a day trip to **Luxembourg** from Brussels (200 km, 2.5 hours), which offers stunning scenery, a rich history, and a unique cultural heritage
