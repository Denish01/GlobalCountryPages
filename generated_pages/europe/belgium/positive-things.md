[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Grand Place** in Brussels, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a masterpiece of Gothic architecture and a testament to the city's rich history.
- The **Rubens House** in Antwerp is a museum showcasing the works and life of the famous Flemish painter Peter Paul Rubens.
- The **Binche Carnival**, a UNESCO-recognized Masterpiece of the Intangible Heritage of Humanity, is a unique and colorful celebration that takes place every year in the city of Binche.
- The **Flemish Primitives**, a group of painters from the 15th and 16th centuries, are renowned for their detailed and realistic works of art, which can be found in museums throughout Belgium.
- The **Atomium**, a iconic landmark in Brussels, is a symbol of Belgian engineering and design, and offers stunning views of the city.
