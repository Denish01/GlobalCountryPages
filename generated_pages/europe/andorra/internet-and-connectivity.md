[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
The internet in Andorra is of good quality, with an average download speed of 20 Mbps, making it suitable for streaming, online gaming, and other bandwidth-intensive activities. The urban areas, such as Andorra la Vella, have excellent coverage, with most residents and visitors having access to fast and reliable internet. However, the rural areas may experience some limitations, with slower speeds and less reliable connections. Despite these limitations, the overall internet infrastructure in Andorra is well-developed, with a strong focus on providing good coverage and speeds to both residents and tourists.

The reliability of the internet in Andorra is also good, with most providers offering stable connections and minimal downtime. This makes it an attractive destination for remote workers and digital nomads who require a reliable internet connection to stay productive. Additionally, the widespread availability of WiFi in public areas, such as cafes, restaurants, and hotels, makes it easy for visitors to stay connected and access the internet on the go.

[SECTION] Getting a SIM Card [/SECTION]
Getting a SIM card in Andorra is relatively straightforward, with several options available for tourists and visitors. The main carriers, such as Andorra Telecom, Orange, and Vodafone, offer a range of prepaid SIM cards and plans that can be purchased at their stores or online. To purchase a SIM card, visitors will typically need to provide a valid passport and proof of address, although some carriers may have additional requirements. It is recommended to purchase a SIM card from a reputable carrier, such as Andorra Telecom, which offers a range of plans and good coverage.

The cost of a SIM card in Andorra can vary depending on the carrier and plan chosen, but tourists can expect to pay around 10 USD for a basic prepaid SIM card. Data plans are also available, with prices starting from around 10 USD per month for a basic plan with limited data. It is recommended to check the carrier's website or visit a store in person to get the most up-to-date information on plans and prices.
