Andorra is a **sovereign** microstate located in the Pyrenees mountains between France and Spain. With a population of approximately 80,000 people, it is one of the smallest countries in the world. The country has a unique **parliamentary democracy** system, where the head of state is a **co-prince**, currently represented by the President of France and the Bishop of Urgell in Spain. The country's unique location and **tax-free** status have also made it a popular destination for **shopping** and **banking**.
