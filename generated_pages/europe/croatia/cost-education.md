The education system in Croatia is highly regarded, with a strong emphasis on public education. Public schools are generally free or subsidized, and the quality of education is considered to be high. However, private schools and international institutions offer alternative options for those who prefer a more personalized or specialized education. The language of instruction in most schools is **Croatian**, but some international schools may offer instruction in **English** or other languages. Compulsory education in Croatia lasts for 12 years, from age 6 to 18, and is divided into primary and secondary education.

Croatia has a well-developed education system, with a range of options available for students.
