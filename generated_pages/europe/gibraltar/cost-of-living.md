[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment or house with others to reduce rent costs
- Take advantage of the territory's duty-free status to save on shopping and alcohol
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls instead of mid-range or fine dining restaurants
- Use public transportation instead of taxis to get around
- Shop at local markets or supermarkets for groceries instead of convenience stores
- Avoid visiting during peak season (June to September) when prices are highest, and consider visiting during the shoulder season (April to May or September to November) for better deals.
