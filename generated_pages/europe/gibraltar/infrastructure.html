Gibraltar's infrastructure is characterized by a high level of development, with a strong emphasis on providing modern and efficient services to its population. The territory has invested heavily in recent years to upgrade its infrastructure, including the development of new housing, commercial areas, and tourist facilities. The urban area of Gibraltar is well-equipped with modern amenities, including a good road network, reliable electricity and water supply, and high-speed internet connectivity. However, the rural areas of Gibraltar are limited, as the territory is predominantly urban.

The Gibraltar government has implemented various initiatives to improve the territory's infrastructure, including the development of a new power station, the upgrade of the water supply system, and the expansion of the airport. These investments have helped to improve the overall quality of life in Gibraltar and have supported the growth of the local economy.
