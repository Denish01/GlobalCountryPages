# Utility & Internet Costs in Gibraltar

The cost of utilities and internet in Gibraltar can range from $150 to $300 per month for a medium-sized apartment, depending on usage and service providers.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Gibraltar's utility infrastructure is well-developed, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and gas. The costs of utilities in Gibraltar are generally stable, with minimal fluctuation throughout the year. However, there may be some seasonal variation in electricity and gas costs, with higher prices during the winter months due to increased demand. The Gibraltar Electricity Authority and the AquaGib water company are the primary providers of electricity and water services in the territory.

The cost of utilities in Gibraltar can vary depending on the size of the apartment and the level of consumption. On average, a small apartment (45 sqm) can expect to pay around $100-150 per month for utilities, while a medium-sized apartment (85 sqm) can expect to pay around $200-250 per month. Large apartments (120 sqm) can expect to pay around $300-350 per month. It's worth noting that these costs are subject to change, and it's always a good idea to check with the service providers for the latest pricing information.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
- To set up utilities in Gibraltar, you will typically need to provide proof of identity and address, as well as a deposit for the services.
- Prepaid electricity meters are available, but postpaid plans are more common and often more cost-effective.
- Gibtelecom and U-mee are the primary internet service providers in Gibraltar, offering a range of plans and speeds.
- To save on utility bills, consider using energy-efficient appliances and turning off lights and electronics when not in use.
- It's also a good idea to shop around and compare prices from different service providers to find the best deals for your needs.
