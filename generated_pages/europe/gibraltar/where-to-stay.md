# Where to Stay in Gibraltar — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

For travelers looking to stay in Gibraltar, the best areas to consider are those that offer a mix of convenience, amenities, and scenic views, with top neighborhoods including **Town Centre**, **Ocean Village**, and **Europa Point**.

For each of the top areas, here's a breakdown of what to expect:
**Town Centre** (Gibraltar)
Best For: luxury / business
Vibe: The Town Centre is the heart of Gibraltar, offering a vibrant atmosphere with a mix of shops, restaurants, and historical landmarks. It's a great place to experience the local culture and be close to all the main attractions.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: central location, easy access to main attractions, variety of dining options
Cons: can be noisy, limited parking options

**Ocean Village** (Gibraltar)
Best For: family / luxury
Vibe: Ocean Village is a modern waterfront development with a relaxed and upscale atmosphere, offering stunning views of the marina and the Mediterranean Sea. It's a great place to stay for those who want to be close to the sea and enjoy water activities.
Price Range: $120-250/night
Pros: scenic views, modern amenities, close to beach
Cons: can be expensive, limited budget options

**Europa Point** (Gibraltar)
Best For: backpacker / family
Vibe: Europa Point is a scenic area located at the southern tip of Gibraltar, offering stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. It's a great place to stay for those who want to be close to nature and enjoy outdoor activities.
Price Range: $80-180/night
Pros: scenic views, close to nature reserves, affordable options
Cons: limited amenities, can be windy

**Upper Town** (Gibraltar)
Best For: luxury / business
Vibe: Upper Town is a historic and upscale area located on the upper part of the rock, offering stunning views of the city and the Mediterranean Sea. It's a great place to stay for those who want to experience the local culture and be close to main attractions.
Price Range: $100-220/night
Pros: historic architecture, scenic views, close to main attractions
Cons: can be steep, limited parking options

**La Caleta** (Gibraltar)
Best For: family / budget
Vibe: La Caleta is a quiet and residential area located on the eastern side of Gibraltar, offering a relaxed atmosphere and stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. It's a great place to stay for those who want to be close to the beach and enjoy water activities.
Price Range: $60-150/night
Pros: affordable options, close to beach, scenic views
Cons: limited amenities, can be far from main attractions
