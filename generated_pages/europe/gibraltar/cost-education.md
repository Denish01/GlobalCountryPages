[SECTION] Education System Overview [/SECTION]
The education system in Gibraltar is based on the British model, with English as the primary language of instruction. The system is divided into primary, secondary, and post-secondary education, with 12 years of compulsory education. Public schools in Gibraltar are generally considered to be of high quality, with a strong focus on academic achievement and extracurricular activities. Private schools, on the other hand, offer a more personalized and specialized education, often with smaller class sizes and a wider range of subjects.
