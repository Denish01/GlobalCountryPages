[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Gibraltar Museum** showcases the territory's rich history and culture, with a collection of artifacts and exhibits that tell the story of Gibraltar's past.
- The **Great Siege Tunnels** are a UNESCO-listed site that dates back to the 18th century, featuring an impressive network of tunnels and fortifications.
- The **Cathedral of St. Mary the Crowned** is a beautiful Catholic cathedral that dominates the skyline of Gibraltar, with stunning architecture and historic significance.
- The **Gibraltar Carnival** is a vibrant and colorful event that takes place every year, featuring music, dance, and elaborate costumes.
- The **Calpe Rowing Regatta** is a popular sporting event that attracts teams from around the world, with a rich history dating back to the 19th century.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **John Galliano**, the famous fashion designer, was born in Gibraltar and has gone on to achieve international success in the fashion world.
- The **Gibraltarian diaspora** has made significant contributions to the cultural and economic development of countries around the world, including the UK, Spain, and the US.
- **Sir Joshua Hassan**, a former Chief Minister of Gibraltar, played a key role in shaping the territory's modern history and politics.
- The **Gibraltar Community** is known for its strong sense of identity and community spirit, with a unique blend of British, Spanish, and Mediterranean cultures.
