[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
The internet quality in San Marino is generally good, with an average internet speed of 24 Mbps download, which is comparable to other European countries. The urban areas, such as the capital city of San Marino, have a more reliable and faster internet connection compared to the rural areas. However, the difference in internet quality between urban and rural areas is not significant, and even in rural areas, the internet connection is still relatively fast and reliable. The reliability of the internet connection in San Marino is also good, with minimal downtime and disruptions, making it suitable for both personal and business use.

The internet infrastructure in San Marino is well-developed, with a wide range of internet service providers offering different types of connections, including fiber-optic, DSL, and mobile broadband. The country's small size and relatively low population also contribute to the good internet quality, as it is easier to maintain and upgrade the internet infrastructure. Overall, the internet in San Marino is well-suited for tourists, remote workers, and residents who need to stay connected.

[SECTION] Getting a SIM Card [/SECTION]
Getting a SIM card in San Marino is relatively easy, with several mobile carriers offering tourist-friendly plans. The main carriers, such as TIM San Marino, San Marino Telecom, and Vodafone Italia, have stores and kiosks located throughout the country, including at the airport and in major towns. To purchase a SIM card, you will need to provide a valid passport and proof of address, such as a hotel reservation or a rental agreement. The cost of a tourist SIM card is approximately 10 USD, and it usually includes a certain amount of data, voice minutes, and SMS.

The recommended carriers for tourists are TIM San Marino and San Marino Telecom, as they offer a wide range of plans and have good coverage throughout the country. Vodafone Italia is also a good option, especially for those who already have a Vodafone plan in their home country, as they can take advantage of the company's international roaming offers.
