[SECTION] Overview: Belarus vs Latvia [/SECTION]
The cost of living in Belarus and Latvia varies significantly, despite both countries being located in Eastern Europe. Belarus has a lower cost of living, with prices for housing, food, and transportation being generally cheaper than in Latvia. The economic context of the two countries also differs, with Belarus having a more **state-controlled economy** and Latvia having a more **market-oriented economy**. This difference in economic systems contributes to the variation in cost of living between the two countries. Overall, Belarus is a more affordable option for expats, students, and retirees, while Latvia offers a more **Western-style lifestyle** at a higher cost.

The main difference in cost of living between Belarus and Latvia can be attributed to the **housing market**. Rent prices in Belarus are significantly lower than in Latvia, with a one-bedroom apartment in the city center costing around 40% less in Belarus. Additionally, the cost of food and transportation in Belarus is also lower, making it an attractive option for those on a budget. However, Latvia offers a more **developed infrastructure** and a higher standard of living, which may be worth the extra cost for some individuals.
