**Upper City** (Minsk)
Best For: Luxury travelers and business visitors

**Trinity Suburb** (Minsk)
Best For: Families and those looking for a local experience
Vibe: This charming neighborhood is known for its picturesque streets, quaint shops, and family-friendly atmosphere, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a more laid-back experience. The area is also home to many parks and green spaces, perfect for outdoor activities and relaxation.

Best For: Backpackers and budget travelers

Best For: History buffs and those looking for a unique experience
