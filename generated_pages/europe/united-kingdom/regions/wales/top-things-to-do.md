# Top Things to Do in Wales — Attractions & Experiences

Wales offers a diverse range of activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring historic castles and cultural attractions to enjoying the country's stunning natural beauty and unique outdoor activities.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Welsh language course** to learn about the country's unique language and culture
- Attend a **male voice choir performance**, a traditional Welsh musical experience
- Go **coasteering**, a unique outdoor activity that involves exploring the coastline by climbing, jumping, and swimming
- Take a **brewery tour**, sampling some of Wales' famous beers and learning about the brewing process
- Visit a **traditional Welsh farm**, to learn about the country's rural heritage and see animals such as sheep and cattle
- Attend a **fest nog**, a traditional Welsh celebration with music, food, and drink
- Take a **horse-drawn carriage ride** through the picturesque Welsh countryside

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore **Snowdonia National Park**, home to the highest mountain in Wales and stunning scenery
- Visit **Pembrokeshire Coast National Park**, with its beautiful coastline and scenic walks
- Go **hiking** in the **Brecon Beacons**, with its stunning mountains and lakes
- Take a **boat trip** on **Lake Bala**, a beautiful lake in the heart of Wales
- Visit **Barafundle Bay**, a stunning beach with golden sand and crystal-clear water
- Go **birdwatching** in **Anglesey**, a haven for birdlife with its coastal wetlands and nature reserves

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Wales**, with its collection of Welsh art and artefacts
- Explore **Cardiff Castle**, with its mix of Roman, medieval, and Victorian architecture
- Attend a **performance** at the **Wales Millennium Centre**, a world-class arts venue
- Take a **tour** of the **Big Pit National Coal Museum**, to learn about Wales' industrial heritage
- Visit the **National Slate Museum**, to learn about the history of slate mining in Wales
- Attend a **traditional Welsh festival**, such as the Eisteddfod, to experience Welsh music, dance, and culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Hay-on-Wye**, with its charming bookshops and literary festivals
- Explore the **Gower Peninsula**, with its stunning coastline and scenic walks
- Take a **trip** to the **isle of Anglesey**, with its beautiful beaches and historic sites
- Visit the **village of Llanberis**, with its stunning scenery and outdoor activities
- Go to the **town of Abergavenny**, with its historic castle and cultural attractions
