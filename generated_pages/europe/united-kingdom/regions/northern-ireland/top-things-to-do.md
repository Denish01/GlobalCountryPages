# Top Things to Do in Northern Ireland — Attractions & Experiences

Northern Ireland offers a diverse range of activities and attractions that cater to different interests, from exploring historic sites and natural wonders to experiencing the unique culture and traditions of the region.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Black Cab Tour** of Belfast to learn about the city's history and see the famous murals
- Explore the **Game of Thrones** filming locations, including the Dark Hedges and Ballintoy Harbour
- Attend a traditional **Irish music session** at a local pub
- Visit the **Bushmills Distillery** to learn about the production of Irish whiskey
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Northern Irish cuisine
- Go on a **ghost tour** of Belfast to explore the city's haunted history
- Visit the **Derry Walls** to learn about the city's complex history and see the famous murals

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Glenarm Castle** and its beautiful gardens
- Hike in the **Mourne Mountains**, an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty
- Visit the **Cave Hill Country Park**, with its stunning views of Belfast
- Go **surfing** or **kayaking** on the north coast
- Explore the **Strangford Lough**, a haven for wildlife and outdoor activities
- Visit the **Tollymore Forest Park**, with its beautiful trees and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ulster American Folk Park** to learn about the history of Irish emigration
- Explore the **Belfast Cathedral**, a stunning example of Hiberno-Romanesque architecture
- Attend a performance at the **Grand Opera House**, a beautiful example of Victorian architecture
- Visit the **Armagh County Museum** to learn about the history and culture of the region
- Take a **pottery class** to learn about traditional Northern Irish crafts
- Visit the **F.E. McWilliam Gallery**, showcasing the work of a famous Northern Irish sculptor

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Grey Abbey**, a beautiful and historic monastery
- Explore the **Castle Ward**, a stunning 16th-century castle
- Go to the **St. George's Market**, a bustling marketplace with local produce and crafts
- Visit the **Somme Heritage Centre**, to learn about the history of World War I
- Take a **boat tour** of **Strangford Lough**, to see the beautiful scenery and wildlife

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Derry**, with its rich history and stunning architecture (distance: 70 miles, travel time: 1 hour 15 minutes, highlights: Derry Walls, Guildhall, St. Columb's Cathedral)
- Visit **Armagh**, with its beautiful cathedral and historic sites (distance: 40 miles, travel time: 45 minutes, highlights: Armagh Cathedral, Armagh County Museum)
- Explore the **Causeway Coastal Route**, with its stunning scenery and historic sites (distance: 120 miles, travel time: 2 hours 30 minutes, highlights: Giant's Causeway, Carrick-a-Rede Rope Bridge, Dark Hedges)
- Take a day trip to **Newcastle**, with its beautiful beaches and stunning scenery (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 45 minutes, highlights: Newcastle Beach, Mourne Mountains)
- Visit **Bangor**, with its beautiful marina and historic sites (distance: 15 miles, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: Bangor Marina, Bangor Abbey)
