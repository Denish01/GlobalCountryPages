# Cost of Living in Bristol - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Bristol is generally considered to be moderate to expensive, with daily budgets ranging from $60-120 USD for backpackers to $250-400 USD for those seeking a more comfortable experience.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate to Expensive
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $120-250 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $250-400 USD
Currency: GBP
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 10-15% in restaurants and bars is common
[/FACTBOX]

Bristol is a city in the United Kingdom, and its cost of living is relatively high compared to other cities in the world.
