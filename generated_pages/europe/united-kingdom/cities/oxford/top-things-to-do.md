# Top Things to Do in Oxford — Attractions & Experiences

Oxford, a city in the United Kingdom, is home to a plethora of historical, cultural, and educational attractions that make it a must-visit destination, with top things to do including exploring the iconic University of Oxford, visiting the Ashmolean Museum, and taking a stroll along the High Street.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **punting** trip down the River Cherwell, a classic Oxford experience
- Attend a lecture or seminar at the University of Oxford, a unique opportunity to learn from world-renowned experts
- Visit the **Oxford Botanic Garden**, the oldest botanic garden in Britain, featuring a wide range of plant species
- Explore the **Oxford Covered Market**, a historic market selling a variety of goods, from food to souvenirs
- Go on a **ghost tour** of Oxford, exploring the city's spooky side and learning about its haunted history
- Take a **cycle tour** of the city, a great way to see Oxford's sights and learn about its history and culture
