# Top Things to Do in Cardiff — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Cardiff, visit the **Cardiff Castle**, explore the **National Museum Cardiff**, and take a stroll along the **Cardiff Bay Barrage**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Cardiff Castle** tour to explore the castle's opulent interiors and historic walls
- Visit the **Doctor Who Experience** to explore the world of the popular BBC sci-fi series
- Explore the **Cardiff Market** for a taste of local food, crafts, and culture
- Attend a performance at the **Wales Millennium Centre**, a world-class arts venue
- Go on a **Ghost Tour** of Cardiff to discover the city's spooky side
- Visit the **Cardiff Story** museum to learn about the city's history and people
- Take a **brewery tour** to sample local beers and learn about Cardiff's brewing heritage

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Bute Park** for a stroll or picnic in the heart of the city
- Explore the **Cardiff Bay Wetlands** for birdwatching and scenic views
- Walk or cycle along the **Taff Trail**, a scenic path that follows the River Taff
- Visit **Penarth Pier**, a charming seaside resort with beautiful beach and promenade
- Go hiking in **Coed y Wenallt**, a woodland area with trails and stunning views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum Cardiff** to explore Wales' art, history, and culture
- Attend a performance at the **Wales Millennium Centre**, a world-class arts venue
- Explore the **Cardiff Arcades**, a collection of Victorian-era shopping arcades
- Visit **Llandaff Cathedral**, a historic cathedral with stunning architecture
- Take a **Welsh language class** to learn about the country's unique language and culture
- Attend a **traditional Welsh folk music** session to experience the country's rich musical heritage

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Riverside Farmers' Market** for local produce and artisanal goods
- Explore the **Glamorgan Building**, a stunning example of Victorian-era architecture
- Take a stroll along the **River Taff**, which runs through the heart of the city
- Visit the **Chapter Arts Centre**, a hub for art, music, and performance
- Discover the **Cardiff Street Art** scene, with vibrant murals and graffiti throughout the city
