# Cost of Living in Cardiff - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Cardiff is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget of $80-150 USD per person for a mid-range traveler, although prices can vary depending on individual circumstances and lifestyle choices.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider visiting Cardiff during the off-season (October to March) when prices for accommodation and tourist attractions are lower.
- Take advantage of the city's free museums and galleries, such as the National Museum of Wales and the Cardiff Castle.
- Use public transportation, such as buses and trains, to get around the city instead of taxis or rental cars.
- Eat at local pubs and restaurants, which often offer affordable and delicious food options.
- Look for discounts and special offers on accommodation and tourist attractions, especially during peak season.
- Consider purchasing a Cardiff Tourist Card, which grants access to many of the city's top attractions and public transportation.
