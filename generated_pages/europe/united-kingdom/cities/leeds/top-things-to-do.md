# Top Things to Do in Leeds — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Leeds, visit iconic attractions like Kirkstall Abbey and Royal Armouries Museum, and explore unique experiences such as taking a **Leeds Owl Trail** or visiting the **Thackray Medical Museum**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Leeds Owl Trail**, a self-guided walking tour that explores the city's owl-themed public art
- Visit the **Thackray Medical Museum** to learn about the history of medicine and healthcare
- Explore the **Granary Wharf**, a vibrant area filled with street food, bars, and live music
- Go on a **Leeds Brewery Tour**, sampling some of the city's best craft beers
- Attend a performance at the **Leeds Grand Theatre**, a stunning example of Victorian architecture
- Take a **Street Art Walking Tour**, discovering the city's vibrant and eclectic street art scene
- Visit the **Belgrave Music Hall**, a unique venue that hosts live music and comedy performances

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]

- **Temple Newsam**, a beautiful Tudor-Jacobean mansion with stunning gardens and a working farm

- **Kirkstall Abbey**, a well-preserved medieval monastery with stunning architecture and beautiful grounds

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]

- **The Leeds Library**, a beautiful and historic library with a stunning reading room and a vast collection of books
- **The Canal Garden**, a peaceful and hidden garden located along the Leeds-Liverpool Canal

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **York**, a historic city with stunning architecture, museums, and a famous cathedral (distance: 25 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
2. **Harrogate**, a beautiful spa town with stunning gardens, a historic centre, and a famous tea room (distance: 20 miles, travel time: 45 minutes)
3. **Haworth**, a charming village famous for its connections to the Bronte sisters, with stunning moorland scenery and a variety of tea rooms (distance: 25 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
4. **Saltaire**, a unique and historic village that was built for the workers of a local mill, with stunning architecture and a variety of shops and cafes (distance: 10 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
5. **Malham**, a beautiful village located in the heart of the Yorkshire Dales, with stunning scenery, hiking trails, and a variety of pubs and cafes (distance: 60 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours)
