[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Black Taxi Tour** to explore the city's street art and learn about its history and culture
- Visit the **George Best Trail** to learn about the life and career of the famous footballer
- Explore the **Belfast Street Art** scene and discover the city's vibrant murals
- Attend a **traditional Irish music session** at a local pub
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Northern Irish cuisine
- Go on a **Belfast Food Tour** to sample the city's best dishes and drinks

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Cave Hill Country Park** and enjoy stunning views of the city
- Visit the **Botanic Gardens** and discover the beautiful plant species
- Take a walk along the **Lagan River** and enjoy the scenic views
- Go hiking in the **Divis and Black Mountain** area and enjoy breathtaking views of the city
- Visit the **Belfast Zoo** and see a wide range of animals from around the world

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ulster Folk and Transport Museum** to learn about the history and culture of Northern Ireland
- Explore the **Belfast Exposed** photography gallery and discover the work of local artists
- Attend a performance at the **Lyric Theatre** and enjoy the city's vibrant arts scene
- Visit the **Naughton Gallery** at Queen's University and discover the work of local and international artists
- Take a tour of the **Belfast Murals** and learn about the city's history and culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kelly's Cellars** pub and enjoy traditional Irish music and atmosphere
- Explore the **St. George's Market** and discover local food, crafts, and souvenirs
- Take a walk through the **Belfast Peace Walls** and learn about the city's history and culture
- Visit the **Linen Hall Library** and discover the city's literary heritage
- Go to the **Sunflower Pub** and enjoy live music and a wide range of beers

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Giant's Causeway** (60 miles, 1 hour 30 minutes) and explore the stunning natural beauty of the area
- Visit **Dunluce Castle** (60 miles, 1 hour 30 minutes) and learn about the history and culture of the area

- Visit **Hillsborough** (15 miles, 30 minutes) and discover the village's historic castle and beautiful countryside
