# Top Things to Do in Bath — Attractions & Experiences

The city of Bath, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offers a wide range of activities and attractions that make it a must-visit destination, from its Roman Baths and Georgian architecture to its natural hot springs and picturesque countryside.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Roman Baths Guided Tour** to explore the ancient ruins and learn about the history of the site
- Indulge in a **Bath Cream Tea** at a traditional tea room, complete with scones, jam, and clotted cream
- Attend a **Bath Literature Festival** event to hear authors and poets read from their works
- Go on a **Ghost Walk of Bath** to discover the city's spooky side and haunted history
- Take a **River Avon Boat Trip** to see the city from a different perspective and enjoy the scenic views
- Learn about the art of **Georgian Architecture** on a guided walking tour of the city's historic buildings

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Prior Park Landscape Garden**, a beautiful 18th-century garden with a grotto and lake
- Walk along the **Kennet and Avon Canal**, a picturesque waterway with towpath and scenic views
- Visit the **Bath Skyline Walk**, a 6-mile circular walk with stunning views of the city
- Go **Cycling in the Cotswolds**, a scenic area of rolling hills and picturesque villages
- Take a **Boat Trip on the River Avon**, a relaxing way to see the city and its surroundings

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Holburne Museum**, an art museum with an impressive collection of art and antiquities
- Attend a concert or event at **The Assembly Rooms**, a stunning 18th-century ballroom
- Explore the **Museum of Bath at Work**, a museum showcasing the city's industrial and commercial history
- Take a tour of **Bath Abbey**, a stunning church with beautiful stained-glass windows and tower tours
- Learn about the life and works of **Jane Austen** at the Jane Austen Centre, a museum dedicated to the celebrated author

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **American Museum in Britain**, a unique museum showcasing American art and culture
- Explore the **Parade Gardens**, a beautiful park with gardens, a bandstand, and scenic views
- Take a stroll along **Great Pulteney Street**, a picturesque street lined with Georgian houses and shops
- Discover the **Bath Arts Workshop**, a community arts center with exhibitions and classes
- Go to the **Theatre Royal Bath**, a historic theater with a varied program of productions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Stonehenge**, a prehistoric monument and one of the world's most famous ancient sites (30 miles, 1 hour)
- Visit **Bristol**, a vibrant city with a rich maritime history and cultural attractions (12 miles, 30 minutes)
- Explore the **Cotswolds**, a scenic area of rolling hills and picturesque villages (20 miles, 45 minutes)
- Go to **Wells**, a historic city with a stunning cathedral and picturesque streets (20 miles, 45 minutes)
- Take a trip to **Lacock**, a picturesque village with historic buildings and scenic views (25 miles, 50 minutes)
