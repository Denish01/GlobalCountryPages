[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment with friends or flatmates to reduce rent costs
- Take advantage of Manchester's affordable public transportation system, including buses and trams
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls, which offer a range of affordable and delicious options
- Visit the city's many free museums and cultural attractions, such as the National Football Museum and the Lowry arts centre
- Look for discounts and promotions at local bars and restaurants, especially during happy hour or off-peak seasons
- Use cashback and rewards credit cards to earn points and save money on everyday purchases

Overall, Manchester is a great destination for anyone looking for a mix of culture, entertainment, and affordability. With some careful planning and budgeting, it's possible to have a fantastic time in Manchester without breaking the bank.
