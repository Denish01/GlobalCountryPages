- The **Lake District**, a national park in northwest England, is known for its stunning scenery and inspiration to famous authors such as William Wordsworth and Beatrix Potter.
- The **Edinburgh Festival Fringe**, the largest arts festival in the world, takes place every August in Scotland's capital city, featuring thousands of performances and events.
- The **Notting Hill Carnival**, a vibrant celebration of Caribbean culture, is held annually in London, attracting millions of visitors with its colorful costumes, music, and food.
