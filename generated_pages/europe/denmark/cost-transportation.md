The cost of transportation in Denmark can vary depending on the mode of transport, with a monthly transit pass costing around $96 USD and taxi start rates beginning at approximately $6 USD.

[SECTION] Getting Around Overview [/SECTION]
Denmark has an efficient and well-developed transportation system, with a strong focus on public transportation, cycling, and walking. The country's compact size and flat terrain make it easy to get around without a car, and many cities have dedicated bike lanes and pedestrian zones. Most people in Denmark use public transportation, with around 70% of the population relying on buses, trains, and metro lines to get to work or school. The quality of public transit is generally high, with frequent and reliable services connecting major cities and towns.

Denmark's transportation network is designed to be user-friendly, with clear signage and easy-to-use ticketing systems. Many public transportation services, including buses and trains, offer free Wi-Fi and comfortable seating, making commuting a relatively pleasant experience. Additionally, Denmark's cycling infrastructure is among the best in the world, with over 12,000 kilometers of dedicated bike lanes and bike-share schemes available in many cities. This emphasis on sustainable transportation has helped to reduce traffic congestion and make Denmark's cities more livable.

[SECTION] Transport Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider purchasing a **Rejsekort**, a prepaid travel card that can be used on public transportation services throughout Denmark.
- Use the **DSB** or **Movia** apps to plan your journey and buy tickets for public transportation.
- Be aware of **peak hours** (7-9 am and 3-5 pm) when public transportation is busiest and more expensive.
- **Cycling** is a great way to get around, with many cities offering bike-share schemes and dedicated bike lanes.
- **Negotiate** with taxi drivers before you start your journey, especially for longer trips or airport transfers.
- Use **ride-hailing apps** like Uber or Taxa 4x35 to get a fixed price for your journey and avoid surprises.
