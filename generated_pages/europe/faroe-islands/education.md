The primary education system is focused on providing a broad-based education that includes subjects such as Faroese, Danish, mathematics, science, and social studies. Secondary education, or **Studentaskúli**, is not compulsory but is highly encouraged, and it provides students with a more specialized education in areas such as business, technology, and healthcare.
