[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]

- The **National Archaeological Museum of Naples** is one of the most important museums in the world, housing a vast collection of artifacts from ancient Greece and Rome, including mosaics, sculptures, and jewelry.
- The **Teatro di San Carlo** is a historic opera house in Naples, known for its stunning architecture and world-class performances.
- The **Cathedral of San Gennaro** is a beautiful and historic church in Naples, dedicated to the city's patron saint and featuring stunning architecture and artwork.
- The **Island of Capri** is a popular tourist destination, known for its stunning natural beauty, charming towns, and rich cultural heritage.
