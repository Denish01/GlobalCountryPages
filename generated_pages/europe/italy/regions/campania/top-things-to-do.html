Campania, a region in southern Italy, offers a diverse range of activities and attractions that cater to different interests, from ancient historical sites to stunning natural landscapes, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to experience the best of Italy.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a limoncello-making class in Sorrento to learn about the production of this iconic Italian liqueur
  • Enjoy a gondola ride through the canals of Naples, a unique way to explore the city's maritime history
  • Visit a local winery in the Avellino province to taste some of the region's renowned wines, such as Aglianico and Greco
  • Attend a Neapolitan music performance, showcasing the region's rich musical heritage
  • Take a cooking class to learn how to prepare traditional Neapolitan dishes, such as pizza and seafood risotto
  • Go on a fishing tour in the Bay of Naples to experience the region's rich marine life and enjoy fresh seafood
  • Explore the underground tunnels of Naples, a network of hidden passageways and ancient ruins

Outdoor & Nature

  • Visit the National Park of Cilento and Vallo di Diano, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring stunning natural landscapes and diverse wildlife
  • Hike the Sentiero degli Dei (Path of the Gods), a scenic trail offering breathtaking views of the Amalfi Coast
  • Relax on the beaches of the Amalfi Coast, such as Positano and Amalfi, known for their crystal-clear waters and stunning scenery
  • Explore the islands of the Bay of Naples, including Capri, Ischia, and Procida, each offering unique natural beauty and charm
  • Go hiking in the Lattari Mountains, a range offering stunning views of the Amalfi Coast and surrounding landscape

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the National Archaeological Museum of Paestum, featuring a collection of artifacts from the ancient Greek ruins
  • Explore the Cathedral of San Gennaro in Naples, a beautiful church dedicated to the city's patron saint
  • Attend a performance at the Teatro di San Carlo, one of the world's oldest and most prestigious opera houses
  • Visit the Museo di Capodimonte, a museum featuring a collection of art and artifacts from the Bourbon era
  • Take a tour of the historic center of Naples, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring a rich collection of historical landmarks and cultural attractions
  • Explore the Castel Nuovo, a medieval castle in Naples that now houses the City Museum

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the town of Ravello, perched on top of a hill, offering stunning views of the Amalfi Coast and a glimpse into the region's medieval past
  • Explore the ancient ruins of Velia, a lesser-known but incredibly well-preserved ancient Greek site
  • Relax on the beach of Marina di Albori, a secluded and picturesque beach on the Amalfi Coast
  • Take a hike in the Sorrento Peninsula, offering stunning views of the Bay of Naples and surrounding landscape
  • Visit the island of Procida, a charming and lesser-known island in the Bay of Naples, featuring stunning natural beauty and a rich cultural heritage

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip to Pompeii and Mount Vesuvius from Naples (distance: 24 km, travel time: 30 minutes), exploring the ancient ruins and hiking to the summit of the volcano
  • Visit the Amalfi Coast from Salerno (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1 hour), enjoying the stunning scenery and picturesque towns
  • Explore the island of Capri from Naples (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour), visiting the charming towns and enjoying the stunning natural beauty
  • Take a day trip to Paestum from Salerno (distance: 85 km, travel time: 1.5 hours), marveling at the ancient Greek ruins and enjoying the surrounding landscape
  • Visit the town of Sorrento from Naples (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour), enjoying the charming town and stunning views of the Bay of Naples

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of Naples and Pompeii), 7-day (comprehensive tour of the region, including the Amalfi Coast and islands), 14-day (deep exploration of the region, including hiking, cooking classes, and cultural experiences). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and popular attractions in advance, especially during peak season, and consider purchasing a Campania Artecard, which grants access to many of the region's museums and historical sites.
