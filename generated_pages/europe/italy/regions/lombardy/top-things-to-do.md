# Top Things to Do in Lombardy — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Lombardy, visit its stunning lakes, historic cities, and breathtaking mountains, which offer a mix of art, architecture, and natural beauty that showcases the region's unique cultural and environmental heritage.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gondola ride** on Lake Como to explore its picturesque towns and villas
- Visit a **local winery** in the Oltrepo Pavese region to taste some of the best Italian wines
- Attend an **opera performance** at Teatro alla Scala in Milan for a unique cultural experience
- Explore the **street art scene** in Milan's Isola district, known for its vibrant murals and graffiti
- Take a **cooking class** in Bergamo to learn about traditional Lombardy cuisine
- Visit the **Festa dei Noantri** in Milan, a traditional festival with music, food, and fireworks

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike in the **Lombardy Prealps**, a mountain range with stunning scenery and picturesque towns
- Visit **Lake Iseo**, a scenic lake with a beautiful island and stunning views
- Explore the **Parco Naturale dell'Adda**, a nature reserve with hiking trails and wildlife
- Take a **bike tour** along the **Ticino River**, a scenic route with stunning views and historic sites
- Visit the **Parco Naturale della Valle del Lambro**, a nature reserve with hiking trails and wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo Poldi Pezzoli** in Milan, a museum with an impressive collection of art and artifacts
- Explore the **Castello Visconteo** in Pavia, a historic castle with stunning architecture and art
- Attend a **concert** at the **Auditorium di Milano**, a modern concert hall with stunning acoustics
- Visit the **Pinacoteca di Brera** in Milan, a museum with an impressive collection of art and artifacts
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Duomo di Milano**, a historic cathedral with stunning architecture and art

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Borghetto**, a picturesque village on the Mincio River with historic buildings and stunning views
- Explore the **Museo del Novecento** in Milan, a museum with an impressive collection of modern art
- Take a **stroll** along the **Navigli**, a historic canal district in Milan with picturesque views and lively atmosphere
- Visit the **Santuario della Madonna del Ghisallo**, a historic church with stunning views of the surrounding countryside

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Lake Maggiore**, a scenic lake with picturesque towns and stunning views (distance from Milan: 100 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit **Bergamo**, a historic town with stunning architecture and scenic views (distance from Milan: 50 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Explore **Pavia**, a historic city with stunning architecture and cultural attractions (distance from Milan: 30 km, travel time: 20 minutes)
- Take a day trip to **Lake Garda**, a scenic lake with picturesque towns and stunning views (distance from Milan: 150 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit **Cremona**, a historic city with stunning architecture and cultural attractions (distance from Milan: 100 km, travel time: 1 hour)
