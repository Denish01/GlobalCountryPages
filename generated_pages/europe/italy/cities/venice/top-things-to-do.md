# Top Things to Do in Venice — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Venice, the best things to do include exploring the city's iconic canals, historic landmarks, and unique cultural experiences, which showcase the city's rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant arts scene.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gondola ride** through the city's canals and admire the stunning architecture and historic landmarks
- Get lost in the narrow streets and discover hidden **cicchetti bars** and local eateries
- Attend a performance at the **Teatro La Fenice**, one of the world's most famous opera houses
- Take a **mask-making workshop** and learn about the traditional Venetian art of mask-making
- Go on a **food tour** and sample the city's unique cuisine, including fresh seafood and traditional dishes
- Explore the **Rialto Market**, a historic marketplace filled with fresh produce, seafood, and souvenirs
- Take a **vaporetto ride** to the nearby islands, such as Murano and Burano, and explore their charming towns and beaches

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lido di Venezia**, a scenic island with beaches and stunning views of the Adriatic Sea
- Explore the **Giardini della Biennale**, a beautiful park with gardens, sculptures, and stunning views of the lagoon
- Take a **hike** in the nearby **Dolomite mountains**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with breathtaking scenery
- Visit the **Venezia Lagoon**, a protected area with stunning natural beauty and diverse wildlife
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Valle Averto**, a protected area with a wide variety of bird species

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Gallerie dell'Accademia**, a museum with an impressive collection of Venetian art, including works by Titian and Tintoretto
- Explore the **Museo Correr**, a museum with a wide range of exhibits on Venetian history, art, and culture
- Attend a **concert** at the **Scuola Grande di San Rocco**, a historic building with stunning architecture and beautiful music
- Visit the **San Zaccaria church**, a beautiful church with stunning **Byzantine mosaics** and impressive art collection
- Take a **glassblowing workshop** on the island of **Murano**, famous for its traditional glassmaking techniques

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **San Sebastiano church**, a beautiful church with stunning **Tiepolo frescoes** and impressive art collection
- Explore the **Campo San Trovaso**, a charming square with historic buildings and scenic canals
- Take a **walk** along the **Fondamenta delle Zattere**, a scenic waterfront promenade with stunning views of the Giudecca Canal
- Visit the **Museo di Palazzo Mocenigo**, a museum with a wide range of exhibits on Venetian fashion, art, and culture
- Discover the **Orto Botanico di Venezia**, a beautiful botanical garden with a wide variety of plant species

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the nearby **Padua**, a charming city with historic landmarks, beautiful canals, and stunning architecture (distance: 30 km, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: Scrovegni Chapel, Padua Cathedral)
- Visit the **Verona**, a romantic city with historic landmarks, beautiful canals, and stunning architecture (distance: 120 km, travel time: 1 hour 30 minutes, highlights: Verona Arena, Juliet's Balcony)
- Explore the **Dolomite mountains**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with breathtaking scenery and outdoor activities (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours, highlights: hiking, skiing, scenic views)
- Take a day trip to the **Murano** and **Burano** islands, famous for their traditional glassmaking and lace-making techniques (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: glassblowing workshops, lace-making demonstrations)
- Visit the **Vicenza**, a charming city with historic landmarks, beautiful canals, and stunning architecture (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1 hour, highlights: Teatro Olimpico, Vicenza Cathedral)
