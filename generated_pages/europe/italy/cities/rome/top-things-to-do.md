# Top Things to Do in Rome — Attractions & Experiences

Rome, the Eternal City, is a must-visit destination that offers a plethora of exciting experiences, from exploring ancient ruins and historical landmarks to indulging in delicious Italian cuisine and wine, and to help you make the most of your trip, here are the top things to do in Rome.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Vespa tour** through the city, exploring hidden corners and scenic views
- Indulge in a **wine and cheese tasting** at a local enoteca, featuring Italian varieties
- Attend an **opera performance** at the Teatro dell'Opera di Roma, a world-renowned institution
- Explore the **Trastevere neighborhood**, known for its narrow streets, charming piazzas, and lively nightlife
- Visit the **Mercato Trionfale**, a large market offering a wide range of food, clothing, and souvenirs
- Take a **cooking class**, learning how to prepare traditional Italian dishes like pasta, pizza, and risotto

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Villa Ada**, a large public park with a lake, walking trails, and plenty of green space
- Explore the **Parco del Colle Oppio**, a scenic park with stunning views of the Colosseum
- Take a **hike** in the **Parco Regionale dell'Appia Antica**, a protected area with ancient ruins and natural beauty
- Relax at the **Lago di Bracciano**, a lake located just outside of Rome, perfect for swimming and boating
- Visit the **Giardino degli Aranci**, a beautiful orange garden with stunning views of the city

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Galleria Borghese**, a museum featuring an impressive collection of Renaissance and Baroque art
- Explore the **National Roman Museum**, which includes four separate museums and a collection of ancient artifacts
- Attend a **concert** at the **Auditorium Parco della Musica**, a modern music venue hosting various performances
- Visit the **Museo Gregoriano Etrusco**, a museum dedicated to Etruscan art and culture
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Vatican Museums**, featuring some of the world's most famous art and artifacts

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **San Luigi dei Francesi**, a beautiful church featuring three famous Caravaggio paintings
- Explore the **Quartiere Coppedè**, a charming neighborhood with unique architecture and a relaxed atmosphere
- Take a **stroll** along the **Tiber Island**, a peaceful spot with beautiful views of the city
- Visit the **Museo di Roma**, a museum showcasing the history and art of Rome
- Discover the **Santa Maria in Trastevere**, a beautiful church with stunning mosaics and a peaceful atmosphere

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Pompeii and Naples**, located about 230 km from Rome (approximately 2.5 hours by car or train), and explore the ancient ruins and vibrant city
- Visit the **Tivoli Gardens**, located about 30 km from Rome (approximately 1 hour by car or train), and explore the beautiful gardens and villas
- Explore the **Ostia Antica**, located about 30 km from Rome (approximately 1 hour by car or train), and discover the ancient ruins and historic significance
- Take a day trip to **Siena**, located about 230 km from Rome (approximately 2.5 hours by car or train), and experience the charming medieval town and stunning countryside
- Visit the **Castelli Romani**, located about 20 km from Rome (approximately 30 minutes by car or train), and explore the beautiful hills, lakes, and charming towns
