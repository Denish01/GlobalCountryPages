# Top Things to Do in Genoa — Attractions & Experiences

The best things to do in Genoa include exploring the historic **Porto Antico**, visiting the stunning **Cathedral of San Lorenzo**, and discovering the city's rich maritime history at the **Galata Museo del Mare**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **guided tour of the historic center** to discover hidden gems and learn about the city's rich history
- Visit the **Fiera di Genova** to experience the city's vibrant market scene and try local delicacies
- Explore the **narrow streets of the Old Town** to discover unique shops and restaurants
- Attend a **performance at the Teatro Carlo Felice** to experience the city's rich cultural scene
- Take a **boat tour of the harbor** to see the city from a different perspective
- Visit the **Mercato Orientale** to experience the sights and smells of the city's bustling market
