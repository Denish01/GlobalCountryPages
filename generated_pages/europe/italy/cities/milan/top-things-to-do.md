# Top Things to Do in Milan — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Milan, you should not miss the iconic Duomo, the stunning Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, and the world-renowned Teatro alla Scala, which are just a few of the many top attractions and experiences that this vibrant city has to offer.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Duomo | Piazza del Duomo | Cathedral | Iconic symbol of Milan and one of the largest cathedrals in the world |
| Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II | Piazza del Duomo | Shopping Mall | Stunning example of 19th-century architecture and luxury shopping |
| Teatro alla Scala | Piazza della Scala | Opera House | World-renowned opera house and one of the most famous in the world |
| Castello Sforzesco | Piazza Castello | Castle | Historic castle and museum complex with beautiful gardens and art collections |

| San Siro Stadium | Via Piccolomini | Stadium | Home of AC Milan and Inter Milan football clubs and a must-visit for sports fans |
| Pinacoteca di Brera | Via Brera | Museum | Public museum featuring an impressive collection of Italian and European art |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **guided tour** of the Duomo's rooftop for breathtaking views of the city
- Attend a performance at the **Teatro alla Scala**, one of the most famous opera houses in the world
- Explore the **Navigli District** and take a boat tour along the canals
- Visit the **Fondazione Prada**, a contemporary art museum with a unique collection of art and culture
- Shop at the **Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II**, one of the oldest and most luxurious shopping malls in the world
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Milanese dishes like risotto and osso buco

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Parco Sempione**, a large public park with gardens, lakes, and walking trails
- **Giardini Pubblici**, a beautiful park with gardens, a lake, and a variety of plant species
- **Lago di Como**, a stunning lake located just outside of Milan, perfect for boat tours and swimming
- **Parco delle Cave**, a unique park featuring a network of underground caves and tunnels
- **Monte Barro**, a nearby mountain with hiking trails and stunning views of the surrounding countryside

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Museo Poldi Pezzoli**, a world-class museum featuring an impressive collection of art and artifacts
- **Pinacoteca di Brera**, a public museum featuring an impressive collection of Italian and European art
- **Castello Sforzesco**, a historic castle and museum complex with beautiful gardens and art collections
- **Teatro alla Scala Museum**, a museum dedicated to the history of the famous opera house
- **Basilica di Sant'Ambrogio**, a historic church and one of the most important in Milan
- **Museo del Novecento**, a museum of 20th-century art located in the **Piazza del Duomo**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Museo Bagatti Valsecchi**, a small museum located in a historic palace and featuring a collection of art and artifacts
- **Orto Botanico di Brera**, a beautiful botanical garden with a variety of plant species
- **San Bernardino alle Ossa**, a unique church featuring a stunning interior and a fascinating history
- **Galleria Borghese**, a small art gallery located in a historic building and featuring a collection of contemporary art
- **Mercato Metropolitano**, a bustling marketplace with food, drinks, and shopping

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Lake Como**, located 50 km north of Milan, a stunning lake with boat tours and swimming (1 hour by car, 2 hours by public transportation)
- **Bergamo**, located 45 km northeast of Milan, a historic city with a beautiful old town and stunning views (1 hour by car, 1.5 hours by public transportation)
- **Pavia**, located 35 km south of Milan, a historic city with a beautiful old town and stunning architecture (30 minutes by car, 1 hour by public transportation)
- **Turin**, located 140 km west of Milan, a historic city with a beautiful old town and stunning architecture (2 hours by car, 2.5 hours by public transportation)
- **Lugano**, located 80 km north of Milan, a beautiful city located on the shores of Lake Lugano (1.5 hours by car, 2.5 hours by public transportation)
