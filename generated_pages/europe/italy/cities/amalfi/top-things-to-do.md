# Top Things to Do in Amalfi — Attractions & Experiences

The best things to do in Amalfi include visiting stunning cathedrals, exploring picturesque towns perched on cliffs, and enjoying the breathtaking beauty of the Amalfi Coast, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **limoncello**-making class in a local farm to learn about the production of this famous Italian liqueur
- Attend a **cooking class** in a local trattoria to learn how to prepare traditional Amalfi dishes
- Go **kayaking** or **paddleboarding** in the crystal-clear waters of the Amalfi Coast
- Take a **wine tour** in a local vineyard to taste some of the best Italian wines
- Visit a **local market** to sample some of the fresh produce and local specialties
- Go **snorkeling** or **scuba diving** to explore the stunning underwater world of the Amalfi Coast

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Sentiero degli Dei** (Path of the Gods) for breathtaking views of the sea and surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Parco Regionale dei Monti Lattari** for stunning views and outdoor activities such as hiking and rock climbing
- Relax on the beautiful **Spiaggia di Positano** beach
- Take a boat tour to explore the stunning **Grotta dello Smeraldo** cave
- Visit the **Giardini di Villa Cimbrone** for stunning gardens and breathtaking views of the Amalfi Coast

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo della Carta** in Amalfi to learn about the history of paper production in the region
- Attend a performance at the **Teatro di Ravello** to enjoy some of the best Italian music and theater
- Explore the **Chiostro del Paradiso** in Amalfi to admire the beautiful cloister and stunning architecture
- Visit the **Cattedrale di Sant'Andrea** in Amalfi to admire the stunning Byzantine architecture and beautiful mosaics
- Take a **pottery class** in a local workshop to learn about the traditional ceramics of the region

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the charming town of **Conca dei Marini** with its stunning beaches and beautiful architecture
- Explore the **Valle delle Ferriere** nature reserve with its stunning waterfalls and beautiful scenery
- Take a boat tour to the **Li Galli Islands** for stunning views and beautiful beaches
- Visit the **Museo Correale di Terranova** in Sorrento to learn about the art and history of the region
- Attend a **local festival** to experience the vibrant culture and traditions of the Amalfi Coast

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Pompeii and Herculaneum** (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1 hour) to explore the ancient Roman cities
- Visit the **Island of Capri** (distance: 20 km, travel time: 30 minutes) for stunning natural beauty and charming towns
- Explore the **town of Ravello** (distance: 10 km, travel time: 20 minutes) with its stunning villas and beautiful gardens
- Take a day trip to **Sorrento** (distance: 30 km, travel time: 45 minutes) to enjoy the stunning views of the Bay of Naples
- Visit the **town of Positano** (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes) with its colorful houses and beautiful beaches
