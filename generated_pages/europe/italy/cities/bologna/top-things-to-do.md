# Top Things to Do in Bologna — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Bologna, visit the iconic **Two Towers**, explore the historic **Piazza Maggiore**, and indulge in the city's renowned **cuisine** at traditional trattorias like **Trattoria al Moro**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **food tour** to sample the city's famous **tagliatelle**, **tortellini**, and **prosciutto di Parma** at local eateries like **Mercato di Mezzo**
- Explore the **Quadrilatero**, a historic neighborhood filled with narrow streets, charming shops, and traditional restaurants
- Visit the **Bologna Cathedral** and climb to the top for breathtaking views of the city
- Attend a performance at the **Teatro Comunale**, one of Italy's most renowned opera houses
- Take a **cooking class** to learn the art of traditional Bolognese cuisine
- Go **shopping** at the **Galleria Cavour**, a luxurious shopping center featuring high-end boutiques and designer brands
- Explore the **Jewish Ghetto**, a historic neighborhood with a rich cultural heritage

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Giardini Margherita**, a beautiful park featuring gardens, walking trails, and a picturesque lake
- Explore the **Parco dei Cedri**, a scenic park with walking trails, gardens, and stunning views of the city
- Take a **hike** in the **Appennine Mountains**, which offer breathtaking views and scenic trails
- Visit the **Reno River**, which runs through the city and offers scenic views and walking trails
- Explore the **Orto Botanico**, a beautiful botanical garden featuring a wide variety of plant species

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]

- Explore the **Pinacoteca Nazionale di Bologna**, which features an extensive collection of Italian and European art
