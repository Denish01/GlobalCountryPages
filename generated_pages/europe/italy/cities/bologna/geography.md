[SECTION] Location & Borders [/SECTION]
Bologna is situated in the heart of the Po Valley, approximately 100 kilometers east of Milan and 300 kilometers north of Florence. The city is nestled in a strategic location, with the Apennine Mountains to the south and the Po River to the north. Bologna is surrounded by the provinces of Modena to the west and Ferrara to the east, and is connected to the Adriatic Sea by the Po River. The city's location has made it an important hub for trade and commerce throughout history.

The city's borders are surrounded by a mix of urban and rural landscapes, with the metropolitan area of Bologna extending beyond the city limits. The neighboring provinces of Modena and Ferrara offer a range of landscapes, from the rolling hills of the Apennines to the flat plains of the Po Valley. The city's proximity to the Adriatic Sea and the Apennine Mountains has also made it a popular destination for tourists and travelers.
