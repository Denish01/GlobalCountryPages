# Utility & Internet Costs in Slovakia

The cost of utilities and internet in Slovakia can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Slovakia has a well-developed utility infrastructure, with a high percentage of households connected to the electricity, water, and gas grids. The quality of the utility infrastructure is generally good, with minimal disruptions to services. However, costs can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher electricity and gas bills during the winter months due to increased demand for heating. The inflation rate in Slovakia is relatively low, at 2.8% in 2024, which helps to keep utility costs stable.

The cost of utilities in Slovakia is also influenced by the country's geography, with colder winters and hotter summers resulting in higher energy consumption. Additionally, the cost of utilities can vary depending on the region, with urban areas tend to have higher costs than rural areas. Overall, the utility costs in Slovakia are relatively affordable compared to other European countries, with an average monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment ranging from $150 to $300 USD.
