[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit a traditional **Slovak village** and experience local customs and cuisine
- Take a **wine tour** in the Small Carpathian Wine Region and taste some of Slovakia's best wines
- Attend a **folk festival** and experience traditional music and dance
- Explore the **caves of the Slovak Karst** and marvel at the stunning rock formations
- Visit a **traditional Slovak market** and try local food and drinks
- Take a **hiking tour** in the High Tatras and enjoy the stunning scenery and wildlife
