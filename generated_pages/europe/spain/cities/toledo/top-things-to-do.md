[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **guided tour** of the city's hidden passageways and tunnels to discover its rich history and secrets
- Visit the **Toledo Market** to sample local delicacies and wines
- Attend a **flamenco show** at a local tablao to experience the passion and energy of this traditional Spanish dance
- Take a **pottery class** to learn about the city's traditional crafts and create your own ceramic masterpiece
- Explore the **Jewish Quarter** to discover the city's rich Jewish heritage and history
- Visit the **Toledo Crystal Factory** to see the artisans at work and learn about the city's famous crystal production
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the city to see its stunning architecture and landscapes from a unique perspective

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Tagus River** and take a boat tour to see the city from a different perspective
- Visit the **Cabañeros National Park** to see its stunning natural beauty and wildlife
- Take a **hike** in the **Toledo Mountains** to enjoy the scenic views and fresh air
- Visit the **Toledo Botanical Garden** to see its beautiful plants and flowers
- Explore the **River Tajo Natural Park** to see its stunning natural beauty and wildlife

- Visit the **Museo de Santa Cruz** to see its stunning art and artifacts
