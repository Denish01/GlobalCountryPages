# Where to Stay in Bilbao — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When deciding where to stay in Bilbao, consider the city's seven main neighborhoods, each offering a unique blend of culture, entertainment, and accommodation options.

**Casco Viejo** (Bilbao)
Best For: backpacker / family
Vibe: This historic neighborhood is known for its narrow streets, picturesque plazas, and lively atmosphere, making it perfect for those who want to experience the authentic Bilbao. The area is filled with shops, restaurants, and bars, creating a vibrant and energetic vibe.
Price Range: $80-150/night
Pros: rich history, lively nightlife, proximity to major landmarks
Cons: can be noisy, limited parking options

**Abando** (Bilbao)
Best For: luxury / business
Vibe: Located in the heart of Bilbao, Abando is a modern and upscale neighborhood that offers a sophisticated and elegant atmosphere, with many high-end hotels, restaurants, and shops. The area is also home to the famous Guggenheim Museum, making it a hub for art and culture.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: modern amenities, proximity to Guggenheim Museum, upscale dining options
Cons: can be expensive, limited budget-friendly options

**Indautxu** (Bilbao)
Best For: family / business
Vibe: This charming neighborhood is known for its beautiful parks, historic buildings, and family-friendly atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for those traveling with children. The area is also home to many restaurants and cafes, offering a wide range of dining options.
Price Range: $100-200/night
Pros: family-friendly, historic architecture, proximity to parks
Cons: can be quiet, limited nightlife options

**Deusto** (Bilbao)
Best For: luxury / family
Vibe: Located on the outskirts of Bilbao, Deusto is a upscale neighborhood that offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere, with many beautiful parks and gardens. The area is also home to many high-end hotels and restaurants, making it a great option for those looking for a luxurious experience.
Price Range: $120-250/night
Pros: peaceful atmosphere, proximity to parks, luxurious amenities
Cons: can be far from city center, limited budget-friendly options

**Bilbao La Vieja** (Bilbao)
Best For: backpacker / artist
Vibe: This bohemian neighborhood is known for its street art, independent shops, and alternative vibe, making it a great destination for artists and backpackers. The area is also home to many bars and restaurants, offering a lively and eclectic atmosphere.
Price Range: $60-120/night
Pros: artistic vibe, independent shops, lively nightlife
Cons: can be noisy, limited amenities

**Santutxu** (Bilbao)
Best For: family / local
Vibe: This charming neighborhood is known for its narrow streets, historic buildings, and family-friendly atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for those looking for a local experience. The area is also home to many restaurants and cafes, offering a wide range of traditional Basque cuisine.
Price Range: $80-150/night
Pros: family-friendly, historic architecture, traditional cuisine
Cons: can be quiet, limited nightlife options

**Errekalde** (Bilbao)
Best For: business / luxury
Vibe: Located in the heart of Bilbao, Errekalde is a modern and upscale neighborhood that offers a sophisticated and elegant atmosphere, with many high-end hotels, restaurants, and shops. The area is also home to many business centers, making it a great option for business travelers.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: modern amenities, proximity to business centers, upscale dining options
Cons: can be expensive, limited budget-friendly options

[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]
- Book in advance, especially during peak season (June to August) to ensure availability and the best prices.
- Use online booking platforms such as Booking.com, Expedia, or Airbnb to compare prices and find the best deals.
- Consider staying in a guesthouse or hostel for a more local experience and to save money.
- Look for hotels or apartments with a central location to make the most of your time in Bilbao.

- Negotiate the price, especially if you're booking directly with a hotel or guesthouse.
