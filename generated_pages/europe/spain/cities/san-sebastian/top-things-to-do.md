# Top Things to Do in San Sebastian — Attractions & Experiences

San Sebastian, a charming coastal city in the Basque region of Spain, offers a wide range of exciting activities and experiences for visitors, from stunning beaches and scenic hiking trails to world-class cuisine and unique cultural attractions.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **pintxo**-hopping tour through the city's old town, sampling the local cuisine and drinks at various bars and restaurants
- Visit the **La Bretxa** market, a bustling marketplace filled with fresh produce, cheese, and other local specialties
- Attend a **jai alai** game, a fast-paced and exciting sport unique to the Basque region
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Basque dishes
- Go **surfing** or **paddleboarding** in the Bay of Biscay
- Explore the **Parte Vieja**, the city's historic old town, filled with narrow streets, charming shops, and picturesque plazas

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Island of Santa Clara**, a small island in the Bay of Biscay with stunning views and beautiful beaches
- Hike to the top of **Monte Ulia**, a scenic hill with breathtaking views of the city and the surrounding countryside
- Explore the **Peñas de Aya**, a range of hills with stunning views and scenic hiking trails
- Relax on the beach at **Ondarreta**, a beautiful urban beach with calm waters and stunning views
- Visit the **Guipuzcoa Coast**, a scenic coastline with stunning cliffs, beaches, and coves

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **San Telmo Museum**, a museum showcasing the history and culture of the Basque people
- Attend a performance at the **Victoria Eugenia Theatre**, a beautiful example of Belle Epoque architecture
- Explore the **Koldo Mitxelena Cultural Centre**, a cultural center with a wide range of exhibitions and performances
- Visit the **Museo Chillida Leku**, a museum showcasing the work of the famous Basque sculptor Eduardo Chillida
- Take a **guided tour** of the city's historic old town, filled with narrow streets, charming shops, and picturesque plazas

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ergobia**, a small, independent bookstore with a wide range of books and a cozy atmosphere
- Explore the **Antiguo**, a charming neighborhood filled with narrow streets, picturesque plazas, and historic buildings
- Relax in the **Cristina Enea Park**, a beautiful green space with stunning views of the city
- Take a **stroll along the river**, a scenic walkway that runs along the river and offers stunning views of the city
- Visit the **Tabakalera**, a former tobacco factory turned cultural center with a wide range of exhibitions and performances

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Bilbao**, the capital of the Basque region, and visit the famous **Guggenheim Museum** (distance: 60 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **French Basque Country**, a scenic region with stunning views, charming villages, and a unique culture (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 45 minutes)
- Explore the **Rioja wine region**, a scenic region with stunning views, charming villages, and some of the world's best wine (distance: 120 miles, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a day trip to **Vitoria-Gasteiz**, the capital of the Basque region's interior, and visit the **Cathedral of Santa Maria** (distance: 60 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Getaria**, a charming fishing village with stunning views, beautiful beaches, and a unique culture (distance: 20 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
