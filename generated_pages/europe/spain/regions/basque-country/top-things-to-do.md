# Top Things to Do in Basque Country — Attractions & Experiences

The Basque Country, an autonomous region in northern Spain and southwestern France, offers a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty, with top things to do including exploring the Guggenheim Museum in Bilbao, visiting the historic city of San Sebastian, and hiking in the Pyrenees mountains.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Pintxos**-hopping in San Sebastian: sample the region's famous small plates and local wines
- Attend a traditional **Basque pelota** match: a fast-paced game played with a long scoop-like basket
- Take a **Txuleta**-making workshop: learn the art of preparing the region's famous steak
- Visit a **Cider House**: taste the region's famous cider and learn about its production
- Explore the **Basque Coast**: scenic coastal route with stunning views and picturesque towns
- Learn **Euskara**, the Basque language: take a language course or attend a cultural event
- Attend a **Festival** in Bilbao or San Sebastian: experience the region's vibrant cultural scene

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]

- **Itzurun Beach**: scenic beach with stunning rock formations and picturesque coastal views
- **Urdaibai Biosphere Reserve**: protected area with diverse wildlife and scenic coastal views
- **Guernica Forest**: scenic forest with historic significance and picturesque walking trails
- **Zumaia Beach**: scenic beach with stunning rock formations and picturesque coastal views
