# Cost of Living in Valencian Community - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Valencian Community is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $60-120 USD per day for a mid-range traveler.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $40-80 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $100-200 USD
Currency: EUR
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 5-10% in restaurants and bars
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Valencian Community? [/SECTION]
Valencian Community, an autonomous region in eastern Spain, offers a unique blend of urban and coastal living at a moderate cost. Compared to other regions in Europe, Valencian Community is relatively affordable, with prices lower than in major cities like Madrid or Barcelona. However, it is more expensive than some of its neighboring regions in Spain. The cost of living in Valencian Community is influenced by its tourist industry, with prices for accommodation and food varying depending on the season and location.

In general, Valencian Community is a great destination for travelers who want to experience the beauty of Spain without breaking the bank. With its rich culture, beautiful beaches, and vibrant cities, Valencian Community offers a great value for money. Whether you're a backpacker, a mid-range traveler, or looking for a comfortable stay, Valencian Community has something to offer.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider visiting during the off-season (October to May) to save on accommodation and tourist attractions.
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls to save on food costs.
- Use public transportation instead of taxis to get around.
- Look for apartments outside the city center to save on rent.
- Take advantage of free walking tours and visit local markets to experience the culture without spending money.
- Avoid eating at touristy restaurants and opt for local cuisine instead to save on food costs.
