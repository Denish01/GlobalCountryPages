# Top Things to Do in Spain — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Spain, you should not miss the opportunity to explore its rich cultural heritage, stunning architecture, and breathtaking natural landscapes, from the artistic treasures of Madrid and Barcelona to the beautiful beaches of the Costa Brava and the historic city of Seville.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **flamenco** dance class in Seville to learn about this traditional Spanish art form
- Visit a **tapas** bar in Madrid to sample local cuisine and wine
- Attend a **tomatina** festival in Buñol, where participants throw tomatoes at each other
- Explore the **caves of Nerja**, a network of underground caves and passageways
- Take a **wine tour** in the Rioja region to learn about Spanish wine production
- Visit the **Feria de Abril** in Seville, a colorful festival featuring music, dance, and food
- Experience the **Running of the Bulls** in Pamplona, a thrilling and unique event

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike in the **Picos de Europa National Park**, a beautiful mountain range with stunning scenery
- Visit the **Costa Brava**, a scenic coastline with beautiful beaches and coves
- Explore the **Doñana National Park**, a protected area with diverse wildlife and ecosystems
- Take a **surfing** lesson in the **Canary Islands**, a popular destination for water sports
- Visit the **Tabernas Desert**, a unique and beautiful desert landscape
- Go **skiing** in the **Pyrenees**, a mountain range with several ski resorts

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo Reina Sofia** in Madrid, featuring an impressive collection of modern and contemporary art
- Explore the **historic center of Salamanca**, a beautiful and historic city with stunning architecture
- Attend a **performance at the Teatro Real** in Madrid, a world-class opera house
- Visit the **Gaudí Museum** in Barcelona, dedicated to the life and work of Antoni Gaudí
- Take a **cooking class** in Barcelona to learn about traditional Catalan cuisine
- Visit the **Museo Thyssen-Bornemisza** in Madrid, featuring an impressive collection of art from the 13th to the 20th centuries

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Cadaqués**, a charming and picturesque coastal town
- Explore the **historic center of Segovia**, a beautiful and historic city with stunning architecture
- Take a **boat tour of the Mar Menor**, a large saltwater lagoon with stunning scenery
- Visit the **monastery of Montserrat**, a historic and beautiful monastery with stunning views
- Visit the **town of Ronda**, a charming and historic town with stunning architecture and scenery

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Madrid to **Toledo**, a historic city with stunning architecture and beautiful views (distance: 70 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **city of Valencia** from Barcelona, a vibrant and beautiful city with stunning architecture and beaches (distance: 300 km, travel time: 3 hours)
- Explore the **Picos de Europa National Park** from Santander, a beautiful mountain range with stunning scenery (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a day trip from Seville to **Cordoba**, a historic city with stunning architecture and beautiful scenery (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **town of Granada** from Malaga, a historic and beautiful town with stunning architecture and scenery (distance: 150 km, travel time: 2 hours)
