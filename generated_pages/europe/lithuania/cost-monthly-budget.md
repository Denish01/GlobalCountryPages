[SECTION] Budget Tier Lifestyles [/SECTION]
A budget of $2,500 per month can provide a modest lifestyle in Lithuania, with some compromises on amenities and luxuries. At this level, you can expect to share a one-bedroom apartment outside of the city center, cook most of your meals, and use public transportation. You can still enjoy some social activities, such as going to the gym or meeting friends for coffee, but you may need to be more selective about your entertainment options. For example, you may need to choose between going to the movies or going to a restaurant, as both options may be too expensive.

A mid-range budget of $3,500 per month can offer a more comfortable lifestyle, with more amenities and luxuries. At this level, you can expect to rent a one-bedroom apartment in the city center, enjoy a mix of cooking and eating out, and use a combination of public and private transportation. You can also enjoy more social activities, such as going to concerts or festivals, and taking weekend trips to nearby cities. For example, you may be able to afford a monthly gym membership, take a cooking class, or go on a weekend getaway to a nearby city.

A comfortable budget of $4,500 per month can provide a high standard of living, with many amenities and luxuries. At this level, you can expect to rent a nice two-bedroom apartment in the city center, eat out frequently, and use private transportation. You can also enjoy a wide range of social activities, such as going to the theater, attending concerts, and taking international trips. For example, you may be able to afford a private healthcare plan, take a language class, or go on a international vacation.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Strategies [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment with roommates to reduce housing costs
- Cook most of your meals instead of eating out
- Use public transportation instead of private cars or taxis
- Take advantage of free or low-cost cultural events and activities
- Shop at local markets or discount stores for groceries and household items
- Use online resources or apps to find deals and discounts on entertainment and services
- Consider purchasing a monthly public transportation pass instead of paying for individual rides
- Take advantage of Lithuania's natural beauty by exploring outdoor activities, such as hiking or cycling, instead of paying for expensive gym memberships
