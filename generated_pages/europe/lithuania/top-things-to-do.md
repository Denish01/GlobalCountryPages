[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a traditional **Lithuanian folk festival**, such as the Vilnius Festival or the Kaunas City Day, to experience the country's vibrant culture and music
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the beautiful landscapes of Lithuania, offering breathtaking views and a unique perspective
- Visit a **traditional Lithuanian village**, such as the Open-Air Museum of Lithuania, to learn about the country's rural heritage and traditions
- Explore the **street art scene** in Vilnius, with its vibrant murals and quirky galleries
- Experience a **Lithuanian sauna**, a traditional and relaxing experience that's an integral part of the country's culture
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Lithuanian cuisine, such as cepelinai and kibinai
- Go **mushroom foraging** in Lithuania's beautiful forests, with its abundant and diverse fungi species
- Attend a **traditional Lithuanian wedding**, a unique and colorful experience that showcases the country's rich cultural heritage

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Curonian Spit National Park**, a unique and fragile landscape with beautiful beaches and traditional fishing villages
- Visit the **Aukštaitija National Park**, with its stunning lakes, forests, and traditional villages
- Hike in the **Dzūkija National Park**, with its beautiful forests, rivers, and traditional villages
- Relax on the **Palanga Beach**, a popular and beautiful beach with stunning views and vibrant atmosphere
- Explore the **Žemaitija National Park**, with its stunning lakes, forests, and traditional villages
- Visit the **Lithuanian Lake District**, with its thousands of lakes, forests, and traditional villages

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Lithuania**, to learn about the country's history, culture, and traditions
- Explore the **Vilnius University**, one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Eastern Europe
- Attend a **performance at the Lithuanian National Opera and Ballet Theatre**, with its stunning architecture and world-class performances
- Visit the **M. K. Čiurlionis National Museum of Art**, with its unique and diverse collection of Lithuanian art
- Take a **guided tour of the Vilnius Old Town**, to learn about the city's rich history and cultural significance
- Attend a **traditional Lithuanian folk dance performance**, with its vibrant music and colorful costumes

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Užupis District**, a quirky and charming neighborhood with its own unique culture and traditions
- Explore the **Kernavė Archaeological Site**, a historic and culturally significant site with its ancient hill forts and museums
- Take a **boat tour of the Kaunas Reservoir**, with its stunning views and beautiful surroundings
- Visit the **Rumsiskes Open-Air Museum**, with its traditional villages and historic buildings
- Attend a **concert at the Vilnius Congress Hall**, with its stunning architecture and world-class performances
