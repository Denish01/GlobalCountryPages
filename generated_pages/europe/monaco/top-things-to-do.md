# Top Things to Do in Monaco — Attractions & Experiences

The best things to do in Monaco include visiting the famous **Casino de Monte-Carlo**, exploring the **Prince's Palace**, and taking in the stunning views of the **Larvotto Beach**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **helicopter tour** over Monaco for breathtaking views of the principality
- Visit the **Monaco Yacht Show** to see some of the world's most luxurious yachts
- Attend a **performance at the Opéra de Monte-Carlo**, one of the most famous opera houses in the world
- Take a **private tour of the Prince's Palace** to get a glimpse into the royal family's life
- Go **snorkeling or scuba diving** in the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean
- Take a **ferry to the nearby island of Sainte-Marguerite** for a relaxing day trip
- Visit the **Monaco Motor Museum** to see a collection of rare and vintage cars
- Attend a **Formula One racing event** to experience the thrill of the Monaco Grand Prix

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Jardin Exotique** for its beautiful gardens and stunning views of the Mediterranean
- Take a **hike in the nearby hills** for breathtaking views of the principality and the sea
- Go **swimming or sunbathing at Larvotto Beach**
- Take a **boat tour of the harbor** to see the city from a different perspective
- Visit the **Monaco Nature Reserve** to see a variety of marine life and plants
- Go **cycling along the coastal path** for stunning views of the Mediterranean

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Oceanographic Museum** to learn about marine life and the history of oceanography
- Attend a **performance at the Opéra de Monte-Carlo**
- Take a **guided tour of the Prince's Palace** to learn about the history of the royal family
- Visit the **Musée Chapiteau** to learn about the history of the circus
- Attend a **concert or show at the Grimaldi Forum**
- Visit the **Monaco Cathedral** to see its beautiful architecture and stunning views of the harbor
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about Monégasque cuisine and wine

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Vieux Monaco** (Old Town) to see its narrow streets and historic buildings
- Take a **walk along the **Rue Grimaldi** to see its beautiful shops and cafes
- Visit the **Marché de la Condamine** to see its colorful market stalls and try some local food
- Go **shopping at the Carrefour Mall** for a wide range of luxury goods and souvenirs
- Take a **stroll along the **Port de Fontvieille** to see its beautiful yachts and stunning views of the sea

- Go **hiking in the nearby hills** of the **Mercantour National Park**, France (approximately 30 km, 45 minutes by car)
- Take a **day trip to the island of Sainte-Marguerite**, France (approximately 10 km, 30 minutes by ferry) to see its beautiful beaches and historic fort
