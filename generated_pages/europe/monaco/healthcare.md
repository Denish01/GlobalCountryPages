- **Private health insurance** is recommended for all residents and visitors to Monaco, as it provides access to a wider range of medical services and facilities.
- **Local insurance options** are available, including the **Caisse Autonome des Retraites et de la Santé** (CARS), which provides health insurance coverage to residents and workers in Monaco.
- **Expatriate insurance** is also available, providing comprehensive coverage for expats living and working in Monaco.
- **Costs** for health insurance in Monaco vary depending on the provider and level of coverage, but are generally higher than in other European countries.
- **European Health Insurance Card** (EHIC) holders are entitled to reciprocal medical care in Monaco, but this does not provide comprehensive coverage and should be supplemented with private insurance.
- **Private insurance companies** such as Allianz and AXA offer a range of health insurance plans in Monaco, including comprehensive and specialist coverage.
