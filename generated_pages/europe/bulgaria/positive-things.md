[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Rila Monastery**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of the largest and most famous monasteries in Bulgaria, known for its stunning architecture and beautiful frescoes.
- The **Thracian Tomb of Sveshtari**, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a unique ancient tomb complex that showcases the culture and traditions of the Thracian people.
- The **Ivan Vazov National Theatre** in Sofia is one of the oldest and most prestigious theaters in Bulgaria, hosting a wide range of performances and events throughout the year.
- The **Plovdiv Old Town**, a historic neighborhood in Plovdiv, is filled with beautiful architecture, charming streets, and a rich cultural heritage.
- The **Bulgarian National Museum of History** in Sofia is one of the largest and most comprehensive museums in the country, showcasing a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits on Bulgarian history and culture.
