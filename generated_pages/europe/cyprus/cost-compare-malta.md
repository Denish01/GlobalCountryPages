[SECTION] Overview: Cyprus vs Malta [/SECTION]
Cyprus and Malta are two popular destinations in the Mediterranean, known for their warm climate, beautiful beaches, and rich history. However, when it comes to the cost of living, there are some significant differences between the two countries. Cyprus has a lower cost of living compared to Malta, with prices for accommodation, food, and transportation being generally lower. This makes Cyprus an attractive option for expats, students, and retirees who are looking for a more affordable lifestyle. The economic context of both countries is also worth considering, with Cyprus having a more diversified economy and a stronger focus on tourism, while Malta has a more service-based economy with a strong focus on finance and gaming.

The cost of living difference between Cyprus and Malta is around 10-20%, with Cyprus being the cheaper option. This means that expats and visitors can enjoy a similar quality of life in Cyprus for a lower price. However, it's worth noting that Malta has a higher GDP per capita and a more developed economy, which may be attractive to some individuals. Overall, the choice between Cyprus and Malta will depend on individual preferences and priorities, but for those looking for a more affordable lifestyle, Cyprus is likely to be the better option.
