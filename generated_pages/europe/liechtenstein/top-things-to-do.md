# Top Things to Do in Liechtenstein — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Liechtenstein, visit its stunning **Vaduz Castle**, explore the **Kunstmuseum Liechtenstein** art museum, and hike through the picturesque **Malbun** mountains.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine tasting tour** at the Prince of Liechtenstein's wine cellar to sample some of the country's best wines
- Explore the **Hans-Waldburger-Haus**, a 15th-century house that showcases the country's traditional architecture
- Visit the **Liechtenstein Olympic Museum** to learn about the country's Olympic history and achievements
- Attend a performance at the **Theater am Kirchplatz** in Schaan, which hosts a variety of cultural events throughout the year
- Go **hiking in the Rätikon mountains**, which offer breathtaking views and challenging trails
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Vaduz City Train**, which provides a unique perspective on the city's history and landmarks

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Rätikon mountains**, which offer excellent hiking and skiing opportunities
- Visit the **Malbun mountain resort**, which provides stunning views and a range of outdoor activities
- Go **hiking in the Drei Schwestern** nature reserve, which features unique rock formations and breathtaking views
- Take a **bike tour** along the **Rhine River**, which offers a scenic and relaxing ride
- Visit the **Gutenberg Castle gardens**, which feature a beautiful and tranquil environment

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kunstmuseum Liechtenstein**, which features an impressive collection of contemporary art
- Explore the **Liechtenstein National Museum**, which exhibits the country's history, culture, and natural environment
- Attend a performance at the **Theater am Kirchplatz** in Schaan, which hosts a variety of cultural events throughout the year
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Vaduz City Train**, which provides a unique perspective on the city's history and landmarks
- Visit the **Walser Museum**, which presents the history and traditions of the Walser people
- Attend a **concert at the Vaduz Cathedral**, which features stunning architecture and excellent acoustics

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museum of Fine Arts** in Vaduz, which features a unique collection of modern and contemporary art
- Explore the **Schellenberg Castle**, which offers stunning views and a rich history
- Take a **hike to the top of the Drei Schwestern**, which provides breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Burg Gutenberg**, which features a beautiful garden and a unique collection of art and artifacts
- Attend a **local festival**, such as the **Vaduz Festival**, which celebrates the country's culture and traditions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Zurich, Switzerland** (120 km, 1.5 hours), which offers a range of cultural and historical attractions
- Visit **Innsbruck, Austria** (140 km, 2 hours), which features a unique blend of modern and historical architecture
- Explore **St. Gallen, Switzerland** (50 km, 45 minutes), which boasts a beautiful old town and a range of cultural attractions
- Take a day trip to **Feldkirch, Austria** (30 km, 30 minutes), which features a charming old town and a range of outdoor activities
- Visit **Lucerne, Switzerland** (150 km, 2 hours), which offers a range of cultural and historical attractions, including the famous Chapel Bridge
