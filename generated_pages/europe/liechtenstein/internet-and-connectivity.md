[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
The internet quality in Liechtenstein is generally high, with fast download speeds and reliable connections. The country's small size and well-developed infrastructure contribute to its excellent mobile network coverage, with all major carriers offering 4G and 5G services. While there is a slight urban vs rural divide in terms of internet speeds, even rural areas have access to reliable and fast internet connections. The country's internet services are also very reliable, with minimal downtime and fast repair times in case of outages.

The high level of internet development in Liechtenstein can be attributed to its strong economy and high standard of living. The country has invested heavily in its telecommunications infrastructure, and it shows in the quality of its internet services. Whether you're a tourist or a remote worker, you can expect to have access to fast and reliable internet connections throughout the country. Additionally, the country's proximity to Switzerland and Austria means that it has access to a large and well-developed telecommunications market, which has driven competition and innovation in the sector.

[SECTION] Getting a SIM Card [/SECTION]
Getting a SIM card in Liechtenstein is relatively easy, with several options available for tourists and visitors. The main carriers, Swisscom, Salt, and Orange, all offer tourist-friendly plans with affordable data rates. You can buy a SIM card at any of the carrier's stores, as well as at many convenience stores and newsstands throughout the country. To purchase a SIM card, you will need to provide a valid passport and proof of address, although some carriers may not require the latter. It's recommended to buy a SIM card from one of the main carriers, as they offer the best coverage and speeds.
