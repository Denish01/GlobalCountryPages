[SECTION] Foreign Ownership Rules [/SECTION]
- Foreigners can buy property in Liechtenstein, but they must obtain a **residence permit** or a **work permit** to do so.
- Non-EU citizens may face additional restrictions and requirements, such as obtaining a **special permit** from the government.
- Foreign buyers can also consider setting up a **company structure** to purchase property, which can provide additional benefits and flexibility.
- Leasehold arrangements are also available, which can provide a more flexible and affordable option for foreign buyers.
- Foreign buyers should be aware of the **tax implications** of buying property in Liechtenstein, including the requirement to pay **wealth tax** and **income tax**.
