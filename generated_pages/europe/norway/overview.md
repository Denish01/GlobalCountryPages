[SECTION] What Is Norway? [/SECTION]
Norway is a **sovereign** country located in Northern Europe, bordering the North Sea and the Arctic Circle. It shares borders with Sweden, Finland, and Russia, and its geographic position has played a significant role in shaping its history and culture. Norway is known for its stunning natural beauty, with fjords, mountains, and waterfalls that attract millions of tourists every year. The country has a strong economy, driven by its oil and gas industry, as well as a highly developed social welfare system.

Norway's unique blend of traditional and modern culture is reflected in its vibrant cities, such as Oslo and Bergen, which offer a range of cultural attractions, including museums, galleries, and festivals. The country is also famous for its **Viking** heritage, with many historical sites and artifacts that showcase its rich history. From the stunning Northern Lights to the picturesque villages, Norway has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Norway's **constitutional monarchy** system of government has been in place since 1814, with the monarch serving as the head of state and the prime minister as the head of government. The country has a strong tradition of democracy and human rights, and is a member of several international organizations, including the United Nations and the European Economic Area.
