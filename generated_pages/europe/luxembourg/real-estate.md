[SECTION] Market Overview [/SECTION]

The Luxembourg government has implemented various measures to regulate the property market and prevent speculation, including **rent control** and **property taxes**. Despite these measures, the market remains attractive to investors and buyers, with many opportunities for **long-term** growth and **rental income**.

[SECTION] Foreign Ownership Rules [/SECTION]
- Foreigners can buy property in Luxembourg, but they must obtain a **residence permit** if they plan to live in the country.
- There are no restrictions on foreigners buying property for **investment purposes**, but they must register with the **Luxembourg tax authorities**.
- Foreigners who buy property in Luxembourg must also comply with **money laundering regulations** and provide proof of **funds**.
- Foreign companies can also buy property in Luxembourg, but they must be registered with the **Luxembourg trade register**.
- Foreigners who plan to **rent out** their property must obtain a **rental license** from the local authorities.
