[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
The internet in Luxembourg is known for its high quality and reliability, with fast average internet speeds of 104 Mbps download, making it one of the fastest in the world. The country's urban areas, such as Luxembourg City, have excellent internet coverage, with most homes and businesses having access to high-speed internet. However, there is a slight urban vs rural divide, with rural areas having slightly slower internet speeds, but still relatively fast compared to other European countries. The internet infrastructure in Luxembourg is highly reliable, with minimal downtime and fast maintenance, making it an attractive destination for remote workers and businesses.

In general, the internet in Luxembourg is well-developed and widely available, with most cafes, restaurants, and public spaces offering free WiFi. The country's mobile network coverage is also excellent, with all major carriers offering high-speed 4G and 5G networks. This makes it easy for tourists and residents to stay connected and access the internet from anywhere in the country. Additionally, Luxembourg has a highly developed fiber-optic network, which provides fast and reliable internet connections to homes and businesses.

[SECTION] Getting a SIM Card [/SECTION]

To buy a SIM card, you will need to provide a valid passport and proof of address, and you can find SIM card vendors at most mobile phone stores, supermarkets, and newsagents. Recommended carriers include POST Luxembourg and Orange Luxembourg, which offer a range of data plans and packages, including unlimited data options.
