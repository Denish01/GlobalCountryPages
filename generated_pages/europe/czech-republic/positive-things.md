[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Historic Centre of Prague** is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its stunning architecture and historic landmarks like Charles Bridge and St. Vitus Cathedral.
- The **Kutná Hora** town is a UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its well-preserved medieval architecture and the stunning St. Barbara's Church.
- The **Czech Philharmonic** is a world-renowned orchestra, founded in 1896, and has performed with many famous conductors and musicians.
- The **National Museum** in Prague is one of the oldest and largest museums in the Czech Republic, with a collection of over 14 million items, including artifacts, artworks, and scientific specimens.
- The traditional **Czech folk music** and dance, such as polka and folk ensembles like the Czech Philharmonic Choir, are an important part of the country's cultural heritage.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Madeleine Albright**, the first female US Secretary of State, was born in Prague and has remained a prominent figure in international relations.
- The **Czech diaspora** has made significant contributions to the world, with notable individuals like **Karel Čapek**, who coined the term "robot", and **Milan Kundera**, a renowned author and playwright.
- The Czech Republic has a strong tradition of **human rights activism**, with figures like **Václav Havel**, who played a key role in the country's transition to democracy.
- The country has a highly developed **social safety net**, with a strong emphasis on social welfare, education, and healthcare.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **hospitality** of the Czech people, who are known for their warm welcome and friendly attitude towards tourists.
- The **traditional Czech cuisine**, which includes dishes like roast pork, beef goulash, and svickova, as well as a wide variety of beers and wines.
- The **stunning natural scenery**, with the **Bohemian Switzerland National Park** and the **Krkonose Mountains** offering beautiful hiking and skiing opportunities.
- The **rich cultural heritage**, with numerous museums, galleries, and historical landmarks like the **Prague Castle** and the **St. Vitus Cathedral**.
- The **vibrant city life**, with cities like **Prague** and **Brno** offering a wide range of cultural events, festivals, and entertainment options.
- The **affordability**, with the Czech Republic being one of the most affordable countries in Europe, with a low cost of living and a wide range of accommodation options.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Wallachian Open-Air Museum** in Rožnov pod Radhoštěm is a unique museum showcasing traditional Wallachian architecture and way of life.
- The **Český Krumlov** town is a charming medieval town with a stunning castle and a beautiful river running through it.
- The **Karlovy Vary** spa town is a popular destination for those seeking relaxation and wellness, with its beautiful architecture and natural hot springs.
- The **Olomouc** city is a hidden gem, with its stunning baroque architecture, beautiful parks, and rich cultural heritage, including the **Olomouc Astronomical Clock**.
