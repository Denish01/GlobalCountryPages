# Top Things to Do in Sweden — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Sweden, some of the top things to do include exploring the historic city of Stockholm, visiting the stunning natural beauty of Lapland, and experiencing the unique culture of this Scandinavian country.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dog sledding** tour through the snowy landscapes of Lapland
- Experience the **Midsummer** celebration, a unique and festive Swedish tradition
- Go on a **moose safari** in the forests of southern Sweden
- Take a **Saami cultural tour** to learn about the history and traditions of Sweden's indigenous people
- Go **ice skating** on one of Sweden's many frozen lakes and rivers
- Take a **Swedish cooking class** to learn about the country's unique cuisine and ingredients
- Go on a **Northern Lights** tour to see the stunning natural phenomenon of the aurora borealis

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the stunning natural beauty of **Abisko National Park**, with its mountains, forests, and lakes
- Hike through the **Kungsleden** trail, a 440km trail that takes you through some of Sweden's most beautiful landscapes
- Visit the **Lake District**, a region of stunning lakes, forests, and mountains
- Go **skiing** or **snowboarding** at one of Sweden's many ski resorts, such as Åre or Riksgränsen
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Stockholm Archipelago**, with its thousands of islands and stunning natural beauty

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Sweden**, which features a vast collection of Swedish art and cultural artifacts
- Explore the **Stockholm City Museum**, which tells the story of the city's history and development
- Attend a performance at the **Royal Swedish Opera**, one of the country's most renowned cultural institutions
- Visit the **Nobel Museum**, which showcases the history and winners of the Nobel Prize
- Take a **Swedish language class** to learn about the country's unique language and culture
- Visit the **Historical Museum of Sweden**, which features a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits on Swedish history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the charming town of **Sigtuna**, with its well-preserved medieval architecture and stunning natural beauty
- Explore the **Tyresta National Park**, a lesser-known park with stunning forests and lakes
- Take a **tour of the Swedish Royal Palaces**, which offers a unique glimpse into the country's royal history and traditions
- Visit the **Bishop's Arms**, a historic pub in Stockholm that features a vast selection of beers and whiskeys
- Go to the **Fotografiska** museum in Stockholm, which features a unique and eclectic collection of photography exhibits

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Stockholm to the **Sigtuna** town, which is located about 45km north of the city and features stunning natural beauty and well-preserved medieval architecture (travel time: 30 minutes by car or train)
- Visit the **Uppsala** city, which is located about 70km north of Stockholm and features a stunning cathedral and a rich cultural heritage (travel time: 30 minutes by car or train)
- Go on a day trip from Gothenburg to the **Bohuslän** coast, which features stunning natural beauty and a unique cultural heritage (travel time: 1 hour by car or train)
- Take a day trip from Malmö to the **Öresund Bridge**, which connects Sweden to Denmark and offers stunning views of the sea and surrounding landscape (travel time: 30 minutes by car or train)
- Visit the **Gotland** island, which is located in the Baltic Sea and features stunning natural beauty and a unique cultural heritage (travel time: 3 hours by ferry from the mainland)
