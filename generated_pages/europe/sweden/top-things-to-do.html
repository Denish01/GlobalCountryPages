Unique Experiences

  • Take a dog sledding tour through the snowy landscapes of Lapland
  • Experience the Midsummer celebration, a unique and festive Swedish tradition
  • Go on a moose safari in the forests of southern Sweden
  • Take a Saami cultural tour to learn about the history and traditions of Sweden's indigenous people
  • Go ice skating on one of Sweden's many frozen lakes and rivers
  • Take a Swedish cooking class to learn about the country's unique cuisine and ingredients
  • Go on a Northern Lights tour to see the stunning natural phenomenon of the aurora borealis

Outdoor & Nature

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the National Museum of Sweden, which features a vast collection of Swedish art and cultural artifacts
  • Explore the Stockholm City Museum, which tells the story of the city's history and development
  • Attend a performance at the Royal Swedish Opera, one of the country's most renowned cultural institutions
  • Visit the Nobel Museum, which showcases the history and winners of the Nobel Prize
  • Take a Swedish language class to learn about the country's unique language and culture
  • Visit the Historical Museum of Sweden, which features a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits on Swedish history

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the charming town of Sigtuna, with its well-preserved medieval architecture and stunning natural beauty
  • Explore the Tyresta National Park, a lesser-known park with stunning forests and lakes
  • Take a tour of the Swedish Royal Palaces, which offers a unique glimpse into the country's royal history and traditions
  • Visit the Bishop's Arms, a historic pub in Stockholm that features a vast selection of beers and whiskeys
  • Go to the Fotografiska museum in Stockholm, which features a unique and eclectic collection of photography exhibits
