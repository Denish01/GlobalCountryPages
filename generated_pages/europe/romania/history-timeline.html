The modern Romanian state began to take shape in the 19th century, with the Wallachian and Moldavian principalities uniting to form the United Principalities of Moldavia and Wallachia in 1859. Romania declared its independence from the Ottoman Empire in 1877 and became a kingdom in 1881.
