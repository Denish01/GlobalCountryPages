[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]

- The **Brâncuși Museum** in Târgu Jiu features the works of famous Romanian sculptor Constantin Brâncuși.
- The **Transylvanian Folk Architecture** is a unique and well-preserved example of traditional Romanian architecture.
- The **Romanian Athenaeum** in Bucharest is a stunning concert hall and one of the most beautiful buildings in the city.
- The **Sibiu International Theatre Festival** is one of the largest and most prestigious theatre festivals in Europe.
