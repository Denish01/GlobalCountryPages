# Top Things to Do in Moldova — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Moldova, visit its historic capital city Chisinau, explore the stunning natural scenery of the Dniester River, and discover the country's unique cultural heritage by visiting its numerous museums, monasteries, and wine cellars.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine tasting tour** at one of Moldova's many wine cellars, such as Cricova or Purcari, to sample some of the country's famous wines
- Explore the **Orheiul Vechi** cave monastery complex, a unique and historic site with stunning views
- Visit a **traditional Moldovan village**, such as Trebujeni or Butuceni, to experience rural life and traditional crafts
- Attend a **folk festival**, such as the National Folk Festival in Chisinau, to see traditional music and dance performances
- Take a **boat tour** of the Dniester River, which forms the border between Moldova and Ukraine
- Visit a **local market**, such as the Central Market in Chisinau, to try traditional foods and drinks
- Learn about **Moldovan cuisine** by taking a cooking class and learning how to make traditional dishes such as mamaliga and sarmale

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Dniester River**, which offers stunning scenery and opportunities for boat tours and fishing
- Explore the **Codrii Plaiului** forest, a protected area with diverse wildlife and hiking trails
- Go **hiking** in the **Orheiul Vechi** nature reserve, which offers scenic trails and stunning views
- Visit the **Prut River**, which forms the border between Moldova and Romania
- Take a **bike tour** of the countryside, which offers a unique perspective on rural life and scenic landscapes

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Ethnography and Natural History** to learn about Moldova's history and culture
- Explore the **Chisinau State Philharmonic**, which hosts a variety of concerts and performances
- Attend a **traditional folk dance** performance, such as the National Folk Dance Ensemble
- Visit the **Soroca Fortress**, a historic site with a museum and cultural events
- Take a **tour of a monastery**, such as the Tipova or Capriana Monastery, to learn about Moldova's religious heritage
- Visit the **National Library of Moldova**, which offers a vast collection of books and cultural events

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Rudi Monastery**, a historic monastery complex with stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere
- Explore the **Trebujeni Village**, a traditional village with historic architecture and scenic surroundings
- Take a **tour of the Chisinau underground tunnels**, a network of hidden passageways and tunnels beneath the city
- Visit the **Saharna Waterfall**, a scenic waterfall with stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere
- Visit the **Butuceni Village**, a traditional village with historic architecture and scenic surroundings

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Orheiul Vechi**, a historic site with stunning views and a unique monastery complex (distance from Chisinau: 45 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit **Soroca**, a historic town with a well-preserved fortress and scenic views (distance from Chisinau: 160 km, travel time: 3 hours)
- Explore **Tiraspol**, the capital of the breakaway region of Transnistria, with its unique Soviet-era architecture and cultural attractions (distance from Chisinau: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Take a day trip to **Purcari**, a wine cellar with stunning views and wine tastings (distance from Chisinau: 120 km, travel time: 2.5 hours)
- Visit **Cahul**, a historic town with a scenic lake and cultural attractions (distance from Chisinau: 200 km, travel time: 4 hours)
