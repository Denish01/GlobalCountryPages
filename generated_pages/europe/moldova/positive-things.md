[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]

- The **National Museum of Ethnography and Natural History** in Chisinau is a leading cultural institution, showcasing the country's rich cultural and natural heritage.
- The **Moldovan folk dance** is a traditional art form that has been recognized by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity.
- The **Soroca Fortress** is a historic fortress that dates back to the 16th century and is a major cultural landmark in Moldova.
- The **Codrii Plaiului** song and dance ensemble is a renowned cultural group that has performed traditional Moldovan music and dance around the world.
