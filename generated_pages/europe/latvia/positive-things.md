- The **National Museum of Art** in Riga showcases a wide range of Latvian and European art, including works by famous Latvian artists such as **Janis Rozentals**.
- The **Rundale Palace** is a beautiful Baroque palace, built in the 18th century and featuring an impressive collection of art and antiques.
- The **Latvian Folk Applied Arts Museum** in Riga showcases traditional Latvian handicrafts, such as textiles, ceramics, and woodcarvings.
