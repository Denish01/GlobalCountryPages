# Utility & Internet Costs in Latvia

The cost of utilities and internet in Latvia can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a medium-sized apartment (85 sqm) can expect to pay around $150-200 USD per month for basic utilities and $20-30 USD per month for internet.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Latvia has a well-developed utility infrastructure, with most households having access to **electricity**, **water**, and **heating**. The costs of utilities in Latvia are generally stable, with some seasonal variation. During the winter months, heating costs can increase significantly, as the country experiences cold temperatures. However, the overall cost of living in Latvia is relatively low compared to other European countries. The Latvian government has implemented measures to reduce energy consumption and promote the use of renewable energy sources, which has helped to keep utility costs in check.

The quality of utility services in Latvia is generally high, with most providers offering reliable and efficient services. However, there can be some variation in the quality of services depending on the location and provider. In general, urban areas tend to have better utility infrastructure and services than rural areas. The cost of utilities can also vary depending on the location, with cities like Riga tend to be more expensive than smaller towns and rural areas.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
