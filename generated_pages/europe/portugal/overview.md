[SECTION] What Is Portugal? [/SECTION]
Portugal is a **sovereign** country located in southwestern Europe, bordered by Spain to the east and north, and the Atlantic Ocean to the west and south. It is a part of the Iberian Peninsula and occupies an area of approximately 92,390 square kilometers. Portugal is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and stunning natural beauty, with a diverse landscape that ranges from rolling hills and mountains to beautiful beaches and coastal cliffs. The country has a mild climate, with warm summers and mild winters, making it a popular destination for tourists and expatriates alike.

Portugal has a long and complex history, with various periods of invasion, colonization, and independence. The country has a strong sense of national identity, with a unique culture that reflects its history, language, and traditions. The Portuguese language is the official language, and it is spoken by the vast majority of the population. Portugal is also known for its cuisine, which is characterized by a mix of Mediterranean and Atlantic influences, with popular dishes such as **bacalhau** (cod), **frango grelhado** (grilled chicken), and **pastéis de nata** (egg tarts).

Portugal is a member of the European Union and the eurozone, and it has a developed economy, with a strong focus on services, industry, and tourism. The country has a high standard of living, with good healthcare, education, and infrastructure. Portugal is also known for its vibrant cities, such as Lisbon and Porto, which offer a rich cultural experience, with historic neighborhoods, museums, and cultural events.
