[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Studenica Monastery**, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a stunning example of medieval Serbian architecture and art, featuring beautiful frescoes and intricate stone carvings.
- The **Gračanica Monastery**, another UNESCO World Heritage site, is a masterpiece of Byzantine architecture and a significant cultural and historical landmark in Serbia.
- The **Guča Trumpet Festival**, a popular cultural event, celebrates traditional Serbian music and dance, featuring performances by local and international artists.
- The **Kalemegdan Fortress**, a historic fortress located in Belgrade, offers stunning views of the city and the confluence of the Sava and Danube rivers, and is a popular tourist destination.
- The **Šumadija region**, known for its beautiful landscapes, traditional villages, and rich cultural heritage, is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in exploring Serbia's rural charm.
