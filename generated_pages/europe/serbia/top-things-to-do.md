# Top Things to Do in Serbia — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Serbia, visit its historic cities, explore its stunning natural beauty, and immerse yourself in its vibrant culture, which seamlessly blends traditional and modern elements.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Đerdap** cruise along the Danube River and explore the stunning landscapes of the Iron Gates gorge
- Enjoy a traditional Serbian **slava** celebration, which is a unique and important part of the country's cultural heritage
- Visit a **vinoduct** in the Vojvodina region and taste some of Serbia's excellent wines
- Explore the **Roman ruins** of Viminacium, which date back to the 1st century AD
- Attend a **Guča Trumpet Festival**, a lively and entertaining event that showcases traditional Serbian music
- Take a **horse-drawn carriage ride** through the scenic streets of Novi Sad's historic center
- Learn about **Serbian folklore** and traditional dance at a local cultural center

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Tara National Park**, which is known for its stunning mountains, forests, and wildlife
- Hike to the **Midžor** peak, the highest point in eastern Serbia
- Visit the **Kopaonik National Park**, which offers great skiing and hiking opportunities
- Take a boat ride on **Lake Palić**, a beautiful and peaceful spot in the north of the country
- Go **rafting** on the **Lim River**, which offers stunning scenery and exciting rapids

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum** in Belgrade, which showcases the country's rich history and culture
- Explore the **Museum of Contemporary Art** in Belgrade, which features a wide range of modern and contemporary art
- Attend a performance at the **National Theatre** in Belgrade, which hosts a variety of cultural events
- Visit the **Smederevo Fortress**, a historic fortress that dates back to the 15th century
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Serbian dishes such as **ćevapi** and **gibanica**
- Explore the **Ethnographic Museum** in Belgrade, which showcases the country's traditional folk culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ravanica Monastery**, a beautiful and historic monastery that dates back to the 14th century
- Explore the **Resava Cave**, a stunning and unique natural wonder
- Take a **wine tour** of the **Fruška Gora** region, which is known for its excellent wines
- Visit the **Bački Petrovac** village, which is known for its traditional folk architecture and cultural heritage
- Attend a **traditional Serbian wedding**, which is a unique and unforgettable experience

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip from Belgrade to **Novi Sad** (100 km, 1 hour), which is known for its historic center and cultural events
2. Visit **Smederevo** (46 km, 30 minutes from Belgrade), which is known for its historic fortress and wine production
3. Explore **Kovilj Monastery** (12 km, 15 minutes from Novi Sad), which is a beautiful and historic monastery
4. Take a day trip from **Niš** to **Mediana** (5 km, 10 minutes), which is a historic archaeological site
5. Visit **Mokra Gora** (220 km, 3 hours from Belgrade), which is known for its stunning natural beauty and the **Šargan Eight Railway**
