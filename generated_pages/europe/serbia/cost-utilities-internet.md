# Utility & Internet Costs in Serbia

The cost of utilities and internet in Serbia can vary depending on factors such as location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure in Serbia is relatively well-developed, with most households having access to basic services such as electricity, water, and gas. However, the quality of these services can vary depending on the location, with urban areas generally having more reliable and efficient utilities than rural areas. The cost of utilities in Serbia has been relatively stable in recent years, but it can fluctuate depending on factors such as inflation and changes in global energy prices. During the winter months, utility bills can be higher due to increased energy consumption for heating.

The Serbian government has implemented various measures to reduce energy consumption and promote the use of renewable energy sources, which has helped to stabilize utility costs. However, the country still relies heavily on fossil fuels, and the cost of utilities can be affected by fluctuations in global energy prices. Additionally, the cost of utilities can vary depending on the provider, with some companies offering more competitive rates than others. It's worth noting that the cost of utilities in Serbia is relatively low compared to other European countries, making it an attractive destination for expats and tourists.
