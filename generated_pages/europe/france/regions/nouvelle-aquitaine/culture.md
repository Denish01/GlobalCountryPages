# Nouvelle-Aquitaine Culture - Food, Traditions & Etiquette

Nouvelle-Aquitaine, a region in southwestern France, boasts a rich and diverse culture shaped by its unique history, geography, and blend of French and regional influences.

Nouvelle-Aquitaine's culture is defined by its strong sense of regional identity, with a blend of French and local customs. The region's cultural identity is also shaped by its geography, with the Pyrenees Mountains to the south, the Atlantic coast to the west, and the rolling hills of the Dordogne and Lot valleys. This diverse landscape has given rise to a range of cultural traditions, from the Basque culture of the Pyrenees to the wine-making traditions of the Bordeaux region. Nouvelle-Aquitaine's cultural identity is a rich tapestry of French and regional influences, with a strong sense of pride and identity among its inhabitants.

Nouvelle-Aquitaine's cultural identity is also reflected in its vibrant cities, such as Bordeaux, Bayonne, and Limoges, which offer a unique blend of history, culture, and entertainment.
