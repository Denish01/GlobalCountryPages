[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a guided **Normandy D-Day tour** to explore the historical sites and beaches of the region
- Visit a local **Norman cheese farm** to learn about the production of famous cheeses like Camembert and Pont l'Évêque
- Explore the **Bay of Mont-Saint-Michel** at low tide to discover the surrounding bay and its unique wildlife
- Attend a **Norman festival**, such as the Festival de Rouen, to experience local music and culture
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the Norman countryside to appreciate the region's rolling hills and picturesque villages
- Sample **Calvados**, a traditional Norman spirit, at a local distillery or bar
- Visit the **Musée de la Mémoire** in Caen to learn about the history of the region and its people

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Normandy American Cemetery** and its surrounding grounds
- Hike in the **Suisse Normande** region, known for its rolling hills and picturesque villages
- Visit the **Côte d'Albâtre**, a stunning coastline with white chalk cliffs
- Relax on the **Deauville Beach**, a popular spot for sunbathing and water sports
- Discover the **Marais du Cotentin et du Bessin**, a protected wetland area with diverse wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Rouen Museum of Fine Arts** to admire an impressive collection of art and artifacts
- Explore the **Bayeux Museum of Art and History** to learn about the town's rich cultural heritage
- Attend a performance at the **Théâtre des 2 Rives** in Rouen
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Norman cuisine and its ingredients
- Visit the **Musée des Beaux-Arts de Caen** to discover a diverse collection of art and artifacts

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Château de Creullet**, a lesser-known but beautifully preserved medieval castle
- Explore the **Haras du Pin**, a historic stud farm with stunning architecture and beautiful gardens
- Discover the **Village of Veules-les-Roses**, a charming and picturesque village with a rich history
- Attend a **local market** in a small town or village to experience the authentic culture and cuisine of Normandy

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Rouen** to the **Côte d'Albâtre** (60 miles, 1.5 hours), exploring the stunning coastline and picturesque villages
- Visit **Giverny** from **Paris** (45 miles, 1 hour), famous for its connection to Claude Monet and beautiful gardens
- Explore the **D-Day Beaches** from **Caen** (20 miles, 30 minutes), a series of beaches where Allied forces landed during World War II
- Discover the **Mont Saint-Michel** from **Rennes** (60 miles, 1.5 hours), a unique tidal island with medieval abbey
- Visit the **Château de Chambord** from **Tours** (100 miles, 2 hours), a beautifully preserved medieval fortress with impressive architecture
