# Top Things to Do in Occitanie — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Occitanie, visit the historic city of Carcassonne, explore the stunning natural beauty of the Pyrenees National Park, and discover the rich cultural heritage of Toulouse, the capital city of Occitanie.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Pyrenees for a breathtaking view of the mountains and valleys
- Explore the **underground caves** of the Aude region, featuring stunning rock formations and ancient fossils
- Visit a **local vineyard** in the Languedoc-Roussillon region to taste some of the world's best wines
- Attend a **traditional festival** in the city of Toulouse, such as the Fête de la Musique or the Fête des Lumières
- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to prepare traditional Occitan cuisine, such as cassoulet or tarte tatin
- Go **hiking** in the Pyrenees National Park, with trails for all levels and stunning views of the surrounding mountains
- Visit a **local market** in the city of Montpellier, featuring fresh produce, crafts, and souvenirs

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Pyrenees National Park**, featuring stunning mountain scenery, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing opportunities
- Visit the **Camargue Regional Park**, a protected area of wetlands and beaches, home to a wide variety of wildlife, including flamingos and horses
- Take a **boat cruise** along the **Canal du Midi**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most beautiful canals in the world
- Go **hiking** in the **Cévennes National Park**, featuring stunning mountain scenery and a wide variety of flora and fauna
- Visit the **Étang de Thau**, a large lake and popular destination for water sports and birdwatching
- Explore the **Gorges du Tarn**, a stunning canyon with hiking trails, waterfalls, and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Musée des Augustins** in Toulouse, a museum of fine arts with a collection of works by European masters
- Explore the **Cité de l'Espace** in Toulouse, a theme park dedicated to space exploration and science
- Attend a **performance** at the **Théâtre du Capitole** in Toulouse, a historic theater with a wide range of productions
- Visit the **Musée Fabre** in Montpellier, a museum of fine arts with a collection of works by European masters
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Château Comtal** in Carcassonne, a medieval castle with a rich history
- Explore the **Maison de la Vieille Ville** in Albi, a museum of local history and culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **village of Saint-Cirq-Lapopie**, perched on a hilltop and featuring stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- Explore the **Grotte de Niaux**, a cave featuring ancient cave paintings and stunning rock formations
- Take a **scenic drive** along the **Route des Crêtes**, a winding road with stunning views of the Pyrenees
- Visit the **Château de Peyrepertuse**, a medieval castle perched on a hilltop with stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- Explore the **village of Castelnau-de-Montmiral**, a charming village with a rich history and stunning views of the surrounding countryside
