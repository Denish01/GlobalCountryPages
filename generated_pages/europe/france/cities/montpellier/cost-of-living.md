# Cost of Living in Montpellier - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Montpellier is moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $60-120 USD for backpackers, $120-250 USD for mid-range travelers, and $250-400 USD for comfort seekers.

Montpellier, a city in southern France, has a cost of living that is relatively moderate compared to other major European cities. The city's prices are slightly lower than those in Paris, but higher than in some other French cities like Lyon or Bordeaux. Compared to global averages, Montpellier is more expensive than many cities in Eastern Europe or Asia, but less expensive than cities like New York or London.
