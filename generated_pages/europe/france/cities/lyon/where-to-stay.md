# Where to Stay in Lyon — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To find the best place to stay in Lyon, consider the top neighborhoods of Vieux Lyon, Croix-Rousse, Presqu'île, Part-Dieu, Bellecour, Guillotière, and Confluence, each offering a unique experience for different types of travelers.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
**Vieux Lyon** (Lyon)
Best For: Luxury / History Buffs
Vibe: This historic neighborhood is known for its cobblestone streets, medieval architecture, and vibrant cultural scene, offering a unique and immersive experience. Its narrow streets are filled with boutique shops, restaurants, and bars, making it perfect for those who want to be in the heart of the action.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: Rich history, cultural attractions, and a wide range of dining options, Vieux Lyon is a great choice for those who want to experience the authentic Lyon. It is also close to many major landmarks and public transportation. The neighborhood is relatively safe and well-lit at night.
Cons: It can be quite touristy and expensive, and some areas can be quite crowded.

**Croix-Rousse** (Lyon)
Best For: Backpacker / Artists
Vibe: This bohemian neighborhood is known for its street art, independent shops, and lively atmosphere, making it a great choice for young travelers and artists. The neighborhood is also home to many bars, clubs, and live music venues, making it a great spot for those who want to experience Lyon's nightlife.
Price Range: $80-150/night
Pros: Affordable, vibrant atmosphere, and a wide range of cultural attractions, Croix-Rousse is a great choice for those who want to experience the alternative side of Lyon. The neighborhood is also relatively safe and has a strong sense of community.
Cons: It can be quite noisy and crowded, especially at night.
