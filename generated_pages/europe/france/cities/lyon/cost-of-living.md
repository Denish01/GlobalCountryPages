# Cost of Living in Lyon - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Lyon is moderate to expensive, with daily budgets ranging from $60-120 USD for backpackers to $200-400 USD for comfort-seekers.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel outside of the city center to save on accommodation costs.
- Take advantage of Lyon's public transportation system, which is efficient and affordable.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls, which offer affordable and delicious meals.
- Visit local markets, such as the Marché aux Puces de Lyon, to find unique souvenirs and try local food.
- Avoid eating at touristy restaurants, which tend to be overpriced and less authentic.
- Look for discounts and promotions for museums and cultural attractions, which can help reduce entertainment costs.
