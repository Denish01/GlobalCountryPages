# Cost of Living in Lille - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Lille is generally considered moderate, with a daily budget of $80-150 USD per person for a mid-range traveler, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Lille? [/SECTION]
Lille, a city in northern France, has a cost of living that is slightly lower than the national average. Compared to other major French cities like Paris, Lille is relatively affordable, making it an attractive destination for students, young professionals, and tourists. The city's proximity to Belgium and the Netherlands also makes it an ideal base for exploring the region. Overall, Lille offers a great balance of quality of life, cultural experiences, and affordability.

In terms of global averages, Lille's cost of living is higher than many cities in Eastern Europe or Asia, but lower than many Western European cities. The city's prices are comparable to those in other mid-sized French cities, such as Lyon or Bordeaux. However, the cost of living in Lille can vary depending on factors like lifestyle, accommodation choices, and personal spending habits.
