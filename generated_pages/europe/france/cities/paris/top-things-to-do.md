# Top Things to Do in Paris — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Paris, visit the iconic Eiffel Tower, explore the world-class Louvre Museum, and stroll along the picturesque Seine River, which offer a glimpse into the city's rich history, art, and culture.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Seine River Cruise** to see the city's landmarks while learning about its history and culture
- Visit a **Parisian Cabaret** like the Moulin Rouge or Lido for a night of entertainment and glamour
- Explore the **Montmartre Neighborhood** and visit the artist studios and galleries
- Indulge in a **French Patisserie** and try a classic dessert like macarons or madeleines
- Attend a **Fashion Show** or visit a **Fashion Museum** to learn about Paris' fashion history
- Take a **Cooking Class** and learn how to prepare classic French dishes like Coq au Vin or Bouillabaisse

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Luxembourg Gardens** for a peaceful stroll and beautiful views
- Explore the **Bois de Vincennes** park and lake
- Take a **Seine River Walk** and enjoy the city's landmarks and street performers
- Visit the **Jardin des Tuileries** for a relaxing walk and beautiful gardens
- Go **Cycling** in the **Bois de Boulogne** park and enjoy the scenery

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Musée Rodin** to see the works of French sculptor Auguste Rodin
- Explore the **Centre Pompidou** and its collection of modern and contemporary art
- Attend a **Performance** at the **Palais Garnier** opera house
- Visit the **Musée Carnavalet** to learn about the history of Paris
- Take a **Guided Tour** of the **Sainte-Chapelle** and learn about its stunning stained glass windows

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Musée de la Vie Romantique** to see a collection of art and artifacts from the Romantic era
- Explore the **Canal Saint-Martin** neighborhood and its charming streets and cafes
- Take a **Stroll** through the **Père Lachaise Cemetery** and visit the graves of famous artists and writers
- Visit the **Marché aux Puces de Saint-Ouen** flea market and find unique souvenirs
- Attend a **Concert** at the **Philharmonie de Paris** and enjoy the city's music scene

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Versailles** (12 miles, 30 minutes by train) and visit the Palace and Gardens
- Visit the **Reims** cathedral and champagne cellars (80 miles, 1 hour by train)
- Explore the **Giverny** village and visit the home of Claude Monet (45 miles, 1 hour by car)
- Take a day trip to **Rouen** (70 miles, 1 hour by train) and visit the cathedral and medieval quarter
- Visit the **Fontainebleau** castle and forest (35 miles, 1 hour by train)
