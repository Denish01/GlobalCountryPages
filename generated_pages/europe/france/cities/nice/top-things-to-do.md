[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **boat tour** of the Baie des Anges, offering stunning views of the city and the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Nice Carnival**, a vibrant and colorful event that takes place every winter, featuring parades, music, and dance performances
- Explore the **Cours Saleya Market** at night, when it is transformed into a vibrant and lively space, featuring live music and local food specialties
- Take a **cooking class**, where you can learn how to prepare traditional Niçoise dishes, such as salade niçoise and ratatouille
- Go on a **wine tasting tour**, visiting local vineyards and sampling some of the region's famous wines, such as Bellet and Vermentino
- Attend a **performance at the Opéra de Nice**, a beautiful and historic theater that hosts a variety of concerts, operas, and ballets throughout the year

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Baie des Anges**, a stunning bay that offers breathtaking views and a variety of water sports, such as sailing and kayaking
- Explore the **Mont Boron**, a beautiful park that offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding landscape, and features a variety of hiking trails and scenic lookout points
- Relax on the **Plage de la Croisette**, a beautiful beach that offers a variety of amenities, including restaurants, bars, and water sports
- Take a **hike in the Mercantour National Park**, a beautiful and rugged park that features a variety of hiking trails, scenic lookout points, and stunning views
- Visit the **Jardin Botanique**, a beautiful botanical garden that features a variety of plants and flowers, and offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding landscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Chagall Museum**, which features an extensive collection of Marc Chagall's works, including paintings, stained glass, and mosaics
- Explore the **Matisse Museum**, which showcases an extensive collection of Henri Matisse's works, including paintings, sculptures, and paper cutouts
- Attend a **performance at the Théâtre National de Nice**, a beautiful and historic theater that hosts a variety of concerts, plays, and dance performances throughout the year
- Visit the **Nice Cathedral**, a beautiful example of Baroque architecture, featuring intricate stone carvings and stunning stained glass windows
- Take a **guided tour of the Old Town**, which offers a glimpse into the city's rich history and culture, and features a variety of historic landmarks and scenic streets

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
