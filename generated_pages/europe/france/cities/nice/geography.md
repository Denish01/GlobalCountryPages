[SECTION] Location & Borders [/SECTION]
Nice is situated on the southeastern coast of France, along the **French Riviera**, also known as the Côte d'Azur. The city is nestled between the **Mediterranean Sea** to the south and the **Provence Alps** to the north. To the east, Nice borders the **Italian Riviera**, while to the west, it is bounded by the cities of Cannes and Antibes. The city's location makes it a popular destination for tourists and travelers, with its stunning beaches, mild climate, and proximity to other European cities.

The city of Nice is surrounded by several smaller towns and villages, including Villefranche-sur-Mer, Beaulieu-sur-Mer, and Èze. The **Var River** flows through the city, emptying into the Mediterranean Sea. Nice is also close to several major airports, including **Nice Côte d'Azur Airport**, which is one of the busiest airports in France. The city's location and accessibility make it an ideal base for exploring the French Riviera and the surrounding regions.
