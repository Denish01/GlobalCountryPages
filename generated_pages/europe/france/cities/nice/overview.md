[SECTION] What Is Nice? [/SECTION]
Nice is a city in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region of France, situated on the Baie des Anges, a beautiful bay of the Mediterranean Sea. The city has a long history dating back to the ancient Greeks and Romans, and has been a popular tourist destination since the 18th century. Nice is known for its stunning beaches, mild climate, and rich cultural heritage, including its historic Old Town, picturesque harbor, and famous Promenade des Anglais.

The city's strategic location on the French Riviera makes it a popular destination for tourists and business travelers alike. Nice is also a major cultural and economic center in the region, with a strong focus on innovation, technology, and sustainability. The city is home to a number of universities, research institutions, and startups, making it a hub for entrepreneurship and innovation.

Nice is also a city with a rich cultural scene, with numerous museums, galleries, and festivals throughout the year. The city is home to the famous Nice Carnival, which takes place every February and March, and attracts millions of visitors from around the world. The city's cuisine is also a major draw, with a focus on fresh seafood, local produce, and traditional French dishes.
