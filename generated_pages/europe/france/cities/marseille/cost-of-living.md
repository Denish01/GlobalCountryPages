# Cost of Living in Marseille - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Marseille is moderate, with a daily budget of $60-120 USD per person for mid-range travelers, making it an affordable destination compared to other major European cities.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $40-80 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $120-250 USD
Currency: EUR
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 5-10% in restaurants and bars
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Marseille? [/SECTION]
Marseille is the second-largest city in France, located in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region. The city has a rich history, cultural attractions, and a vibrant atmosphere, making it a popular destination for tourists and expats. Compared to other major European cities, Marseille is relatively affordable, with a cost of living similar to that of other French cities like Lyon or Bordeaux. However, it is still more expensive than many Eastern European cities. The cost of living in Marseille is influenced by factors such as the city's proximity to the Mediterranean Sea, its cultural attractions, and its strong economy.

Marseille is a popular destination for tourists, with over 1 million visitors per year. The city's mild climate, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage make it an attractive destination for people from all over the world. The cost of living in Marseille is also influenced by the city's size and population, with over 850,000 inhabitants. The city has a wide range of accommodations, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels, and a diverse range of restaurants and cafes. Overall, Marseille is a great destination for travelers who want to experience the best of French culture without breaking the bank.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel outside of the city center to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls to enjoy affordable and delicious meals.
- Use public transport instead of taxis to get around the city.
- Buy a **Marseille City Pass** to enjoy discounts and free entry to many attractions.
- Shop at local markets or supermarkets to save on food and other essentials.
- Take a **free walking tour** of the city to explore its hidden gems and attractions without spending a fortune.
