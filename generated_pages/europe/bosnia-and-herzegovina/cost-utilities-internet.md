# Utility & Internet Costs in Bosnia and Herzegovina

The cost of utilities and internet in Bosnia and Herzegovina can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $250 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Bosnia and Herzegovina has a well-developed utility infrastructure, with most households having access to electricity, water, and gas. However, the cost of utilities can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher costs during the winter months due to increased heating demands. The country has a relatively stable economy, with a low inflation rate of 1.7% in 2024, which helps to keep utility costs stable. However, prices may vary depending on the region and provider, so it's essential to research and compare prices before setting up utilities.

The quality of utility infrastructure in Bosnia and Herzegovina is generally good, with most households having access to modern amenities. However, some areas may experience occasional power outages or water disruptions, especially during heavy rainfall or extreme weather conditions. It's essential to check with the provider and the local authorities for any planned maintenance or outages that may affect utility services.
