Unique Experiences

  • Take a seaweed-foraging tour along Jersey's coastline to learn about the island's unique seaweed species and their uses
  • Explore the island's vineyards and taste some of the excellent wines produced locally
  • Go on a coasteering adventure, which involves exploring the island's rugged coastline by swimming, climbing, and jumping
  • Take a fishing trip to catch some of the island's famous seafood, including lobster and crab
  • Visit a local farm to learn about Jersey's agricultural heritage and sample some of the island's delicious produce
  • Attend a traditional Jersey festival, such as the Jersey Folk Festival or the Jersey Live Music Festival

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the Jersey Coastal Path, a 48-mile trail that takes you along the island's stunning coastline
  • Visit St. Brelade's Bay, a beautiful beach with clear waters and a picturesque village
  • Go hiking in Val de la Mare, a scenic valley with stunning views of the surrounding countryside
  • Take a boat trip to the Ecrehous, a group of islands off Jersey's east coast that are home to a wide range of wildlife
  • Visit the Jersey Wetland Centre, a nature reserve with a wide range of bird species and other wildlife

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Jersey Museum and Art Gallery to learn about the island's history, art, and culture
  • Explore the Hamptonne Country Life Museum, which showcases Jersey's rural heritage and traditional way of life
  • Attend a performance at the Jersey Opera House, a historic theater that hosts a wide range of productions
  • Take a cooking class to learn about Jersey's unique cuisine and how to prepare some of the island's traditional dishes

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip to Guernsey, a neighboring island with a rich history and stunning natural beauty (distance: 43 miles, travel time: 1 hour by ferry)
  • Visit Sark, a small island with a unique culture and stunning scenery (distance: 55 miles, travel time: 2 hours by ferry)
  • Explore the Normandy coast, which is easily accessible by ferry from Jersey (distance: 100 miles, travel time: 4 hours by ferry and car)
  • Take a day trip to Cherbourg, a charming French town with a rich history and stunning architecture (distance: 120 miles, travel time: 5 hours by ferry and car)
  • Visit the Cotentin Peninsula, a scenic region with a wide range of natural beauty and cultural attractions (distance: 130 miles, travel time: 6 hours by ferry and car)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights), 7-day (comprehensive), 14-day (deep exploration). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book your accommodations and ferry tickets in advance, especially during peak season; prioritize your must-see attractions and experiences, and consider purchasing a Jersey Heritage Pass for discounted entry to many of the island's top attractions.
