[SECTION] Common Risks & Scams [/SECTION]
- Be cautious of pickpocketing in crowded tourist areas, especially during peak season.
- Bicycle theft is a common problem in many German cities, so make sure to lock your bike securely.
- Avoid carrying large amounts of cash and use reputable ATMs or currency exchange services.
- Be wary of street performers or beggars who may be aggressive or distracting.
- Some scams target tourists at airports or train stations, offering fake taxi services or overpriced transportation.
- Be mindful of your belongings in crowded bars or clubs, especially at night.
- Some areas in larger cities can experience higher crime rates, so it's essential to research your destination and stay informed.
- Be prepared for occasional demonstrations or protests, which can cause disruptions to traffic and public transportation.
