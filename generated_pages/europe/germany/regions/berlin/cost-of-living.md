[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel, which can be significantly cheaper than mid-range or luxury options.
- Take advantage of Berlin's public transportation system, which is efficient and affordable.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls, which offer a wide range of delicious and affordable options.
- Visit museums and attractions on certain days of the week or month, when admission is free or discounted.
- Avoid visiting during peak season (June to August), when prices tend to be higher.
- Look for apartments or rooms outside of the city center, which can be significantly cheaper than those in popular neighborhoods.
