# Top Things to Do in Dresden — Attractions & Experiences

Dresden, the capital city of the German state of Saxony, is a must-visit destination that offers a unique blend of stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant cultural scene, with top things to do including visiting the **Zwinger Palace**, exploring the **Dresden Elbe Valley**, and experiencing the city's renowned **Dresden State Opera**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gondola ride** on the Elbe River and enjoy the city's skyline from a different perspective
- Visit the **Dresden Striezelmarkt**, one of Germany's oldest and most famous Christmas markets
- Explore the **Kunsthofpassage**, a unique complex of courtyards and buildings with street art and independent shops
- Attend a performance at the **Dresden State Opera**, one of the world's most renowned opera companies
- Visit the **Museum of Hygiene**, a quirky museum showcasing the history of hygiene and medicine
- Take a **wine tour** in the nearby **Saxon Wine Region**, known for its excellent white wines
- Explore the **Dresden Neustadt**, a vibrant neighborhood with a rich history and cultural scene

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Saxon Switzerland National Park**, a beautiful protected area with unique rock formations and hiking trails
- Explore the **Dresden Elbe Valley**, a scenic area with stunning views of the Elbe River and city skyline
- Take a **hike** in the **Tharandt Forest**, a beautiful woodland area with scenic trails and picturesque villages
- Visit the **Pillnitz Palace and Park**, a stunning Baroque palace with beautiful gardens and a scenic location on the Elbe River
- Explore the **Königstein Fortress**, a historic fortress with stunning views of the Elbe River and surrounding countryside

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Gemäldegalerie Alte Meister**, a world-class museum with an outstanding collection of European art
- Explore the **Grünes Gewölbe**, a historic treasure chamber with precious artifacts and stunning decorations
- Attend a performance at the **Semper Opera House**, one of the world's most famous opera houses
- Visit the **Dresden Museum of Ethnology**, a museum showcasing the culture and traditions of peoples from around the world
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Dresden Castle**, a historic residence of the electors and kings of Saxony
- Explore the **Kunsthaus Dresden**, a contemporary art museum with a unique collection of modern and contemporary art

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Dresden Panometer**, a unique museum with a massive panoramic painting of the city
- Explore the **Neustadt neighborhood**, a vibrant area with a rich history and cultural scene
- Take a **stroll** along the **Elbe River**, a scenic area with stunning views of the city skyline
- Visit the **Dresden City Museum**, a museum showcasing the history and culture of the city
- Explore the **Weinbergkirche**, a historic church with stunning views of the city skyline
