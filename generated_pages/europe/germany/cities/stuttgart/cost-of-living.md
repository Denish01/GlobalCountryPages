# Cost of Living in Stuttgart - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Stuttgart, Germany is moderately high, with daily expenses ranging from $80 to $250 per person, depending on lifestyle and accommodation choices.

Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $120-180 USD

Currency: EUR
Tipping Custom: expected (5-10%)
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Stuttgart? [/SECTION]
Stuttgart, the capital of Baden-Württemberg, is a major economic and cultural hub in southern Germany. Compared to other European cities, Stuttgart has a moderate to high cost of living, similar to other major German cities like Munich and Frankfurt. However, it is still relatively more affordable than cities like Zurich or Geneva. The city's strong economy, driven by the automotive and manufacturing industries, contributes to its higher cost of living. Nevertheless, Stuttgart offers a high quality of life, with a rich cultural scene, excellent education system, and a wide range of outdoor activities, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

Overall, Stuttgart's cost of living is influenced by its proximity to the Swiss and French borders, as well as its strong local economy. While it may not be the cheapest destination in Europe, it offers a unique blend of traditional German culture, modern amenities, and natural beauty, making it a popular choice for visitors and residents.
