- The **Wieliczka Salt Mine** is a unique cultural and historical site that features intricate salt carvings and underground chapels.
- The **Auschwitz-Birkenau Memorial and Museum** is a powerful and moving tribute to the victims of the Holocaust.
- The **Polish folk music and dance** tradition is a vibrant and colorful part of the country's cultural heritage, with many different regional styles and customs.
- The **Royal Castle in Warsaw** is a stunning example of Polish architecture and a symbol of the country's rich history and cultural heritage.
