# Top Things to Do in Poland — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Poland, visit the historic city of Krakow, explore the somber Auschwitz-Birkenau Memorial and Museum, and stroll through the picturesque Old Town of Warsaw, which offer a glimpse into the country's rich history, cultural heritage, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **pierogi-making class** in Krakow to learn the art of traditional Polish cuisine
- Explore the **Wieliczka Salt Mine**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and marvel at its underground chapels and sculptures
- Attend a **Polish folk show** in Warsaw, featuring traditional music and dance
- Visit the **Wolf's Lair**, a historic military headquarters and museum, to learn about Poland's WWII history
- Go on a **Vistula River cruise** in Warsaw, offering a unique perspective on the city's landmarks
- Sample **Polish craft beer** at a local brewery or pub, and discover the country's thriving beer culture
- Visit the **European Bison Show Reserve** in Bialowieza, and spot these majestic animals in their natural habitat

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike in the **Tatra National Park**, with its stunning mountain scenery and diverse wildlife
- Explore the **Bialowieza Forest**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and spot European bison and other wildlife
- Relax on the **Baltic coast**, with its beautiful beaches and scenic coastal towns
- Visit the **Great Masurian Lakes**, a popular spot for boating and water sports
- Go skiing or snowboarding in the **Zakopane** region, with its scenic slopes and picturesque villages

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum in Krakow**, featuring an extensive collection of Polish art and artifacts
- Explore the **Warsaw Uprising Museum**, with its interactive exhibits and personal stories
- Attend a **performance at the Grand Theatre** in Warsaw, a stunning example of neoclassical architecture
- Visit the **Copernicus Science Centre** in Warsaw, with its interactive exhibits and experiments
- Take a **walking tour of Krakow's Jewish Quarter**, and learn about the city's rich Jewish heritage
- Visit the **Royal Castle** in Warsaw, a historic residence with impressive architecture and beautiful gardens

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kazimierz Dolny** town, with its picturesque streets and stunning castle ruins
- Explore the **Sandomierz** town, with its well-preserved medieval architecture and scenic views
- Go to the **Gdansk** city, with its colorful buildings and rich maritime history
- Visit the **Torun** town, the birthplace of Copernicus, with its stunning Gothic architecture
- Relax in the **Karpacz** town, a popular ski resort with scenic views and picturesque villages

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Warsaw to **Kazimierz Dolny** (120 km, 1.5 hours), and explore its picturesque streets and stunning castle ruins
- Visit **Auschwitz-Birkenau** from Krakow (60 km, 1 hour), and pay respects to the victims of the Holocaust
- Go on a day trip from Gdansk to **Sopot** (15 km, 30 minutes), and enjoy its beautiful beaches and scenic coastal promenade
- Take a day trip from Krakow to **Wieliczka** (15 km, 30 minutes), and explore the stunning salt mines and underground chapels
- Visit **Zakopane** from Krakow (100 km, 2 hours), and enjoy its scenic mountain vistas and outdoor activities
