# Poland Internet & SIM Cards — Connectivity Guide

The internet in Poland is well-developed, with reliable and fast connections available in most parts of the country, making it easy for tourists and remote workers to stay connected.

[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
Poland has made significant investments in its internet infrastructure, resulting in a high level of connectivity throughout the country. The average internet speed in Poland is 24.42 Mbps download, which is comparable to other European countries. While there is a notable urban-rural divide in terms of internet quality, with faster speeds and more reliable connections available in cities like Warsaw and Krakow, the overall internet quality is good. The mobile network coverage is excellent, with all major carriers offering 4G and 5G services in most areas.

The reliability of the internet in Poland is also noteworthy, with minimal downtime and fast repair times in case of outages. This makes Poland an attractive destination for remote workers and digital nomads who require a stable and fast internet connection to perform their work. Additionally, the widespread availability of WiFi in public places, such as cafes, restaurants, and hotels, makes it easy to stay connected even when you're not in your accommodation.

[SECTION] Getting a SIM Card [/SECTION]
Getting a SIM card in Poland is a straightforward process, with several options available for tourists and visitors. You can purchase a SIM card from one of the major carriers, such as Orange, T-Mobile, or Plus, at their retail stores or at airports and train stations. To buy a SIM card, you will typically need to provide your passport and proof of address, although some carriers may have additional requirements. It's recommended to purchase a SIM card from a major carrier, as they offer the best coverage and fastest speeds.

The cost of a SIM card in Poland varies depending on the carrier and the plan you choose, but you can expect to pay around 10-20 USD for a tourist SIM with a data plan. Here are some popular tourist plans from major carriers:
