[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Schönbrunn Palace** and Gardens, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a former imperial summer residence and one of Austria's most popular tourist attractions.
- The **Vienna State Opera** is a world-renowned opera house that hosts over 350 performances annually, featuring a wide range of opera and ballet productions.
- The **Mozart Museum** in Salzburg is dedicated to the life and works of the famous composer, and showcases his musical instruments, manuscripts, and personal belongings.
- The **Linz Festival** is a annual cultural event that celebrates music, theater, and dance, featuring performances by renowned artists and companies.
- The **Graz Old Town**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a charming and historic district filled with narrow streets, picturesque squares, and well-preserved medieval architecture.
