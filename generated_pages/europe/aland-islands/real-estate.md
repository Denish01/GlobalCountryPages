[SECTION] Foreign Ownership Rules [/SECTION]
- Foreigners are allowed to buy property in Aland Islands, but they need to obtain a **permit** from the Finnish government.
- The permit is usually granted if the buyer is a citizen of an EU or EEA country, or if the buyer has a **residence permit** in Finland.
- Non-EU citizens may face **restrictions** on buying property, but they can still buy through a **Finnish company** or a **trust**.
- Foreign buyers should also be aware of the **tax implications** of buying property in Aland Islands, including the payment of **capital gains tax**.
- It is recommended that foreign buyers **consult a lawyer** or a **real estate agent** to ensure they comply with all the necessary regulations and procedures.
