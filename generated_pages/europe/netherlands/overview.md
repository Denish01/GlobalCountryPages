[SECTION] What Is Netherlands? [/SECTION]
The Netherlands, also known as Holland, is a **sovereign** country located in Northwest Europe. It is bordered by Belgium to the south and Germany to the east, with the North Sea to the northwest. The country is known for its **flat landscape**, with a significant portion of its land below sea level, and its **elaborate system of dikes and canals** that have been built to protect the land from flooding. The Netherlands is also famous for its **tolerant and liberal society**, with a strong emphasis on **human rights** and **social welfare**.

The Netherlands has a long and rich history, with a strong **maritime tradition** that has played a significant role in shaping the country's culture and economy. The country has a **high standard of living**, with a strong economy and a high level of **education** and **healthcare**. The Netherlands is also known for its **cultural heritage**, with numerous **museums**, **art galleries**, and **historical landmarks** that attract millions of tourists every year.

The Netherlands is a **constitutional monarchy**, with a **parliamentary system** of government. The country is divided into **twelve provinces**, each with its own **regional government**. The Netherlands is a member of the **European Union** and the **United Nations**, and plays an active role in **international affairs**.
