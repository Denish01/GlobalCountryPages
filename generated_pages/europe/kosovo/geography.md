# Kosovo Geography - Location, Terrain & Climate

Kosovo is a small, landlocked country located in the Balkan Peninsula in southeastern Europe, bordered by Serbia to the north and east, North Macedonia to the south, Albania to the west, and Montenegro to the northwest.

The country's strategic location has made it an important crossroads between Europe and the Middle East.
