[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Gračanica Monastery**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a stunning example of medieval Serbian architecture and a significant cultural landmark in Kosovo.
- The **Prizren League**, a historic gathering of Albanian leaders, is celebrated as a symbol of national unity and a testament to Kosovo's rich history.
- **Epic Poetry**, a traditional form of Albanian literature, is still practiced and performed in Kosovo, with many famous poets and writers hailing from the region.
- The **Kosovo Museum**, located in Pristina, showcases the country's history, art, and culture, with a collection of artifacts and exhibits.
- The **Rugova Mountains**, a beautiful range with stunning scenery, offer opportunities for hiking, skiing, and other outdoor activities.
