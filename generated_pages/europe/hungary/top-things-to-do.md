# Top Things to Do in Hungary — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Hungary, visit its stunning capital city Budapest, explore the scenic Lake Balaton, and discover the country's rich history and culture by visiting its numerous museums, historical sites, and traditional festivals.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine tour** in the famous wine regions of Tokaj, Eger, or Villány to taste some of the country's renowned wines
- Attend a traditional **Hungarian folk dance** performance to experience the country's rich cultural heritage
- Visit a **thermal bath** and enjoy the unique Hungarian spa culture
- Explore the **ruin bars** of Budapest, a unique and quirky nightlife scene
- Take a **Danube River cruise** to see the city's stunning skyline and landmarks from a different perspective
- Learn how to make traditional **Hungarian cuisine**, such as goulash or paprikás csirke, in a cooking class

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the stunning **Aggtelek National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring unique caves and wildlife
- Hike in the **Buda Hills**, a scenic range with great views of the city and the surrounding countryside
- Visit **Lake Balaton**, the largest lake in Central Europe, perfect for swimming, sailing, and hiking
- Discover the **Danube-Ipoly National Park**, a protected area with unique wildlife and scenic landscapes
- Take a **horse-riding tour** through the Hungarian countryside to experience the country's rural beauty

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Hungarian National Museum** to learn about the country's rich history and culture
- Explore the **Budapest History Museum** to discover the city's fascinating past
- Attend a performance at the **Hungarian State Opera House**, a world-class opera company
- Visit the **St. Stephen's Basilica**, the largest church in Budapest and a prominent example of Neo-Renaissance architecture
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Parliament Building**, the seat of the National Assembly and an iconic example of Neo-Gothic architecture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the charming town of **Szentendre**, known for its colorful buildings, vibrant arts scene, and stunning river views
- Explore the **Castle District** in Budapest, a historic neighborhood with narrow streets, charming cafes, and stunning views
- Discover the **Street Art** scene in Budapest, with many unique and quirky murals and graffiti
- Take a **day trip** to the scenic town of **Eger**, known for its well-preserved castle and stunning baroque architecture
- Visit the **Hungarian Railway Museum**, a unique and fascinating museum showcasing the country's railway history

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip** to **Szentendre** (35 km, 45 minutes), a charming town with a rich history and a vibrant arts scene
- Visit **Eger** (130 km, 1.5 hours), a scenic town with a well-preserved castle and stunning baroque architecture
- Explore **Lake Balaton** (100 km, 1.5 hours), the largest lake in Central Europe, perfect for swimming, sailing, and hiking
- Discover the **Danube Bend** (50 km, 1 hour), a scenic region with stunning river views and historic towns
- Take a **day trip** to **Bratislava** (200 km, 2.5 hours), the capital city of Slovakia, with a rich history and a vibrant cultural scene
