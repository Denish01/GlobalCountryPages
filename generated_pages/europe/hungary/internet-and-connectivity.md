[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
Hungary has a well-developed internet infrastructure, with a high level of penetration and widespread availability of broadband connections. The average internet speed in Hungary is 24.51 Mbps, which is relatively fast compared to other European countries. The urban-rural divide is still present, with faster speeds and more reliable connections available in cities like Budapest, Debrecen, and Szeged, while rural areas may experience slower speeds and more frequent outages. However, the Hungarian government has been investing heavily in expanding and upgrading the country's internet infrastructure, which has improved the overall quality and reliability of the internet.

The reliability of the internet in Hungary is generally good, with most providers offering stable and consistent connections. However, as with any country, outages and disruptions can occur, especially during severe weather events or maintenance periods. It's worth noting that Hungary has a well-developed **fiber-optic network**, which provides fast and reliable connections to many parts of the country. Additionally, the country has a number of **internet exchange points**, which help to improve the overall quality and speed of the internet.
