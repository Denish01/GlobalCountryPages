# Top Things to Do in Malta — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Malta, visit its historic cities, explore ancient temples, and enjoy the beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters that surround this stunning European island nation.

[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |

| Mdina Old City | Mdina | Historic City | Well-preserved ancient city with a rich history |
| Valletta Old Town | Valletta | Historic City | UNESCO World Heritage site and capital city |
| Blue Lagoon | Comino | Beach | Famous for its turquoise waters and white sandy beach |
| Hagar Qim Temples | Qrendi | Ancient Temple | One of the oldest free-standing structures in the world |
| Tarxien Temples | Tarxien | Ancient Temple | Complex of four ancient temples dating back to 3600 BC |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Maltese boat** tour around the Blue Lagoon and Comino Island
- Explore the **underwater world** of Malta through scuba diving or snorkeling
- Attend a **Maltese festival**, such as the Malta International Arts Festival or the Valletta Baroque Festival
- Visit a **local market**, such as the Marsaxlokk Market, to experience the island's culture and cuisine
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the countryside of Malta or Gozo
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Maltese cuisine and cooking techniques

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Gozo Nature Reserve**, a protected area with stunning landscapes and wildlife
- Visit the **Blue Hole**, a natural sea cave with crystal-clear waters
- Relax on the beach at **Golden Bay**, one of Malta's most popular and beautiful beaches
- Hike through the **Victoria Lines**, a scenic trail with stunning views of the island
- Go **rock climbing** in the scenic countryside of Malta or Gozo
- Take a **boat tour** to the **Filfla Island**, a small island with a unique ecosystem and stunning scenery

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Archaeology**, which features a collection of artifacts from Malta's ancient history
- Explore the **St. John's Co-Cathedral**, a stunning Baroque church with intricate architecture and art
- Attend a **traditional Maltese folk show**, which features music, dance, and theater
- Take a **tour of the Grandmaster's Palace**, the former residence of the Grand Master of the Knights of St. John
- Visit the **Mnajdra Temples** at sunrise or sunset to experience the alignment of the ancient stones
- Explore the **Gozo Museum of Archaeology**, which features a collection of artifacts from Gozo's ancient history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Popeye Village** at night, when it is less crowded and more peaceful
- Explore the **town of Sliema**, which features a beautiful promenade and stunning views of Valletta
- Take a **hike through the countryside** of Malta or Gozo to experience the island's natural beauty
- Visit the **Xwejni Bay**, a secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and stunning scenery
- Attend a **traditional Maltese village festa**, which features music, food, and celebration

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Gozo**, which features stunning landscapes, ancient temples, and a beautiful citadel (distance: 30 km, travel time: 30 minutes by ferry)
- Visit the **Blue Lagoon** and **Comino Island**, which feature stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes by boat)
- Explore the **Mdina Old City** and the surrounding countryside, which feature stunning architecture and natural beauty (distance: 10 km, travel time: 20 minutes by car)
- Take a day trip to the **Sicilian city of Pozzallo**, which features stunning beaches and a beautiful old town (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours by ferry and car)
- Visit the **island of Filfla**, which features a unique ecosystem and stunning scenery (distance: 5 km, travel time: 1 hour by boat)
