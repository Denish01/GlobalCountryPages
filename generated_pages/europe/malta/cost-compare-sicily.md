[SECTION] Overview: Malta vs Sicily [/SECTION]
Malta and Sicily are two popular destinations in the Mediterranean, known for their rich history, stunning landscapes, and unique cultural heritage. When it comes to the cost of living, Sicily is generally considered to be more affordable than Malta. The main reason for this difference is the lower cost of housing, food, and transportation in Sicily compared to Malta. Additionally, Sicily has a lower overall cost of living index, which takes into account the prices of everyday items such as groceries, utilities, and entertainment.

The economic context of the two regions also plays a role in the cost of living difference. Malta has a more developed economy, with a strong focus on finance, tourism, and technology, which drives up the cost of living. Sicily, on the other hand, has a more traditional economy, with a strong focus on agriculture, manufacturing, and tourism, which keeps costs lower. Overall, Sicily offers a more affordable lifestyle, with a lower cost of living that is around 15-20% cheaper than Malta.
