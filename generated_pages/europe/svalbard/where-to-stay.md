[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]

[SECTION] Safety & Location Tips [/SECTION]
- Be aware of the polar bear risk and take necessary precautions, such as carrying a rifle or joining a guided tour
- Stay in well-lit and populated areas, especially at night
- Keep a safe distance from wildlife, including reindeer and arctic foxes
- Be prepared for extreme weather conditions, including cold temperatures and strong winds
- Stay informed about local conditions and follow instructions from local authorities
- Consider staying in a hotel or resort that offers guided activities and safety briefings
