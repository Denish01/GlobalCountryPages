[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dog sledding** tour across Svalbard's snowy landscapes
- Go on a **snowmobile** safari to explore the Arctic wilderness
- Experience the **Midnight Sun** during the summer months
- Join a **Northern Lights** tour to witness the spectacular display
- Participate in a **citizen science** project to contribute to Arctic research
- Take a **polar bear** viewing tour to see these majestic creatures in their natural habitat
- Go **fjord kayaking** to explore Svalbard's stunning coastline

- Go **whale watching** in the waters surrounding Svalbard
- Take a **glacier hiking** tour to explore Svalbard's glaciers up close
