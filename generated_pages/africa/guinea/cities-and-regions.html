Getting Around

Guinea has a relatively underdeveloped transportation network, with limited domestic flights, no trains, and poor road conditions. However, there are many buses and taxis available, making it possible to travel between cities and towns. The main airport is located in Conakry, and there are also several smaller airports in other cities.
