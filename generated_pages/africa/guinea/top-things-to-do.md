# Top Things to Do in Guinea — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Guinea, visit the iconic **Mount Gangan** and explore the vibrant markets of **Conakry**, but also be sure to venture out to the country's stunning natural wonders, unique cultural sites, and off-the-beaten-path destinations.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Fula** language class and learn about the culture of Guinea's largest ethnic group
- Attend a **West African** music and dance performance in Conakry
- Visit a local **jewelry market** and learn about traditional Guinean craftsmanship
- Go on a **safari** in the Fouta Djallon Highlands to spot rare wildlife
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Guinean dishes like **jollof rice** and **groundnut stew**
- Participate in a **traditional Guinean wedding** ceremony and experience local customs

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Fouta Djallon Highlands**, a scenic region with hiking trails and traditional villages
- Visit **Îles de Los**, a group of islands off the coast of Conakry with pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters
- Hike to the **Waterfalls of Ditinn**, a picturesque series of waterfalls and surrounding tropical rainforest
- Go on a **safari** in the **Badiar National Park**, home to a variety of wildlife including elephants and lions
- Visit the **Canyon de Kakoulima**, a unique rock formation with stunning views
- Relax on the **beaches of Conakry**, including the popular **Riviera** and **Kassa** beaches

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Guinea**, which showcases the country's rich history and cultural heritage
- Explore the **Grand Mosque of Conakry**, one of the largest mosques in West Africa
- Attend a **traditional Guinean** music and dance performance at the **Palais du Peuple** in Conakry
- Visit the **Almamya Mosque**, a historic mosque and important Islamic site in Guinea
- Take a **craft workshop** and learn about traditional Guinean craftsmanship, such as **textiles** and **woodcarving**
- Visit the **Maison des Esclaves**, a museum showcasing the history of the transatlantic slave trade

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **village of Dalaba**, a scenic town with traditional architecture and stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- Explore the **markets of Labé**, a city in the Fouta Djallon Highlands known for its vibrant markets and traditional crafts
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Îles de Los**, a group of islands off the coast of Conakry with pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters
- Visit the **waterfalls of Kambadaga**, a picturesque series of waterfalls and surrounding tropical rainforest
- Go on a **hike** in the **Badiar National Park**, home to a variety of wildlife including elephants and lions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Conakry to the **Îles de Los**, a group of islands with pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Fouta Djallon Highlands** from Labé, a scenic region with hiking trails and traditional villages (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Explore the **Canyon de Kakoulima** from Kindia, a unique rock formation with stunning views (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Take a day trip from Conakry to the **Waterfalls of Ditinn**, a picturesque series of waterfalls and surrounding tropical rainforest (distance: 70 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Grand Mosque of Conakry** and the **National Museum of Guinea** from Conakry, two of the city's most important cultural sites (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
