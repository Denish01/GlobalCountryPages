Practical Tips

  • Book your flights and accommodations well in advance, especially during the peak season, to avoid high prices and availability issues.
  • Consider visiting Guinea during the shoulder season, from April to May or from September to November, to avoid the crowds and enjoy lower prices.

  • Be respectful of the local culture and traditions, especially during Ramadan and other Muslim holidays.
  • Learn some basic French phrases, such as "bonjour" and "merci," to interact with the locals and show respect for their culture.
