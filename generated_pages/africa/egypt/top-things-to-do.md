# Top Things to Do in Egypt — Attractions & Experiences

Egypt is a country with a rich history and diverse landscapes, offering a wide range of activities and experiences that cater to different interests, from exploring ancient **pyramids** and **temples** to relaxing on beautiful **beaches** and enjoying the vibrant city life.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pyramids of Giza | Giza | Ancient Monument | The last of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World still standing |
| The Great Sphinx | Giza | Ancient Monument | A giant limestone statue with the body of a lion and the head of a human |
| Egyptian Museum | Cairo | Museum | Home to the world's largest collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts |
| Abu Simbel Temples | Aswan | Ancient Temple | A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring two massive rock-cut temples |
| Valley of the Kings | Luxor | Ancient Necropolis | A vast burial ground for pharaohs and nobles of ancient Egypt |
| Khan el-Khalili Market | Cairo | Market | One of the oldest and most famous bazaars in the Middle East |
| Saint Catherine's Monastery | Sinai | Monastery | A historic monastery located at the foot of Mount Sinai |
| Temple of Karnak | Luxor | Ancient Temple | A vast complex of sanctuaries, halls, and obelisks dedicated to the Theban gods |
| Citadel of Cairo | Cairo | Fortress | A medieval Islamic fortification that offers stunning views of the city |
| Philae Temple | Aswan | Ancient Temple | A beautiful island temple dedicated to the goddess Isis |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **felucca** ride along the Nile River and enjoy the sunset while learning about the history and culture of Egypt
- Explore the **Nubian villages** and experience the traditional way of life of the Nubian people
- Visit a **local market** and try some of the traditional Egyptian food, such as **koshari** and **ful medames**
- Attend a **Sufi whirling dervish** performance and learn about the mystical traditions of Islam
- Take a **camel ride** through the desert and enjoy the stunning views of the **pyramids** at sunset
- Visit a **papyrus factory** and learn about the ancient art of papyrus making
- Enjoy a **traditional Egyptian coffee** at a local café and watch the world go by

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **White Desert** and its unique limestone formations
- Visit the **Ras Mohammed National Park** and snorkel or dive in the beautiful coral reefs of the Red Sea
- Take a **hike** in the **Sinai Mountains** and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Relax on the **beaches** of **Sharm El-Sheikh** and enjoy the crystal-clear waters of the Red Sea
- Visit the **Wadi El-Hitan** and see the fossils of ancient whales and other marine creatures
- Take a **boat tour** of **Lake Nasser** and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding landscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Egyptian Museum of Antiquities** and learn about the history and culture of ancient Egypt
- Explore the **Mosque of Muhammad Ali** and admire its stunning architecture and beautiful decorations
- Attend a **traditional Arabic music** performance and learn about the rich cultural heritage of Egypt
- Visit the **Coptic Museum** and learn about the history and culture of the Coptic Christian community in Egypt
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Egyptian dishes, such as **koshari** and **mahshi**
- Visit the **Khan el-Khalili Market** and shop for traditional Egyptian handicrafts and souvenirs

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Wadi El-Natrun** and explore the beautiful monasteries and churches of the Coptic Christian community
- Explore the **Tahrir Square** and learn about the modern history of Egypt and the Arab Spring
- Take a **stroll** along the **Nile Corniche** and enjoy the stunning views of the river and the city
- Visit the **Manial Palace** and learn about the history and culture of the Egyptian royal family
- Visit the **Gayer-Anderson Museum** and learn about the history and culture of the Islamic world

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Alexandria** (distance: 220 km, travel time: 3 hours) and visit the **Citadel of Qaitbay** and the **Bibliotheca Alexandrina**
- Visit the **Saint Catherine's Monastery** (distance: 350 km, travel time: 5 hours) and learn about the history and culture of the monastery
- Take a day trip to **Saqqara** (distance: 20 km, travel time: 1 hour) and visit the **Step Pyramid** and the **Tomb of Unas**
- Visit the **Dahab** (distance: 530 km, travel time: 7 hours) and enjoy the beautiful beaches and coral reefs of the Red Sea
- Take a day trip to **Abu Simbel** (distance: 950 km, travel time: 12 hours) and visit the **Abu Simbel Temples**
