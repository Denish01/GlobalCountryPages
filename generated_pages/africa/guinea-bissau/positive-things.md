- The **Fortaleza de São José da Barra** is a historic fort that dates back to the 18th century and is now a museum.
- The **Djembe** is a traditional drum that is an important part of Guinea-Bissau's cultural heritage.
- The **Fula** people have a rich cultural tradition, with their own language, music, and art forms.
- The **Bissau Cathedral** is a beautiful example of Portuguese colonial architecture and is one of the city's most iconic landmarks.
