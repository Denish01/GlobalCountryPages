# Where to Stay in Port Elizabeth — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Port Elizabeth, you should stay in areas like Summerstrand, Humewood, or Richmond Hill for a mix of beach activities, city life, and cultural experiences.

For each of the top areas in Port Elizabeth, consider the following:

**Summerstrand** (Port Elizabeth)
Best For: Families, luxury travelers
Vibe: Summerstrand offers a relaxed, beach-oriented vibe with easy access to the city's main attractions, making it perfect for families and those seeking luxury accommodations. The area is known for its beautiful beaches and scenic coastal walks.
Price Range: $80-250/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, variety of restaurants, close to the city's main attractions
Cons: Can be crowded during peak season, limited nightlife options

**Humewood** (Port Elizabeth)
Best For: Backpackers, budget travelers
Vibe: Humewood has a lively, youthful atmosphere with a range of budget-friendly accommodations and activities, including surfing and beach volleyball. The area is also close to the city's main nightlife spots.
Price Range: $20-80/night
Pros: Affordable, lively atmosphere, close to the beach
Cons: Can be noisy, limited parking options

**Richmond Hill** (Port Elizabeth)
Best For: Business travelers, couples
Vibe: Richmond Hill offers a more subdued, sophisticated atmosphere with a range of boutique hotels and restaurants, making it ideal for business travelers and couples. The area is also close to the city's main business district.
Price Range: $60-200/night
Pros: Convenient location, range of restaurants, sophisticated atmosphere
Cons: Limited parking options, can be pricey

**Walmer** (Port Elizabeth)
Best For: Families, long-stay travelers
Vibe: Walmer is a quiet, residential area with a range of self-catering accommodations, making it perfect for families and those planning to stay in Port Elizabeth for an extended period. The area is also close to the city's main shopping centers.
Price Range: $40-150/night
Pros: Affordable, self-catering options, close to shopping centers
Cons: Limited nightlife options, can be far from the beach

**Central** (Port Elizabeth)
Best For: Business travelers, solo travelers
Vibe: The Central area of Port Elizabeth has a bustling, urban atmosphere with a range of hotels, restaurants, and entertainment options, making it ideal for business travelers and solo travelers. The area is also close to the city's main transport hubs.
Price Range: $50-180/night
Pros: Convenient location, range of amenities, close to transport hubs
Cons: Can be noisy, limited parking options
