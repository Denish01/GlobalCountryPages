# Cost of Living in Port Elizabeth - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Port Elizabeth is generally considered affordable, with a daily budget of $30-60 USD per person for backpackers, $60-120 USD for mid-range travelers, and $120-250 USD for those seeking comfort.

Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $120-250 USD
Currency: ZAR
Tipping Custom: 10-15% expected
[/FACTBOX]
