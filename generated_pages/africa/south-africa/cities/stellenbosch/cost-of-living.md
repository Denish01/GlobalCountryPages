# Cost of Living in Stellenbosch - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Stellenbosch is moderate, with a daily budget of $30-60 USD for backpackers, $60-120 USD for mid-range travelers, and $120-250 USD for those seeking comfort.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $120-250 USD
Currency: ZAR
Tipping Custom: 10-15% expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Stellenbosch? [/SECTION]
Stellenbosch is a city located in the Western Cape province of South Africa, known for its natural beauty, rich history, and world-class wine estates. Compared to other major cities in South Africa, Stellenbosch has a relatively moderate cost of living. The city's prices are lower than those in Cape Town, but higher than in smaller towns in the region. Overall, Stellenbosch offers a great balance between quality of life and affordability, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

In terms of global averages, Stellenbosch is more expensive than many cities in Asia and Eastern Europe, but more affordable than major cities in Western Europe and North America. The city's economy is driven by tourism, wine production, and education, which contributes to its moderate cost of living. Visitors can expect to find a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxury hotels, and a vibrant food scene with a mix of local and international cuisine.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a guesthouse or hostel instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local eateries and food stalls to experience authentic cuisine at affordable prices.
- Use public transport or walk/bike whenever possible to reduce transport costs.
- Take advantage of free wine tastings and tours at local wine estates.
- Shop at local markets and grocery stores for fresh produce and staples.
- Avoid visiting during peak season (December to February) when prices tend to be higher.
