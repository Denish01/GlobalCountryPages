[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine safari** through the vineyards of Spier Wine Estate, where you can taste some of the best wines in the region while learning about the wine-making process.
- Go on a **hot air balloon ride** over the Stellenbosch winelands, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
- Visit the **Stellenbosch Slow Market**, where you can sample local foods and drinks, and buy handmade crafts and gifts.
- Take a **cooking class** at the Stellenbosch Cooking School, where you can learn how to prepare traditional South African dishes.
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the beautiful countryside surrounding Stellenbosch, offering stunning views of the mountains and vineyards.
- Take a **guided tour** of the Stellenbosch University campus, which offers a glimpse into the history and culture of the city.

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Jonkershoek Nature Reserve**, which offers hiking trails, stunning scenery, and a wide range of wildlife.
- **Assegaaibosch Nature Reserve**, which offers hiking trails, picnic areas, and stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
- **Stellenbosch Botanical Garden**, which features a wide range of plant species and a beautiful setting.
- **Dornier Wine Estate**, which offers wine tastings and stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
- **Vergelegen Wine Estate**, which features beautiful gardens and a wide range of plant species.

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Stellenbosch Museum**, which offers a glimpse into the history and culture of the city.
- **Rupert Museum**, which features an impressive collection of South African art.
- **Stellenbosch University Art Gallery**, which features a wide range of artworks by local and international artists.
- **The Fugard Theatre**, which offers a wide range of performances, including plays, musicals, and dance productions.
- **Dorp Street**, which features a wide range of shops, restaurants, and cafes, and offers a glimpse into the city's rich cultural heritage.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Lanzerac Hotel**, which offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside and a wide range of luxurious amenities.
- **The Stellenbosch Craft Beer Brewery**, which offers a wide range of craft beers and a relaxed atmosphere.
- **The **Oude Libertas** amphitheater, which offers a wide range of performances, including concerts and plays.
- **The **Stellenbosch Farmers' Market**, which offers a wide range of local foods and drinks, and a glimpse into the city's vibrant culture.

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Cape Town**, which is located approximately 50 km from Stellenbosch and offers a wide range of attractions, including Table Mountain, the V&A Waterfront, and Robben Island.
- **Franschhoek**, which is located approximately 30 km from Stellenbosch and offers a wide range of wine estates, restaurants, and shops.
- **Hermanus**, which is located approximately 100 km from Stellenbosch and offers a wide range of outdoor activities, including whale watching and hiking.
- **Paarl**, which is located approximately 20 km from Stellenbosch and offers a wide range of attractions, including the Paarl Mountain Nature Reserve and the Paarl Wine Route.
- **Somerset West**, which is located approximately 20 km from Stellenbosch and offers a wide range of attractions, including the Vergelegen Wine Estate and the Helderberg Nature Reserve.
