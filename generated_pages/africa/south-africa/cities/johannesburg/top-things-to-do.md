# Top Things to Do in Johannesburg — Attractions & Experiences

Johannesburg, a vibrant city in South Africa, offers a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that cater to different interests and preferences, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **township tour** to experience the vibrant culture and history of Soweto and other surrounding townships
- Visit the **Apartheid Museum** to learn about the country's complex past and its journey towards democracy
- Go on a **street art walking tour** to discover the city's vibrant graffiti and murals
- Explore the **Maboneng Precinct**, a trendy neighborhood filled with art galleries, restaurants, and boutiques
- Attend a **traditional African music and dance performance** to experience the city's rich cultural heritage
- Take a **gourmet food tour** to sample the city's diverse culinary scene, from traditional African cuisine to modern fusion dishes

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Walter Sisulu National Botanical Garden**, a beautiful park featuring a wide range of plant species and a stunning waterfall
- Go **hiking** in the **Magaliesberg Mountains**, a scenic range with stunning views and diverse wildlife
- Explore the **Klipriviersberg Nature Reserve**, a large park with hiking trails, birdwatching, and picnic areas
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the city for a unique and breathtaking perspective
- Visit the **Johannesburg Zoo**, a large zoo featuring a wide range of animals, including elephants, lions, and giraffes

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Market Theatre**, a renowned theater and cultural center that hosts a wide range of performances and exhibitions
- Explore the **FNB Stadium**, a large stadium that hosted the 2010 World Cup and offers guided tours
- Attend a **traditional African craft workshop** to learn about local crafts and traditions
- Visit the **Johannesburg Holocaust & Genocide Centre**, a museum that commemorates the victims of the Holocaust and other genocides
- Take a **historical walking tour** of the city to learn about its complex past and cultural heritage

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Neighbourgoods Market** on a Saturday morning to experience the city's vibrant food and craft scene
- Explore the **Turbine Hall**, a former power station turned art gallery and event space
- Take a **stroll through the trendy suburb of Melville**, known for its boutique shops, restaurants, and bars
- Visit the **Bensusan Museum of Photography**, a unique museum showcasing a wide range of photographic equipment and exhibitions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Pilanesberg Game Reserve**, located about 2 hours from Johannesburg, to go on a safari adventure and spot the Big Five
- Visit the **Sun City resort**, located about 2 hours from Johannesburg, to enjoy a range of activities, including golf, water sports, and casino games
- Explore the **Magaliesberg Mountains**, located about 1 hour from Johannesburg, to go hiking, rock climbing, or take a scenic drive
- Take a day trip to the **Cradle of Humankind**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site located about 1 hour from Johannesburg, to learn about human evolution and history
- Visit the **Lesedi Cultural Village**, located about 1 hour from Johannesburg, to learn about traditional African cultures and traditions
