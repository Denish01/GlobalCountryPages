# Where to Stay in Bloemfontein — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Bloemfontein, the best areas to stay include the city center, Universitas, and Westdene, which offer a mix of luxury, family-friendly, and budget-friendly accommodations to suit different types of travelers. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas/neighborhoods in Bloemfontein, here's what you can expect:
**City Center** (Bloemfontein)
Best For: Luxury, Business
Vibe: The city center has a vibrant and modern atmosphere, with easy access to shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment options. It's a great place to experience the city's nightlife and cultural events.
Price Range: $80-200/night
Pros: Central location, easy access to amenities, vibrant nightlife
Cons: Can be noisy, limited parking options

**Universitas** (Bloemfontein)
Best For: Family, Business
Vibe: Universitas is a quiet and upscale neighborhood with a mix of residential and commercial properties, offering a relaxed atmosphere and easy access to the university and other amenities.
Price Range: $60-150/night
Pros: Quiet and safe, close to university, easy access to amenities
Cons: Limited entertainment options

**Westdene** (Bloemfontein)
Best For: Backpacker, Family
Vibe: Westdene is a charming and historic neighborhood with a mix of old and new buildings, offering a unique and laid-back atmosphere.
Price Range: $30-80/night
Pros: Affordable, historic charm, close to city center
Cons: Limited parking options, can be noisy

**Langenhoven Park** (Bloemfontein)
Best For: Luxury, Family
Vibe: Langenhoven Park is an upscale neighborhood with large homes and beautiful gardens, offering a peaceful and secure atmosphere.
Price Range: $100-250/night
Pros: Safe and secure, beautiful homes and gardens, close to amenities
Cons: Limited entertainment options, can be expensive

**Fichardt Park** (Bloemfontein)
Best For: Family, Business
Vibe: Fichardt Park is a quiet and residential neighborhood with a mix of old and new homes, offering a relaxed atmosphere and easy access to amenities.
Price Range: $50-120/night
Pros: Quiet and safe, close to amenities, easy access to city center
Cons: Limited entertainment options, can be noisy

**Bayswater** (Bloemfontein)
Best For: Backpacker, Family
Vibe: Bayswater is a charming and historic neighborhood with a mix of old and new buildings, offering a unique and laid-back atmosphere.
Price Range: $25-60/night
Pros: Affordable, historic charm, close to city center
Cons: Limited parking options, can be noisy
