[SECTION] What Is Pretoria? [/SECTION]
Pretoria is a city located in the northern part of the Gauteng province of South Africa. It is situated in the northern part of the Highveld, a high-altitude grassland region, and is known for its beautiful natural surroundings, including the Magaliesberg mountain range. Pretoria is a significant urban center, with a diverse economy, a rich cultural heritage, and a strong sense of history. The city is home to many important institutions, including the University of Pretoria, one of the largest universities in South Africa.

Pretoria is also known for its historic architecture, including the **Voortrekker Monument**, a large monument that commemorates the Voortrekkers, a group of Boer pioneers who left the British Cape Colony in the mid-19th century. The city is also home to the **Union Buildings**, a historic building complex that serves as the seat of the South African government. Pretoria is a popular tourist destination, with many attractions, including museums, galleries, and historic sites.

The city has a subtropical climate, with warm summers and mild winters. Pretoria is also known for its beautiful gardens and parks, including the **National Botanical Garden**, which is home to a wide variety of plant species. The city is a significant cultural center, with a vibrant arts scene, and is home to many festivals and events throughout the year.
