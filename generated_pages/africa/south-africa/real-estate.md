[SECTION] Foreign Ownership Rules [/SECTION]
- Foreigners can buy property in South Africa, but they must comply with the **Immigration Act** and the **Exchange Control Regulations**.
- Foreigners must obtain a **certificate of compliance** from the **South African Reserve Bank** to purchase property.
- Foreigners can also buy property through a **leasehold agreement**, which allows them to use the property for a specified period.
- Foreigners can also set up a **company structure** to buy property, but this requires **tax registration** and **company registration**.
- Foreigners must also comply with the **FICA regulations**, which require them to provide **identification documentation** and **proof of address**.

- **Deposits** are typically **1-2 months' rent**, which must be **refunded** at the end of the lease.
