[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Nelson Mandela Museum** in Mthatha showcases the life and legacy of the former South African president and anti-apartheid activist.
- The **Grahamstown National Arts Festival** is one of the largest and most prestigious arts festivals in South Africa, featuring a diverse range of music, theater, and dance performances.
- The **Eastern Cape's traditional Xhosa culture** is known for its vibrant clothing, music, and dance, including the ** Reed Dance**, which is an annual ceremony where young women present reeds to the king.
- The **Amathole Mountains** are home to several **rock art** sites, which feature ancient paintings and engravings created by the region's indigenous people.
- The **Steve Biko Centre** in Ginsberg commemorates the life and legacy of the anti-apartheid activist Steve Biko, who was born in the Eastern Cape.
