[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **iSimangaliso Wetland Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a unique cultural and natural treasure, with a rich history and diverse wildlife.
- The **Zulu Reed Dance** is an important cultural tradition, where thousands of **Zulu maidens** gather to dance and celebrate their heritage.
- The **Durban City Hall** is a historic building that showcases the region's **colonial architecture** and **cultural heritage**.
- The **Tatham Art Gallery** in Pietermaritzburg is a premier art museum, featuring a collection of **South African art** and **contemporary exhibitions**.
- The **KwaZulu-Natal Museum** in Pietermaritzburg is a leading cultural institution, with a vast collection of **natural history specimens** and **cultural artifacts**.
