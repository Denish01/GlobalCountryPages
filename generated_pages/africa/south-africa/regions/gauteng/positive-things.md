[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Apartheid Museum** in Johannesburg is a UNESCO-recognized site that provides a poignant and thought-provoking look at South Africa's history of apartheid and its legacy.
- The **Soweto Township** is a cultural treasure that showcases the resilience and creativity of its residents, with its vibrant street art, music, and traditional cuisine.
- The **FNB Stadium**, also known as Soccer City, is a iconic sports venue that hosted the 2010 FIFA World Cup final and is a symbol of South African pride and achievement.
- The **Lesedi Cultural Village** is a living museum that celebrates the traditional cultures of South Africa, with its replica homesteads, craft market, and cultural performances.
- The **Market Theatre** in Johannesburg is a renowned arts venue that has hosted numerous productions, including plays, musicals, and dance performances, and is a hub for creative expression.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Nelson Mandela**, the former President of South Africa, is arguably the most famous son of Gauteng, and his legacy continues to inspire people around the world with his message of forgiveness, reconciliation, and justice.
- The **Gauteng diaspora community** has made significant contributions to the global economy, with many expatriates working in fields such as finance, technology, and healthcare, and sending remittances back to their families in South Africa.
- The **values of Ubuntu**, which emphasize the importance of community, respect, and compassion, are deeply ingrained in the culture of Gauteng and are reflected in the region's warm hospitality and welcoming nature.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **warm hospitality** of the people of Gauteng, who are known for their friendly and welcoming nature, makes visitors feel at home and comfortable in the region.
- The **vibrant nightlife** of Johannesburg, with its many bars, clubs, and live music venues, offers something for every taste and preference, from jazz and blues to rock and hip-hop.

- The **easy access** to other parts of South Africa, including the Kruger National Park and the Garden Route, makes Gauteng an ideal base for exploring the country.
