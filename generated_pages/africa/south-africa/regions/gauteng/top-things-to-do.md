# Top Things to Do in Gauteng — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Gauteng, visit its vibrant cities, explore its rich cultural heritage, and discover its stunning natural attractions, which offer a unique blend of urban excitement and outdoor adventure.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **township tour** to experience the vibrant culture and history of Soweto and other townships
- Visit the **Neighbourgoods Market** in Johannesburg for a taste of local food, craft beer, and live music
- Explore the **Maboneng Precinct**, a trendy neighborhood with street art, boutiques, and restaurants
- Go on a **hot air balloon ride** over the Magaliesberg Mountains for breathtaking views
- Learn about **traditional African medicine** at the Lesedi Cultural Village
- Attend a **live music performance** at the Bassline in Johannesburg, featuring local and international artists
- Take a **stargazing tour** at the Johannesburg Planetarium, exploring the night sky and its wonders
- Visit the **Turbine Hall** in Johannesburg, a former power station turned art gallery and event space

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Magaliesberg Mountains**, a range with scenic hiking trails and stunning views
- Visit the **Pilanesberg Game Reserve**, a malaria-free reserve with a wide variety of wildlife
- Go **hiking** at the Suikerbosrand Nature Reserve, with trails for all levels and scenic views
- Relax at the **Hartebeespoort Dam**, a popular spot for boating, fishing, and water sports
- Visit the **Rietvlei Nature Reserve**, a wetland area with birdwatching and game viewing opportunities
- Explore the **Walter Sisulu National Botanical Garden**, featuring a wide range of plant species and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Origins Centre** at the University of the Witwatersrand, exploring the history of humanity and the origins of our species
- Explore the **Fietas Museum** in Johannesburg, showcasing the history and culture of the Indian community
- Attend a **performance** at the Market Theatre in Johannesburg, a renowned venue for live music, dance, and theater
- Visit the **Pretoria Art Museum**, featuring a collection of South African art from the 19th century to the present
- Take a **cookery class** and learn about traditional South African cuisine and cooking techniques
- Visit the **Bensusan Museum of Photography**, showcasing a collection of photography and photographic equipment

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Klipriviersberg Nature Reserve**, a lesser-known reserve with hiking trails and scenic views
- Explore the **Johannesburg Art Gallery**, featuring a collection of contemporary and historical art
- Go to the **Neighbourgoods Market** in Braamfontein, a smaller market with a unique atmosphere
- Take a **street art tour** in the Maboneng Precinct, exploring the vibrant murals and graffiti
- Visit the **Satyagraha House**, a museum showcasing the life and legacy of Mahatma Gandhi

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Pilanesberg Game Reserve** ( approx. 2 hours from Johannesburg), with game drives and wildlife viewing
- Visit the **Hartebeespoort Dam** ( approx. 1 hour from Johannesburg), with boating, fishing, and water sports
- Explore the **Magaliesberg Mountains** ( approx. 1.5 hours from Johannesburg), with hiking trails and scenic views
- Go to the **Cradle of Humankind** ( approx. 1.5 hours from Johannesburg), a UNESCO World Heritage Site with historical and cultural significance
- Take a day trip to the **Sun City resort** ( approx. 2 hours from Johannesburg), with golfing, gaming, and entertainment options
