# Top Things to Do in Uganda — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Uganda, you should not miss the opportunity to explore its diverse wildlife, experience the local culture, and take in the breathtaking natural beauty of the country, from the savannas of Queen Elizabeth National Park to the vibrant streets of Kampala.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Bwindi Impenetrable National Park | Southwestern Uganda | National Park | Home to over 400 **mountain gorillas** and a UNESCO World Heritage Site |
| Queen Elizabeth National Park | Western Uganda | National Park | Known for its **lion prides**, **leopard** sightings, and stunning **Lake Edward** views |
| Murchison Falls National Park | Northwestern Uganda | National Park | Famous for its **waterfalls** and **wildlife**, including **lions**, **leopards**, and **giraffes** |
| Lake Bunyonyi | Southwestern Uganda | Lake | A beautiful lake with **islands** and **swimming** opportunities, perfect for relaxation |
| Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary | Lake Victoria | Sanctuary | A haven for **chimpanzees** and a great spot for **birdwatching** |
| Kasubi Tombs | Kampala | Historical Site | A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the final resting place of the **Kabakas** of the Buganda Kingdom |
| Ssese Islands | Lake Victoria | Archipelago | A group of **islands** with **beaches**, **forests**, and **wildlife**, ideal for **fishing** and **boating** |
| Owiny High School Museum | Lira | Museum | A museum showcasing the **history** and **culture** of the Lango people |
| Ndere Cultural Centre | Kampala | Cultural Centre | A centre promoting **Ugandan culture** through **music**, **dance**, and **art** |
| Fort Portal | Western Uganda | Town | A charming town with **colonial architecture**, **tea plantations**, and **hiking** trails |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gorilla trekking** tour in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park or Mgahinga Gorilla National Park
- Go on a **lion tracking** expedition in Queen Elizabeth National Park
- Experience the **local culture** by attending a **traditional wedding** or **cultural festival**
- Visit a **coffee farm** and learn about the **coffee-making process**
- Take a **sunset cruise** on Lake Victoria or Lake Bunyonyi
- Learn about **traditional medicine** and **herbal remedies** at a local **medicine man's** clinic
- Participate in a **local cooking class** and learn how to make **Ugandan dishes** like **matooke** and **luwombo**

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **savannas** and **wildlife** of Queen Elizabeth National Park
- Hike to the **top of Mount Stanley**, the highest peak in the **Rwenzori Mountains**
- Visit the **Murchison Falls**, a breathtaking **waterfall** on the Nile River
- Go **white-water rafting** or **kayaking** on the Nile River
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Ssese Islands** and enjoy the **beaches** and **forests**
- Visit the **Kibale National Park**, home to **chimpanzees**, **monkeys**, and **birds**

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Uganda Museum** in Kampala to learn about the **history** and **culture** of Uganda
- Explore the **Kasubi Tombs**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the final resting place of the **Kabakas** of the Buganda Kingdom
- Attend a **traditional dance** performance at the **Ndere Cultural Centre**
- Visit the **Owiny High School Museum** to learn about the **history** and **culture** of the Lango people
- Take a **tour of a local market**, such as the **Owino Market** in Kampala, to experience the **local cuisine** and **handicrafts**
- Learn about the **history** and **significance** of the **Bahai Temple** in Kampala

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Itanda Falls**, a beautiful **waterfall** on the Nile River
- Explore the **Kidepo Valley National Park**, a remote and **wild** national park in northeastern Uganda
- Take a **hike** through the **Rwenzori Mountains** and enjoy the **scenic views**
- Visit the **Kabarole Museum** to learn about the **history** and **culture** of the Tooro Kingdom
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Mabira Forest**, home to over **300 species** of birds

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Kampala to the **Jinja** town, located 80 km away, and visit the **source of the Nile River** (travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Entebbe** town, located 35 km from Kampala, and explore the **Entebbe Botanical Gardens** (travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a day trip from Fort Portal to the **Kibale National Park**, located 30 km away, and go **chimpanzee tracking** (travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Murchison Falls National Park**, located 300 km from Kampala, and take a **boat tour** of the **Nile River** (travel time: 5 hours)
- Take a day trip from Kampala to the **Mpanga Forest**, located 40 km away, and go **hiking** and **birdwatching** (travel time: 1.5 hours)
