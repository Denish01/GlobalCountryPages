# Utility & Internet Costs in Libya

The average cost of utilities and internet in Libya can range from $100 to $300 per month for a medium-sized apartment, depending on usage and provider.

Electricity Cost: ($0.10-$0.20 per kWh)
Currency: LYD
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Libya's utility infrastructure is relatively underdeveloped, with frequent power outages and water shortages, especially during the summer months. The cost of utilities in Libya can be unstable and fluctuate depending on the season, with higher costs during the summer due to increased demand for electricity and water. The country's utility sector is also heavily subsidized by the government, which can impact the cost of utilities for consumers.

The quality of utility services in Libya varies greatly depending on the region, with urban areas such as Tripoli and Benghazi generally having better access to reliable utilities than rural areas. The cost of utilities is also affected by the country's economic situation, with inflation and currency fluctuations impacting the price of utilities. As a result, consumers in Libya often have to adapt to changing utility costs and availability.
