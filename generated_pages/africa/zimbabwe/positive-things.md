# Positive Things About Zimbabwe - Achievements & Culture

Zimbabwe is a country with a rich history, diverse culture, and notable achievements in various fields, including science, sports, arts, and innovation, making it a fascinating destination to explore and learn about.

[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Matobo Hills**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, are known for their unique rock formations and ancient rock art.
- The **Zimbabwe International Film Festival** showcases local and international films, promoting the country's film industry.
- The **Mbira**, a traditional instrument, is an important part of Zimbabwean music and culture.
- The **Shona People** have a rich tradition of storytelling and oral history, with many stories passed down through generations.
- The **Zimbabwe Museum of Human Sciences** in Harare showcases the country's history, culture, and natural heritage.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Tatenda Taibu**, a former Zimbabwean cricketer, has made significant contributions to the sport, including being the youngest captain of the national team.
- **Tsitsi Dangarembga**, a Zimbabwean author, has written several acclaimed novels, including **Nervous Conditions**, which won the Commonwealth Writers' Prize.
- The **Zimbabwean diaspora** has made significant contributions to the global economy, with many Zimbabweans working in fields such as medicine, engineering, and finance.
- **Oliver Mtukudzi**, a legendary Zimbabwean musician, has been recognized for his contributions to music, including being awarded the **Order of the Legion of Honor** by the French government.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **hospitality** of the Zimbabwean people, with many visitors commenting on the warm welcome they receive.
- The **scenic beauty** of the country, including the **Victoria Falls**, **Matobo Hills**, and **Hwange National Park**.
- The **rich cultural heritage**, with many visitors enjoying the traditional music, dance, and art.
- The **delicious cuisine**, including dishes such as **sadza**, **stew**, and **braai**.
- The **exciting outdoor activities**, including **safari**, **hiking**, and **white-water rafting**.
- The **vibrant cities**, including **Harare** and **Bulawayo**, which offer a mix of colonial history, modern architecture, and cultural attractions.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Chimanimani Mountains**, a lesser-known destination, offer stunning scenery and hiking opportunities.
- The **Gonarezhou National Park**, one of the largest national parks in Zimbabwe, is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including **elephants**, **lions**, and **giraffes**.
- The **Zimbabwean craft industry**, which produces high-quality **woodcarvings**, **textiles**, and **basketry**, is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered.
- The **Hot Springs** in **Chinhoyi**, a natural wonder, offer a unique and relaxing experience for visitors.
