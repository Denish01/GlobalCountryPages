Unique Experiences

  • Take a sunset cruise on Lake Kariba, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
  • Go on a walking safari in Hwange National Park, getting up close and personal with wildlife
  • Visit a traditional village, such as the Monomotapa Village, to learn about the local culture and customs
  • Take a train ride on the Bulawayo-Victoria Falls railway, offering scenic views of the countryside
  • Go bungee jumping off the Victoria Falls Bridge, an adrenaline-pumping experience
  • Attend a traditional dance performance, such as the Mbira dance, to experience the local music and culture
  • Go on a horseback ride through the Matobo Hills, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape

Outdoor & Nature

  • Hike to the top of Mount Nyangani, the highest peak in Zimbabwe, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
  • Visit Lake Mutirikwe, a popular spot for boating and fishing
  • Go on a game drive in Hwange National Park, home to a wide variety of wildlife
  • Explore the Matobo Hills, known for their unique rock formations and diverse wildlife
  • Visit the Victoria Falls National Park, home to the majestic Victoria Falls
  • Take a canoe trip down the Zambezi River, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the National Museum and Monuments of Zimbabwe, showcasing the history and culture of the country
  • Attend a traditional music performance, such as the Mbira music, to experience the local culture
  • Take a cooking class, learning how to prepare traditional Zimbabwean dishes
  • Visit the Bulawayo Art Gallery, showcasing the work of local artists
  • Explore the Great Zimbabwe Ruins, an ancient city and former capital of the Kingdom of Zimbabwe
  • Attend a traditional festival, such as the Harare International Festival of the Arts, to experience the local culture

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Chirinda Forest, a lush and scenic forest with stunning views and diverse wildlife
  • Explore the Gona-re-Zhou National Park, a lesser-known national park with stunning scenery and diverse wildlife
  • Take a boat trip on Lake Chivero, a popular spot for boating and fishing
  • Go on a hike in the Vumba Mountains, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape
  • Visit the Zimbabwe Museum of Human Sciences, showcasing the history and culture of the country

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip to Victoria Falls from Harare, a 1.5-hour flight or 10-hour drive, to witness the majestic waterfall
  • Visit Hwange National Park from Bulawayo, a 2-hour drive, to go on a game drive and witness the diverse wildlife
  • Take a day trip to Matobo National Park from Bulawayo, a 1-hour drive, to explore the unique rock formations and diverse wildlife
  • Go on a day trip to Eastern Highlands from Harare, a 3-hour drive, to hike and explore the scenic mountains and valleys
  • Visit Great Zimbabwe Ruins from Masvingo, a 30-minute drive, to explore the ancient city and former capital of the Kingdom of Zimbabwe

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of Harare and Victoria Falls), 7-day (comprehensive tour of Zimbabwe's national parks and cultural sites), 14-day (deep exploration of Zimbabwe's history, culture, and wildlife). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and tours in advance, prioritize safety and security, and respect local customs and traditions.
