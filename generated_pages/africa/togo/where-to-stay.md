# Where to Stay in Togo — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To find the best place to stay in Togo, consider the coastal city of Lome, which offers a range of accommodations and neighborhoods that cater to different types of travelers, from budget-friendly options in the city center to luxury resorts along the beach.

For each of the top areas in Togo, the following neighborhoods are recommended:
**Lome City Center** (Lome)
Best For: backpacker / business
Vibe: The city center is a bustling area with a mix of modern and traditional architecture, offering easy access to markets, restaurants, and government buildings. The area is vibrant and lively, with a rich cultural atmosphere.
Price Range: $20-50/night
Pros: proximity to major attractions, affordable accommodation options, vibrant nightlife
Cons: can be noisy and crowded

**Beachside** (Lome)
Best For: luxury / family
Vibe: The beachside area is a tranquil and picturesque neighborhood with stunning ocean views, perfect for relaxation and water activities. The area is known for its beautiful beaches, restaurants, and bars.
Price Range: $100-200/night
Pros: stunning ocean views, access to water sports, luxurious amenities
Cons: can be expensive, limited budget options

**Aneho** (Aneho)
Best For: family / luxury
Vibe: Aneho is a charming coastal town with a rich history and culture, offering a relaxing atmosphere and stunning ocean views. The area is known for its beautiful beaches, historic landmarks, and traditional architecture.
Price Range: $50-100/night
Pros: historic landmarks, beautiful beaches, relaxing atmosphere
Cons: limited accommodation options, can be quiet

**Kpalime** (Kpalime)
Best For: backpacker / adventure
Vibe: Kpalime is a scenic town surrounded by mountains and forests, offering a range of outdoor activities and stunning natural beauty. The area is known for its hiking trails, waterfalls, and traditional villages.
Price Range: $10-30/night
Pros: stunning natural beauty, outdoor activities, affordable accommodation
Cons: limited amenities, can be remote

**Sokode** (Sokode)
Best For: business / luxury
Vibe: Sokode is a major city in central Togo, known for its vibrant market, rich culture, and modern amenities. The area is a hub for business and commerce, with a range of accommodation options.
Price Range: $50-100/night
Pros: modern amenities, vibrant market, business opportunities
Cons: can be busy and noisy, limited tourist attractions
