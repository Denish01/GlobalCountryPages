- Explore the vibrant markets of N'Djamena, featuring a wide range of local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Take a camel ride through the stunning scenery of the Ennedi Natural and Cultural Reserve
- Visit a traditional Chadian village, with unique opportunities to learn about local customs and traditions
- Attend a traditional Chadian festival, such as the **Gerewol Festival**, featuring music, dance, and vibrant costumes
- Go on a **safari** adventure in Zakouma National Park, with opportunities to see diverse wildlife in their natural habitat
- Take a boat trip on Lake Chad, with stunning scenery and opportunities to see local fishermen at work
