[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Fort James Island**, a **UNESCO World Heritage Site**, is a historic fort that showcases the country's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Kankurang**, a traditional masked dance, is an important part of Gambian culture and is often performed during festivals and celebrations.
- The **Gambian Griot**, a traditional storyteller and musician, plays a significant role in preserving and sharing the country's history and cultural traditions.
- The **Janjangbureh**, a historic town, is known for its well-preserved colonial architecture and is a popular tourist destination.
- The **Kombo**, a type of traditional Gambian music, is characterized by its unique rhythms and is often performed during social gatherings and celebrations.

- The **Gambian diaspora** has made significant contributions to the country's development, with many Gambians living abroad sending remittances and investing in local businesses.

- The **Gambian community** values **hospitality** and **respect for elders**, with visitors often commenting on the warm and welcoming nature of the Gambian people.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **friendly and welcoming locals** are often cited as one of the highlights of visiting Gambia, with many visitors commenting on the warm hospitality they receive.
- The **beautiful beaches**, such as **Kololi Beach** and **Cape Point Beach**, are popular destinations for tourists, offering a range of water sports and activities.
- The **delicious local cuisine**, which includes dishes such as **jollof rice** and **benachin**, is a favorite among visitors, with many restaurants and food stalls serving traditional Gambian dishes.
- The **vibrant markets**, such as the **Serrekunda Market**, offer a range of local handicrafts, souvenirs, and fresh produce, providing a glimpse into the country's rich cultural heritage.
- The **exciting wildlife**, including **monkeys**, **birds**, and **crocodiles**, can be found in the country's various national parks and wildlife reserves, offering a unique and thrilling experience for visitors.
- The **relaxed and peaceful atmosphere**, which is often described as **"the smiling coast of Africa"**, makes Gambia an ideal destination for those seeking a tranquil and rejuvenating vacation.
