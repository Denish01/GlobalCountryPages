The best things to do in Gambia include visiting its stunning beaches, exploring its vibrant culture, and experiencing its unique wildlife, with top attractions like the Kunta Kinteh Island, the Gambia National Museum, and the Abuko National Park.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a pirogue trip along the River Gambia to see dolphins and other marine life
  • Visit a locally-owned farm to learn about Gambia's agricultural practices and sample local produce
  • Attend a traditional wrestling match to experience the country's vibrant culture and sports
  • Take a cookery class to learn about Gambian cuisine and how to prepare traditional dishes
  • Go on a guided tour of the Gambia's traditional villages to learn about the country's history and culture
  • Visit the Gambia's sacred crocodile pool to see these incredible creatures up close
  • Take a star-gazing tour to see the incredible night sky and learn about astronomy

Outdoor & Nature

  • Visit the Abuko National Park to see a variety of wildlife, including monkeys, birds, and crocodiles
  • Explore the Makasutu Forest and its beautiful hiking trails and opportunities for birdwatching
  • Relax on the Tanji Beach and enjoy the sun, sea, and sand
  • Take a boat trip along the River Gambia to see dolphins and other marine life
  • Visit the Bijilo Forest Park and its beautiful hiking trails and opportunities for birdwatching
  • Go on a hiking trip through the Kombo District to see the beautiful scenery and wildlife

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Gambia National Museum to learn about the country's history, culture, and natural environment
  • Attend a traditional music and dance performance to experience the country's vibrant culture
  • Take a guided tour of the Gambia's historical sites, including the Kunta Kinteh Island and James Island
  • Visit the Tanje Village Museum to learn about the country's traditional crafts and way of life
  • Attend a local festival or celebration to experience the country's vibrant culture and traditions
  • Visit the Bakau Craft Village to see local craftsmen at work and buy traditional handicrafts

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Lamin Lodge to see the beautiful views of the River Gambia and enjoy a relaxing stay
  • Explore the Tendaba Village and its beautiful scenery and traditional way of life
  • Take a trip to the Janjanbureh Island to see the beautiful scenery and wildlife
  • Visit the All Saints Church in Bakau to see the beautiful architecture and learn about the country's history
  • Go on a hiking trip through the Fathala Game Reserve to see the beautiful scenery and wildlife

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip to the Kunta Kinteh Island (distance: 30km, travel time: 1 hour, highlights: historical site, beautiful scenery)
  • Visit the Abuko National Park (distance: 25km, travel time: 45 minutes, highlights: wildlife, hiking trails)
  • Take a day trip to the Makasutu Forest (distance: 40km, travel time: 1.5 hours, highlights: hiking trails, birdwatching)
  • Go on a day trip to the Tanji Beach (distance: 30km, travel time: 1 hour, highlights: swimming, sunbathing, water sports)
  • Take a day trip to the Bijilo Forest Park (distance: 20km, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: hiking trails, birdwatching)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights: Kunta Kinteh Island, Abuko National Park, Tanji Beach), 7-day (comprehensive: Gambia National Museum, Makasutu Forest, Bijilo Forest Park), 14-day (deep exploration: Fathala Game Reserve, Janjanbureh Island, Tendaba Village). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and tours in advance, prioritize the most popular attractions and experiences, and consider visiting during the dry season (November to June) for the best weather and road conditions.
