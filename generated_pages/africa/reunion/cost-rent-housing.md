# Rent & Housing Prices in Reunion

The cost of rent in Reunion varies, with average monthly rent for a one-bedroom apartment in the city center ranging from $400-$700 USD (approximately 350-600 EUR) and $250-$450 USD (approximately 220-390 EUR) outside the city center.

[SECTION] Housing Market Overview [/SECTION]
The rental market in Reunion is relatively stable, with a mix of old and new buildings, apartments, and houses available for rent. Typical lease terms range from 3-12 months, with a deposit requirement of one to two months' rent. Tenant rights are protected under French law, as Reunion is an overseas department of France. Compared to its regional neighbors, such as Madagascar and Mauritius, Reunion's housing costs are relatively high due to its French affiliation and higher standard of living. However, the island's unique cultural blend and stunning natural beauty make it an attractive destination for expats and tourists alike.

The housing market in Reunion is also influenced by the island's limited size and geographic constraints, which can drive up prices for housing, especially in the city center. Additionally, the island's economy is heavily reliant on tourism, which can lead to fluctuations in the housing market, particularly during peak travel seasons. Despite these challenges, Reunion's housing market remains relatively stable, with a range of options available to suit different budgets and preferences.
