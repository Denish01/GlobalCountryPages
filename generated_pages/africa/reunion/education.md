Tertiary education, or **lycée**, is not compulsory but provides students with a range of academic and professional programs, including **baccalauréat** programs, which are highly regarded in France and other European countries.

The education system in Reunion is also known for its **bilingual** programs, which provide students with the opportunity to learn French and other languages, such as English, Spanish, and Chinese.
