# Utility & Internet Costs in Reunion

The cost of utilities and internet in Reunion can vary, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD, while internet plans with 60 Mbps speed can cost between $30 to $50 USD per month.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Reunion's utility infrastructure is well-developed, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and gas. The costs of utilities in Reunion are generally stable, with minimal fluctuations throughout the year. However, there may be some seasonal variation in electricity costs, with higher rates during the summer months (December to March) due to increased demand for air conditioning. The water supply is also subject to seasonal variation, with higher rates during the dry season (May to October) due to increased demand for irrigation.

The cost of utilities in Reunion is affected by various factors, including the size of the apartment, the number of occupants, and the level of consumption. On average, a small apartment (45 sqm) can expect to pay around $100 to $200 USD per month for utilities, while a medium apartment (85 sqm) can expect to pay around $150 to $300 USD per month. Large apartments (120 sqm) can expect to pay around $250 to $500 USD per month. It's worth noting that these costs are estimates and can vary depending on individual circumstances.
