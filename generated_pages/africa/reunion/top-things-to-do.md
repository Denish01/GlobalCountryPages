# Top Things to Do in Reunion — Attractions & Experiences

Reunion offers a diverse range of activities, from exploring **Pitons**, **Cirques**, and **Plaine** landscapes to experiencing the unique cultural heritage of this French territory in the Indian Ocean.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **helicopter tour** over the island for a bird's-eye view of the stunning landscapes
- Go **whale watching** in the Indian Ocean and spot humpback whales and dolphins
- Learn about the production of **Reunionese rum** at a local distillery
- Explore the **underwater world** of Reunion on a snorkeling or diving trip
- Attend a **Sega music** performance, a unique cultural expression of the island
- Visit a **vanilla plantation** and learn about the production of this fragrant spice
- Take a **Creole cooking class** and discover the flavors of Reunionese cuisine

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike to the **Piton des Neiges**, the highest peak in Reunion
- Explore the **Cirque de Mafate**, a remote and rugged natural reserve
- Visit the **Jardin Botanique de Mascarin**, a beautiful botanical garden
- Relax on the beach at **L'Etang-Sale**, a tranquil coastal village
- Go **hiking** in the **Plaine des Palmistes**, a unique volcanic landscape
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Lagoon of Saint-Pierre**, a stunning marine reserve

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Musee d'Art de Reunion**, a museum showcasing local and international art
- Explore the **Historical Village of Hell-Bourg**, a beautifully restored 19th-century village
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, such as the Sega or Maloya
- Learn about the history of Reunion at the **Musee de Villèle**, a museum located in a historic mansion
- Visit the **Cathedrale de Saint-Denis**, a stunning Catholic cathedral
- Take a **Creole language class** and learn about the unique cultural heritage of Reunion

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Village of Cilaos**, a charming mountain village with stunning views
- Explore the **Gorges de la Riviere de l'Est**, a rugged and scenic canyon
- Take a **hike** to the **Piton de la Petite Riviere**, a lesser-known peak with breathtaking views
- Relax on the beach at **La Plage de l'Hermitage**, a tranquil coastal village
- Visit the **Jardin des Parfums et des Epices**, a beautiful perfume garden

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Cirque de Salazie**, a stunning natural reserve with lush forests and waterfalls (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Visit the **Piton de la Fournaise**, an active volcano with stunning views (distance: 40 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Explore the **Village of Cilaos**, a charming mountain village with stunning views (distance: 50 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a day trip to the **Lagoon of Saint-Pierre**, a stunning marine reserve with crystal-clear waters (distance: 70 km, travel time: 2.5 hours)
- Visit the **Grand Anse**, a beautiful white sandy beach with crystal-clear waters (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
