# Where to Stay in Port Harcourt — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Port Harcourt, the best areas to stay include Old GRA, New GRA, and Port Harcourt Township, which offer a range of accommodation options to suit different types of travelers and budgets.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas in Port Harcourt, here's what you can expect:
**Old GRA** (Port Harcourt)
Best For: Luxury/Business
Vibe: Old GRA is a high-end neighborhood with a mix of residential and commercial properties, offering a serene and secure environment, with easy access to top-notch restaurants and bars. The area is known for its tree-lined streets and well-maintained buildings.
Price Range: $100-$500/night
Pros: Proximity to major businesses, high-quality amenities, and a secure environment. Old GRA is also close to the Port Harcourt International Airport, making it a convenient option for business travelers.
Cons: Traffic congestion during rush hours, limited budget-friendly options.

**New GRA** (Port Harcourt)
Best For: Family/Luxury
Vibe: New GRA is a modern and upscale neighborhood with a range of amenities, including shopping malls, restaurants, and entertainment centers. The area is known for its wide roads and well-planned layout, making it easy to navigate.
Price Range: $80-$300/night
Pros: Modern amenities, secure environment, and easy access to shopping and entertainment options. New GRA is also home to several top-rated schools, making it a great option for families.
Cons: Limited budget-friendly options, can be noisy due to traffic.

**Port Harcourt Township** (Port Harcourt)
Best For: Backpacker/Budget
Vibe: Port Harcourt Township is a bustling and vibrant area with a mix of local markets, restaurants, and bars. The area is known for its lively atmosphere and affordable prices, making it a great option for budget-conscious travelers.
Price Range: $20-$100/night
Pros: Affordable prices, lively atmosphere, and easy access to local markets and restaurants. Port Harcourt Township is also close to the city's major transport hubs, making it easy to get around.
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, limited high-end amenities.

**D-Line** (Port Harcourt)
Best For: Mid-range/Business
Vibe: D-Line is a commercial area with a range of shops, restaurants, and bars. The area is known for its central location and easy access to major businesses, making it a great option for business travelers.
Price Range: $50-$200/night
Pros: Central location, easy access to businesses, and a range of amenities. D-Line is also close to the city's major transport hubs, making it easy to get around.
Cons: Can be noisy due to traffic, limited high-end options.

**Elelenwo** (Port Harcourt)
Best For: Family/Mid-range
Vibe: Elelenwo is a residential area with a range of amenities, including schools, shops, and restaurants. The area is known for its peaceful environment and easy access to the city center, making it a great option for families.
Price Range: $30-$150/night
Pros: Peaceful environment, easy access to amenities, and affordable prices. Elelenwo is also close to several top-rated schools, making it a great option for families.
Cons: Limited high-end options, can be far from major businesses.

[SECTION] Safety & Location Tips [/SECTION]
- Avoid areas with high crime rates, such as some parts of Diobu and Borikiri.
- Stay in well-lit and populated areas, especially at night.
- Keep valuables secure and be mindful of pickpocketing in crowded areas.
- Use reputable taxi services or ride-hailing apps to get around.
- Be aware of traffic congestion and plan your routes accordingly.
