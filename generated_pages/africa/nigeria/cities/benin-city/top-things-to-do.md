[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **brass casting** workshop on Igun Street to learn about the traditional craft
- Explore the **Benin City walls and moats**, a historic defensive structure
- Attend a traditional **Edo festival**, such as the Igue Festival, to experience the city's vibrant culture
- Visit the **Ogba River** for a scenic boat ride and fishing opportunities
- Learn about the **history of the Benin Empire** at the Benin National Museum
- Go on a **guided tour** of the Oba of Benin Palace to learn about its historical significance
- Sample local **Edo cuisine**, such as banga soup and starch, at a traditional restaurant
- Take a **stroll through the city's historic neighborhoods**, such as the Oba Market area

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ogba Zoo** to see a variety of African wildlife, including monkeys, lions, and crocodiles
- Explore the **Gegele Forest Reserve**, a protected area with diverse plant and animal species
- Go **hiking** in the hills surrounding Benin City for scenic views and outdoor adventure
- Take a **boat ride** on the **Ogba River** for a relaxing and scenic experience
- Visit the **Okomu National Park**, a protected area with diverse wildlife and plant species

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Benin National Museum** to learn about the city's history and cultural heritage
- Explore the **Edo State Museum** to see a collection of artifacts and exhibits on Edo State's history and culture
- Attend a traditional **Edo performance**, such as a dance or music concert, at a local venue
- Take a **workshop on traditional Edo crafts**, such as woodcarving or weaving
- Visit the **Oba of Benin Palace** to learn about the history and cultural significance of the palace
- Go on a **guided tour** of the city's historic sites, such as the Benin Moat and King's Square

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ekenwan Road Market** for a vibrant and bustling marketplace experience
- Explore the **Ring Road** area, a hub for local food, clothing, and household items
- Take a **stroll through the city's historic neighborhoods**, such as the Oba Market area
- Visit the **Urhokpota Hall**, a historic building and former meeting place for the Oba
- Go on a **self-guided tour** of the city's street art and murals, which showcase local culture and creativity

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Okomu National Park**, approximately 60 km from Benin City, to see diverse wildlife and plant species
- Visit the **Gegele Forest Reserve**, approximately 30 km from Benin City, for hiking and outdoor adventure
- Go on a day trip to the **Ogba River**, approximately 10 km from Benin City, for a scenic boat ride and fishing opportunities
- Take a day trip to the **Igue Festival**, held annually in Benin City, to experience the city's vibrant culture
- Visit the **Edo State Museum**, approximately 5 km from Benin City, to learn about Edo State's history and culture
