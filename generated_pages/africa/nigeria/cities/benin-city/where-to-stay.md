# Where to Stay in Benin City — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Benin City, the best areas to stay are in the city center, where you can find a mix of modern and traditional accommodations, as well as easy access to local attractions and transportation.

Best For: Luxury/Business

Price Range: $100-300/night

**Ring Road** (Benin City)
Best For: Family/Backpacker

[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]
- Book in advance, especially during peak travel seasons, to ensure availability and the best prices
- Use online booking platforms like Booking.com, Expedia, or Hotels.com to compare prices and find deals
- Consider negotiating the price, especially when booking directly with a hotel or guesthouse
- Look for package deals that include accommodation, food, and activities for a discounted rate
- Be aware of seasonal pricing, with prices often higher during holidays and festivals
- Read reviews and check the hotel's policies before booking, to ensure it meets your needs
- Use travel agents or local tour operators to book accommodations and tours, for a more personalized service

[SECTION] Safety & Location Tips [/SECTION]
- Research the area before booking, to ensure it is safe and secure
- Avoid areas with high crime rates, such as some parts of the city center

- Keep valuables secure, and be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas
