# Top Things to Do in Enugu — Attractions & Experiences

Enugu, a city in southeastern Nigeria, offers a mix of natural attractions, cultural experiences, and unique activities that make it a must-visit destination for travelers, with top attractions including the National Museum, Enugu Zoo, and Milken Hills.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **coal mining tour** to learn about Enugu's coal mining history and see the mines up close
- Attend a traditional **Igbo festival**, such as the New Yam Festival, to experience the local culture
- Visit the **Enugu Art Market** to see and buy local handicrafts and artwork
- Go on a **hiking trip** to the Milken Hills to enjoy the scenic views and exercise
- Take a **Nigerian cooking class** to learn how to make local dishes like jollof rice and egusi soup
- Explore the **Enugu nightlife** by visiting popular bars and clubs like the Polo Park Mall
- Visit the **New Berries Park** for a fun day of amusement park rides and games
