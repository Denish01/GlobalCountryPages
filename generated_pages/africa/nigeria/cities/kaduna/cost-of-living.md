[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls to enjoy affordable and delicious meals.
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to reduce transport costs.
- Shop at local markets or stores for groceries and other essentials to avoid high prices at supermarkets.
- Avoid exchanging money at airports or hotels, and instead use ATMs or local banks to get a better exchange rate.
- Learn some basic Hausa phrases to negotiate prices and interact with locals, which can help you save money and have a more authentic experience.
