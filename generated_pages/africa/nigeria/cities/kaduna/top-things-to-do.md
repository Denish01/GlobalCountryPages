# Top Things to Do in Kaduna — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Kaduna, visit the **Kaduna National Museum**, explore the **Kajuru Castle**, and stroll along the **Kaduna River**, which offer a glimpse into the city's rich history, cultural heritage, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Hausa language class** to learn about the local culture and customs
- Visit a **traditional market** to experience the sights, sounds, and smells of local commerce
- Attend a **Durbar festival** to witness the rich cultural heritage of the city
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the scenic countryside
- Learn about **traditional crafts** such as weaving and pottery
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about local cuisine and ingredients
- Explore the **Kaduna nightlife** and enjoy live music and entertainment
