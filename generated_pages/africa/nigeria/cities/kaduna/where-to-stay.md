# Where to Stay in Kaduna — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To stay in Kaduna, consider the city's best areas, including **Kaduna North**, **Kaduna South**, **Barnawa**, **Kakuri**, **Tudun Wada**, **Ungwan Rimi**, and **Kawo**, each offering a unique experience for different types of travelers.

For each of the top areas, here's a brief overview:
**Kaduna North** (Kaduna)
Best For: Business travelers
Vibe: Known for its commercial centers and government institutions, Kaduna North has a bustling and professional atmosphere, with many amenities and services available.
Price Range: $50-$200/night
Pros: Close proximity to major businesses, easy access to transportation, and a variety of restaurants and shops.
Cons: Traffic congestion and noise pollution.

**Kaduna South** (Kaduna)
Best For: Families and luxury travelers
Vibe: This area is characterized by its serene and peaceful environment, with many upscale residences and amenities, making it ideal for families and those seeking a high-end experience.
Price Range: $100-$500/night
Pros: Quiet and secure, with access to quality schools and healthcare facilities, and a range of recreational activities.
Cons: Limited nightlife and entertainment options.

**Barnawa** (Kaduna)
Best For: Backpackers and budget travelers
Vibe: A lively and vibrant neighborhood, Barnawa is known for its affordable accommodations, local markets, and traditional restaurants, offering a unique cultural experience.
Price Range: $10-$50/night
Pros: Affordable prices, rich cultural heritage, and a variety of local cuisine.
Cons: Limited amenities and services, and a somewhat chaotic environment.

**Kakuri** (Kaduna)
Best For: Mid-range travelers
Vibe: A residential area with a mix of old and new buildings, Kakuri offers a balance of affordability and comfort, with easy access to public transportation and local amenities.
Price Range: $20-$100/night
Pros: Convenient location, affordable prices, and a range of accommodation options.
Cons: Traffic congestion during peak hours.

**Tudun Wada** (Kaduna)
Best For: Students and young travelers
Vibe: A bustling and lively neighborhood, Tudun Wada is home to several educational institutions and offers a range of affordable accommodations and social activities.
Price Range: $15-$70/night
Pros: Affordable prices, vibrant social scene, and access to educational institutions.
Cons: Noise pollution and limited parking.

**Ungwan Rimi** (Kaduna)
Best For: Families and long-stay travelers
Vibe: A quiet and peaceful neighborhood, Ungwan Rimi is known for its serene environment, with many residential areas and local amenities, making it ideal for families and those seeking a relaxed atmosphere.
Price Range: $30-$150/night
Pros: Quiet and secure, with access to local markets and amenities, and a range of recreational activities.
Cons: Limited nightlife and entertainment options.

**Kawo** (Kaduna)
Best For: Luxury travelers and business executives
Vibe: An upscale neighborhood, Kawo is characterized by its modern infrastructure, upscale residences, and high-end amenities, offering a luxurious and convenient experience.
Price Range: $150-$500/night
Pros: Modern infrastructure, high-end amenities, and easy access to major businesses and transportation.
Cons: High prices and limited availability of budget options.
