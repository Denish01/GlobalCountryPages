# Where to Stay in Calabar — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Calabar, the best areas to stay are in the city center, near major landmarks and transportation hubs, offering a range of options for different types of travelers.

For each of the top areas in Calabar, here's a breakdown of what to expect:
**Ekim Street** (Calabar)
Best For: Luxury travelers
Vibe: Ekim Street is known for its upscale atmosphere, with many high-end restaurants and shops, making it a great spot for those looking for a luxurious experience. The area is also relatively quiet and safe.
Price Range: $100-$300/night
Pros: Close proximity to major landmarks, high-end amenities, safe and secure environment
Cons: Can be quite expensive, limited options for budget travelers

**Parliamentary Road** (Calabar)
Best For: Business travelers
Vibe: Parliamentary Road is a bustling area, with many government offices and businesses, making it a great spot for those traveling for work. The area is also close to many restaurants and entertainment options.
Price Range: $80-$200/night
Pros: Convenient location, many amenities nearby, easy access to transportation
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, limited parking options

**Marian Road** (Calabar)
Best For: Families
Vibe: Marian Road is a quiet and residential area, with many parks and green spaces, making it a great spot for families. The area is also close to many schools and educational institutions.
Price Range: $50-$150/night
Pros: Safe and secure environment, many amenities for families, quiet and peaceful atmosphere
Cons: Limited options for nightlife and entertainment, can be far from city center

**Ibb Way** (Calabar)
Best For: Backpackers
Vibe: Ibb Way is a lively and vibrant area, with many budget-friendly options for accommodation and dining. The area is also close to many cultural attractions and landmarks.
Price Range: $20-$50/night
Pros: Affordable options, lively atmosphere, close to cultural attractions
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, limited amenities for luxury travelers

**Atimbo** (Calabar)
Best For: Mid-range travelers
Vibe: Atimbo is a bustling area, with many markets and shopping centers, making it a great spot for those looking for a mix of culture and convenience. The area is also close to many restaurants and entertainment options.
Price Range: $30-$80/night
Pros: Convenient location, many amenities nearby, affordable prices
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, limited options for luxury travelers

**State Housing** (Calabar)
Best For: Long-stay travelers
Vibe: State Housing is a quiet and residential area, with many apartments and houses available for rent. The area is also close to many amenities, including shops and restaurants.
Price Range: $40-$100/night
Pros: Affordable options, quiet and peaceful atmosphere, many amenities nearby
Cons: Limited options for nightlife and entertainment, can be far from city center
