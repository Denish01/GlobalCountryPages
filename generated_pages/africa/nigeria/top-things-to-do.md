[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a traditional **Yoruba festival**, such as the Osun Osogbo festival, to experience the rich cultural heritage of Nigeria
- Take a **woodcarving workshop** in the city of Aburi, where you can learn about the traditional craft of woodcarving
- Go on a **safari adventure** in the **Yankari Game Reserve**, where you can see a variety of wildlife, including elephants and lions
- Explore the **ancient Kano City**, with its rich history and cultural significance
- Visit a **local market**, such as the bustling **Balogun Market** in Lagos, to experience the sights and sounds of Nigerian commerce
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Nigerian cuisine, including dishes such as **jollof rice** and **egusi soup**
- Go on a **boat tour** of the **Lagos Lagoon**, where you can see the city from a different perspective

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Yankari Game Reserve**, a wildlife reserve with a variety of animals, including elephants and lions
- Explore the **Osun Osogbo Sacred Grove**, a sacred forest with a variety of flora and fauna
- Go **hiking** in the **Obudu Plateau**, a scenic area with stunning views
- Visit the **Gurara Falls**, a breathtaking waterfall in a scenic setting
- Relax on the **Bar Beach** in Lagos, a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Kainji Lake**, a large man-made lake with a variety of wildlife
