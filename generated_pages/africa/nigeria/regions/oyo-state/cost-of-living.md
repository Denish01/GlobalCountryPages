# Cost of Living in Oyo State - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Oyo State is generally affordable, with a daily budget of $20-50 USD for backpackers, $30-70 USD for mid-range travelers, and $50-100 USD for those seeking comfort.

Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $20-50 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $30-70 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $50-100 USD
Currency: NGN
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Oyo State? [/SECTION]
Oyo State, located in Nigeria, offers a relatively low cost of living compared to other regions in Africa. The state's economy is primarily driven by agriculture, which contributes to the availability of affordable food and other essential items. Compared to its neighboring states, Oyo State has a similar cost of living, making it an attractive destination for both locals and tourists. The cost of living in Oyo State is significantly lower than in major cities like Lagos, making it an excellent choice for those seeking an affordable Nigerian experience.

In terms of global averages, Oyo State is considered one of the more affordable regions to live in or visit. The cost of accommodation, food, and transportation is relatively low, making it an attractive destination for backpackers and budget travelers. However, it's essential to note that prices can vary depending on the location, with cities like Ibadan tend to be more expensive than rural areas.
