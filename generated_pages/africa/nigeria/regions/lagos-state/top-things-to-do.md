[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **stilt walking** lesson with a local expert in the coastal town of Badagry
- Attend a **traditional Yoruba wedding** ceremony to experience the rich cultural heritage
- Go on a **street food tour** to taste the delicious and spicy Nigerian cuisine
- Learn how to make **Adire** textile, a traditional Yoruba fabric dyeing technique
- Take a **boat ride** on the Lagos Lagoon to see the city from a different perspective
- Visit the **Lagos Fish Market** to see the fresh seafood and interact with the local traders
- Explore the **Balogun Market** to shop for traditional Nigerian clothing and souvenirs
- Learn about the **history of the Efik people** and their cultural significance in Lagos State

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lekki Conservation Centre** to see the wildlife and walk on the canopy walkway
- Relax on the **Bar Beach** and enjoy the scenic views of the Atlantic Ocean
- Explore the **Badagry Beach** and learn about its historical significance
- Go **hiking** in the **Akodo Forest Reserve** to see the unique flora and fauna
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Lagos Lagoon** to see the city from a different perspective
- Visit the **Elegushi Royal Beach** to enjoy the recreational facilities and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]

- Explore the **Nike Art Gallery** to see traditional and contemporary Nigerian art
