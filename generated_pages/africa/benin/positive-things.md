[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Royal Palaces of Abomey** are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a testament to the rich cultural heritage of the Fon people.
- The **Benin National Museum** in Porto-Novo is home to a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the country's history and culture.
- The **Ganvie Stilt Village** is a unique and fascinating example of traditional Beninese architecture, with hundreds of houses built on stilts over the water.
- The **Dassa-Zoume Forest** is a sacred site for the local population, with several ancient shrines and temples dedicated to the **Vodun** deity.
- The **Fête du Vodun** is a colorful and vibrant festival that celebrates the **Vodun** culture and traditions of Benin.
