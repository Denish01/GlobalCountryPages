# Top Things to Do in Rwanda — Attractions & Experiences

Rwanda offers a diverse range of exciting activities and experiences, from trekking to see **mountain gorillas** in Volcanoes National Park to exploring the vibrant city of Kigali, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Gorilla trekking**: hike through Volcanoes National Park to see these majestic creatures up close
- **Traditional Intore dance performances**: experience Rwanda's rich cultural heritage through music and dance
- **Local craft workshops**: learn traditional crafts like basket weaving and woodcarving
- **Coffee plantation tours**: discover Rwanda's coffee production process and taste some of the world's best coffee
- **Community-based tourism**: engage with local communities and learn about their daily lives and traditions
- **Birdwatching**: spot over 700 bird species in Rwanda's diverse landscapes
- **Drumming lessons**: learn traditional Rwandan drumming and rhythms
- **Rwandan cuisine cooking classes**: discover the unique flavors and ingredients of Rwandan cooking

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Volcanoes National Park**: home to mountain gorillas, golden monkeys, and diverse wildlife
- **Nyungwe Forest National Park**: lush forest with chimpanzees, monkeys, and over 300 bird species
- **Akagera National Park**: big five game viewing, birdwatching, and scenic landscapes
- **Lake Kivu**: stunning scenery, water sports, and relaxation
- **Mount Bisoke**: hike to the crater lake and enjoy breathtaking views
- **Gishwati-Mukura National Park**: diverse wildlife, including chimpanzees and monkeys

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Kigali Genocide Memorial Centre**: a poignant tribute to the 1994 genocide, offering insight into Rwanda's history
- **Rwanda National Museum**: showcasing Rwanda's history, culture, and natural heritage
- **Iby'Iwacu Cultural Village**: traditional Rwandan village with music, dance, and crafts
- **Musanze Caves**: ancient caves with unique rock formations and bat colonies
- **Butare Royal Palace**: a historic palace showcasing Rwanda's traditional architecture and history
- **Kigali City Market**: a bustling marketplace with local crafts, food, and souvenirs

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Twin Lakes of Burera and Ruhondo**: stunning scenery and traditional fishing villages
- **The Congo Nile Trail**: a scenic hiking trail with breathtaking views of Lake Kivu
- **The village of Kinigi**: a charming village with traditional crafts and stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- **The hot springs of Gisenyi**: a relaxing spot with natural hot springs and stunning scenery
- **The Kigali Public Library**: a modern library with a wide range of books and a peaceful atmosphere

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Kigali to Musanze**: 80 km, 1.5 hours, visit Volcanoes National Park and the Musanze Caves
- **Kigali to Butare**: 130 km, 2.5 hours, visit the Rwanda National Museum and the Butare Royal Palace
- **Kigali to Lake Kivu**: 150 km, 3 hours, enjoy water sports and stunning scenery
- **Musanze to Gisenyi**: 60 km, 1 hour, visit the hot springs and enjoy stunning views of Lake Kivu
- **Butare to Nyungwe Forest National Park**: 50 km, 1 hour, hike in the forest and spot chimpanzees and monkeys
