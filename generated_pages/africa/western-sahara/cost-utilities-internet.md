# Utility & Internet Costs in Western Sahara

The cost of utilities and internet in Western Sahara can vary, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $100 to $200 USD, while internet plans can start from $20 to $50 USD per month.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Western Sahara's utility infrastructure is relatively underdeveloped, with most services provided by the Moroccan government. The cost of utilities can fluctuate, but overall, they tend to be stable. However, there can be seasonal variations in costs, particularly during the summer months when electricity demand increases due to the hot desert climate. The Moroccan government has implemented measures to improve the utility infrastructure, including investments in renewable energy and water desalination plants.

The quality of utility services in Western Sahara can vary depending on the location. In urban areas such as Laayoune, the capital, services are generally more reliable, while in rural areas, they can be less consistent. The cost of utilities also varies depending on the location, with prices tend to be higher in urban areas. Additionally, the cost of utilities can be affected by the political situation in the region, with prices potentially increasing due to tensions or conflicts.
