[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Explore the **medina** of Algiers, a historic neighborhood with narrow streets, traditional shops, and stunning views of the Mediterranean
- Take a **camel trek** through the Sahara Desert, experiencing the unique culture and stunning natural beauty of the region
- Attend a **traditional Berber wedding**, with its unique music, dance, and cuisine
- Visit a **local market**, with its vibrant colors, unique smells, and stunning array of traditional products
- Take a **cooking class**, learning how to prepare traditional Algerian dishes such as **couscous** and **chakchouka**
- Go **sandboarding** in the Sahara Desert, a unique and exhilarating experience
- Explore the **caves** of the Tassili n'Ajjer National Park, with their stunning rock formations and ancient paintings
