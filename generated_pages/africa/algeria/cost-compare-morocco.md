[SECTION] Overview: Algeria vs Morocco [/SECTION]
The cost of living in Algeria and Morocco can vary significantly, with Algeria being the more affordable option. Overall, Algeria is generally cheaper than Morocco, with a difference of around 20-30% in the overall cost of living.

Additionally, the cost of food and transportation in Algeria is also lower, making it a more attractive option for expats and travelers on a budget.
