# Utility & Internet Costs in Algeria

The cost of utilities and internet in Algeria can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Algeria's utility infrastructure is generally well-developed, with a high percentage of the population having access to electricity, water, and gas. However, the costs of these utilities can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher demand during the summer months leading to increased prices. The government has implemented measures to stabilize prices, but the overall cost of utilities can still vary significantly depending on the region and usage patterns. Additionally, the quality of utility services can differ between urban and rural areas, with cities like Algiers and Oran tend to have more reliable and efficient services.

The cost of utilities in Algeria is also influenced by the country's economic conditions, including inflation and currency fluctuations. With an inflation rate of 4.0% in 2024, the cost of utilities is likely to increase over time. Furthermore, the government's efforts to reduce subsidies and increase efficiency in the utility sector may lead to changes in pricing and service quality. As a result, it is essential for consumers to stay informed about the latest developments and adjust their usage patterns accordingly.
