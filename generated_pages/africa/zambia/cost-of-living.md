# Cost of Living in Zambia - Prices & Budget Guide

Living in or visiting Zambia can cost between $30-100 per day, depending on your lifestyle and accommodation choices. 

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable

Currency: ZMW
Tipping Custom: not expected

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Zambia? [/SECTION]
Zambia is considered an affordable country to live in or visit, with a cost of living that is lower than many of its neighbors in Southern Africa. The country's economy is largely driven by agriculture, mining, and tourism, which has helped to keep prices relatively low. Compared to other countries in the region, Zambia is generally cheaper than South Africa and Botswana, but more expensive than neighboring countries like Malawi and Mozambique. Overall, Zambia offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

In general, the cost of living in Zambia is influenced by the country's location, climate, and economy. The capital city, Lusaka, tends to be more expensive than other parts of the country, while smaller towns and rural areas are generally cheaper. Additionally, the cost of living can vary depending on the time of year, with prices tend to be higher during the peak tourist season (June to October).

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local restaurants and try traditional Zambian cuisine, which is often cheaper and more flavorful than international options.
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to save on transport costs.
- Shop at local markets for fresh produce and other essentials.
- Avoid changing money at airports or hotels, where exchange rates tend to be unfavorable.
- Negotiate prices when shopping at markets or taking taxis to get the best deal. 

[CALLOUT] Be sure to research and budget for any additional costs, such as visa fees, travel insurance, and activities like safaris or national park visits, which can add up quickly. [/CALLOUT]
