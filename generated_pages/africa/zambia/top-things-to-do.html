When visiting Zambia, the best things to do include exploring the stunning natural beauty of its national parks, experiencing the vibrant culture of its towns and villages, and taking part in exciting outdoor activities like safaris and white-water rafting.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a microlight flight over Victoria Falls for a breathtaking bird's-eye view
  • Go on a walking safari in South Luangwa National Park, guided by experienced local guides
  • Visit a local village and learn about traditional Zambian customs and way of life
  • Take a boat cruise on Lake Kariba, with stunning views of the surrounding landscape
  • Go white-water rafting on the Zambezi River, an exhilarating adventure experience
  • Learn about conservation efforts at a local wildlife sanctuary, and get up close to rescued animals

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the beautiful scenery and diverse wildlife of Kasanka National Park
  • Hike to the top of Chimfunshi Hill, with stunning views of the surrounding countryside
  • Visit the stunning Kundalila Falls, a picturesque waterfall with a natural pool
  • Go on a canoeing trip down the Zambezi River, with opportunities to spot wildlife and enjoy the scenery
  • Take a birdwatching tour in the Bangweulu Wetlands, a haven for birdlife and other wildlife

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Lusaka National Museum to learn about the history and culture of Zambia
  • Explore the Livingstone Museum, with its collection of artifacts and exhibits on the country's natural history and culture
  • Attend a traditional dance performance, with colorful costumes and energetic music
  • Take a cooking class and learn how to make traditional Zambian dishes
  • Visit a local market, with a vibrant atmosphere and a wide range of goods on sale

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Munda Wanga Environmental Park, a beautiful botanical garden with a variety of plants and animals
  • Explore the Choma Museum, a small but fascinating museum with a collection of artifacts and exhibits on the local history and culture
  • Take a horseback ride through the beautiful scenery of the Zambezi Valley
  • Go on a fishing trip on Lake Kariba, with opportunities to catch a variety of fish species

Day Trips

  1. Take a day trip from Lusaka to the Chongwe River, with stunning scenery and opportunities for fishing and boat cruises (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
  2. Visit the Victoria Falls from Livingstone, with a range of activities including white-water rafting and bungee jumping (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
  3. Explore the Kafue National Park from Lusaka, with a range of wildlife and outdoor activities (distance: 200 km, travel time: 4 hours)
  4. Take a day trip from Livingstone to the Makuni Village, with a chance to learn about traditional Zambian customs and way of life (distance: 20 km, travel time: 1 hour)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries:

3-day itinerary: visit Victoria Falls, take a microlight flight, and go on a walking safari

7-day itinerary: explore the South Luangwa National Park, visit the Lusaka National Museum, and take a boat cruise on Lake Kariba

14-day itinerary: visit all of the major national parks, take a horseback ride through the Zambezi Valley, and attend a traditional dance performance

Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and tours in advance, especially during peak season, and prioritize activities based on your interests and the time of year you visit. Consider visiting during the dry season (May to October) for the best wildlife viewing and outdoor activities.
