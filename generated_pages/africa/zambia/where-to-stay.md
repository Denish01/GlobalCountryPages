# Where to Stay in Zambia — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Zambia, the best places to stay are in Lusaka, Livingstone, and South Luangwa, which offer a range of accommodations to suit different types of travelers and budgets.

For each of the top areas, here's what you can expect:
**Lusaka City Center** (Lusaka)
Best For: business / luxury
Vibe: Lusaka City Center is a bustling area with a mix of modern and colonial architecture, offering a range of amenities and services, from high-end restaurants to shopping malls. The area is known for its vibrant atmosphere and easy access to major attractions.
Price Range: $100-300/night
Pros: convenient location, modern amenities, easy access to major attractions
Cons: traffic congestion, noise pollution

**Livingstone Town** (Livingstone)
Best For: family / adventure
Vibe: Livingstone Town is a charming area with a rich history and stunning natural scenery, offering a range of outdoor activities and cultural experiences. The area is known for its friendly locals and relaxed atmosphere.
Price Range: $50-150/night
Pros: stunning natural scenery, outdoor activities, cultural experiences
Cons: limited amenities, seasonal flooding

**South Luangwa National Park** (Mfuwe)
Best For: luxury / safari
Vibe: South Luangwa National Park is a remote and pristine area with stunning wildlife and natural scenery, offering a range of luxury safari lodges and game drives. The area is known for its tranquil atmosphere and exceptional game viewing.
Price Range: $200-500/night
Pros: stunning wildlife, luxury accommodations, exceptional game viewing
Cons: remote location, high prices

**Kitwe City** (Kitwe)
Best For: business / budget
Vibe: Kitwe City is a bustling area with a mix of modern and industrial architecture, offering a range of amenities and services, from budget-friendly accommodations to shopping centers. The area is known for its vibrant atmosphere and easy access to major attractions.
Price Range: $20-50/night
Pros: affordable prices, convenient location, easy access to major attractions
Cons: noise pollution, limited amenities

**Ndola City** (Ndola)
Best For: business / family
Vibe: Ndola City is a charming area with a mix of modern and colonial architecture, offering a range of amenities and services, from budget-friendly accommodations to shopping centers. The area is known for its friendly locals and relaxed atmosphere.
Price Range: $30-80/night
Pros: affordable prices, convenient location, easy access to major attractions
Cons: limited amenities, traffic congestion
