[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Makishi** mask is a traditional Zambian art form that is used in storytelling and cultural ceremonies.
- The **Kazanga** festival is a popular cultural event that celebrates Zambian music, dance, and theater.
- The **Victoria Falls** is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the world's most spectacular natural wonders.
- The **Lusaka National Museum** showcases Zambia's history, culture, and art, with a collection of traditional artifacts and contemporary artworks.
- The **Batonga** tribe is known for its traditional basket-weaving skills, which are an important part of Zambian cultural heritage.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Levy Mwanawasa**, the third president of Zambia, played a key role in promoting regional integration and economic development in Southern Africa.
- **Dambisa Moyo**, a Zambian economist and author, has written extensively on global economic issues and has been recognized for her contributions to international development.
- **Slapdee**, a Zambian musician, has gained international recognition for his unique blend of traditional and modern music styles.
- The **Zambian diaspora** has made significant contributions to the development of Zambia, with many Zambians living abroad providing financial and technical support to their home country.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **warm hospitality** of the Zambian people is often cited as one of the country's most attractive features, with visitors praising the friendly and welcoming nature of the locals.
- The **traditional Zambian cuisine**, which includes dishes like **nshima** and **kapenta**, is a favorite among visitors, who enjoy the unique flavors and spices of Zambian food.
- The **scenic beauty** of Zambia, including the **Victoria Falls** and the **South Luangwa National Park**, is a major draw for tourists, who come to experience the country's stunning natural landscapes.
- The **vibrant markets** in Lusaka and other towns, which sell everything from traditional crafts to fresh produce, are a great place for visitors to experience Zambian culture and shop for souvenirs.
- The **game reserves** and **national parks**, which offer **safari** tours and **wildlife viewing** opportunities, are a major attraction for visitors who love nature and wildlife.
- The **Lake Kariba**, a massive man-made lake that offers **boating** and **fishing** opportunities, is a popular destination for water sports enthusiasts.
