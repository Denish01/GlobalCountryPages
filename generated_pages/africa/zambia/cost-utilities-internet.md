# Utility & Internet Costs in Zambia

The cost of utilities and internet in Zambia can range from $50 to $200 per month, depending on the type of accommodation and usage, with internet speeds of up to 60 Mbps available for around $30-50 per month.

Currency: ZMW

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Zambia's utility infrastructure is relatively well-developed, with most urban areas having access to electricity, water, and sanitation. However, the quality of these services can vary depending on the location and provider. The cost of utilities in Zambia has been fluctuating in recent years due to factors such as inflation and currency exchange rates. The inflation rate in Zambia is currently around 15.0%, which can affect the cost of utilities and other living expenses. Additionally, there can be seasonal variation in utility costs, with higher costs during the dry season when water and electricity demand is higher.

The Zambian government has been working to improve the country's utility infrastructure, including investments in renewable energy and water supply systems. However, the cost of utilities remains a significant expense for many households, particularly those in urban areas. To manage these costs, households can take steps such as using energy-efficient appliances, conserving water, and negotiating with service providers for better rates.
