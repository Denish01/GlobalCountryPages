# Top Things to Do in Cameroon — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Cameroon, visit its stunning natural attractions, immerse yourself in its rich cultural heritage, and explore its vibrant cities, which showcase a unique blend of African, French, and English influences.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional canoe** ride down the Sanaga River and experience the local fishing culture
- Attend a **traditional dance performance** by the Bamum people in Foumban
- Visit a **local market** in Yaounde and try some of the exotic fruits and spices
- Go on a **safari adventure** in the Waza National Park and spot the Big Five
- Learn about the **art of woodcarving** from local artisans in the Southwest Region
- Experience the **hospitality of the Fulani people** in the North Region and learn about their nomadic culture
- Take a **guided tour** of the coffee plantations in the Northwest Region and learn about the production process

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike to the summit of **Mount Cameroon**, an active volcano and the highest peak in West Africa
- Explore the **Dja Faunal Reserve**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to endangered species
- Visit the **Korup National Park**, a lush rainforest with exotic birds and animals
- Relax on the beautiful beaches of **Kribi** and enjoy the crystal-clear waters
- Take a boat ride on **Lake Nyos** and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding landscape
- Go on a **wildlife safari** in the Waza National Park and spot lions, elephants, and giraffes

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Bamenda Museum of Arts and Culture** and learn about the rich cultural heritage of the region
- Explore the **Maroua Museum** and learn about the history and culture of the Far North Region
- Attend a **traditional festival** in Foumban and experience the vibrant culture of the Bamum people
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Limbe Botanic Garden** and learn about the local flora
- Visit the **Foumban Palace**, a historic palace and museum that showcases the culture of the Bamum people
- Learn about the **art of traditional weaving** from local artisans in the Northwest Region

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Mefou National Park**, a primate sanctuary and conservation center
- Explore the **Kola Njock Village**, a traditional village with a rich cultural heritage
- Take a **hike** through the **Rumpi Hills**, a scenic area with stunning views
- Visit the **Ekom-Nkam Waterfalls**, a beautiful waterfall and picnic spot
- Go on a **boat ride** on the **Sanaga River** and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding landscape

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Yaounde to the **Mefou National Park**, located 30 km away and accessible by car in 1 hour
- Visit the **Kribi Beach** from Douala, located 150 km away and accessible by car in 3 hours
- Explore the **Waza National Park** from Maroua, located 170 km away and accessible by car in 4 hours
- Take a day trip from Bamenda to the **Lake Nyos**, located 100 km away and accessible by car in 2.5 hours
- Visit the **Foumban Palace** from Yaounde, located 300 km away and accessible by car in 5 hours
