# Where to Stay in Cameroon — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When traveling to Cameroon, the best areas to stay include Yaounde, Douala, and Limbe, which offer a range of accommodations and experiences for different types of travelers.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, here's a breakdown of what to expect:
**Bastos** (Yaounde)
Best For: Luxury/Business
Vibe: Known for its upscale atmosphere and modern amenities, Bastos is a popular choice for business travelers and those seeking a luxurious experience. The area is surrounded by embassies, restaurants, and shops, making it a convenient and comfortable place to stay.
Price Range: $100-300/night
Pros: Modern amenities, convenient location, upscale atmosphere
Cons: Traffic can be heavy, limited budget options

**Bonapriso** (Douala)
Best For: Family/Backpacker
Vibe: This vibrant neighborhood is a hub for nightlife, restaurants, and cultural events, making it an excellent choice for families and backpackers looking for an authentic experience. The area is also close to the city center and major attractions.
Price Range: $20-100/night
Pros: Vibrant atmosphere, affordable options, close to attractions
Cons: Can be noisy, limited luxury options

**Limbe** (Limbe)
Best For: Nature Lover/Relaxation
Vibe: Located on the coast, Limbe is a charming town surrounded by beautiful beaches, waterfalls, and lush forests. The area is perfect for nature lovers and those seeking a relaxing getaway.
Price Range: $30-150/night
Pros: Stunning natural beauty, relaxing atmosphere, affordable options
Cons: Limited amenities, can be isolated

**Akwa** (Douala)
Best For: Business/Luxury
Vibe: As the business hub of Douala, Akwa offers a range of modern amenities, restaurants, and shops, making it an ideal choice for business travelers and those seeking a luxurious experience.
Price Range: $80-250/night
Pros: Modern amenities, convenient location, upscale atmosphere
Cons: Traffic can be heavy, limited budget options

**Mvog-Ada** (Yaounde)
Best For: Backpacker/Budget
Vibe: This neighborhood is a popular choice for backpackers and budget travelers, offering affordable accommodations and a vibrant atmosphere. The area is also close to the city center and major attractions.
Price Range: $10-50/night
Pros: Affordable options, vibrant atmosphere, close to attractions
Cons: Can be noisy, limited luxury options
