# Education Costs in Mayotte

The cost of education in Mayotte varies depending on the type of institution and the level of education, with public schools being generally free or subsidized, while private schools and international institutions can range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars per year.

[SECTION] Education System Overview [/SECTION]
The education system in Mayotte is based on the French model, with a strong emphasis on core subjects such as mathematics, science, and language. Public schools in Mayotte are generally considered to be of good quality, with a high literacy rate and a well-structured curriculum. However, private schools and international institutions may offer more specialized programs and better facilities, making them a popular choice for expat families. The language of instruction in Mayotte is French, which can be a challenge for non-French speaking students. Compulsory education in Mayotte lasts for 10 years, from the age of 6 to 16, and is divided into primary and secondary education.

The availability of international schools in Mayotte is limited, with only a few institutions offering programs such as the International Baccalaureate. These schools cater mainly to expat families and offer a more diverse and international curriculum. However, they can be quite expensive, with annual fees ranging from $5,000 to $10,000 or more. Public schools, on the other hand, are generally free or subsidized, making them a more affordable option for local families. Despite the differences in quality and cost, both public and private schools in Mayotte offer a good education, with a strong focus on core subjects and a well-structured curriculum.
