- The **Mayotte Museum** showcases the island's rich cultural heritage, with exhibits on traditional crafts, music, and dance.
- The **Dzama** dance, a traditional dance of Mayotte, is performed during special occasions and festivals, such as the **Grand Marriage**.
- The **Sultani Palace**, a historic palace in Mamoudzou, is a testament to the island's rich history and cultural heritage.
- The **Mayotte Carnival**, a vibrant and colorful festival, celebrates the island's cultural diversity and creativity.
- The **Shimaore** language, a local language spoken in Mayotte, is an important part of the island's cultural identity and heritage.
