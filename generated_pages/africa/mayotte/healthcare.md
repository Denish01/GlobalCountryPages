[SECTION] Healthcare Overview [/SECTION]
Mayotte's healthcare system is based on the French model, as it is an overseas department of France. The system is designed to provide comprehensive coverage to all residents, with a mix of public and private healthcare providers. The quality of care is generally good, especially in urban areas like Mamoudzou, where there are several well-equipped hospitals and clinics. However, in rural areas, access to healthcare can be limited, and the quality of care may be more variable. The public healthcare system is generally considered to be of good quality, but private healthcare providers may offer more specialized services and shorter waiting times.

The public healthcare system in Mayotte is funded through a combination of government funding and patient contributions. Patients are required to pay a portion of their healthcare costs, but the majority of costs are covered by the government. Private healthcare providers, on the other hand, are typically paid directly by patients or through private insurance. Despite the overall good quality of care, there are some challenges facing the healthcare system in Mayotte, including a shortage of specialized healthcare professionals and limited access to certain medical specialties.

Overall, Mayotte's healthcare system is well-organized and provides a good standard of care, especially in urban areas. However, there are some limitations and challenges that need to be addressed, particularly in rural areas. With the right insurance coverage and access to quality healthcare facilities, individuals can receive excellent medical care in Mayotte.

[SECTION] Health Insurance [/SECTION]
- **Private health insurance** is recommended for all residents and visitors to Mayotte, as it can provide access to better quality care and shorter waiting times.
- **Mutuelle de Mayotte** is a local insurance provider that offers a range of health insurance plans, including coverage for hospital stays, doctor visits, and prescription medications.
- **Expatriate health insurance** is available for foreigners living in Mayotte, and can provide coverage for medical expenses, including evacuation and repatriation.
- The cost of health insurance in Mayotte can vary depending on the provider and the level of coverage, but on average, a basic plan can cost around **500-1000 EUR** per month.
- **Supplementary insurance** may be necessary to cover certain medical specialties or services that are not covered by the public healthcare system.
- **Travel insurance** is also recommended for visitors to Mayotte, as it can provide coverage for unexpected medical expenses, including emergency evacuations.
