# Where to Stay in Mayotte — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To find the best place to stay in Mayotte, consider the island's capital Mamoudzou and its surrounding neighborhoods, which offer a range of accommodation options for different types of travelers.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, Mayotte offers a unique blend of culture, nature, and relaxation. 
**Mamoudzou** (Mamoudzou)
Best For: luxury / business
Vibe: Mamoudzou is the bustling capital of Mayotte, with a mix of modern and traditional architecture, and a vibrant market scene. The city offers a range of amenities, including restaurants, shops, and cultural attractions.
Price Range: $100-300/night
Pros: central location, access to amenities, stunning ocean views
Cons: traffic and noise

**Dzaoudzi** (Dzaoudzi)
Best For: family / backpacker
Vibe: Dzaoudzi is a charming coastal town with a relaxed atmosphere, known for its beautiful beaches and water sports. The town has a strong sense of community, with a weekly market and cultural events.
Price Range: $80-200/night
Pros: beach access, water sports, affordable accommodation
Cons: limited nightlife

**Pamandzi** (Pamandzi)
Best For: luxury / family
Vibe: Pamandzi is a picturesque coastal town with a luxurious feel, featuring high-end resorts and stunning beaches. The town is surrounded by lush vegetation and offers a range of outdoor activities, including hiking and snorkeling.
