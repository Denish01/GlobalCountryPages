[SECTION] Overview: Mayotte vs Comoros [/SECTION]
Mayotte and Comoros are two island nations located in the Indian Ocean, off the coast of East Africa. Mayotte is an overseas department of France, while Comoros is an independent country. The economic context of the two nations is vastly different, with Mayotte having a more developed economy and a higher standard of living. The cost of living in Mayotte is generally higher than in Comoros, due to its connection to France and the subsequent higher prices for goods and services. However, Comoros offers a more affordable lifestyle, making it an attractive option for expats and travelers on a budget.

The cost of living in Mayotte is around 20-30% higher than in Comoros, with prices for housing, food, and transportation being significantly higher. However, Mayotte offers a more modern and developed infrastructure, with better access to amenities and services. Comoros, on the other hand, offers a more traditional and authentic island experience, with a lower cost of living and a more relaxed pace of life. Overall, the choice between Mayotte and Comoros depends on individual preferences and priorities, with Mayotte offering a more luxurious and modern lifestyle, and Comoros offering a more affordable and traditional experience.
