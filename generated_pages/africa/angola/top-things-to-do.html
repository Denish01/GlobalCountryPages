Unique Experiences

  • Take a sunset cruise along the coast of Luanda and enjoy the stunning views of the city skyline
  • Experience the unique Afro-Portuguese cuisine of Angola, with its blend of African, Portuguese, and Brazilian influences
  • Visit a traditional market in Luanda and browse the colorful stalls selling local handicrafts and souvenirs
  • Attend a traditional music performance, such as a concert of semba music, which is a unique blend of African and Portuguese rhythms
  • Take a desert safari in the Namib Desert and experience the stunning natural beauty of the desert landscape
  • Visit a local coffee plantation and learn about the production of coffee in Angola
  • Go on a whale watching tour and see the humpback whales that migrate to the coast of Angola each year
