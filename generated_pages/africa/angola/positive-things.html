Cultural Treasures

  • The Kissama National Park is a UNESCO-recognized site and one of the most important wildlife reserves in southern Africa.
  • The Mbanza-Kongo city is a historic site and former capital of the Kingdom of Kongo, showcasing the country's rich cultural heritage.
  • The Tchokwe people are an ethnic group known for their traditional masks and sculptures, which are highly valued for their cultural significance.
  • The Cuito Cuanavale museum showcases the country's history and struggle for independence.
  • The Luanda Carnival is a vibrant festival that celebrates Angolan music, dance, and culture.
