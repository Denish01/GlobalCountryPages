Cocody (Abidjan)

Best For: Luxury travelers

Vibe: Cocody is an upscale neighborhood with a relaxed atmosphere, known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife. The area is home to many embassies and international organizations, giving it a cosmopolitan feel.

Pros: Close proximity to the city center, beautiful beaches, and a wide range of restaurants and bars. The area is also known for its high level of security, making it a safe choice for travelers.

Cons: Can be quite expensive, and traffic in the area can be heavy at times.

Treichville (Abidjan)

Best For: Backpackers and budget travelers

Vibe: Treichville is a lively and vibrant neighborhood, known for its bustling markets and street food. The area is a great place to experience the local culture and meet other travelers.

Booking Tips

  • Book in advance, especially during peak season (December to April)
  • Use online booking platforms such as Booking.com or Airbnb to compare prices and find deals
  • Consider negotiating the price, especially for long stays or off-season bookings
  • Be aware of seasonal pricing, with prices tend to be higher during peak season
  • Use a credit card or other secure payment method to protect against scams or fraud
  • Read reviews and check the location before booking, to ensure that the accommodation meets your needs and is safe
  • Consider booking a package deal, which can include flights, accommodation, and activities, for a discounted price

Safety & Location Tips

  • Research the area before booking, to ensure that it is safe and suitable for your needs
  • Avoid areas with high crime rates, such as the neighborhood of Yopougon in Abidjan
  • Stay in well-lit and populated areas, especially at night
  • Use a reputable taxi or transportation service, and always agree on the price before starting the journey
  • Keep valuables secure, and be aware of your surroundings to avoid pickpocketing or scams
  • Consider staying in a hotel or guesthouse with good security, such as 24-hour guards or CCTV cameras
