# Where to Stay in Ivory Coast — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Ivory Coast, the best areas to stay are generally located in the major cities of Abidjan, Yamoussoukro, and San-Pédro, which offer a range of accommodations for different types of travelers.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, here is a brief overview:
**Cocody** (Abidjan)
Best For: Luxury travelers
Vibe: Cocody is an upscale neighborhood with a relaxed atmosphere, known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife. The area is home to many embassies and international organizations, giving it a cosmopolitan feel.
Price Range: $100-300/night
Pros: Close proximity to the city center, beautiful beaches, and a wide range of restaurants and bars. The area is also known for its high level of security, making it a safe choice for travelers.
Cons: Can be quite expensive, and traffic in the area can be heavy at times.

**Treichville** (Abidjan)
Best For: Backpackers and budget travelers
Vibe: Treichville is a lively and vibrant neighborhood, known for its bustling markets and street food. The area is a great place to experience the local culture and meet other travelers.
Price Range: $20-50/night
Pros: Affordable accommodations, lively atmosphere, and a wide range of street food options. The area is also close to the city center, making it easy to get around.
Cons: Can be quite noisy, and the area is not as safe as some other parts of the city.

**Yamoussoukro City Center** (Yamoussoukro)
Best For: Business travelers and those interested in history and culture
Vibe: Yamoussoukro City Center is a quiet and laid-back area, with a rich history and cultural significance. The area is home to many landmarks, including the Basilica of Our Lady of Peace of Yamoussoukro.
Price Range: $50-150/night
Pros: Close proximity to historical landmarks, quiet atmosphere, and a range of accommodations options. The area is also known for its beautiful parks and gardens.
Cons: Can be quite quiet, and the area is not as lively as some other parts of the city.

**San-Pédro** (San-Pédro)
Best For: Families and those looking for a beach vacation
Vibe: San-Pédro is a coastal town with a relaxed atmosphere, known for its beautiful beaches and water sports. The area is a great place to unwind and enjoy the sun and sea.
Price Range: $30-100/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, relaxed atmosphere, and a range of water sports options. The area is also known for its delicious seafood.
Cons: Can be quite remote, and the area is not as well-connected to the rest of the country as some other parts.

**Plateau** (Abidjan)
Best For: Business travelers and those looking for a central location
Vibe: Plateau is a bustling neighborhood, known for its skyscrapers and lively atmosphere. The area is home to many businesses and organizations, making it a great place to stay for work.
Price Range: $80-200/night
Pros: Central location, close proximity to businesses and organizations, and a wide range of restaurants and bars. The area is also known for its high level of security.
Cons: Can be quite crowded, and the area is not as laid-back as some other parts of the city.

**Abobo** (Abidjan)
Best For: Families and those looking for a local experience
Vibe: Abobo is a lively and vibrant neighborhood, known for its markets and street food. The area is a great place to experience the local culture and meet other travelers.
Price Range: $20-50/night
Pros: Affordable accommodations, lively atmosphere, and a wide range of street food options. The area is also close to the city center, making it easy to get around.
Cons: Can be quite noisy, and the area is not as safe as some other parts of the city.

[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]
- Book in advance, especially during peak season (December to April)
- Use online booking platforms such as Booking.com or Airbnb to compare prices and find deals
- Consider negotiating the price, especially for long stays or off-season bookings
- Be aware of seasonal pricing, with prices tend to be higher during peak season
- Use a credit card or other secure payment method to protect against scams or fraud
- Read reviews and check the location before booking, to ensure that the accommodation meets your needs and is safe
- Consider booking a package deal, which can include flights, accommodation, and activities, for a discounted price

[SECTION] Safety & Location Tips [/SECTION]
- Research the area before booking, to ensure that it is safe and suitable for your needs
- Avoid areas with high crime rates, such as the neighborhood of Yopougon in Abidjan
- Stay in well-lit and populated areas, especially at night
- Use a reputable taxi or transportation service, and always agree on the price before starting the journey
- Keep valuables secure, and be aware of your surroundings to avoid pickpocketing or scams
- Consider staying in a hotel or guesthouse with good security, such as 24-hour guards or CCTV cameras
