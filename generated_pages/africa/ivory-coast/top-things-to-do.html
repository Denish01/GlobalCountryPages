  • Experience the FÃªte des Masques, a traditional festival where locals wear elaborate masks and costumes
  • Visit a cocoa farm, where you can learn about the production process and taste some of the world's best chocolate
  • Go on a safari in one of the many national parks, where you can spot a wide range of wildlife, including elephants, lions, and giraffes
  • Take a boat tour of the Ebrie Lagoon, where you can see a wide range of aquatic life, including hippos and crocodiles
  • Attend a traditional dance performance, where you can experience the vibrant culture and music of the Ivory Coast
  • Visit a local market, where you can try traditional foods, buy handicrafts, and experience the vibrant atmosphere

Outdoor & Nature

  • Tai National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring a diverse range of flora and fauna

Day Trips

  1. Abidjan to Grand Bassam (30km, 1 hour), a colonial town with well-preserved French architecture and a rich history
  2. Yamoussoukro to Tai National Park (200km, 3 hours), a UNESCO World Heritage Site with a diverse range of flora and fauna
  3. San Pedro to Sassandra (100km, 2 hours), a beach town with a vibrant atmosphere and a wide range of water sports
  4. Abidjan to Assinie Beach (80km, 2 hours), a stunning beach with crystal-clear waters and a vibrant atmosphere
  5. Man to Mount Nimba (50km, 1 hour), a mountain with stunning views, hiking trails, and a chance to spot rare wildlife

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries:

3-day (highlights): Visit Abidjan, Yamoussoukro, and Grand Bassam to experience the country's vibrant culture and history

7-day (comprehensive): Explore the national parks, including Tai and Comoe, and visit the beach towns, such as Sassandra and Assinie

14-day (deep exploration): Take your time to experience the country's diverse culture, history, and natural beauty, including the mountains, forests, and wildlife

Key tips for booking and prioritizing: plan ahead, book accommodations and tours in advance, and prioritize the most popular attractions and experiences.
