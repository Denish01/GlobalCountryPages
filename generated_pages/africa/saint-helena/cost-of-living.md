# Cost of Living in Saint Helena - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Saint Helena is relatively high due to its remote location and limited access to goods and services, making it a challenging destination for budget travelers.

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Saint Helena? [/SECTION]
Saint Helena is a British Overseas Territory located in the South Atlantic Ocean, and its remote location contributes to its high cost of living. The island has a limited economy, and most goods are imported, which drives up prices. Compared to other destinations in Africa, Saint Helena is one of the most expensive places to visit or live. The cost of living is similar to that of other remote islands, such as the Falkland Islands or the Isle of Man. However, the unique culture and history of Saint Helena make it an attractive destination for travelers who are willing to pay a premium for the experience.

The high cost of living in Saint Helena is also due to the lack of competition and the limited availability of certain goods and services. The island has a small population, and the demand for certain products is low, which can drive up prices. Additionally, the cost of transportation to and from the island is high, which can make it difficult for travelers to find affordable options. Despite the high cost of living, Saint Helena offers a unique and rewarding experience for travelers who are willing to pay the price.
