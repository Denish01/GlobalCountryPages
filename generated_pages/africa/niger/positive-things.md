[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Ayórú** festival is a significant cultural event in Niger, celebrating the harvest season and featuring traditional music, dance, and food.
- The **Djenné Mosque** is a famous architectural landmark and a symbol of Niger's rich cultural heritage, built in the 13th century and rebuilt in the 19th century.
- The **Hausa people** are a prominent ethnic group in Niger, known for their rich cultural traditions, including their unique language, music, and art.
- The **Niger River** is a vital cultural and economic resource, supporting fishing, agriculture, and trade, and is also a popular destination for tourists and travelers.
- The **Wodaabe Gerewol Festival** is a unique cultural event, where men from the Wodaabe ethnic group gather to compete in a beauty pageant, showcasing their traditional clothing and accessories.
