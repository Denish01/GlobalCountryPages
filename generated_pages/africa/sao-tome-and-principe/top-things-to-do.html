Unique Experiences

  • Take a whale watching tour to spot humpback whales, pilot whales, and other marine life in their natural habitat
  • Go on a coffee plantation tour to learn about the production of high-quality coffee and taste some of the best coffee in the world
  • Experience the traditional music and dance of Sao Tome and Principe, including the famous santomean genre
  • Take a cooking class to learn how to prepare traditional dishes such as calulu and arroz de pescado
  • Go birdwatching in the Obo National Park to spot some of the over 900 species of birds found in the country
  • Take a fishing tour to catch some of the fresh seafood found in the waters surrounding the islands
  • Explore the colonial-era architecture of Sao Tome and Principe, including the historic town of Sao Tome

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the Obo National Park, which covers over 30% of the country and features lush tropical forests, diverse wildlife, and stunning natural scenery
  • Hike to the Pico CÃ£o Grande peak, a unique rock formation and challenging hike with breathtaking views
  • Relax on the stunning beaches of Praia de Santo Antonio, Praia de Micondo, and Praia de Roca Sao Joao
  • Take a boat tour to explore the surrounding waters and spot marine life such as dolphins and turtles
  • Go snorkeling or scuba diving to explore the coral reefs and shipwrecks found in the waters surrounding the islands

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Museu Nacional de Sao Tome e Principe to learn about the country's history, culture, and natural heritage
  • Explore the historic town of Sao Tome, including the Fortaleza de Sao Tome and the Roca Sao Joao
  • Attend a traditional music and dance performance to experience the vibrant culture of Sao Tome and Principe
  • Take a cooking class to learn how to prepare traditional dishes and learn about the local cuisine
  • Visit the Jardim Botanico to see the beautiful gardens featuring a wide variety of tropical plants and flowers
  • Explore the Roca Agostinho Neto, a historic plantation house and museum showcasing the country's colonial past

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Praia de Abrolhos, a secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and stunning rock formations
  • Explore the Sao Nicolau Waterfall, a beautiful waterfall located in the heart of the Obo National Park
  • Take a hike to the Pico Papagaio, a challenging hike with breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
  • Visit the Vila de Sao Joao, a historic town with a rich cultural heritage and stunning colonial-era architecture
  • Explore the Mercado de Sao Tome, a bustling marketplace where you can find fresh produce, handicrafts, and local souvenirs

  • Visit the Praia de Micondo, which is located about 20 km from Sao Tome and takes about 30 minutes to reach by car
  • Take a day trip to the Roca Sao Joao, which is located about 15 km from Sao Tome and takes about 20 minutes to reach by car
