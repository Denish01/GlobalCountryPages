# Where to Stay in Gabon — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To find the best place to stay in Gabon, consider the coastal city of Libreville for its vibrant atmosphere and easy access to amenities, or the scenic town of Port-Gentil for its beautiful beaches and water sports.

For each of the top areas to stay in Gabon, the following neighborhoods are recommended:
**Owendo** (Libreville)
Best For: business travelers
Vibe: Owendo is a bustling commercial area with a mix of modern and traditional architecture, offering a unique blend of African and French influences. The neighborhood is known for its lively markets and restaurants.
Price Range: $100-$300/night
Pros: close proximity to the port and major businesses, a wide range of dining options, easy access to public transportation
Cons: can be noisy and crowded, limited options for budget travelers

**Glass** (Libreville)
Best For: luxury travelers
Vibe: Glass is an upscale neighborhood with beautiful beaches and stunning ocean views, offering a tranquil and relaxing atmosphere. The area is known for its high-end hotels and resorts.
Price Range: $250-$500/night
Pros: stunning ocean views, beautiful beaches, high-end amenities
Cons: can be expensive, limited options for budget travelers

**Louis** (Libreville)
Best For: families
Vibe: Louis is a quiet and residential neighborhood with a mix of modern and traditional homes, offering a peaceful and laid-back atmosphere. The area is known for its beautiful parks and gardens.
Price Range: $80-$200/night
Pros: peaceful and quiet, beautiful parks and gardens, easy access to public transportation
Cons: limited options for dining and entertainment

**Port-Gentil** (Port-Gentil)
Best For: backpackers and adventure seekers
Vibe: Port-Gentil is a scenic town with beautiful beaches and a vibrant atmosphere, offering a mix of water sports and outdoor activities. The town is known for its stunning natural beauty and friendly locals.
Price Range: $20-$100/night
Pros: beautiful beaches, vibrant atmosphere, affordable prices
Cons: limited options for luxury travelers, can be crowded during peak season

**Oyem** (Oyem)
Best For: nature lovers
Vibe: Oyem is a scenic town surrounded by beautiful forests and wildlife, offering a peaceful and tranquil atmosphere. The town is known for its stunning natural beauty and outdoor activities.
Price Range: $30-$150/night
Pros: stunning natural beauty, peaceful and tranquil atmosphere, affordable prices
Cons: limited options for luxury travelers, can be difficult to access
