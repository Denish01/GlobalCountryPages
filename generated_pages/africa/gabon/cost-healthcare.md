# Healthcare Costs in Gabon

The cost of healthcare in Gabon can vary significantly depending on whether you use public or private facilities, with public healthcare generally being more affordable but often less reliable, while private healthcare offers better quality but at a higher cost.

[SECTION] Healthcare System Overview [/SECTION]
Gabon's healthcare system is a mixed model, combining both public and private providers. Public healthcare facilities are generally less expensive but often lack the quality and resources of private facilities, which are more commonly used by foreigners and wealthy locals. For locals, the public healthcare system is often the only option due to financial constraints, while foreigners and expats usually opt for private healthcare due to its higher quality and better equipment. Having health insurance is highly recommended, especially for foreigners, as it can help cover the high costs of private healthcare.

The healthcare system in Gabon is not universal, and many people rely on out-of-pocket payments or private insurance to access healthcare services. The government has been working to improve the public healthcare system, but it still faces significant challenges, including a lack of resources, equipment, and trained healthcare professionals. Private healthcare facilities, on the other hand, offer better quality care but are often expensive and out of reach for many locals.
