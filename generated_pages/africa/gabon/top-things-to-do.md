[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Witness the **surfing hippos** at Loango National Park, a rare and unique sight
- Take a **forest elephant safari** in Ivindo National Park, home to a large population of these majestic creatures
- Explore the **traditional villages** of the Bwiti people, known for their vibrant culture and colorful masks
- Visit the **Okanda Village**, a traditional fishing village with a rich history and stunning coastal scenery
- Go on a **nighttime guided tour** to spot the **African civet**, a rare and elusive animal
- Learn about the **Gabonese wrestling tradition**, a popular sport in the country
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Gabon Estuary**, home to a diverse range of marine life and stunning scenery

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Ivindo National Park**: hiking trails, waterfalls, and diverse wildlife
- **Loango National Park**: beach, forest, and savannah ecosystems
- **Crystal Mountains**: scenic hiking trails and stunning rock formations
- **Pongara National Park**: mangrove forests and diverse marine life
- **Mayumba National Park**: pristine beaches and coral reefs
- **Batéké Plateau National Park**: savannah and forest ecosystems

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Libreville Museum**: insight into Gabon's history, culture, and natural environment
- **Gabonese National Museum of Arts and Traditions**: traditional art and crafts
- **St. Michel Cathedral**: historic church with stunning architecture
- **Owendo Market**: vibrant marketplace with local goods and crafts
- **Bwiti Village**: traditional village with vibrant culture and colorful masks
- **Gabonese Cultural Center**: performances, exhibitions, and workshops

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]

- **Sette Cama**: a picturesque village with stunning coastal scenery and vibrant culture
- **Kango**: a charming town with a rich history and stunning natural beauty
