# Top Things to Do in Kenya — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Kenya, some of the top things to do include exploring the country's vast wildlife reserves, experiencing the unique cultural heritage of its people, and taking in the breathtaking natural beauty of its landscapes.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Maasai Mara National Reserve | Narok County | Wildlife Reserve | Witness the **Great Migration** of wildebeests, zebras, and gazelles |
| Lake Nakuru National Park | Nakuru County | National Park | See the **famous flamingos** and black rhinos |
| Amboseli National Park | Kajiado County | National Park | Enjoy **stunning views** of Mount Kilimanjaro |
| Nairobi National Museum | Nairobi | Museum | Learn about Kenya's **natural history** and cultural heritage |
| Fort Jesus | Mombasa | Historical Site | Explore the **16th-century Portuguese fort** and its significance in Kenyan history |
| Hell's Gate National Park | Naivasha | National Park | Take a **hike or bike ride** through the park's unique landscapes |
| Tsavo East National Park | Taita-Taveta County | National Park | Spot **elephants, lions, and giraffes** in their natural habitats |
| Mount Kenya National Park | Tharaka Nithi County | National Park | Climb **Africa's second-highest mountain** and enjoy breathtaking views |
| Lamu Island | Lamu County | Island | Experience the **traditional Swahili culture** and stunning beaches |
| Lake Victoria | Kisumu County | Lake | Take a **boat tour** and see the local fishing villages |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Maasai Mara National Reserve for a unique perspective on the wildlife and landscapes
- Visit a **Maasai village** and learn about the traditional customs and way of life of the Maasai people
- Go on a **safari walk** with a guide and get up close and personal with the wildlife
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Kenyan dishes such as **nyama choma** and **sukuma wiki**
- Attend a **traditional music and dance performance** and experience the vibrant culture of Kenya
- Visit a **local market** and try some of the fresh produce and handicrafts on offer
- Go **stargazing** in the clear skies of the Kenyan wilderness and learn about the constellations and astronomy of the region

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Aberdare National Park** and see the **waterfalls, mountains, and forests**
- Take a **hike** up **Mount Longonot** and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Lake Bogoria** and see the **hot springs and geysers**
- Go on a **boat tour** of **Lake Naivasha** and see the **hippos, birds, and fish**
- Take a **safari drive** through the **Tsavo West National Park** and spot the **wildlife and scenic landscapes**

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Kenya** and learn about the **history and culture** of the country
- Explore the **K Karen Blixen Museum** and learn about the **life and works** of the famous author
- Attend a **traditional wedding** and experience the **local customs and celebrations**
- Take a **tour of the city** and see the **historical landmarks and architecture**
- Visit the **Giraffe Centre** and learn about the **conservation efforts** and **interact with the giraffes**
- Go to a **local church** and experience the **vibrant music and worship**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ol Pejeta Conservancy** and see the **chimpanzees and rhinos**
- Explore the **Meru National Park** and spot the **wildlife and scenic landscapes**
- Take a **tour of the tea plantations** and learn about the **tea production process**
- Go to the **Kakamega Forest** and see the **unique flora and fauna**
- Visit the **Lewa Wildlife Conservancy** and learn about the **conservation efforts** and **interact with the wildlife**

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip to Lake Naivasha** from Nairobi (100 km, 2 hours) and see the **hell's gate national park** and **take a boat tour**
- Visit the **Amboseli National Park** from Nairobi (240 km, 4 hours) and see the **wildlife and stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro**
- Go on a **day trip to the Aberdare National Park** from Nairobi (180 km, 3 hours) and see the **waterfalls, mountains, and forests**
- Take a **day trip to the Lake Bogoria** from Nairobi (280 km, 4 hours) and see the **hot springs and geysers**
- Visit the **Tsavo East National Park** from Mombasa (200 km, 3 hours) and see the **wildlife and scenic landscapes**
