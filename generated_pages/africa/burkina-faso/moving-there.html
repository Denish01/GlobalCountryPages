Burkina Faso is a West African country that attracts expats and travelers with its rich cultural heritage, vibrant music scene, and stunning natural beauty. Many people move to Burkina Faso for work, with the country's growing economy and abundant natural resources making it an attractive destination for entrepreneurs and professionals. Others come to Burkina Faso as part of international aid organizations or non-governmental organizations (NGOs), working on development projects and humanitarian initiatives.
