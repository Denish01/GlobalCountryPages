# Top Things to Do in Burkina Faso — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Burkina Faso, visit the vibrant city of Ouagadougou, explore the stunning natural beauty of the Banfora region, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of the Mossi people, including the impressive **Morondava** and **Dourtenga** villages.
