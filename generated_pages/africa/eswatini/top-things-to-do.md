# Top Things to Do in Eswatini — Attractions & Experiences

Eswatini, a small but vibrant country in southern Africa, offers a wide range of exciting activities and experiences that showcase its unique culture, stunning natural beauty, and rich history, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking for an unforgettable adventure.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take part in a traditional **Swazi** dance performance, learning about the country's rich cultural heritage
- Visit a local **sangoma** (traditional healer) to learn about the country's unique spiritual practices
- Go on a **horseback safari** through the stunning countryside, offering breathtaking views and a chance to spot wildlife
- Attend a ** Reed Dance** ceremony, a unique and colorful event where thousands of women gather to dance and celebrate
- Take a **tour of a traditional Swazi village**, learning about the country's history, customs, and way of life
- Go **stargazing** in the beautiful **Malolotja Nature Reserve**, offering stunning views of the night sky

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the stunning **Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary**, home to a wide range of wildlife, including hippopotamuses, crocodiles, and antelopes
- Hike through the beautiful **Malolotja Nature Reserve**, offering breathtaking scenery and a wide range of flora and fauna
- Visit the **Hlane Royal National Park**, home to the Big Five and offering guided game drives and walking safaris
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Ezulwini Valley**, known for its wide range of bird species
- Take a **boat tour** on the **Maguga Dam**, offering stunning views and a chance to spot wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum** in **Lobamba**, showcasing the country's history, culture, and traditions
- Explore the **Ludzidzini Royal Village**, a historic village and former royal capital
- Attend a **traditional Swazi wedding**, a unique and colorful ceremony
- Take a **tour of the Embo State Palace**, the official residence of the King of Eswatini
- Visit the **Swazi Candles Centre**, a local craft center where you can learn about traditional crafts and buy handmade souvenirs

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Mantenga Nature Reserve**, a beautiful and lesser-known reserve with stunning scenery and a wide range of wildlife
- The **Phophonyane Falls**, a picturesque waterfall and a great spot for hiking and picnicking
- The **Piggs Peak**, a stunning mountain peak with breathtaking views and a wide range of outdoor activities
- The **Mlawula Nature Reserve**, a beautiful and remote reserve with stunning scenery and a wide range of wildlife
