# Where to Stay in Eswatini — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Eswatini, the best areas to stay are in the cities of Mbabane, Manzini, and Ezulwini, which offer a range of accommodation options to suit different types of travelers.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, the following are recommended:
**Mbabane City Centre** (Mbabane)
Best For: Business, Luxury
Vibe: Mbabane City Centre is the hub of Eswatini's government and commerce, offering a vibrant and modern atmosphere with easy access to amenities and services. The area is surrounded by stunning mountain scenery, making it a great base for exploring the country.
Price Range: $100-300/night
Pros: Convenient location, modern amenities, stunning views
Cons: Traffic can be heavy during rush hour, limited budget options

**Ezulwini Valley** (Mbabane)
Best For: Family, Luxury
Vibe: Ezulwini Valley is a picturesque area with stunning scenery, offering a range of outdoor activities and attractions, including the famous Milwane Wildlife Sanctuary. The area has a relaxed and peaceful atmosphere, making it ideal for families and those seeking a tranquil retreat.
Price Range: $80-250/night
Pros: Scenic views, outdoor activities, peaceful atmosphere
Cons: Limited dining options, can be far from city centre

**Manzini** (Manzini)
Best For: Backpacker, Mid-range
Vibe: Manzini is Eswatini's largest city, offering a bustling and vibrant atmosphere with a range of affordable accommodation options. The city has a rich cultural heritage, with many historical sites and traditional markets to explore.
Price Range: $20-100/night
Pros: Affordable, cultural heritage, lively atmosphere
Cons: Can be noisy, limited high-end options

**Hlatikulu** (Hlatikulu)
Best For: Nature Lover, Adventure Seeker
Vibe: Hlatikulu is a small town surrounded by stunning natural beauty, offering a range of outdoor activities, including hiking and birdwatching. The area has a peaceful and serene atmosphere, making it ideal for those seeking a relaxing retreat.
Price Range: $30-150/night
Pros: Scenic views, outdoor activities, peaceful atmosphere
Cons: Limited amenities, can be far from city centre

**Lobamba** (Lobamba)
Best For: History Buff, Culture Vulture
Vibe: Lobamba is Eswatini's legislative capital, offering a rich cultural heritage and historic significance. The area has a unique and fascinating atmosphere, with many traditional sites and landmarks to explore.
Price Range: $40-200/night
Pros: Cultural heritage, historic significance, unique atmosphere
Cons: Limited accommodation options, can be far from city centre
