Unique Experiences

  • Take a pirogue ride along the Niger River and experience the local fishing culture
  • Attend a traditional Dogon mask dance performance in the Dogon Country
  • Visit a local market in Bamako or other towns to experience the vibrant colors and sounds of Malian culture
  • Go on a camel safari in the desert regions of northern Mali
  • Learn about the ancient trade routes that once connected Mali to other parts of Africa and the world
  • Participate in a traditional tea ceremony with a local family
  • Explore the underground tunnels and passageways of the ancient city of Timbuktu

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the Bandiagara Escarpment, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to the Dogon people
  • Take a boat ride on Lake Debo, a beautiful and peaceful spot for birdwatching and relaxation
  • Visit the Niger River, which runs through the heart of Mali and offers stunning scenery and wildlife
  • Go hiking in the Fouta Djallon highlands, a beautiful and rugged region with stunning views
  • Explore the desert landscapes of northern Mali, including the Sahara Desert and the Adrar des Iforas mountain range

  • Visit the Maison des Artisans in Bamako, a center for local crafts and artisans
  • Explore the historical city of Timbuktu, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a center of Islamic learning and culture
