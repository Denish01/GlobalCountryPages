# Top Things to Do in DR Congo — Attractions & Experiences

The Democratic Republic of the Congo offers a diverse array of exciting activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring the vast wilderness of Virunga National Park to immersing oneself in the vibrant culture of Kinshasa.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gorilla trekking** tour in Virunga National Park, offering a chance to see these magnificent creatures up close
- Visit the **Inga Dam**, a massive hydroelectric dam on the Congo River, and learn about its history and significance
- Explore the **caves of Mount Hoyo**, a series of limestone caves and tunnels near the town of Virunga
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, showcasing the vibrant culture and music of the region
- Take a **boat cruise** on Lake Kivu, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Visit a **local mine**, such as the **Kipushi Mine**, to learn about the mining industry and its impact on the region
- Go **birdwatching** in the Ituri Rainforest, home to a wide variety of bird species

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Virunga National Park**, home to mountain gorillas, chimpanzees, and active volcanoes
- **Garamba National Park**, known for its elephant and rhino populations
- **Kahuzi-Biega National Park**, home to lowland gorillas, chimpanzees, and a variety of bird species
- **Mount Nyiragongo**, an active volcano with a highly fluid and fast-moving lava lake
- **Lake Kivu**, a stunning lake with crystal-clear water and surrounding mountains
- **Ituri Rainforest**, home to a wide variety of bird species and other wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Lubumbashi National Museum**, showcasing the history and culture of the region
- **Kinshasa Grand Market**, a bustling marketplace offering a wide range of local goods and souvenirs
- **The Royal Museum of Central Africa**, a museum in Kinshasa showcasing the art and culture of the region
- **The Congo River Festival**, a festival celebrating the music, dance, and culture of the region
- **The Académie des Beaux-Arts**, a school of fine arts in Kinshasa, offering a glimpse into the local art scene
- **The Cathedral of the Sacred Heart**, a historic church in Kinshasa, showcasing the city's colonial past

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Tshopo River**, a lesser-known river with stunning scenery and opportunities for boat cruises
- The **Mangrove Forests of Muanda**, a unique and threatened ecosystem, home to a variety of marine life
- The **caves of Mount Hoyo**, a series of limestone caves and tunnels near the town of Virunga
- The **Lake Mai-Ndombe**, a stunning lake with crystal-clear water and surrounding mountains
- The **Kundelungu National Park**, a lesser-known national park with a wide variety of wildlife and stunning scenery
