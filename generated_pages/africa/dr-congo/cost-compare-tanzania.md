[SECTION] Overview: DR Congo vs Tanzania [/SECTION]
The Democratic Republic of the Congo and Tanzania are two neighboring countries in Africa with distinct economic profiles. The DR Congo has a larger population and a more significant mining industry, while Tanzania has a more diversified economy with a strong focus on agriculture and tourism. In terms of cost of living, the DR Congo is generally cheaper than Tanzania, particularly when it comes to housing and food. However, Tanzania has a more stable economy and a lower inflation rate, which can make it a more attractive option for some expats and investors.

The cost of living in the DR Congo is heavily influenced by the country's economic instability and infrastructure challenges. The capital city, Kinshasa, is one of the most expensive cities in Africa, but the cost of living in other parts of the country can be significantly lower. In contrast, Tanzania has a more consistent cost of living across the country, with the largest city, Dar es Salaam, being relatively affordable compared to other major cities in Africa. Overall, the DR Congo offers a more affordable lifestyle, but Tanzania provides a more stable and secure environment.
