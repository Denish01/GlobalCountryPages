Lesotho is situated in the **Drakensberg Mountains** and the **Maluti Mountains**, which provide a unique and breathtaking landscape. The country's location in the southern hemisphere also means that its seasons are reversed compared to those in the northern hemisphere.

Lesotho's border with South Africa stretches for approximately 909 kilometers, making it one of the longest international borders in southern Africa.
