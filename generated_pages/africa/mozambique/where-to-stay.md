# Where to Stay in Mozambique — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When deciding where to stay in Mozambique, consider the coastal city of Maputo for its vibrant culture and nightlife, or the scenic beach towns of Tofo and Vilankulo for a more relaxed atmosphere. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, Mozambique offers a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. 
**Maputo City** (Maputo)
Best For: luxury / business
Vibe: Maputo City is a bustling metropolis with a rich cultural scene, featuring historic landmarks, vibrant markets, and a variety of restaurants. The city has a lively atmosphere, with a mix of Portuguese and African influences.
Price Range: $100-500/night
Pros: convenient access to major attractions, a wide range of accommodation options, and a vibrant nightlife. 
Cons: traffic congestion, limited parking options.

**Tofo Beach** (Inhambane)
Best For: backpacker / family
Vibe: Tofo Beach is a laid-back coastal town with a stunning beach, crystal-clear waters, and a variety of water sports. The town has a relaxed atmosphere, with a strong focus on outdoor activities and eco-tourism.
Price Range: $20-150/night
Pros: beautiful beach, plenty of outdoor activities, and a range of accommodation options. 
Cons: limited dining options, can be crowded during peak season.

**Vilankulo** (Inhambane)
Best For: luxury / family
Vibe: Vilankulo is a charming coastal town with a rich history, featuring historic landmarks, beautiful beaches, and a variety of water sports. The town has a peaceful atmosphere, with a focus on relaxation and outdoor activities.
Price Range: $50-300/night
Pros: stunning beach, historic landmarks, and a range of accommodation options. 
Cons: limited nightlife, can be expensive.

**Beira City** (Sofala)
