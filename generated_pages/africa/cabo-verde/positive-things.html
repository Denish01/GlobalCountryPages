ScienceRenewable EnergyCabo Verde has made significant strides in renewable energy, with a goal of becoming 100% renewable by 2025, and has already increased its renewable energy production to over 25% of its total energy output
SportsCape Verdean FootballThe Cabo Verde national football team has qualified for the Africa Cup of Nations multiple times, and has produced notable players such as Marco Soares and Ryan Mendes
ArtsMorna MusicCabo Verde is the birthplace of Morna music, a unique genre that combines African, Portuguese, and Brazilian influences, and has been recognized by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity
DevelopmentEconomic GrowthCabo Verde has experienced steady economic growth, with a GDP growth rate of over 4% in recent years, and has made significant progress in reducing poverty and improving living standards
InnovationICT SectorThe government of Cabo Verde has invested heavily in the development of the ICT sector, with the aim of creating a technological hub in West Africa, and has established partnerships with international companies such as Microsoft and IBM
Global ContributionsPeacekeeping MissionsCabo Verde has contributed troops to United Nations peacekeeping missions in several countries, including Mozambique and Sudan, and has played a key role in promoting regional stability and security

People & Global Impact
