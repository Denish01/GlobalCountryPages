[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **morabeça** lesson, a traditional Cape Verdean dance, and learn about the country's vibrant cultural heritage
- Go on a **whale watching** tour and spot humpback whales, pilot whales, and other marine life
- Attend a **Cesária Évora** tribute concert, celebrating the life and music of the legendary Cape Verdean singer
- Visit a **local market** and try traditional foods, such as **cachupa** and **arroz de pescado**
- Take a **yoga class** on the beach and enjoy the stunning views and relaxing atmosphere
- Go on a **fishing trip** and catch fresh seafood, such as tuna and lobster
- Learn about **Cape Verdean music** and its unique blend of African, Portuguese, and Brazilian influences

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Fogo National Park** and hike to the summit of Pico do Fogo
- Visit the **Monte Gordo Natural Park** and discover the park's diverse wildlife and flora
- Relax on the **Santa Maria Beach** and enjoy the crystal-clear waters and white sandy beach
- Go **hiking** in the **São Nicolau** mountains and take in the stunning views
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Maio** coastline and spot dolphins and other marine life
- Visit the **Boa Vista** desert and explore the unique landscape and wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museu Nacional de Cabo Verde** and learn about the country's history, culture, and natural environment
- Explore the **Historic Center of Praia** and discover the city's colonial architecture and rich history
- Attend a **traditional festival**, such as the **Festival de Música de Cabo Verde**
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Cape Verdean dishes
- Visit the **São Filipe** historic town and discover the town's rich history and cultural significance
- Explore the **Vila do Maio** historic town and learn about the town's colonial past

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tarrafal** village and explore the town's historic sites and natural beauty
- Take a **hike** to the **Pico do Fogo** crater and enjoy the stunning views
- Relax on the **Ponta Preta** beach and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere
- Explore the **São Nicolau** island and discover the island's unique culture and history
- Visit the **Boa Vista** island and take part in **water sports** and activities

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip from **Praia** to **Cidade Velha** (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes) and explore the historic town's colonial architecture and rich history
2. Visit **Fogo** island (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour) and hike to the summit of Pico do Fogo
3. Take a day trip from **Sal** to **Santa Maria** (distance: 20 km, travel time: 30 minutes) and relax on the beach and enjoy water sports
4. Explore the **Maio** island (distance: 25 km, travel time: 45 minutes) and discover the island's unique culture and history
5. Visit the **São Nicolau** island (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours) and take part in **hiking** and **water sports**
