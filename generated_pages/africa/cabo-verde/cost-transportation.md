[SECTION] Getting Around Overview [/SECTION]
Cabo Verde offers a range of transportation options, including public buses, taxis, and ride-hailing services. The quality of public transit is generally good, with frequent bus services connecting major cities and towns. Most people in Cabo Verde get around using public buses or shared taxis, known as "aluguer" or "hiace", which are a cost-effective and convenient way to travel. The country's roads are generally in good condition, making it easy to get around by car or taxi.

The main transportation modes in Cabo Verde are buses, taxis, and ride-hailing services, with buses being the most affordable option. The public bus system is well-organized, with routes connecting major cities and towns, and buses running frequently throughout the day. Taxis are also widely available, but can be more expensive than buses. Ride-hailing services, such as Bolt and inDriver, are also available in major cities, offering a convenient and affordable way to get around.
