[SECTION] Foreign Ownership Rules [/SECTION]
- Foreigners are allowed to buy property in the Cayman Islands, but they must obtain a **licence** from the Cayman Islands Government.
- Foreign buyers can purchase property through a **company structure**, which can provide tax benefits and anonymity.
- There are no restrictions on the type of property that can be purchased, but buyers must comply with **local zoning regulations**.
- Foreign buyers must also obtain a **permit** from the Cayman Islands Department of Immigration, which can take several months to process.
- Foreign buyers can also consider **leasehold** options, which can provide a more affordable entry point into the market.
