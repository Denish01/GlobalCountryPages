- Share an apartment with others to reduce rent costs.
- Take advantage of free or low-cost entertainment options, such as hiking or visiting the local museum.
- Buy local products and avoid imported goods, which can be expensive.
- Use the local healthcare system instead of private insurance, as it is generally less expensive.
- Consider buying a used car instead of a new one, as the cost of owning a car can be high in Saint Pierre and Miquelon.
- Use cashback or rewards credit cards for daily purchases, as they can provide significant savings over time.
