[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **snorkeling** or **scuba diving** trip to the **Antilla Shipwreck**, a 400-foot German freighter that sank off the coast of Aruba in 1940
- Explore the **Butterfly Farm**, a tropical garden filled with exotic butterflies and plants
- Go on a **donkey sanctuary tour**, where you can interact with and learn about these gentle creatures
- Take a **stargazing** tour, where you can learn about the constellations and enjoy the clear night sky
- Visit the **Aloe Vera Factory**, where you can learn about the production process and benefits of aloe vera products
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the **Arikok National Park**, where you can explore the island's diverse wildlife and landscapes

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Arikok National Park**, which covers nearly 20% of the island and features diverse wildlife and unique geological formations
- Explore the **Guadirikiri Cave**, an ancient cave with petroglyphs and stunning rock formations
- Relax on **Palm Beach**, a white sand beach with crystal-clear waters
- Go **hiking** in the **Arikok National Park**, where you can explore the island's diverse landscapes and wildlife
- Visit the **California Sand Dunes**, a unique landscape feature with stunning views of the surrounding area
- Take a **kayaking** or **paddleboarding** trip through the **Malmok Beach** area, where you can explore the island's coastline and marine life
