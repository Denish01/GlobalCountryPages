# Top Things to Do in Nicaragua — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **volcano boarding** trip down the slopes of Cerro Negro, one of the most active volcanoes in the country
- Visit a **coffee plantation** in the highlands of Matagalpa and learn about the production process
- Explore the **isletas de Granada**, a series of small islands in Lake Nicaragua with stunning natural beauty
- Go on a **surfing trip** to the Pacific Coast and ride the waves at one of the many popular surf spots
- Take a **Spanish language course** in Granada or León and immerse yourself in the local culture
- Visit a **local market** in Managua or Masaya and try some of the traditional cuisine
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the countryside and explore the rolling hills and scenic landscapes

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Mombacho Volcano National Park** and hike through the stunning cloud forests
- Visit the **Masaya Volcano National Park** and see the active volcano up close
- Relax on the beach at **San Juan del Sur** and enjoy the lively surf culture
- Take a boat trip to **Ometepe Island** and explore the unique wildlife and natural beauty
- Hike through the **Somoto Canyon National Park** and swim in the stunning natural pools
- Visit the **Apoyo Lagoon Natural Reserve** and hike through the stunning natural scenery

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Nicaragua** in Managua and learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Granada Cathedral** and see the stunning colonial architecture
- Take a **cooking class** in León or Granada and learn about the traditional cuisine
- Visit the **Ruins of León Viejo** and see the historic site of the old city
- Attend a **traditional festival** in Masaya or Managua and experience the local culture
- Visit the **Museum of the Revolution** in Managua and learn about the country's recent history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Estelí** and explore the stunning natural scenery and vibrant cultural scene
- Take a trip to **the Solentiname Islands** and see the unique wildlife and stunning natural beauty
- Explore the **city of Bluefields** and experience the unique culture and stunning natural scenery of the Caribbean Coast
- Visit the **El Chocoyero-El Brujo Natural Reserve** and see the stunning natural beauty and unique wildlife
- Go on a **kayaking trip** through the **Isletas de Granada** and explore the stunning natural scenery

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Managua to Masaya** (30 km, 1 hour) and visit the Masaya Volcano National Park
- Visit **Granada** (45 km, 1.5 hours from Managua) and explore the historic city and stunning natural scenery
- Take a day trip from **León to the Ruins of León Viejo** (30 km, 1 hour) and see the historic site of the old city
- Go on a day trip from **San Juan del Sur to the nearby beaches** (10-20 km, 30 minutes) and enjoy the stunning natural scenery and lively surf culture
- Take a day trip from **Estelí to the nearby natural reserves** (20-30 km, 1 hour) and explore the stunning natural scenery and unique wildlife
