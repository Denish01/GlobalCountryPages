# Utility & Internet Costs in Costa Rica

The cost of utilities and internet in Costa Rica can range from $100 to $300 per month for a medium-sized apartment, depending on usage and service providers. 

[FACTBOX]
Monthly Utilities (85 sqm apt): ($150-$250 USD)
Internet (60 Mbps): ($30-$50 USD/month)

Electricity Cost: ($0.15-$0.25 per kWh)
Currency: CRC
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Costa Rica has a well-developed utility infrastructure, with most areas having access to **electricity**, **water**, and **internet**. The cost of utilities in Costa Rica can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher costs during the dry season (December to April) due to increased energy consumption. The country's utility costs are generally stable, but prices may vary depending on the provider and location. For example, the cost of electricity can range from $0.15 to $0.25 per kilowatt-hour (kWh), depending on the time of day and the provider.

The quality of utility infrastructure in Costa Rica is generally good, with most areas having access to reliable **electricity** and **water** services. However, some rural areas may experience occasional outages or disruptions. The cost of utilities can also vary depending on the region, with urban areas like San Jose tend to have higher costs than rural areas. Overall, the cost of utilities in Costa Rica is relatively affordable compared to other countries in the region.
