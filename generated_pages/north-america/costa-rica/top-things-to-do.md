# Top Things to Do in Costa Rica — Attractions & Experiences

Costa Rica offers a diverse range of activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring the **Arenal Volcano** to zip-lining through the **Monteverde Cloud Forest**, and relaxing on the stunning beaches of the **Pacific Coast**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **surfing lesson** in Tamarindo and ride the waves of the Pacific Coast
- Go **zip-lining** through the Monteverde Cloud Forest for a thrilling and breathtaking experience
- Explore the **Arenal Volcano** at night for stunning views of the stars and the volcano's glow
- Visit a **coffee plantation** in the Central Valley and learn about the production process and taste some of the world's best coffee
- Take a **cooking class** in San Jose and learn how to make traditional Costa Rican dishes, including **gallo pinto** and **casados**
- Go **rafting** on the Reventazon River for an adrenaline-pumping adventure through the jungle
- Relax in a **hot spring** surrounded by lush rainforest and stunning views of the Arenal Volcano

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike through the **Corcovado National Park** to spot **jaguars**, **monkeys**, and **macaws** in this diverse ecosystem
- Visit the **Manuel Antonio National Park** for stunning beaches and hiking trails through the lush rainforest
- Explore the **Monteverde Cloud Forest** for unique plant and animal species, including **orchids** and **quetzals**
- Go **snorkeling** or **scuba diving** in the **Cahuita National Park** to spot **sea turtles** and **rays** in the coral reef
- Take a **boat tour** through the **Tortuguero Canals** to spot **monkeys**, **birds**, and **caimans** in this unique ecosystem
- Visit the **Arenal Volcano National Park** for stunning views and hiking trails through the surrounding rainforest

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Pre-Columbian Gold Museum** in San Jose to learn about the history and culture of Costa Rica's indigenous peoples
- Explore the **San Jose Central Market** for a vibrant and colorful experience, including local cuisine and handicrafts
- Attend a **traditional dance performance** in San Jose, including **salsa** and **merengue**
- Take a **Spanish language class** in San Jose to learn about the local culture and improve your language skills
- Visit the **National Museum of Costa Rica** to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Teatro Nacional de Costa Rica** in San Jose for a stunning example of neoclassical architecture and cultural performances

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kekoldi Indigenous Reserve** to learn about the culture and traditions of Costa Rica's indigenous peoples
- Explore the **Cahuita National Park** for stunning beaches and hiking trails through the lush rainforest
- Take a **boat tour** through the **Gandoca-Manzanillo National Wildlife Refuge** to spot **monkeys**, **birds**, and **caimans** in this unique ecosystem
- Relax in the **hot springs** of the **Tabacon Thermal Resort** surrounded by lush rainforest and stunning views of the Arenal Volcano
- Visit the **town of Turrialba** to explore the stunning **Turrialba Volcano** and learn about the local culture and history

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from San Jose to the **Poas Volcano** (distance: 37 km, travel time: 1 hour) to visit the crater lake and explore the surrounding national park
- Visit the **La Paz Waterfall Gardens** (distance: 31 km, travel time: 45 minutes) for stunning views of the waterfalls and surrounding rainforest
- Explore the **Tortuguero Canals** (distance: 80 km, travel time: 2 hours) for a unique ecosystem and stunning wildlife, including **sea turtles** and **monkeys**
- Take a day trip from Tamarindo to the **Rincon de la Vieja National Park** (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours) to explore the stunning landscapes and spot **monkeys** and **birds**
- Visit the **Monteverde Cloud Forest** (distance: 140 km, travel time: 3 hours) to explore the unique ecosystem and spot **quetzals** and **toucans**
