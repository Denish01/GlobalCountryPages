[SECTION] By Visitor Type [/SECTION]
**Tourists:** Tourists can enter Bonaire without a visa if they are citizens of a visa-exempt country. The typical stay for tourists is 30 days, and extensions are not possible. Tourists must have a valid passport, proof of onward travel, and sufficient funds to support themselves during their stay.
**Business Travelers:** Business travelers may need to apply for a special visa, depending on the purpose and duration of their stay. They must provide an invitation letter from a local company, proof of business activities, and other supporting documents.
**Students:** Students who want to study in Bonaire must apply for a student visa. They must provide proof of enrollment in a local educational institution, financial support, and health insurance.
**Workers:** Workers who want to work in Bonaire must apply for a work permit. They must have a sponsorship from a local employer, and the employer must demonstrate that they cannot find a suitable candidate from the local labor market.
