Historical Context

In 2010, the Netherlands Antilles was dissolved, and Bonaire became a special municipality of the Netherlands.

Today, Bonaire is a popular tourist destination, known for its beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and diving opportunities. The island's unique blend of Dutch, African, and indigenous cultures has created a distinct creole culture, with Papiamento being the primary language spoken by the island's inhabitants.
