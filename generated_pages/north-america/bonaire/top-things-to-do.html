Unique Experiences

  • Take a donkey tour to explore the island's rugged terrain and learn about its history and culture
  • Go on a stargazing tour to take advantage of Bonaire's incredibly clear night skies
  • Learn about the island's sea turtle conservation efforts and participate in a turtle-watching tour
  • Explore the island's cave systems, such as the Cave of the Guanine Bat
  • Participate in a traditional Papiamento cooking class to learn about local cuisine and culture
  • Go on a kayaking tour through the mangroves of Lac Bay to spot marine life
  • Take a sunset sail around the island to enjoy the scenic views and marine life

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the Washington Slagbaai National Park, which covers over 20% of the island
  • Visit the Bonaire National Marine Park, which protects the island's coral reefs and marine life
  • Go hiking at the Gotomeer trail, which offers scenic views of the surrounding landscape
  • Relax on the beach at Lac Bay, which is known for its calm and crystal-clear waters
  • Take a snorkeling or diving tour to explore the island's coral reefs and marine life
  • Go birdwatching at the Gotomeer lake, which is a popular spot for spotting flamingos and other bird species

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Bonaire Museum, which showcases the island's history and culture
  • Explore the Fort Oranje, a 19th-century fort that now serves as a museum
  • Attend a traditional Papiamento festival, such as the Bonaire Day celebrations
  • Take a tour of the slave huts, which provide insight into the island's history and the transatlantic slave trade
  • Visit the Donkey Sanctuary Bonaire, which is home to over 600 donkeys and offers a glimpse into the island's culture and history
  • Go on a guided tour of Kralendijk, which offers insight into the island's history, culture, and architecture

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Seru Largu, a scenic spot with stunning views of the surrounding landscape
  • Explore the Rincon, a small village that is known for its traditional Papiamento culture
  • Go caving at the Cave of the Guanine Bat, which is home to a large colony of bats
  • Relax on the beach at Sorobon Beach, which is a secluded spot with crystal-clear waters
  • Take a tour of the island's south, which offers scenic views and a glimpse into the island's culture and history

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip to Klein Bonaire, a small island located off the coast of Bonaire that is known for its beautiful beaches and coral reefs (distance: 0.5 miles, travel time: 10 minutes, highlights: snorkeling, diving, and relaxing on the beach)
  • Visit Curacao, a neighboring island that is known for its vibrant culture and stunning natural beauty (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 2 hours, highlights: visiting the capital city of Willemstad, exploring the Christoffel National Park, and relaxing on the beach)
  • Explore the Washington Slagbaai National Park, which offers scenic hiking trails and stunning views of the surrounding landscape (distance: 10 miles, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: hiking, birdwatching, and spotting wildlife)
  • Take a day trip to Lac Bay, which is known for its beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters (distance: 5 miles, travel time: 15 minutes, highlights: windsurfing, kayaking, and relaxing on the beach)
  • Visit the Donkey Sanctuary Bonaire, which is home to over 600 donkeys and offers a glimpse into the island's culture and history (distance: 5 miles, travel time: 15 minutes, highlights: learning about donkey conservation and interacting with the donkeys)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights: visit the Washington Slagbaai National Park, explore the Bonaire National Marine Park, and relax on the beach at Lac Bay), 7-day (comprehensive: explore the island's culture and history, visit the Donkey Sanctuary Bonaire, and take a tour of the island's south), 14-day (deep exploration: take a diving certification course, go on a snorkeling tour, and explore the island's cave systems). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and tours in advance, especially during peak season, and prioritize activities based on your interests and the time of year you visit.
