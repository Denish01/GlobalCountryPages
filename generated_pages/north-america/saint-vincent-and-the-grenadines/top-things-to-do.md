# Top Things to Do in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines, visit the stunning **Tobago Cays**, explore the historic **Fort Charlotte**, and relax on the picturesque **Villa Beach**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **yacht tour** to the Tobago Cays and snorkel or dive in the crystal-clear waters
- Explore the **volcanic landscape** of Saint Vincent on a guided hike
- Visit a **working plantation** to learn about the island's agricultural heritage
- Attend a **traditional Vincentian festival**, such as the Bequia Music Festival
- Go on a **whale-watching tour** to spot humpback, sperm, and pilot whales
- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to prepare traditional Vincentian dishes
- Visit the **Petit St. Vincent Resort** for a luxurious and secluded getaway
- Go **kayaking** or **paddleboarding** through the island's stunning coastline

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **La Soufrière National Park**, home to the active volcano and stunning scenery
- Hike to the **Dark View Falls**, a scenic hike to two stunning waterfalls
- Visit the **Owia Salt Pond**, a unique saltwater pool surrounded by stunning scenery
- Relax on the **Villa Beach**, a picturesque beach with calm waters and scenic views
- Explore the **Tobago Cays Marine Park**, a stunning marine park with coral reefs and crystal-clear waters
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Kingstown Botanical Gardens**, home to a wide variety of tropical birds

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kingstown Market**, a vibrant marketplace with local produce, handicrafts, and souvenirs
- Explore the **Fort Charlotte**, a historic fort with insight into the island's colonial past
- Attend a **traditional Vincentian festival**, such as the Bequia Music Festival
- Visit the **Wallilabou Heritage Park**, a historic site with insight into the island's history and culture
- Take a **tour of the Petit St. Vincent Resort**, a luxurious and secluded resort with stunning views
- Visit the **Montreal Gardens**, beautiful gardens with stunning views of the surrounding countryside

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]

- Visit the **Morrisville**, a small town with a rich history and stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- Relax on the **Princess Margaret Beach**, a picturesque beach with calm waters and scenic views
