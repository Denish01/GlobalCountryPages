# Top Things to Do in Panama — Attractions & Experiences

Panama offers a diverse array of exciting experiences, from exploring the **Panama Canal** to relaxing on the beautiful beaches of **Bocas del Toro**, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Panama Canal | Panama City | Engineering Marvel | Witness the eighth wonder of the world, a 50-mile shipping canal connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans |
| Casco Viejo | Panama City | Historic Neighborhood | Explore the charming cobblestone streets, colonial architecture, and vibrant nightlife of Panama City's oldest neighborhood |
| Gamboa Rainforest Reserve | Gamboa | Nature Reserve | Hike through the lush rainforest, take an aerial tram ride, and spot exotic wildlife like sloths and toucans |
| Boquete | Chiriqui Highlands | Mountain Town | Enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, rafting, and coffee tastings in this picturesque town surrounded by mountains and waterfalls |
| Bocas del Toro | Bocas del Toro Province | Island Archipelago | Relax on stunning beaches, snorkel or dive in the crystal-clear waters, and experience the laid-back island culture |
| Soberania National Park | Panama City | National Park | Spot monkeys, sloths, and over 500 species of birds in this protected area, just a short drive from the city |
| El Valle de Anton | Cocle Province | Town | Visit the vibrant market, explore the nearby waterfalls and hiking trails, and take in the stunning views of the surrounding mountains |
| Fort San Lorenzo | Colon Province | Historic Fort | Learn about the country's pirate history and explore the ruins of this 16th-century Spanish fort |
| Miraflores Locks | Panama City | Visitor Center | Watch ships pass through the Panama Canal and learn about its history and operation at this interactive museum |
| Punta Pacifica | Panama City | Peninsula | Enjoy stunning ocean views, upscale shopping and dining, and a lively nightlife in this modern neighborhood |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gondola ride** through the Panama Canal, offering a unique perspective on this engineering marvel
- Visit an **indigenous community**, such as the Guna or Kuna people, to learn about their traditional way of life and culture
- Go on a **coffee tour** in the Chiriqui Highlands, where you can learn about the production process and taste some of the world's best coffee
- Take a **surfing lesson** in the Pacific coastal town of Santa Catalina, known for its consistent waves and beautiful beaches
- Explore the **street art scene** in Panama City, where you can find vibrant murals and graffiti in neighborhoods like Casco Viejo and El Chorrillo
- Attend a **traditional festival**, such as the Boquete Flower and Coffee Fair or the Panama Jazz Festival, to experience the local music, food, and culture

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Darien National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most biodiverse places on the planet
- Hike to the **Volcan Baru**, the highest peak in Panama, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Gulf of Chiriqui**, a protected area known for its whale watching, snorkeling, and diving opportunities
- Relax on the beach in **Pedasi**, a charming coastal town on the Azuero Peninsula
- Go on a **jungle trek** in the Soberania National Park, where you can spot monkeys, sloths, and toucans

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museum of Biodiversity**, designed by Frank Gehry, to learn about Panama's unique wildlife and ecosystems
- Explore the **Casco Viejo neighborhood**, with its charming colonial architecture, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, such as the Tamborito or the Cumbia, to experience the local music and dance
- Visit the **Panama Viejo**, the ruins of the old city, to learn about the country's history and colonial past
- Take a **cooking class**, where you can learn about traditional Panamanian cuisine and ingredients like coconut, plantains, and seafood

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of El Copé**, known for its stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and traditional cuisine
- Explore the **island of Contadora**, a hidden gem in the Pearl Islands, with its beautiful beaches, snorkeling, and diving opportunities
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the rolling hills and scenic countryside of the Azuero Peninsula
- Visit the **village of Gamboa**, a charming town surrounded by rainforest, with its stunning views and outdoor activities
- Relax in the **hot springs** of the Chiriqui Highlands, a natural and rejuvenating experience

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Colon Free Zone**, a major shopping and trade hub, with its duty-free stores and vibrant atmosphere (distance: 50 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Portobelo National Park**, a historic site with its stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and scenic views (distance: 60 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Explore the **town of Gamboa**, with its stunning natural beauty, outdoor activities, and traditional cuisine (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 45 minutes)
- Go on a day trip to the **Pearl Islands**, a beautiful archipelago with its stunning beaches, snorkeling, and diving opportunities (distance: 50 miles, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Soberania National Park**, with its stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing opportunities (distance: 20 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
