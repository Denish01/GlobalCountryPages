The cost of living in Guatemala is relatively low, making it an attractive destination for tourists, expats, and digital nomads. [SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in hostels or budget hotels, which offer affordable rates and a social atmosphere.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls, which serve delicious and affordable meals.
- Use public transportation, such as buses and shuttles, which are cheap and convenient.
- Avoid tourist areas and opt for local markets and shops, which offer better prices and a more authentic experience.
- Learn some basic Spanish phrases, which can help you negotiate prices and interact with locals.
- Consider visiting during the off-season (April-May or September-November), when prices are lower and the weather is still pleasant.
