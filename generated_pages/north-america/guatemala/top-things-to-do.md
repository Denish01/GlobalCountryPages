# Top Things to Do in Guatemala — Attractions & Experiences

Guatemala offers a diverse range of activities and attractions for visitors, from exploring ancient Mayan ruins to hiking through stunning natural landscapes, making it a unique and unforgettable travel destination.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional weaving workshop** in the highlands to learn about the local textiles and techniques
- Participate in a **coffee tour** in the western highlands to discover the production process and sample local flavors
- Visit a **traditional Mayan village** to experience the local customs, food, and way of life
- Go on a **birdwatching tour** in the cloud forests to spot the unique and exotic species
- Attend a **traditional festival** or celebration, such as the **Feria de Santiago** in Antigua Guatemala
- Take a **Spanish language course** in a local school to improve your language skills and immerse in the culture
- Explore the **local markets** and try the traditional street food, such as **tamales** and **rellenitos**

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Tikal National Park** for hiking, wildlife spotting, and exploration of the ancient Mayan ruins
- Hike to the summit of **Pacaya Volcano** for stunning views and lava flows
- Enjoy the scenic views and boat tours on **Lake Atitlán**
- Explore the **Semuc Champey** natural park and its turquoise pools and waterfalls
- Go **rafting** or **kayaking** on the **Rio Cahabón** for an adrenaline-pumping adventure
- Visit the **Biotopo del Quetzal** nature reserve to spot the rare and colorful quetzal bird

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology** in Guatemala City to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Museo Popol Vuh** in Guatemala City to discover the Mayan artifacts and textiles
- Attend a **traditional dance performance** or **marimba concert** in Antigua Guatemala
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the local cuisine and ingredients
- Visit the **Iglesia de La Merced** in Antigua Guatemala to admire the stunning colonial architecture and historic significance
- Explore the **Chichicastenango Market** to experience the vibrant atmosphere and local handicrafts

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of San Juan La Laguna** on Lake Atitlán to experience the traditional Mayan culture and stunning scenery
- Explore the **Cuevas de Lanquín** cave system in the eastern highlands for its unique rock formations and adventure activities
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Rio Dulce** to spot the local wildlife and enjoy the scenic views
- Visit the **Hacienda San Lucas** in the western highlands to learn about the coffee production process and enjoy the stunning scenery
- Attend a **local festival** or celebration in a small town or village to experience the authentic culture and traditions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Antigua Guatemala to **Lake Atitlán** (distance: 80 km, travel time: 2 hours) to enjoy the scenic views and boat tours
- Visit the **Pacaya Volcano** from Guatemala City (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour) for a guided hike and stunning views
- Explore the **Chichicastenango Market** from Quetzaltenango (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2.5 hours) to experience the vibrant atmosphere and local handicrafts
- Take a day trip from Flores to **Tikal National Park** (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour) to explore the ancient Mayan ruins and wildlife
- Visit the **Semuc Champey** natural park from Cobán (distance: 60 km, travel time: 2 hours) to marvel at the turquoise pools and waterfalls
