[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]

- Visit the **Anguilla Archipelago** to see the diverse marine life and coral reefs
- Go on a **snorkeling trip** to Shoal Bay to see the colorful fish and coral
- Attend a **traditional Anguillian festival** to experience the local culture and music
- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to prepare traditional Anguillian dishes
- Go on a **fishing charter** to catch some of the fresh seafood that Anguilla is known for
