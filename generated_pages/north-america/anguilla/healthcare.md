However, due to the island's small size, some specialized services may not be available, and patients may need to be referred to larger medical centers in neighboring islands or overseas. The quality of care in Anguilla is generally good, with well-trained medical professionals and modern equipment. Overall, the healthcare system in Anguilla is well-developed, but it can be limited by the island's small size and remote location.
