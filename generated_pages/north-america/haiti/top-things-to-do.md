# Top Things to Do in Haiti — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Haiti, be sure to explore the historic **Citadelle Laferrière**, relax on the beautiful **Bassin-Bleu** beaches, and experience the vibrant culture of **Port-au-Prince** to get a true taste of this incredible Caribbean nation.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Vodou** ceremony tour to learn about and experience this unique aspect of Haitian culture
- Visit a **Lakou**, a traditional Haitian community, to see firsthand the daily life and customs of rural Haitians
- Attend a **Rara** festival, a traditional Haitian music and dance celebration
- Go on a **Coffee** plantation tour to learn about Haiti's famous coffee production
- Take a **Horseback** ride through the beautiful Haitian countryside
- Learn about **Haitian Cuisine** with a cooking class, featuring dishes like **Riz et Pois** and **Griot**
- Visit the **Festival du Film** in Port-au-Prince, showcasing Haitian and international films

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Parc National La Visite**, a beautiful national park with hiking trails and stunning views
- Relax on the beaches of **Jacmel**, a charming coastal town known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant arts scene
- Visit the **Bassin-Bleu** waterfalls, a series of four waterfalls and natural pools perfect for swimming
- Go **Hiking** in the **Massif du Nord** mountain range, offering stunning views and challenging trails
- Take a **Boat Tour** of the **Tortuga Island**, a beautiful island with stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Haiti** to learn about Haitian history, art, and culture
- Explore the **Cathédrale Notre-Dame de l'Assomption**, a beautiful example of Haitian architecture and faith
- Attend a **Traditional Music** performance, featuring Haitian instruments like the **Rara** horn
- Take a **Dance** class, learning traditional Haitian dances like the **Kompa** and **Zouk**
- Visit the **Musée d'Art Haïtien**, featuring a wide range of Haitian art and artifacts
- Learn about **Haitian History** at the **Museum of the Haitian Revolution**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the charming town of **Cap-Haïten**, known for its beautiful beaches and historic architecture
- Explore the **Grotte Marie-Jeanne**, a stunning cave system with beautiful rock formations
- Relax on the beautiful beaches of **Montrouis**, a charming coastal town with a rich history
- Take a **Tour** of the **Hacienda** coffee plantation, learning about Haiti's famous coffee production
- Visit the **Fondation Zanmi Timoun**, a community center and school supporting local children

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
