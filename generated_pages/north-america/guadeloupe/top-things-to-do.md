# Top Things to Do in Guadeloupe — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Creole cooking class** to learn about the local cuisine and ingredients
- Go on a **sailing trip** around the islands to explore the beautiful coastline and marine life
- Visit a **rum distillery** to learn about the production process and taste some of the local rums
- Explore the **underwater world** of Guadeloupe with a snorkeling or scuba diving trip
- Attend a **traditional dance performance** to experience the local culture and music
- Take a **hiking trip** to the **Col de la Croix** for stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Go on a **kayaking trip** through the **Mangrove Forest** to explore the unique ecosystem

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Parc National de la Guadeloupe**: a national park with lush rainforest, hiking trails, and scenic landscapes
- **La Grande-Terre**: a island with beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and scenic coastline
- **Plage de la Caravelle**: a beach with water sports and activities, such as kayaking and paddleboarding
- **Cascade aux Ecrevisses**: a waterfall with a natural pool, perfect for swimming and relaxation
- **Les Saintes**: an archipelago with beautiful beaches, coves, and coral reefs, perfect for snorkeling and diving

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Musée d'Art et d'Histoire**: a museum showcasing the island's history, art, and culture
- **Fort Delgrès**: a historic fort with impressive fortifications and stunning views
- **Jardin Botanique**: a botanical garden with a wide variety of tropical plants and flowers
- **Cathédrale de Basse-Terre**: a historic cathedral with stunning architecture and beautiful stained-glass windows
- **Marché de Basse-Terre**: a market with local produce, handicrafts, and souvenirs
- **Centre des Arts et de la Culture**: a cultural center with exhibitions, performances, and workshops

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Anse du Petit Bas Vent**: a secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and stunning views
- **La Maison du Crabe**: a restaurant serving delicious seafood and local cuisine
- **Le Jardin des Sens**: a botanical garden with a wide variety of plants and flowers
- **La Pointe des Colibris**: a scenic viewpoint with stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- **La Plage de l'Autre Bord**: a secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and stunning views

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **Iles des Saintes**: take a ferry from Trois-Rivières to explore the beautiful beaches and coves of Les Saintes (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
2. **Pointe des Châteaux**: take a drive from Saint-François to explore the scenic peninsula and stunning views (distance: 20 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
3. **La Soufrière**: take a hike from Basse-Terre to explore the volcano and stunning views (distance: 10 km, travel time: 2 hours)
4. **Plage de Raisins Clairs**: take a drive from Sainte-Rose to relax on the beautiful white sand beach (distance: 15 km, travel time: 20 minutes)
5. **Terre-de-Bas Island**: take a ferry from Pointe-à-Pitre to explore the beautiful beaches and coves of the island (distance: 20 km, travel time: 1 hour)
