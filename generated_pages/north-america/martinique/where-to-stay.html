Booking Tips

  • Book in advance, especially during peak season (December to April), to ensure availability and the best rates.
  • Use online booking platforms, such as Booking.com or Expedia, to compare prices and find deals.
  • Consider negotiating the price, especially for longer stays or off-season bookings.
  • Be aware of seasonal pricing, with rates tend to be higher during peak season and lower during the off-season.
  • Read reviews and check the location, amenities, and services offered by the accommodation provider.
  • Look for special offers, discounts, or package deals, especially for resorts and hotels.
  • Consider booking a package that includes flights, accommodation, and activities, for a hassle-free experience.

Safety & Location Tips

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas or at night, and take necessary precautions to ensure your safety.
  • Avoid walking alone in isolated areas, and use reputable taxi services or public transportation.
  • Keep valuables secure, and use hotel safes or lockers to store important documents and belongings.
  • Be mindful of local customs and traditions, and respect the island's cultural heritage.
  • Choose accommodations in safe and reputable areas, with good lighting and security.
  • Consider staying in areas with easy access to beaches, amenities, and public transportation, for a convenient and enjoyable experience.
  • Research the area before booking, and read reviews from other travelers to get a sense of the local environment and safety concerns.
