[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **rum tasting tour** at one of Martinique's many distilleries, such as Habitation Clément or Rhum J.M, to learn about the production process and sample some of the island's famous rums.
- Visit the **Empress Josephine Museum** to learn about the life and legacy of the island's most famous resident, who was born in Les Trois-Ilets.
- Explore the **Les Salines** nature reserve, which is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including sea turtles and tropical birds.
- Go **hiking** in the **Pitons du Carbet**, a range of volcanic peaks that offer stunning views of the surrounding area.
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Baie de Fort-de-France**, which offers stunning views of the city and its historic harbor.
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, such as the **Quadrille**, which is a staple of Martinican culture.
- Visit the **Marché aux Puces**, a bustling marketplace in Fort-de-France where you can find a variety of local goods and souvenirs.
