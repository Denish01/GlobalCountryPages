The population of Turks and Caicos is relatively small, with a total of approximately 50,828 people, according to the latest estimates. The population of Turks and Caicos has been growing steadily over the years, with a significant increase in the number of immigrants and expatriates moving to the islands. The majority of the population is of African descent, with a significant proportion of people of European and mixed ancestry. The population is also becoming increasingly urbanized, with more people moving to the capital city and other urban areas in search of better job opportunities and amenities.

The migration patterns in Turks and Caicos are also worth noting, with many people moving to the islands from other Caribbean countries, as well as from the United States and the United Kingdom. This has resulted in a diverse and multicultural population, with a unique blend of different cultures and traditions.
