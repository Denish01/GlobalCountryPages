Antigua and Barbuda is a sovereign island nation located in the eastern Caribbean Sea, where the Caribbean Sea meets the Atlantic Ocean. The nation consists of two major islands, Antigua and Barbuda, as well as several smaller islands. The country is surrounded by the Caribbean Sea to the south and west, and the Atlantic Ocean to the north and east.
