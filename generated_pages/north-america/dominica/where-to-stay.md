# Where to Stay in Dominica — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To find the best place to stay in Dominica, consider the island's unique blend of natural beauty, outdoor activities, and cultural attractions, with top areas including Roseau, Portsmouth, and Calibishie. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, the character and advantages vary, catering to different types of travelers:
**Roseau** (Roseau)
Best For: Luxury / Business
Vibe: As the capital city, Roseau offers a bustling atmosphere with a mix of historic and modern architecture, and easy access to amenities and services. The city's vibrant markets and cultural events make it an exciting place to stay.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: Convenient location, access to amenities, and a range of dining options, with many historical sites and cultural attractions nearby. 
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, with limited parking options.

**Portsmouth** (Portsmouth)
Best For: Family / Backpacker
Vibe: This coastal town has a laid-back atmosphere, with beautiful beaches and a historic fort, making it ideal for families and backpackers looking for a relaxed vibe. The town's friendly locals and waterfront restaurants add to its charm.
Price Range: $80-200/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, historic fort, and a range of outdoor activities, with a more laid-back atmosphere than Roseau. 
Cons: Limited amenities and services compared to Roseau.

**Calibishie** (Calibishie)
Best For: Luxury / Nature lover
Vibe: This picturesque coastal village offers stunning views of the ocean and surrounding landscape, with a range of outdoor activities and a peaceful atmosphere. The village's secluded beaches and scenic hiking trails make it perfect for nature lovers.
Price Range: $120-250/night
Pros: Breathtaking views, peaceful atmosphere, and access to outdoor activities, with a range of luxury accommodations available. 
Cons: Limited amenities and services, and can be difficult to access.

**Morne Bruce** (Roseau)
Best For: Backpacker / Hiker
Vibe: This hillside neighborhood offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and easy access to hiking trails, with a range of budget-friendly accommodations. The area's scenic views and proximity to nature reserves make it ideal for hikers and backpackers.
Price Range: $40-100/night
Pros: Affordable, stunning views, and access to hiking trails, with a range of budget-friendly options available. 
Cons: Limited amenities and services, and can be steep and difficult to navigate.

**Laudat** (Laudat)
Best For: Nature lover / Family
Vibe: This rural village is surrounded by lush rainforest and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape, with a range of outdoor activities and a peaceful atmosphere. The village's scenic views and proximity to nature reserves make it perfect for nature lovers and families.
Price Range: $80-180/night
Pros: Stunning views, peaceful atmosphere, and access to outdoor activities, with a range of accommodations available. 
Cons: Limited amenities and services, and can be difficult to access.

**Castle Bruce** (Castle Bruce)
Best For: Backpacker / Budget
Vibe: This small coastal village offers a range of budget-friendly accommodations and a laid-back atmosphere, with beautiful beaches and a historic fort. The village's friendly locals and waterfront restaurants add to its charm.
Price Range: $30-80/night
Pros: Affordable, beautiful beaches, and historic fort, with a range of budget-friendly options available. 
Cons: Limited amenities and services, and can be crowded during peak season.

[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]
- Book in advance, especially during peak season (December to April), to ensure availability and the best rates.
- Consider booking a package deal that includes accommodation, transportation, and activities for a discounted rate.
- Look for discounts and promotions on hotel and resort websites, as well as on booking platforms like Expedia and Booking.com.
- Negotiate the rate, especially if booking directly with a hotel or guesthouse.
- Read reviews and check the location and amenities before booking to ensure it meets your needs.
- Consider booking a room with a kitchen or kitchenette to save money on food and drinks.
- Be aware of any additional fees or charges, such as taxes or service fees, when booking.

[SECTION] Safety & Location Tips [/SECTION]
- Research the area and neighborhood before booking to ensure it is safe and convenient.
- Avoid areas with high crime rates, such as some parts of Roseau, and opt for well-lit and populated areas instead.
- Consider staying in a hotel or resort with 24-hour security for added peace of mind.
- Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night, and take necessary precautions to stay safe.
- Stay in touch with local authorities and your hotel or resort for any updates on safety and security.
- Choose a location that is close to amenities, such as restaurants, shops, and transportation, to make your stay more convenient.
