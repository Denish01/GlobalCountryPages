Cultural Identity

Dominica's culture is a unique blend of African, European, and indigenous Kalinago influences, shaped by its history as a British colony and its strategic location in the Caribbean. The island's cultural identity is characterized by a strong sense of community, with a focus on family, tradition, and the natural environment.
