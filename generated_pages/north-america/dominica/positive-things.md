- The **Kalinago Barana Aute** is a cultural village that showcases the traditions and way of life of the indigenous **Kalinago people**, featuring crafts, music, and dance performances.
- The **Dominica Museum** is a repository of the island's history and culture, featuring exhibits on its natural history, colonial past, and artistic heritage.
- The **Roseau Market** is a bustling marketplace that offers a wide range of local produce, handicrafts, and souvenirs, and is a great place to experience the island's vibrant culture.
