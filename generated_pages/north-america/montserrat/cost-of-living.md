[SECTION] How Expensive Is Montserrat? [/SECTION]
Montserrat is a British Overseas Territory in the Caribbean, and its cost of living is relatively moderate compared to other islands in the region. The territory's economy is primarily driven by tourism and financial services, which contributes to the moderate cost of living. Compared to its neighbors, such as Antigua and Barbuda, Montserrat's prices are slightly lower, but still higher than those in some other Caribbean islands. Overall, Montserrat offers a unique blend of Caribbean culture and British influences, making it an attractive destination for visitors and expatriates alike.

The cost of living in Montserrat is influenced by its remote location and limited access to certain goods and services. However, the territory has a relatively low crime rate and a high standard of living, making it an attractive destination for those seeking a safe and comfortable Caribbean experience. Visitors and residents can enjoy a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, diving, and exploring the island's natural beauty, all while experiencing the local culture and hospitality.
