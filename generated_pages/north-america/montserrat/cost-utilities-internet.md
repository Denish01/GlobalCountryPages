# Utility & Internet Costs in Montserrat

The cost of utilities and internet in Montserrat can vary, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $200 to $400 USD, while internet plans with 60 Mbps speed can cost around $100 to $150 USD per month.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Montserrat's utility infrastructure is relatively well-developed, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and telecommunications services. However, the costs of utilities can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher demand during the peak tourist season from December to April. The government and utility providers have implemented measures to stabilize prices, but residents and visitors can still expect some variation in their utility bills. The territory's remote location and limited resources also contribute to higher costs for certain utilities, such as electricity and water.

The quality of utility services in Montserrat is generally good, with modern infrastructure and regular maintenance. However, the territory's rugged terrain and exposure to natural disasters can occasionally disrupt services. The government and utility providers have implemented contingency plans to minimize the impact of outages and ensure prompt restoration of services. Despite these challenges, Montserrat's utility infrastructure is well-suited to support the needs of residents and visitors, with a range of services and providers available to choose from.
