# Top Things to Do in US Virgin Islands — Attractions & Experiences

The US Virgin Islands offer a wide range of exciting activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring historic sites and pristine beaches to snorkeling and hiking in the beautiful national parks.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **glass-bottom boat tour** to explore the underwater world of the US Virgin Islands without getting wet
- Go on a **bioluminescent bay tour** to see the magical glow of the islands' bioluminescent bays
- Experience the **St. Thomas Carnival**, a colorful and vibrant celebration of music, dance, and culture
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the unique cuisine of the US Virgin Islands and how to prepare traditional dishes
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the beautiful trails of St. John, offering stunning views of the surrounding waters
- Take a **snorkeling or diving trip** to explore the underwater world of the US Virgin Islands, featuring an incredible array of marine life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Virgin Islands National Park**, which covers over 60% of St. John and features stunning beaches, hiking trails, and historic sites
- Visit **Magens Bay Beach**, one of the most beautiful beaches in the world, with crystal-clear waters and powdery white sand
- Go **hiking** on the scenic trails of St. John, offering stunning views of the surrounding waters and unique opportunities to spot wildlife
- Take a **kayaking trip** through the beautiful waters of the US Virgin Islands, offering a unique perspective on the islands' stunning natural beauty
- Visit **Trunk Bay**, a picturesque beach with a coral reef perfect for snorkeling and swimming

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Fort Christian Museum**, which showcases the history and culture of the US Virgin Islands
- Explore the **St. Thomas Synagogue**, a historic synagogue that dates back to the 18th century and offers insight into the island's Jewish heritage
- Take a **tour of the Annaberg Plantation**, a historic sugar plantation that offers stunning views of the surrounding waters and insight into the island's history
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, featuring the unique music and dance of the US Virgin Islands
- Visit the **Virgin Islands Museum**, which showcases the history, culture, and natural beauty of the US Virgin Islands

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit **Hassel Island**, a small island with a rich history, featuring the remains of a 19th-century shipyard and a scenic hiking trail
- Explore the **St. John Marketplace**, a bustling marketplace featuring local vendors, artisans, and musicians
- Take a **tour of the Cruzan Rum Distillery**, a historic distillery that offers insight into the production of rum and the unique culture of the US Virgin Islands
- Visit **Salt Pond Bay**, a secluded beach with stunning views and a unique opportunity to spot wildlife
- Go on a **stargazing tour**, taking advantage of the US Virgin Islands' incredibly clear skies to explore the night sky

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Puerto Rico**, located just a short ferry ride from St. Thomas, and explore the historic city of San Juan
- Visit **St. Croix**, the largest of the US Virgin Islands, and explore its beautiful beaches, historic sites, and unique culture
- Go on a **sailing trip** to the **British Virgin Islands**, located just a short sail from the US Virgin Islands, and explore the beautiful islands and cays of this stunning archipelago
- Take a day trip to **Jost Van Dyke**, a small island in the British Virgin Islands, and explore its beautiful beaches, historic sites, and unique culture
- Visit **Tortola**, the largest island in the British Virgin Islands, and explore its beautiful beaches, historic sites, and unique culture
