Cultural Treasures

  • The Annaberg Sugar Plantation, a historic sugar plantation that now serves as a museum, showcases the island's colonial past and the history of the sugar industry.
  • The Reef Bay Sugar Mill, a historic sugar mill that dates back to the 19th century, is a popular tourist attraction and offers insight into the island's industrial heritage.
  • The Quadrille Dancers, a traditional dance troupe that performs the quadrille, a traditional folk dance, is a beloved part of the island's cultural heritage.
  • The St. Thomas Synagogue, a historic synagogue that dates back to the 18th century, is one of the oldest synagogues in the Western Hemisphere and offers a glimpse into the island's Jewish heritage.
  • The Virgin Islands Folk Life Festival, an annual festival that celebrates the island's music, dance, and art, is a popular event that showcases the island's rich cultural diversity.

People & Global Impact

  • Tim Duncan, a professional basketball player who was born in St. Croix, is a notable figure from the US Virgin Islands who has made a significant impact on the sports world.
  • Rita Cannon, a politician who served as a delegate to the US Congress, is a prominent figure in the island's politics and has worked to promote the interests of the US Virgin Islands.
  • The US Virgin Islands diaspora, which includes people of US Virgin Islands descent who live in the US mainland and other countries, has made significant contributions to the fields of medicine, law, and business.
  • The US Virgin Islands values, which include a strong emphasis on community, family, and respect for the environment, are an important part of the island's culture and are reflected in its close-knit communities and environmental initiatives.

What Visitors Love

  • The beautiful beaches, including Magens Bay Beach and Trunk Bay, which offer stunning views, crystal-clear waters, and a range of water sports and activities.
  • The delicious cuisine, which includes popular dishes such as conch fritters, fish tacos, and johnnycake, and offers a unique blend of Caribbean, American, and European flavors.
  • The friendly hospitality, which is reflected in the island's welcoming atmosphere and the warm hospitality of its people.
  • The stunning scenery, which includes hiking trails, waterfalls, and scenic overlooks, and offers breathtaking views of the island's natural beauty.

  • The wide range of activities, which includes snorkeling, diving, kayaking, and paddleboarding, and offers something for every interest and skill level.
