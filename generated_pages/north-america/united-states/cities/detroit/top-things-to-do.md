# Top Things to Do in Detroit — Attractions & Experiences

Detroit is a city with a rich history, vibrant culture, and exciting attractions, offering a wide range of activities for visitors to enjoy, from exploring historic landmarks and museums to experiencing the city's renowned music scene and outdoor recreation opportunities.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **stroll along the RiverWalk**, which offers beautiful views of the Detroit River and Lake Erie, as well as access to several parks and public art installations
- Explore the **Street Art** scene in the Eastern Market neighborhood, where you can find vibrant murals and graffiti adorning the walls of buildings
- Attend a **live music performance** at the legendary **Fillmore Detroit**, which hosts a variety of acts, from rock and pop to jazz and hip hop
- Visit the **African Bead Museum**, which showcases a stunning collection of beads and beadwork from around the world
- Go on a **food tour** of the city, sampling the diverse culinary delights of Detroit, from classic **Coney Islands** to **Soul Food** and **Mexican cuisine**
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Fisher Building**, which offers a glimpse into the city's rich history and architectural heritage

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Belle Isle Nature Center**, which features exhibits on the natural history of the region, as well as a beautiful conservatory and gardens
- Visit the **Detroit River International Wildlife Refuge**, which provides a habitat for a wide variety of birds, fish, and other wildlife
- Go **hiking** or **biking** on the **Dequindre Cut**, a former rail line turned greenway, which offers a unique perspective on the city
- Take a **stroll** through the **Matthaei Botanical Gardens**, which features a beautiful collection of plants and flowers from around the world
- Enjoy the **beach** at **Lake Erie Metropark**, which offers swimming, kayaking, and other water sports

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Detroit Institute of Arts**, which features a world-class collection of art from around the globe
- Explore the **Charles H. Wright Museum of African American History**, which chronicles the history and culture of African Americans
- Attend a **performance** at the **Detroit Symphony Orchestra**, which offers a wide range of classical and contemporary music
- Take a **tour** of the **Motown Museum**, which explores the history of Motown Records and its legendary artists
- Visit the **Arab American National Museum**, which showcases the history and culture of Arab Americans
- Explore the **Michigan Science Center**, which features interactive exhibits on science and technology

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Heidelberg Project**, a unique outdoor art installation made from recycled materials
- Explore the **Eastern Market** neighborhood, which features a vibrant mix of street art, shops, and restaurants
- Take a **tour** of the **Fisher Building**, which offers a glimpse into the city's rich history and architectural heritage
- Visit the **African Bead Museum**, which showcases a stunning collection of beads and beadwork from around the world
- Go to the **John K. King Used and Rare Books**, a vast used bookstore with a wide selection of titles

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip** to **Ann Arbor**, home of the University of Michigan, which offers a variety of cultural and recreational attractions (distance: 45 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit **Windsor, Ontario**, which features a rich history, cultural attractions, and stunning views of the Detroit River (distance: 5 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Explore the **Lake Erie Islands**, which offer a relaxing getaway with beautiful beaches, parks, and outdoor recreation opportunities (distance: 100 miles, travel time: 2.5 hours)
- Go to **Toledo, Ohio**, which features a rich history, cultural attractions, and outdoor recreation opportunities (distance: 60 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Take a **day trip** to **Lansing**, the state capital of Michigan, which offers a variety of cultural and historical attractions (distance: 125 miles, travel time: 2.5 hours)
