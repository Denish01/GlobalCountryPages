[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **coffee tour** to sample some of the city's famous coffee and learn about its history and production
- Visit the **Gum Wall**, a quirky attraction where visitors stick their used gum to the wall
- Explore the **Ye Olde Curiosity Shop**, a bizarre and fascinating store filled with oddities and curiosities
- Attend a **Seattle Seahawks** or **Mariners** game to experience the city's sports culture
- Go on a **food tour** to sample the city's diverse culinary scene, including seafood, Asian cuisine, and food trucks
- Take a **stargazing** tour at the University of Washington's Planetarium to explore the night sky
- Visit the **Seattle Pinball Museum**, where you can play a wide variety of pinball machines

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Discovery Park**, a 740-acre park with forests, meadows, and stunning views of the Olympic Mountains
- Explore the **Washington Park Arboretum**, a beautiful park with gardens, wetlands, and forests
- Hike the **Rattlesnake Ridge Trail**, a challenging hike with stunning views of the Cascades
- Visit **Golden Gardens Park**, a scenic beach with picnic areas, hiking trails, and stunning sunsets
- Take a **whale-watching tour** to spot orcas, gray whales, and other marine wildlife in the Puget Sound

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Frye Art Museum**, a free museum with a diverse collection of 19th- and 20th-century European art
- Explore the **Wing Luke Museum of the Asian Pacific American Experience**, a museum showcasing the history and culture of Asian Pacific Americans
- Attend a performance at the **Seattle Repertory Theatre**, a renowned theater company with a diverse range of productions
- Visit the **Henry Art Gallery**, a contemporary art museum with rotating exhibits and installations
- Take a **glassblowing class** at the Seattle Glassblowing Studio to learn about this unique art form

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Seattle Pinball Museum**, a unique attraction with a wide variety of pinball machines
- Explore the **Fremont Sunday Market**, a bustling market with local vendors, food, and live music
- Take a stroll through the **Chinatown-International District**, a vibrant neighborhood with a rich cultural heritage
- Visit the **Kerry Park**, a scenic park with stunning views of the city skyline and Puget Sound
- Attend a **comedy show** at the Comedy Underground, a popular comedy club with local and national performers

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Mount Rainier National Park**, a stunning park with hiking trails, glaciers, and wildflowers (distance: 54 miles, 1 hour 30 minutes)
- Visit **Bainbridge Island**, a charming island with a thriving arts scene, beautiful beaches, and a historic lighthouse (distance: 10 miles, 35 minutes by ferry)
- Explore the **Snoqualmie Falls**, a stunning waterfall with hiking trails and scenic views (distance: 25 miles, 45 minutes)
- Take a day trip to **Leavenworth**, a charming Bavarian-themed town with shops, restaurants, and outdoor activities (distance: 115 miles, 2 hours 30 minutes)
- Visit the **Woodinville Wine Country**, a region with numerous wineries, vineyards, and tasting rooms (distance: 20 miles, 30 minutes)
