To experience the best of Minneapolis, visit iconic landmarks like the Walker Art Center, explore the city's vibrant neighborhoods, and enjoy the numerous lakes and parks that offer a wide range of outdoor activities.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a St. Anthony Falls tour to learn about the historic milling industry and see the only waterfall on the Mississippi River
  • Explore the Mill District, a vibrant neighborhood with converted warehouses, boutique shops, and trendy restaurants
  • Visit the American Swedish Institute to learn about Swedish heritage and culture
  • Go on a brewery tour to sample local craft beers and learn about the brewing process
  • Attend a Twins game at Target Field, the home stadium of the Minnesota Twins baseball team
  • Take a Mississippi River cruise to see the city from a different perspective
  • Explore the Nicollet Mall, a pedestrian shopping and dining street lined with unique boutiques and eateries

Outdoor & Nature

  • Visit Lake Calhoun, a popular spot for boating, fishing, and swimming
  • Explore the Minnehaha Falls, a 53-foot waterfall in a scenic park
  • Hike the Grand Rounds Scenic Byway, a 13-mile trail that takes you through several parks and neighborhoods
  • Visit the Fort Snelling State Park, a historic fort and park with hiking trails and scenic views
  • Go kayaking or paddleboarding on one of the many lakes and rivers in the city
  • Explore the Eloise Butler Wildflower Garden, a beautiful garden with over 500 plant species

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Minneapolis Institute of Art, which features a diverse collection of art from around the world
  • Explore the Weisman Art Museum, which showcases a unique collection of American modernism
  • Attend a performance at the Guthrie Theater, a world-renowned theater company
  • Visit the American Indian Center, which showcases the history and culture of Native American communities
  • Take a tour of the First Avenue nightclub, a legendary music venue that has hosted famous acts
  • Explore the Hennepin Theatre District, a vibrant neighborhood with historic theaters and live music venues

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Washburn A Mill, a historic mill turned museum that showcases the city's milling history
  • Explore the Longfellow House, a historic house museum that showcases the life and works of Henry Wadsworth Longfellow
  • Take a stroll through the Loring Park, a beautiful park with walking trails and scenic views
  • Visit the Minneapolis Farmers Market, a bustling market with fresh produce and local goods
  • Explore the Uptown neighborhood, a vibrant and eclectic neighborhood with unique shops and restaurants

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip to Stillwater, a charming town with historic architecture and scenic views of the St. Croix River (30 miles, 45 minutes)
  • Visit Valleyfair, an amusement park with roller coasters and water rides (20 miles, 30 minutes)
  • Explore the Minnesota Zoo, a zoo with a diverse collection of animals from around the world (20 miles, 30 minutes)
  • Take a day trip to Duluth, a scenic city with a beautiful lakefront and outdoor activities (150 miles, 2.5 hours)
  • Visit the Mall of America, a massive shopping center with over 520 stores and attractions (10 miles, 20 minutes)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of downtown Minneapolis, including the Walker Art Center and First Avenue), 7-day (comprehensive tour of the city, including neighborhoods and outdoor activities), 14-day (deep exploration of the city and surrounding areas, including day trips to Stillwater and Duluth). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations in downtown Minneapolis for easy access to attractions, prioritize outdoor activities during the summer months, and take advantage of the city's public transportation system to get around.
