[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Little Havana Food Tour** to sample the local cuisine and drinks, such as Cuban coffee and croquetas
- Go on a **Sunset Cruise** in Biscayne Bay to admire the Miami skyline and marine life
- Attend a **Wynwood Art Walk** to explore the local art scene and meet the artists
- Visit the **Miami Seaquarium** to watch dolphin and sea lion shows
- Take a **Street Art Workshop** in the Wynwood district to learn about street art and create your own mural
- Go **Paddleboarding** in the calm waters of the Miami Beach Boardwalk
- Experience the **South Beach Nightlife** at one of the many clubs, bars, or lounges

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Everglades National Park**: explore the unique ecosystem and wildlife of the Everglades through hiking, biking, or airboat tours
- **Oleta River State Park**: kayak or paddleboard through the mangrove tunnels and spot local wildlife
- **Crandon Park Beach**: relax on the beach and enjoy the scenic views of the Miami skyline
- **Bill Baggs Cape Florida State Park**: hike, bike, or kayak through the park's natural areas and historic sites
- **Haulover Beach**: surf, swim, or simply enjoy the sun and sand at this popular beach

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Vizcaya Museum and Gardens**: explore the historic estate and European-inspired gardens
- **Pérez Art Museum Miami**: discover an extensive collection of international and local art
- **Little Havana**: experience the sights, sounds, and flavors of Cuban culture
- **Miami Children's Museum**: interactive exhibits and play areas for kids of all ages
- **Bass Museum of Art**: a collection of international art from the 15th to the 20th centuries
- **The Barnacle State Historic Site**: learn about the history of the area and the pioneer families who settled in Miami

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Kampong**: a historic estate and botanical garden with a unique collection of plants and trees
- **The Wolfsonian-FIU**: a museum of art and design with a collection of objects from the 1850s to the 1950s
- **The Street Art in the Wynwood District**: explore the vibrant murals and street art in the area
- **The Miami Beach Eats Food Tour**: sample the local cuisine and drinks in the Miami Beach area
- **The Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden**: a beautiful garden with a collection of tropical plants and trees

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]

- **Key Largo**: 60 miles, 1 hour 15 minutes, snorkeling, diving, and marine life
- **Naples**: 150 miles, 2 hours 30 minutes, beaches, shopping, and dining
