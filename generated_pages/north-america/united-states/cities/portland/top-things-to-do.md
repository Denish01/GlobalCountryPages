# Top Things to Do in Portland — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Portland, you should not miss exploring the city's vibrant **food truck scene**, visiting the iconic **Pittock Mansion**, and strolling through the beautiful **International Rose Test Garden**, which showcase the city's unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **brewery tour** to sample some of Portland's famous craft beers
- Explore the city's **street art scene** by walking through the Alberta Arts District
- Visit the **Saturday Market** to see local vendors selling handmade goods
- Go **geocaching** in Forest Park, one of the largest urban forests in the United States
- Take a **food cart tour** to sample the city's diverse culinary offerings
- Attend a **Portland Timbers** soccer game at Providence Park

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Forest Park**: over 80 miles of hiking trails and stunning views of the city
- **Columbia River Gorge**: a scenic river canyon with hiking and water sports
- **Mount Hood**: a nearby mountain with skiing, hiking, and scenic drives
- **Washington Park**: a large park with multiple attractions, including the Japanese Garden and the World Forestry Center
- **Hoyt Arboretum**: a beautiful arboretum with walking trails and stunning views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Portland Art Museum**: featuring a diverse collection of art from around the world
- **Oregon Historical Society Museum**: showcasing the history of Oregon and the surrounding region
- **Japanese American Historical Plaza**: a memorial to the Japanese American experience in Portland
- **Portland Institute for Contemporary Art**: a contemporary art space with rotating exhibits
- **The Grotto**: a beautiful Catholic sanctuary with stunning gardens and architecture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Shanghai Tunnels**: a network of hidden tunnels and secret passages beneath the city
- **The Witch's Castle**: a unique stone structure in Forest Park with a mysterious history
- **The Velvet Rope**: a hidden cocktail bar with a secret entrance
- **The city's many **little free libraries**_: small, community-run libraries where you can borrow and leave books

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Cannon Beach**: a charming coastal town with stunning beaches and scenic views (80 miles, 1.5 hours)
- **Hood River**: a picturesque town in the Columbia River Gorge with outdoor recreation opportunities (60 miles, 1 hour)
- **Willamette Valley**: a scenic wine country region with vineyards and tasting rooms (30 miles, 45 minutes)
- **Mount St. Helens**: a national monument with hiking and scenic views (150 miles, 3 hours)
- **The Oregon Coast**: a scenic drive along the coast with stunning views and charming towns (100 miles, 2 hours)
