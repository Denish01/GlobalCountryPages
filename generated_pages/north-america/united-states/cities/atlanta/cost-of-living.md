[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider visiting during the shoulder season (April-May or September-October) to avoid peak tourist prices and enjoy milder weather.
- Use public transportation, such as the MARTA bus and rail system, to get around the city instead of renting a car or taking taxis.
- Take advantage of free attractions like the Martin Luther King Jr. National Historical Park and the Atlanta BeltLine.
- Look for affordable dining options like food trucks, street vendors, and local eateries.
- Use coupons or discount codes to save money on attractions and activities, such as the Georgia Aquarium or the World of Coca-Cola.
- Consider staying in a neighborhood outside of the city center, such as Inman Park or Virginia-Highland, to find more affordable accommodation options.
