# Top Things to Do in Austin — Attractions & Experiences

The best things to do in Austin include exploring its vibrant music scene, taking a dip in the natural springs, and experiencing the city's eclectic cultural attractions, which showcase the unique blend of Texas hospitality and artistic expression that defines this iconic city.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **bat-watching tour** on Lady Bird Lake, where you can see millions of Mexican free-tailed bats emerge from under the Congress Avenue Bridge at sunset
- Explore the **Hope Outdoor Gallery**, a vibrant street art park with stunning views of the city
- Attend a **live music performance** at the Continental Club, a legendary music venue that has hosted famous musicians like Stevie Ray Vaughan
- Visit the **SouthBark Dog Park**, a unique dog park with a dog-friendly bar and restaurant
- Go on a **food truck tour**, sampling the city's famous barbecue, Tex-Mex, and other cuisines
- Take a **dip in the natural springs** at Barton Springs Pool, a spring-fed pool surrounded by beautiful gardens and trees
- Attend a **festival**, such as the Austin City Limits Music Festival or the South by Southwest (SXSW) conference, which showcase the city's vibrant music and arts scene

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **McKinney Falls State Park**: hike and explore the scenic trails, waterfalls, and swimming holes
- **Barton Creek Greenbelt**: hike, bike, or swim in the natural springs and enjoy the beautiful scenery
- **Lady Bird Lake**: rent a kayak or paddleboard and explore the lake, or hike and bike along the scenic trail
- **Zilker Park**: attend outdoor festivals, hike, or swim in Barton Springs Pool
- **Wild Basin Wilderness Preserve**: hike and explore the scenic trails and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding hills

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Blanton Museum of Art**: explore the diverse collection of art from around the world, including European, Latin American, and Asian art
- **Mexic-Arte Museum**: learn about the art, history, and culture of Mexico and the Mexican-American community
- **Bullock Texas State History Museum**: discover the rich history of Texas, from pre-Columbian times to the present day
- **LBJ Presidential Library**: learn about the life and legacy of President Lyndon B. Johnson and explore the museum's interactive exhibits
- **Texas State Capitol**: take a guided tour of the impressive Capitol building and learn about Texas history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Oasis on Lake Travis**: enjoy stunning views of the lake and the surrounding hills, and sample the restaurant's famous Tex-Mex cuisine
- **The Contemporary Austin**: explore the innovative and thought-provoking art exhibitions at this contemporary art museum
- **The Hope Gallery**: admire the stunning street art and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of this outdoor art park
- **The Austin Central Library**: explore the stunning architecture and browse the extensive collection of books and media

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Wimberley**: visit this charming hill country town, known for its art galleries, wineries, and natural beauty (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 45 minutes)
- **Fredericksburg**: explore this historic German town, with its charming shops, restaurants, and wineries (distance: 70 miles, travel time: 1 hour 15 minutes)
- **Lockhart**: visit this historic town, known for its barbecue, antique shops, and natural beauty (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 45 minutes)
- **San Antonio**: explore the famous River Walk, visit the Alamo, and enjoy the vibrant arts and culture scene (distance: 80 miles, travel time: 1 hour 30 minutes)
- **Dripping Springs**: visit this charming hill country town, known for its natural beauty, wineries, and craft breweries (distance: 25 miles, travel time: 40 minutes)
