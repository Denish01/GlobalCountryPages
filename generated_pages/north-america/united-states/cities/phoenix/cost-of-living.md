[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider visiting Phoenix during the off-season (May-September) to avoid high prices and crowds.

- Take advantage of happy hour deals and discounts at local restaurants and bars.
- Look for affordable accommodation options outside of the city center, such as in nearby neighborhoods or suburbs.
- Plan ahead and book flights and accommodation well in advance to get the best prices.
- Use cashback and rewards credit cards to earn points and save on everyday purchases.
