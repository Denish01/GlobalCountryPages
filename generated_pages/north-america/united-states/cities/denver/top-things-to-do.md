# Top Things to Do in Denver — Attractions & Experiences

Denver, the vibrant capital of Colorado, offers a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that showcase its unique blend of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure, making it an ideal destination for visitors to explore its many **museums**, **breweries**, and **parks**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **brewery tour** to sample some of Denver's famous craft beers and learn about the brewing process
- Explore the vibrant **street art scene** in the Art District on Santa Fe, featuring numerous murals and galleries
- Visit the **Avanti Food and Beverage** collective, a unique dining experience offering a variety of cuisines
- Attend a **game at Coors Field**, home of the Colorado Rockies baseball team
- Go on a **ghost tour** to discover Denver's spooky side and haunted history
- Experience the **Denver March Powwow**, a celebration of Native American culture and traditions
- Take a **stroll along the Cherry Creek Bike Path**, a scenic route that follows the creek through the city

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Rocky Mountain National Park**, a stunning national park with hiking trails, mountains, and wildlife
- **Cherry Creek Reservoir**, a popular spot for boating, fishing, and birdwatching
- **Washington Park**, a large urban park with lakes, gardens, and recreational facilities
- **Mount Evans**, one of the highest paved roads in the world, offering breathtaking views
- **Confluence Park**, a scenic spot where the South Platte River and Cherry Creek meet

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Denver Art Museum**, featuring an extensive collection of American Indian art and a unique architectural design
- **History Colorado Center**, with interactive exhibits showcasing the state's history and culture
- **Denver Museum of Nature & Science**, offering exhibits on science, nature, and culture
- **Boettcher Concert Hall**, home of the Colorado Symphony and a venue for various performances
- **Kirkland Museum of Fine & Decorative Art**, a unique museum showcasing a wide range of art and decorative pieces

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Buckhorn Exchange**, a historic restaurant and bar featuring a vast collection of taxidermy and Western memorabilia
- **The Forney Museum of Transportation**, a quirky museum showcasing a wide range of vehicles and transportation-related items
- **The Molly Brown House Museum**, a historic house museum dedicated to the life and legacy of Margaret "Molly" Brown
- **The Black American West Museum**, a museum showcasing the history and culture of African Americans in the American West

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **Boulder**, a charming college town with a vibrant downtown area and stunning natural scenery, located 30 miles northwest of Denver (45-minute drive)
2. **Golden**, a historic town with a rich mining history and a famous brewery, located 15 miles west of Denver (30-minute drive)
3. **Estes Park**, a picturesque mountain town at the entrance of Rocky Mountain National Park, located 75 miles northwest of Denver (1.5-hour drive)
4. **Colorado Springs**, a city with a rich military history and stunning natural scenery, located 70 miles south of Denver (1.5-hour drive)
5. **Mount Evans**, a scenic mountain with breathtaking views, located 60 miles west of Denver (2-hour drive)

[SECTION] Planning Tips [/SECTION]
