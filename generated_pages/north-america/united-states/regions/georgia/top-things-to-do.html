The best things to do in Georgia include exploring its rich history, experiencing its vibrant culture, and enjoying its stunning natural beauty, with top attractions ranging from historic sites to beautiful beaches and mountains.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a stroll through the historic district of Savannah, admiring its beautiful architecture and picturesque parks
  • Visit the World of Coca-Cola in Atlanta, a museum showcasing the history of the iconic brand
  • Explore the Georgia Aquarium, one of the largest aquariums in the world, featuring thousands of aquatic animals
  • Attend a Braves game at Truist Park in Atlanta, a state-of-the-art baseball stadium
  • Take a ghost tour of Savannah, learning about the city's haunted history and paranormal activity
  • Visit the Center for Civil and Human Rights in Atlanta, a museum dedicated to the history of the civil rights movement
  • Go kayaking through the Okefenokee Swamp, a unique and breathtaking natural wonder

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the Blue Ridge Mountains, with hiking trails, scenic vistas, and stunning natural beauty
  • Visit Tybee Island, a charming beach town with a historic lighthouse and beautiful beaches
  • Hike to the top of Brasstown Bald, the highest point in Georgia, with stunning views of the surrounding mountains
  • Go hiking in the Chattahoochee National Forest, with thousands of acres of wilderness and scenic trails
  • Visit the Okefenokee National Wildlife Refuge, a unique and fascinating ecosystem with alligators, birds, and other wildlife

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the High Museum of Art in Atlanta, a premier art museum with a diverse collection of works
  • Explore the Atlanta History Center, a museum showcasing the city's history and culture
  • Attend a performance at the Fox Theatre, a historic theater with stunning architecture and live performances
  • Visit the Telfair Museums in Savannah, a collection of art museums with a diverse range of works
  • Take a cooking class in Savannah, learning about the city's unique cuisine and culinary traditions
  • Visit the Martin Luther King Jr. Center for Nonviolent Social Change, a museum and research center dedicated to the civil rights leader

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the town of Helen, a charming alpine-style town in the Blue Ridge Mountains
  • Explore the historic district of Milledgeville, a charming town with a rich history and beautiful architecture
  • Go hiking in the Tallulah Gorge State Park, a stunning natural wonder with hiking trails and scenic vistas
  • Visit the Hay House in Macon, a historic mansion with stunning architecture and beautiful gardens
  • Take a tour of the historic district of Madison, a charming town with a rich history and beautiful architecture

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip from Atlanta to Helen, a charming alpine-style town in the Blue Ridge Mountains (distance: 90 miles, travel time: 2 hours)
  • Visit Savannah from Tybee Island, exploring the city's historic district and beautiful architecture (distance: 18 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
  • Go on a day trip from Savannah to Jekyll Island, exploring the island's pristine beaches and historic district (distance: 90 miles, travel time: 2 hours)
  • Take a day trip from Atlanta to Chattanooga, Tennessee, exploring the city's scenic vistas and outdoor activities (distance: 120 miles, travel time: 2.5 hours)
  • Visit Augusta from Savannah, exploring the city's historic district and beautiful architecture (distance: 130 miles, travel time: 2.5 hours)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of Atlanta and Savannah), 7-day (comprehensive tour of the state's historic sites and natural wonders), 14-day (deep exploration of the state's culture, history, and outdoor activities). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: plan ahead for popular attractions, book accommodations in advance, and consider visiting during the shoulder season to avoid crowds.
