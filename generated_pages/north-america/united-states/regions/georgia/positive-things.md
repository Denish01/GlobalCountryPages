[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Gelati Monastery**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most important cultural and historical landmarks in Georgia.
- The **Svaneti Region**, known for its unique and ancient traditions, including the **Svaneti Tower Houses**.
- The **Georgian Wine**, with a history dating back over 8,000 years, and a wide range of varieties, including **Qvevri** and **Saperavi**.
- The **Tbilisi Opera and Ballet Theater**, a stunning example of neo-Moorish architecture and a popular venue for cultural events.
- The **Kartvelian Language**, an ancient and unique language, spoken by around 4 million people around the world.
