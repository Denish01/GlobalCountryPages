# Top Things to Do in Massachusetts — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Massachusetts, visit iconic attractions like the Freedom Trail, Fenway Park, and Cape Cod, and explore its unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **whale watching** tour from Provincetown or Boston to spot humpback, finback, and minke whales
- Visit the **Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum** in Boston to see an impressive collection of art and experience the museum's unique atmosphere
- Explore the **New Bedford Whaling Museum** to learn about the city's rich whaling history
- Attend a **Cape Cod Baseball League** game to watch top college players compete
- Visit the **Plymouth Rock** in Plymouth to see the site where the Pilgrims landed in 1620
- Take a **stroll along the Charles River** in Boston or Cambridge to enjoy the scenic views and parks
- Visit the **Eric Carle Museum of Picture Book Art** in Amherst to see original artwork from beloved children's books

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Cape Cod National Seashore** to enjoy hiking, biking, and swimming in a beautiful coastal setting
- Explore the **Blue Hills Reservation** in Milton to hike and ski in a scenic park
- Take a **hike in the Berkshire Mountains** to enjoy stunning views and outdoor adventures
- Visit **Parker River National Wildlife Refuge** in Newburyport to spot birds and other wildlife
- Relax on the beach at **Good Harbor Beach** in Gloucester or **Coast Guard Beach** in Eastham

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Institute of Contemporary Art** in Boston to see innovative and thought-provoking art
- Explore the **Peabody Essex Museum** in Salem to discover a diverse collection of art and culture from around the world
- Attend a performance at the **Boston Symphony Orchestra** or the **Boston Pops**
- Visit the **Museum of the National Center of Afro-American Artists** in Boston to learn about African American art and culture
- Take a **guided tour of the Boston Public Library** to see the stunning architecture and learn about its history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Gardner Museum's Courtyard** in Boston to see a beautiful courtyard filled with flowers and art
- Explore the **Arnold Arboretum** in Jamaica Plain to see a stunning collection of trees and plants
- Take a **stroll through the charming town of Provincetown** to enjoy its unique shops, galleries, and restaurants
- Visit the **Worcester Art Museum** to see a diverse collection of art from around the world
- Visit the **Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame** in Springfield to learn about the history of basketball

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Boston to **Plymouth** (35 miles, 1 hour) to visit the Plimoth Plantation and Plymouth Rock
- Visit **Cape Cod** (60 miles, 1.5 hours) from Boston to enjoy the beach and outdoor activities
- Take a day trip from Boston to **Newport, Rhode Island** (70 miles, 1.5 hours) to visit the historic mansions and enjoy the scenic coastal views
- Visit **The Berkshires** (120 miles, 2.5 hours) from Boston to enjoy outdoor activities and cultural attractions
- Take a day trip from Boston to **Salem** (16 miles, 30 minutes) to visit the Salem Witch Museum and explore the historic town
