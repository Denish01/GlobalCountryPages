[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Statue of Liberty**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an iconic symbol of freedom and democracy, located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor.
- The **Guggenheim Museum**, designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, is a renowned art museum in Manhattan, featuring an impressive collection of modern and contemporary art.
- The **Albany Institute of History & Art** is a premier cultural institution in the state capital, showcasing the art, history, and culture of New York State.
- The **Brooklyn Academy of Music (BAM)** is a leading performing arts center, presenting a diverse range of music, theater, and dance performances.
- The **National Baseball Hall of Fame and Museum**, located in Cooperstown, is a must-visit destination for baseball fans, dedicated to preserving the history of the sport.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Alexander Hamilton**, one of the founding fathers of the United States, was a key figure in shaping the country's history and is still celebrated today as a hero of American independence.
- The **New York State diaspora** has made significant contributions to the global community, with notable figures like **Henry Kissinger**, the former US Secretary of State, and **Ruth Bader Ginsburg**, the Supreme Court Justice, having strong ties to the state.
- The state's **values of diversity and inclusion** are reflected in its vibrant cultural landscape, with a long history of welcoming immigrants and promoting social justice.
- **Franklin D. Roosevelt**, the 32nd President of the United States, was a native New Yorker who played a crucial role in shaping the country's foreign policy and leading the nation through World War II.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **natural beauty** of New York State, from the Adirondack Mountains to the Niagara Falls, offers endless opportunities for outdoor recreation and exploration.
- The **world-class cuisine** of New York City, with its diverse range of restaurants and food markets, is a major draw for visitors from around the world.
- The **iconic landmarks** of New York City, such as the Empire State Building and Times Square, are must-see destinations for anyone visiting the state.
- The **vibrant arts and culture scene** of New York State, with its numerous museums, galleries, and performance venues, offers something for every interest and taste.
- The **friendly hospitality** of New Yorkers, despite their reputation for being tough and fast-paced, is often commented on by visitors as a pleasant surprise.
- The **unique experiences** of New York State, such as visiting a **Broadway show** or taking a **helicopter tour** of the city, make for unforgettable memories.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Hudson Valley**, located just north of New York City, is a scenic region with a rich history, beautiful landscapes, and a thriving arts community.
- The **Finger Lakes region**, known for its stunning natural beauty and world-class wineries, is a hidden gem for wine enthusiasts and nature lovers.
- The **Buffalo and Erie County Public Library**, a stunning example of neoclassical architecture, is a must-visit destination for book lovers and architecture enthusiasts.
