# Top Things to Do in Washington — Attractions & Experiences

The state of Washington offers a diverse range of activities and attractions, from the vibrant city of Seattle to the stunning natural beauty of the Olympic Peninsula, making it an ideal destination for travelers with varying interests.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **food tour** of Seattle's Pike Place Market to sample local specialties like seafood, cheese, and chocolate
- Visit the **Museum of Pop Culture** in Seattle to learn about popular culture, music, and science fiction
- Go on a **whale watching tour** in Puget Sound to see orcas, gray whales, and other marine wildlife
- Explore the **Hoh Rainforest** on the Olympic Peninsula, one of the largest temperate rainforests in the United States
- Attend a **music festival** in Seattle, such as Bumbershoot or the Seattle Jazz and Blues Festival
- Visit the **Tulalip Resort Casino** in Tulalip to try your luck at the slots or table games
- Take a **coffee tour** in Seattle to learn about the city's coffee culture and visit iconic cafes

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Hurricane Ridge Trail** in Olympic National Park for stunning views of the Strait of Juan de Fuca
- Visit **Ruby Beach** in Olympic National Park to see dramatic sea stacks and tidepools
- Explore the **Columbia River Gorge** and visit the **Vista House** for panoramic views
- Go **skiing or snowboarding** at **Mount Baker Ski Area** or **The Summit at Snoqualmie**
- Take a **kayaking tour** of Puget Sound to see marine wildlife and enjoy the scenery

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Frye Art Museum** in Seattle to see a collection of 19th- and 20th-century European art
- Attend a **performance** at the **Seattle Repertory Theatre** or **5th Avenue Theatre**
- Explore the **Wing Luke Museum of the Asian Pacific American Experience** in Seattle's Chinatown-International District
- Visit the **Suquamish Museum** to learn about the history and culture of the Suquamish Native American tribe
- Take a **cooking class** in Seattle to learn about the city's food scene and try new recipes

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Center for Wooden Boats** in Seattle to see historic boats and learn about maritime history
- Explore the **Washington Park Arboretum** in Seattle to see beautiful gardens and natural areas
- Go to the **Georgetown neighborhood** in Seattle to see street art, visit unique shops, and enjoy the nightlife
- Take a **tour of the Fremont Sunday Market** in Seattle to see local vendors and artisans

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Mount Rainier National Park** from Seattle (distance: 54 miles, travel time: 1 hour) to hike and explore the park's natural beauty
- Visit **Leavenworth** from Seattle (distance: 115 miles, travel time: 2 hours) to experience a charming Bavarian-themed town
- Go to **Olympic National Park** from Seattle (distance: 140 miles, travel time: 3 hours) to explore the park's diverse ecosystems and natural wonders
- Take a day trip to **Tacoma** from Seattle (distance: 32 miles, travel time: 45 minutes) to visit the **Tacoma Art Museum** and **Point Defiance Park**
- Visit **Bellingham** from Seattle (distance: 85 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours) to explore the **Whatcom Museum** and enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and kayaking
