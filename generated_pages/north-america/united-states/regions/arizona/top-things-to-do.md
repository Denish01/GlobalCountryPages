# Top Things to Do in Arizona — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a scenic drive along the **Oak Creek Canyon** with its stunning red rock formations
- Explore the **Havasupai Falls**, a series of turquoise waterfalls in the Grand Canyon
- Attend a **Native American powwow**, a celebration of traditional music, dance, and food
- Visit the **Biosphere 2**, a unique research facility with diverse ecosystems
- Go on a **hot air balloon ride** over the Sonoran Desert
- Take a **stargazing tour** in the dark skies of the **Coconino National Forest**
- Learn about **Navajo code talkers** and their role in World War II at the Navajo Code Talkers Museum

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Bright Angel Trail**, one of the most popular trails in the Grand Canyon
- Explore the **Saguaro National Park**, home to the iconic saguaro cactus
- Visit the **Petrified Forest National Park**, with its stunning rock formations and petrified wood
- Take a **rafting trip** down the **Colorado River**, which forms the border between Arizona and California
- Go **hiking** in the **Superstition Mountains**, with its unique rock formations and stunning views
- Visit the **Glen Canyon National Recreation Area**, with its stunning lake and canyon scenery

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Heard Museum**, a showcase of Native American art and culture
- Explore the **Tucson Museum of Art**, with its diverse collection of American, European, and Latin American art
- Attend a performance at the **Phoenix Symphony Hall**, home of the Phoenix Symphony Orchestra
- Take a **tour of Taliesin West**, the former home of Frank Lloyd Wright and a National Historic Landmark
- Visit the **Arizona Capitol Museum**, with its exhibits on the history and government of Arizona
- Learn about the **history of the Navajo people** at the Navajo Nation Museum

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tumacácori National Historical Park**, a historic mission and one of the oldest in the United States
- Explore the **Patagonia Lake State Park**, a scenic park with hiking trails and birdwatching opportunities
- Take a **wine tour** in the **Verde Valley**, with its scenic vineyards and wineries
- Visit the **Bisbee 1000 Stairs**, a unique stairway that connects the town's hills and neighborhoods
- Go **stargazing** in the **Kartchner Caverns State Park**, with its dark skies and stunning cave formations

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Phoenix** to the **Sedona Red Rocks** (120 miles, 2 hours), with its stunning red rock formations and scenic hiking trails
- Visit the **Tombstone** historic town (60 miles, 1 hour from Tucson), with its preserved 19th-century architecture and Wild West history
- Explore the **Wupatki National Monument** (30 miles, 45 minutes from Flagstaff), with its preserved ancient Native American ruins
- Take a day trip from **Tucson** to the **Biosphere 2** (30 miles, 45 minutes), with its unique research facility and diverse ecosystems
- Visit the **Montezuma Castle National Monument** (90 miles, 1.5 hours from Phoenix), with its well-preserved ancient cliff dwelling
