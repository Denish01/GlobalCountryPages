To experience the best of Texas, visit iconic attractions like the Space Center Houston, explore the vibrant cities of Austin and Dallas, and immerse yourself in the state's rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural beauty.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a Texas BBQ tour to sample the state's famous slow-cooked meats and learn about the different regional styles
  • Attend a Dallas Cowboys football game at AT&T Stadium, one of the most iconic sports venues in the country
  • Explore the Marfa Prada art installation, a unique outdoor exhibit featuring a replica of a Prada store in the middle of the desert
  • Visit the Cadillac Ranch, a public art installation featuring ten vintage Cadillacs buried nose-first in a field
  • Go horseback riding through the scenic hills and trails of Bandera, known as the "Cowboy Capital of the World"
  • Take a stargazing tour in Big Bend National Park, which offers some of the darkest skies in the country
  • Learn about the Texas wine industry with a tour and tasting at a local vineyard, such as Driftwood Estate Winery

Outdoor & Nature

  • Hike the Guadalupe Peak Trail in Guadalupe Mountains National Park, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
  • Explore the Padre Island National Seashore, a protected barrier island with pristine beaches and diverse wildlife
  • Visit the Big Thicket National Preserve, a unique ecosystem featuring a mix of swamps, forests, and prairies
  • Go kayaking or paddleboarding on Lady Bird Lake in Austin, which offers stunning views of the city skyline
  • Take a scenic drive through the Hill Country, which features rolling hills, wildflowers, and picturesque towns

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Bullock Texas State History Museum in Austin, which tells the story of Texas' rich history and culture
  • Explore the Dallas Museum of Art, which features a diverse collection of art from around the world
  • Attend a performance at the Houston Grand Opera, one of the largest and most renowned opera companies in the country
  • Learn about the Texas Ranger Hall of Fame and Museum in Waco, which showcases the history and legacy of the Texas Rangers
  • Visit the San Antonio Missions National Historical Park, a collection of four Spanish colonial missions that are now a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Take a tango lesson in Austin, which has a thriving dance scene and a variety of studios offering classes and workshops

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Terlingua Ghost Town, a former mining town that's now a popular destination for off-roading and stargazing
  • Explore the Wimberley Valley, a scenic area with rolling hills, wildflowers, and a variety of art galleries and studios
  • Take a tour of the Shiner Brewery, a family-owned brewery that's been producing beer in Texas since 1909
  • Go antique shopping in Round Top, a small town that's home to a variety of vintage shops and dealers
  • Visit the Caverns of Sonora, a unique cave system featuring stunning rock formations and underground pools

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip from Austin to Fredericksburg (distance: 130 miles, travel time: 2.5 hours), which features a charming downtown area, wineries, and scenic hiking trails
  • Visit Galveston Island from Houston (distance: 50 miles, travel time: 1 hour), which offers a variety of beaches, amusement parks, and historic sites
  • Explore the Hill Country from San Antonio (distance: 70 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours), which features rolling hills, wildflowers, and picturesque towns
  • Take a day trip from Dallas to Fort Worth (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 45 minutes), which features a rich cowboy heritage, cultural attractions, and a vibrant downtown area
  • Visit Big Bend National Park from El Paso (distance: 230 miles, travel time: 4 hours), which offers stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and scenic drives

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of Austin and San Antonio), 7-day (comprehensive tour of major cities and attractions), 14-day (deep exploration of Texas' natural beauty, culture, and history). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: plan ahead for popular attractions, book accommodations in advance, and consider visiting during the shoulder season to avoid crowds.
