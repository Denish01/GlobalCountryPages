- The Dallas Symphony Orchestra, founded in 1900, is one of the oldest and most respected orchestras in the United States, known for its classical music performances.
- The King Ranch, a historic ranch located in southern Texas, is a testament to the state's cattle ranching heritage and offers tours and educational programs.
- The El Paso Mission Trail, a collection of historic missions and churches, preserves the region's rich cultural and architectural heritage.
