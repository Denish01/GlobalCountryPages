- The **Ohio Light Opera**, based in Wooster, is a professional opera company that produces a wide range of classical and modern operas, with a focus on American musical theater.

- The **Dayton Aviation Heritage National Historical Park** commemorates the lives and achievements of the Wright brothers, who invented and built the world's first successful airplane in Dayton.

- The **sports culture** in Ohio is vibrant and passionate, with numerous professional and collegiate teams, including the Cincinnati Bengals, Cleveland Browns, and Ohio State Buckeyes.
- The **festivals and events** in Ohio, such as the Ohio State Fair, the Cleveland International Film Festival, and the Cincinnati Music Festival, offer a wide range of entertainment and cultural experiences.
- The **historic architecture** of Ohio, including the Ohio Statehouse, the Cleveland Terminal Tower, and the Cincinnati Music Hall, is a testament to the state's rich history and cultural heritage.
