# Top Things to Do in Hawaii — Attractions & Experiences

Hawaii offers a diverse range of activities and experiences that cater to different interests, from visiting iconic natural wonders like **Haleakala National Park** to immersing oneself in the local culture at the **Bishop Museum**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **surfing lesson** on the North Shore of Oahu, where famous surf competitions are held
- Attend a traditional **luau** at a local resort or cultural center to experience Hawaiian music, dance, and cuisine
- Go on a **whale watching tour** off the coast of Maui to see humpback whales in their natural habitat
- Explore the **Kona coffee farms** on the Big Island and sample some of the world's best coffee
- Visit a **macadamia nut farm** on the Big Island to learn about the production process and taste the famous nuts
- Take a **helicopter tour** over the islands to see the stunning natural beauty from a unique perspective
- Learn about the ancient Hawaiian art of **hula dancing** at a local cultural center or workshop

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Kalalau Trail** along the Na Pali Coast on Kauai for breathtaking ocean views and challenging terrain
- Visit **Volcanoes National Park** on the Big Island to explore volcanic landscapes and lava tubes
- Snorkel or kayak in **Molokini Crater**, a marine sanctuary and one of the best snorkeling spots in Hawaii
- Explore the **Waipio Valley** on the Big Island, a lush and remote valley with stunning natural beauty
- Visit **Kanaha Beach Park** on Maui, a popular spot for windsurfing and kitesurfing

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Bishop Museum** on Oahu to learn about Hawaiian history, culture, and natural environment
- Explore the **Hawaii State Capitol** building in Honolulu, a unique example of modern architecture
- Attend a performance at the **Honolulu Theatre**, which hosts a variety of cultural events and productions
- Visit the **Queen Emma Summer Palace** on Oahu, a historic palace that showcases Hawaiian royalty
- Take a **hula dance class** at a local cultural center to learn about traditional Hawaiian dance and music

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Dole Plantation** on Oahu, a working pineapple farm with a variety of activities and attractions
- Explore the **Hilo Farmers Market** on the Big Island, a bustling market with local produce and handicrafts
- Take a **stargazing tour** at the Mauna Kea Visitor Information Station, which offers free telescope viewing and astronomy talks
- Visit the **Lahaina Heritage Trail** on Maui, a self-guided walking tour that explores the town's rich history and culture
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the scenic countryside of Kauai, offering stunning views of the island's natural beauty

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Honolulu to the **North Shore** of Oahu, famous for its surfing beaches and laid-back atmosphere (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Road to Hana** on Maui, a scenic drive with stunning ocean views and waterfalls (distance: 52 miles, travel time: 2-3 hours)
- Explore the **Puna District** on the Big Island, known for its black sand beaches and lava tubes (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a day trip from Kauai to the **Na Pali Coast**, a rugged and scenic coastline with hiking trails and stunning views (distance: 40 miles, travel time: 1-2 hours)
- Visit the **Kohala Coast** on the Big Island, a scenic area with white sand beaches and luxury resorts (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
