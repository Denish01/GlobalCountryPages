# Top Things to Do in Florida — Attractions & Experiences

Florida is a top travel destination in the United States, offering a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that cater to different interests and ages, from beautiful beaches and theme parks to vibrant cities and unique wildlife. 

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Walt Disney World | Orlando | Theme Park | The largest and most iconic theme park in the world, featuring four separate parks and two water parks |
| Universal Orlando Resort | Orlando | Theme Park | A movie-themed park with thrilling rides, shows, and attractions based on popular films and TV series |
| Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex | Merritt Island | Museum | A unique opportunity to learn about space exploration and see real rockets and spacecraft |
| Everglades National Park | Homestead | National Park | A vast and diverse ecosystem, home to numerous wildlife species, including alligators, crocodiles, and numerous bird species |
| Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum | Key West | Museum | The former residence of the famous author, featuring a beautiful garden and a collection of his memorabilia |
| Busch Gardens Tampa Bay | Tampa | Theme Park | An African-themed park with thrilling rides, animal exhibits, and live performances |
| SeaWorld Orlando | Orlando | Theme Park | A marine-life theme park with shows, exhibits, and thrill rides, focusing on conservation and education |
| Vizcaya Museum and Gardens | Miami | Historic Estate | A stunning estate with European-inspired gardens and architecture, featuring an impressive collection of art and furnishings |
| Ringling Museum of Art | Sarasota | Museum | A museum showcasing a diverse collection of European and American art, as well as a beautiful garden and historic mansion |
| Little Havana | Miami | Neighborhood | A vibrant and colorful neighborhood, known for its rich Cuban culture, street art, and lively atmosphere |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take an **airboat tour** of the Everglades, exploring the unique ecosystem and spotting wildlife such as alligators and birds
- Visit the **Mermaid City of Weeki Wachee**, where you can see live mermaid shows and learn about the history of this kitschy attraction
- Go on a **swim with the manatees** tour in Crystal River, a unique opportunity to interact with these gentle creatures in their natural habitat
- Take a **ghost tour** of St. Augustine, exploring the city's haunted history and paranormal activity
- Visit the **Robert Frost Farm**, the former residence of the famous poet, featuring a museum and beautiful gardens
- Attend a **Key West food tour**, sampling the local cuisine and learning about the island's rich culinary history
- Explore the **Dry Tortugas National Park**, a remote island with a historic fort and an incredible array of marine life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Biscayne National Park**, a protected area featuring coral reefs, mangrove forests, and a diverse array of wildlife
- Explore the **Florida Keys**, a chain of islands with beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a vibrant coral reef ecosystem
- Hike the **Florida Trail**, a 1,100-mile trail that spans the state, offering stunning natural scenery and opportunities to spot wildlife
- Visit **Caladesi Island State Park**, a pristine island with beautiful beaches, nature trails, and a historic lighthouse
- Go **kayaking** or **paddleboarding** in the bioluminescent waters of **Tampa Bay** or **Indian River Lagoon**

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Pérez Art Museum Miami**, a modern art museum featuring a diverse collection of international and local artists
- Explore the **Ringling Museum of Art**, a museum showcasing a diverse collection of European and American art, as well as a beautiful garden and historic mansion
- Attend a performance at the **Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts**, a state-of-the-art venue featuring a wide range of cultural events and performances
- Visit the **Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum**, the former residence of the famous author, featuring a beautiful garden and a collection of his memorabilia
- Take a **Cuban coffee tour** in Miami's Little Havana, learning about the history and culture of Cuban coffee and sampling local cuisine

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Cassadaga Hotel**, a historic hotel and spiritual center, offering guided tours and psychic readings
- Explore the **Wynwood Walls**, an outdoor art exhibit featuring vibrant street art and murals
- Take a **bioluminescent kayak tour** in **Titusville**, paddling through the glowing waters of the Indian River Lagoon
- Visit the **Leu Gardens**, a beautiful botanical garden featuring a variety of plant species and a peaceful atmosphere
- Attend a **jazz performance** at the **Ball & Chain**, a historic bar and lounge in Miami's Little Havana

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Miami** to **Key Largo** (60 miles, 1 hour), exploring the island's beautiful beaches and coral reefs
- Visit **St. Augustine** (115 miles, 2 hours from Jacksonville), a historic city with a rich cultural heritage and plenty of attractions
- Take a day trip from **Tampa** to **Clearwater Beach** (20 miles, 30 minutes), enjoying the beach and the city's vibrant atmosphere
- Explore the **Florida Keys** from **Key West** (100 miles, 2 hours), visiting the islands' beautiful beaches and historic lighthouses
- Visit **Cedar Key** (130 miles, 2.5 hours from Gainesville), a charming island with a rich artistic community and beautiful natural scenery
