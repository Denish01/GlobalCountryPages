[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum** in Key West is a National Historic Landmark and a testament to the author's life and works.
- The **Vizcaya Museum and Gardens** in Miami is a stunning example of European-inspired architecture and gardens, featuring an impressive collection of art and furnishings.
- The **Ringling Museum of Art** in Sarasota is a premier cultural institution, showcasing a diverse collection of European and American art, as well as a beautiful circus museum.
- The **Key West Lighthouse** is a historic landmark and a symbol of the state's rich maritime heritage.
- The **Florida Folk Festival** is an annual celebration of the state's folk music, crafts, and traditions, featuring live performances and demonstrations.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Jim Morrison**, the legendary lead singer of The Doors, was born in Melbourne, Florida, and is still celebrated as a cultural icon.
- **Toni Morrison**, the Nobel Prize-winning author, had a home in Florida and drew inspiration from the state's unique cultural landscape.
- The **Cuban diaspora** has had a significant impact on Florida's culture and economy, with many Cubans settling in the state and contributing to its rich cultural heritage.
- **Jacques Cousteau**, the famous oceanographer, had a research center in Florida and worked to promote marine conservation and education in the state.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **beautiful beaches** and **clear waters** of the Florida Keys, perfect for snorkeling, diving, and relaxing in the sun.
- The **vibrant city life** of Miami, with its diverse cultural scene, world-class restaurants, and exciting nightlife.
- The **theme parks** of Orlando, including **Walt Disney World** and **Universal Studios**, which offer a wide range of entertainment options for visitors of all ages.
- The **natural beauty** of the Everglades, with its unique wildlife and ecosystems, and the opportunity to take an **airboat tour** or hike through the **Big Cypress National Preserve**.
- The **fresh seafood** and **local cuisine**, which showcases the state's rich culinary heritage and features a wide range of dishes, from **Key lime pie** to **stone crab claws**.
- The **friendly hospitality** of the Floridians, who are known for their warm welcome and Southern charm.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Crystal River National Wildlife Refuge**, a protected area that is home to a large population of **manatees** and offers opportunities for kayaking and wildlife viewing.
- The **Fort Zachary Taylor** in Key West, a historic fort that played a significant role in the **Civil War** and offers stunning views of the surrounding waters.
- The **Corkscrew Swamp Sanctuary**, a nature reserve that features a **2.5-mile boardwalk** through a stunning cypress forest and offers opportunities to spot **alligators**, **birds**, and other wildlife.
- The **St. Augustine Lighthouse**, a historic landmark that offers breathtaking views of the **Matanzas Bay** and the **Atlantic Ocean**, and features a **lighthouse museum** and a **gift shop**.
