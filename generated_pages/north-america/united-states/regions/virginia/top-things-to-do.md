# Top Things to Do in Virginia — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Virginia, visit iconic attractions like Monticello, Shenandoah National Park, and the Virginia Beach Boardwalk, which showcase the state's rich history, natural beauty, and vibrant culture.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Shenandoah Valley for a breathtaking view of the countryside
- Visit a **tobacco barn** in the rural countryside to learn about Virginia's agricultural heritage
- Go on a **ghost tour** in historic Williamsburg or Richmond to explore the state's paranormal side
- Attend a **NASCAR race** at the Martinsville Speedway or Richmond International Raceway
- Explore the **underground tunnels** of the Maggie L. Walker National Historic Site in Richmond
- Learn about the **Native American tribes** of Virginia at the Pamunkey Indian Reservation Museum
- Visit a **historic lighthouse**, such as the Cape Henry Lighthouse in Virginia Beach

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Appalachian Trail**, which spans over 550 miles through Virginia's mountains and forests
- Explore the **Chesapeake Bay**, with its abundant wildlife and water sports
- Visit **First Landing State Park**, with its beautiful beaches and outdoor recreational activities
- Go **kayaking** or **canoeing** on the James River or other waterways
- Take a scenic drive through the **Blue Ridge Parkway**, which runs through Virginia's mountains
- Visit the **Great Falls Park**, with its stunning waterfalls and hiking trails

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Virginia Museum of Fine Arts** in Richmond, with its diverse collection of art from around the world
- Explore the **Historic District** of Alexandria, with its charming streets, shops, and restaurants
- Attend a performance at the **Wolf Trap National Park for the Performing Arts** in Vienna
- Learn about Virginia's **African American history** at the Black History Museum and Cultural Center in Richmond
- Visit the **Taubman Museum of Art** in Roanoke, with its collection of American art from the 19th century to the present
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about Virginia's unique cuisine, such as peanut soup and ham biscuits

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Barboursville Vineyards**, a winery located in a historic estate with stunning views
- Explore the **Lexington Historic District**, with its charming shops, restaurants, and historic homes
- Go to the **Virginia Quilt Museum** in Harrisonburg, with its collection of historic and contemporary quilts
- Take a **scenic drive** through the **Shenandoah Valley**, with its picturesque countryside and historic towns
- Visit the **Mabry Mill**, a historic gristmill in Floyd County with stunning views of the surrounding countryside

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Richmond** to **Williamsburg** (70 miles, 1 hour), with its historic district and colonial attractions
- Visit **Monticello** from **Charlottesville** (15 miles, 30 minutes), with its stunning architecture and beautiful gardens
- Explore the **Shenandoah National Park** from **Harrisonburg** (45 miles, 1 hour), with its hiking trails and scenic views
- Go to the **Virginia Beach** from **Norfolk** (20 miles, 30 minutes), with its beautiful beaches and boardwalk
- Take a day trip from **Arlington** to **Mount Vernon** (15 miles, 30 minutes), with its historic estate and stunning views of the Potomac River
