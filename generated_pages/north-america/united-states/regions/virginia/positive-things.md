[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Monticello**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the former estate of Thomas Jefferson, showcasing his architectural and design skills.
- The **Arlington National Cemetery** is a historic military cemetery that honors the nation's fallen heroes, with over 400,000 graves and the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier.
- The **Virginia Opera** is a professional opera company that performs a wide range of productions, from classic operas to contemporary works.
- The **Richmond Jazz Society** promotes and preserves the rich jazz heritage of the city, with regular concerts and events featuring local and international musicians.
- The **Shenandoah Valley** is a scenic region known for its natural beauty, with numerous hiking trails, waterfalls, and picturesque towns.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Thomas Jefferson**, the third President of the United States, was a native Virginian who played a significant role in shaping the country's history and politics.
- **Toni Morrison**, the Nobel Prize-winning author, was born in Ohio but spent much of her life in Virginia, where she taught at the University of Virginia.
- The **African American diaspora** has had a profound impact on Virginia's culture and history, with many notable figures, such as Booker T. Washington and Maggie Lena Walker, contributing to the state's rich heritage.
- The **values of community and hospitality** are deeply ingrained in Virginia's culture, with many residents actively involved in volunteer work and community service.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **natural beauty** of Virginia, with its stunning mountains, scenic coastlines, and picturesque countryside, is a major draw for tourists.
- The **historic sites**, such as Monticello, Mount Vernon, and Williamsburg, offer a glimpse into the state's rich history and cultural heritage.
- The **food**, including traditional dishes like Virginia Diner pie, barbecue, and seafood, is a highlight of any visit to the state.

- The **wine country**, with numerous vineyards and wineries, offers a chance to sample some of the state's excellent wines and enjoy the scenic countryside.
