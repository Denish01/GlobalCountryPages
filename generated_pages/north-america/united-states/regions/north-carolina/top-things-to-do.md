# Top Things to Do in North Carolina — Attractions & Experiences

North Carolina offers a diverse range of activities and attractions that cater to different interests, from visiting the **Biltmore Estate** in Asheville to exploring the **Great Smoky Mountains National Park**, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking for a mix of culture, nature, and adventure.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Blue Ridge Mountains for a breathtaking view
- Explore the **Outer Banks**, a chain of barrier islands with a rich maritime history
- Visit the **Barbecue Festival** in Lexington, a celebration of the state's famous barbecue
- Go **whitewater rafting** in the Nantahala River, a thrilling adventure for experienced rafters
- Attend a **music festival** in Asheville, a city known for its vibrant music scene
- Learn about the **history of motorsports** at the NASCAR Hall of Fame in Charlotte
- Take a **wine tour** in the Yadkin Valley, a region known for its vineyards and wineries
- Go **skydiving** in the Triangle area, an exhilarating experience for adventure-seekers

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Great Smoky Mountains National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Hike the **Appalachian Trail**, a 2,190-mile trail that passes through North Carolina
- Visit the **Cape Hatteras National Seashore**, a pristine beach with wild horses
- Kayak through the **Alligator River National Wildlife Refuge**, a habitat for alligators and birds
- Go **hiking** in the **Pisgah National Forest**, a scenic forest with waterfalls and trails
- Visit the **North Carolina Zoo**, a world-class zoo with a variety of animals

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **North Carolina Museum of Art**, a museum with a diverse collection of art
- Explore the **Nasher Museum of Art** at Duke University, a museum with a focus on contemporary art
- Attend a **performance** at the **Durham Performing Arts Center**, a state-of-the-art theater
- Learn about the **history of the state** at the **North Carolina Museum of History**, a museum with interactive exhibits
- Visit the **Billy Graham Library**, a museum dedicated to the life and legacy of the famous evangelist
- Take a **tour** of the **Biltmore Estate**, a historic house with beautiful gardens

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Asheville River Arts District**, a former industrial area turned arts district
- Explore the **Town Creek Indian Mound**, a historic site with a reconstructed Native American village
- Go **hiking** in the **Uwharrie National Forest**, a lesser-known forest with scenic trails
- Take a **tour** of the **New Belgium Brewing Company**, a craft brewery with a unique tasting room
- Visit the **Wright Brothers National Memorial**, a historic site dedicated to the inventors of the airplane

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Charlotte** to **Asheville** (130 miles, 2 hours), a vibrant city with a thriving arts scene
- Visit **Wilmington** from **Raleigh** (130 miles, 2.5 hours), a historic city with a beautiful riverwalk
- Explore the **Outer Banks** from **Raleigh** (200 miles, 4 hours), a chain of barrier islands with a rich maritime history
- Go to **Great Smoky Mountains National Park** from **Asheville** (60 miles, 1.5 hours), a UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Take a day trip from **Durham** to **Chapel Hill** (10 miles, 20 minutes), a charming college town with a vibrant music scene
