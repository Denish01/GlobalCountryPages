[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **ghost tour** of Calgary's historic sites and hear spooky stories about the city's past
- Attend a **Calgary Flames** hockey game at the Scotiabank Saddledome for an exhilarating experience
- Explore the **street art** scene in the Beltline neighborhood and discover vibrant murals
- Visit the **Calgary Farmers' Market** to sample local produce and artisanal products
- Go **horseback riding** through the scenic trails of Fish Creek Provincial Park
- Learn about **Indigenous culture** at the Nickle Galleries, which features a collection of Indigenous art and artifacts
- Take a **brewery tour** and sample some of Calgary's best craft beers

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Banff National Park**, a stunning mountain park with glaciers, lakes, and hiking trails, located just an hour and a half west of Calgary
- Visit the **Fish Creek Provincial Park**, a large urban park with hiking trails, picnic areas, and opportunities for birdwatching
- Go **hiking** in the **Kananaskis Country**, a scenic region with mountains, forests, and lakes
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Glenmore Reservoir**, a large lake with opportunities for sailing and kayaking
- Visit the **Inglewood Bird Sanctuary**, a nature reserve with over 200 species of birds
- Go **skiing** at the **Nakiska Ski Resort**, a world-class ski resort located in the Canadian Rockies

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Glenbow Museum** to learn about Western Canadian art and history
- Attend a performance at the **Southern Alberta Jubilee Auditorium**, a theater that hosts a variety of shows and concerts
- Explore the **Esker Foundation**, a contemporary art gallery with rotating exhibits
- Take a **cooking class** and learn about Canadian cuisine at the **Calgary Cooking School**
- Visit the **Nickel Galleries**, a museum that features a collection of Indigenous art and artifacts
- Attend a **festival** or **event** at the **Olympic Plaza**, a public square that hosts various cultural events throughout the year

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lougheed House**, a historic mansion with beautiful gardens and a restaurant
- Explore the **Riley Park**, a scenic park with a large pond and walking trails
- Go **shopping** in the **Inglewood neighborhood**, a charming area with unique boutiques and antique stores
- Take a **stroll** along the **Bow River Pathway**, a scenic walking trail that follows the Bow River
- Visit the **The Nash and Off Cut Bar**, a unique restaurant and bar that serves creative dishes and cocktails

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Banff National Park** (130 km, 1 hour 30 minutes), and explore the stunning mountain scenery and abundant wildlife
- Visit the **Drumheller Badlands** (135 km, 1 hour 30 minutes), and explore the unique landscape and dinosaur fossils
- Go to **Kananaskis Country** (80 km, 1 hour), and enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, skiing, and snowshoeing
- Take a day trip to **Lake Louise** (180 km, 2 hours), and enjoy the scenic lake and surrounding mountains
- Visit the **Royal Tyrrell Museum** in Drumheller (135 km, 1 hour 30 minutes), and learn about dinosaurs and paleontology
