[SECTION] How Expensive Is Calgary? [/SECTION]
Calgary is a major city in Alberta, Canada, known for its high standard of living and moderate cost of living. Compared to other Canadian cities like Toronto or Vancouver, Calgary is relatively affordable, but still more expensive than many cities in the United States. The cost of living in Calgary is influenced by its strong economy, driven by the energy industry, and its proximity to the Rocky Mountains, which makes it a popular tourist destination. Overall, Calgary offers a great balance between quality of life and affordability, making it an attractive option for visitors and residents alike.

In terms of global averages, Calgary is more expensive than many cities in Asia or Latin America, but more affordable than many cities in Western Europe or Australia. The city's cost of living is also influenced by its location in the province of Alberta, which has a relatively low tax rate compared to other provinces in Canada. This makes Calgary an attractive option for businesses and individuals looking to relocate to Canada.
