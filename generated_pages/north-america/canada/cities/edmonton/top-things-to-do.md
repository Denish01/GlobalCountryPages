[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Festival** tour to experience Edmonton's vibrant arts and culture scene, with over 50 festivals throughout the year, including the **Edmonton Folk Music Festival** and **Edmonton International Fringe Theatre Festival**.
- Explore the **Ice on Whyte** festival, where you can see intricate ice sculptures and enjoy winter activities.
- Visit the **Old Strathcona Farmers' Market** to sample local food and drinks.
- Go on a **Ghost Tour** of Edmonton to discover the city's haunted history and paranormal activity.
- Take a **Brewery Tour** to sample local craft beers and learn about Edmonton's brewing scene.
- Experience the **Culina Muttart** restaurant, located in the beautiful Muttart Conservatory, offering fine dining with a botanical twist.

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Elk Island National Park**: hike, camp, and spot wildlife, including bison and elk.
- **Edmonton River Valley**: enjoy scenic views, hiking, and biking in North America's largest urban park.
- **William Hawrelak Park**: visit this scenic park, featuring a large lake, hiking trails, and plenty of wildlife.
- **Mill Woods Park**: explore the trails, lakes, and forests of this large urban park.
- **Whitemud Ravine**: hike and explore the beautiful ravine, featuring scenic views and unique rock formations.
- **Terwillegar Park**: visit this scenic park, featuring a large lake, hiking trails, and plenty of wildlife.

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Art Gallery of Alberta**: discover contemporary and historical art from Alberta and around the world.
- **Royal Alberta Museum**: learn about Alberta's natural and human history.
- **Fort Edmonton Park**: explore Canada's largest living history museum.
- **Old Strathcona**: experience Edmonton's vibrant arts and culture scene, featuring unique shops, restaurants, and theaters.
- **Muttart Conservatory**: marvel at the beautiful gardens and plant species.
- **Francis Winspear Centre for Music**: attend a performance by the Edmonton Symphony Orchestra or other local music groups.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The End of the World**: a unique, quirky shop featuring vintage and second-hand items.
- **The Art of Cake**: a bakery and cafe featuring delicious treats and beautiful artwork.
- **The Blue Plate Diner**: a retro-style diner serving classic comfort food.
- **The Old Chelsea**: a historic bed and breakfast featuring beautiful rooms and a charming atmosphere.
- **The Wee Book Inn**: a used bookstore featuring a vast selection of titles and a cozy atmosphere.

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Jasper National Park**: located approximately 365 km west of Edmonton, featuring stunning mountain scenery, hiking trails, and abundant wildlife (4-hour drive).
- **Banff National Park**: located approximately 415 km southwest of Edmonton, featuring beautiful mountains, glaciers, and abundant wildlife (4.5-hour drive).
- **Calgary**: located approximately 300 km south of Edmonton, featuring a vibrant city scene, historic landmarks, and cultural attractions (3-hour drive).
- **Drumheller**: located approximately 135 km southeast of Edmonton, featuring the Royal Tyrrell Museum and the Badlands (1.5-hour drive).
- **Camrose**: located approximately 90 km southeast of Edmonton, featuring a charming downtown area, historic landmarks, and outdoor recreation opportunities (1-hour drive).
