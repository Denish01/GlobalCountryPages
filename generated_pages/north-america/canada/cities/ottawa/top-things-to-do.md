# Top Things to Do in Ottawa — Attractions & Experiences

Ottawa, the capital city of Canada, offers a diverse range of activities and attractions that cater to different interests and preferences, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **sunset cruise** on the Ottawa River to see the city's landmarks from a different perspective
- Visit the **Rideau Canal** in winter to see it frozen and turned into the world's largest naturally frozen ice skating rink
- Explore the **street art scene** in the ByWard Market and Hintonburg neighborhoods
- Attend a **festival or event**, such as the Ottawa Bluesfest or the Winterlude festival
- Visit the **Diefenbunker**, a Cold War-era bunker turned museum
- Go on a **food tour** to sample Ottawa's diverse culinary scene, including its famous BeaverTails and poutine

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Gatineau Park**, a large park with hiking and biking trails, beaches, and scenic lookouts
- Explore the **Ottawa Greenbelt**, a network of parks and green spaces surrounding the city
- Go **hiking or biking** on the Ottawa River Pathway, a scenic path that runs along the river
- Visit the **Mer Bleue Bog**, a unique wetland ecosystem with boardwalks and hiking trails
- Go **skiing or snowshoeing** at Camp Fortune or Mont Ste-Marie, two nearby ski resorts

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Arts Centre**, a performing arts center with a variety of shows and concerts
- Explore the **Canadian Museum of Contemporary Photography**, a museum showcasing Canadian and international photography
- Attend a **performance** at the Great Canadian Theatre Company or the Ottawa Little Theatre
- Visit the **Shaw Centre**, a convention center with a variety of exhibitions and events
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Supreme Court of Canada**, a historic building with a unique architecture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Caribbean Market**, a small market selling Caribbean food and crafts
- Explore the **Wellington West** neighborhood, a charming area with unique shops and restaurants
- Go to the **Mayfair Theatre**, a historic theater showing independent and art-house films
- Visit the **Billings Estate Museum**, a historic house museum showcasing Ottawa's history
- Take a **stroll** through the **Lindenlea** neighborhood, a charming area with beautiful gardens and historic homes

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Montreal**, a city located 166 km east of Ottawa, with a travel time of approximately 2 hours
- Visit **Kingston**, a city located 180 km southwest of Ottawa, with a travel time of approximately 2.5 hours
- Explore the **Thousand Islands**, a scenic archipelago located 120 km south of Ottawa, with a travel time of approximately 2 hours
- Go to **Algonquin Provincial Park**, a large park located 220 km northwest of Ottawa, with a travel time of approximately 3 hours
- Visit the **Upper Canada Village**, a historic village located 120 km south of Ottawa, with a travel time of approximately 2 hours
