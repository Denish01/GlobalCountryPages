# Top Things to Do in Victoria — Attractions & Experiences

Victoria, the capital of British Columbia, Canada, is a must-visit destination with a plethora of exciting activities and attractions to explore, including the famous Butchart Gardens, the historic Fairmont Empress Hotel, and the vibrant Inner Harbour.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **whale watching** tour to spot orcas, humpback whales, and other marine life in their natural habitat
- Explore the **street art** scene in the **Fernwood** neighborhood, known for its vibrant murals and eclectic shops
- Visit the **Victoria Public Market**, a bustling marketplace featuring local produce, crafts, and food vendors
- Go on a **ghost tour** of the city, visiting haunted sites and learning about Victoria's spooky history
- Take a **brewery or winery tour**, sampling local craft beers and wines
- Attend a performance at the **Royal Theatre**, a historic venue hosting a variety of shows and concerts
- Visit the **Abkhazi Garden**, a stunning garden featuring a unique collection of plants and flowers

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Juan de Fuca Trail**, a scenic hiking trail along the west coast of Vancouver Island
- Visit **East Sooke Park**, a beautiful park featuring hiking trails, beaches, and stunning views
- Go **kayaking** or **paddleboarding** in the Inner Harbour or nearby lakes and rivers
- Take a **hiking** trip to the **Mount Work Regional Park**, featuring scenic trails and stunning views
- Visit the **Butchart Gardens** at night, when the gardens are illuminated with thousands of twinkling lights
- Explore the **Saanich Peninsula**, known for its scenic farmland, beaches, and wineries

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Royal British Columbia Museum**, featuring exhibits on the natural and human history of British Columbia
- Explore the **Art Gallery of Greater Victoria**, showcasing a diverse collection of local and international art
- Attend a performance at the **Belfry Theatre**, a professional theatre company producing a variety of shows
- Visit the **Chinese Temple**, a beautiful and historic temple featuring stunning architecture and gardens
- Take a **cooking class**, learning about local cuisine and cooking techniques
- Visit the **British Columbia Parliament Buildings**, a historic site featuring stunning architecture and guided tours

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Hatley Castle**, a historic mansion featuring stunning gardens and architecture
- Explore the **Ross Bay Cemetery**, a historic cemetery featuring the graves of many notable Victorians
- Take a **ferry ride** to **Salt Spring Island**, a charming island featuring art galleries, wineries, and scenic views
- Visit the **Murchison Gallery**, a unique gallery featuring a collection of local and international art
- Go **birdwatching** at the **Victoria Bird Sanctuary**, a protected area featuring a variety of bird species

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Sooke**, a charming town featuring scenic views, hiking trails, and a beautiful beach (45 km, 30 minutes)
- Visit **Cowichan Bay**, a picturesque town featuring art galleries, wineries, and a beautiful marina (60 km, 45 minutes)
- Explore **Sidney**, a charming town featuring bookstores, art galleries, and a beautiful waterfront (30 km, 20 minutes)
- Take a day trip to **Tofino**, a popular surf town featuring stunning beaches and scenic views (340 km, 4 hours)
- Visit **Salt Spring Island**, a charming island featuring art galleries, wineries, and scenic views (35 km, 1 hour by ferry)
