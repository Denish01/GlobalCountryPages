[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Forks Market Food Tour** and sample local cuisine and drinks
- Visit the **Winnipeg Farmers' Market** and shop for fresh produce, meats, and artisanal goods
- Explore the **Exchange District**, a National Historic Site featuring stunning turn-of-the-century architecture
- Attend a **Winnipeg Jets** hockey game at the Bell MTS Place
- Visit the **Canadian Grain Commission** and learn about the country's grain industry
- Take a **Riverwalk** tour and explore the city's scenic waterfront
- Experience the **Winnipeg Fringe Theatre Festival**, one of the largest fringe festivals in North America

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore **Assiniboine Forest**, a 287-hectare nature preserve featuring hiking trails and wildlife viewing opportunities
- Visit **Birds Hill Park**, a scenic park featuring hiking trails, beaches, and picnic areas
- Take a **River Boat Tour** and explore the city's waterways and scenic views
- Hike the **Trans Canada Trail**, a 27-kilometer trail that runs through the city and surrounding countryside
- Visit **FortWhyte Alive** and explore the park's nature trails, lakes, and wildlife viewing areas

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Manitoba Theatre for Young People** and watch a performance by the city's premier youth theater company
- Explore the **Plug In Institute of Contemporary Art**, a contemporary art gallery featuring local and international artists
- Attend a **Winnipeg Symphony Orchestra** performance at the Centennial Concert Hall
- Visit the **Ukrainian Cultural and Educational Centre** and learn about the city's Ukrainian heritage
- Take a **Historic Walking Tour** of the city's neighborhoods and explore its rich history and architecture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Dalnavert Museum**, a historic house museum featuring a collection of Victorian-era artifacts
- Explore the **Osborne Village**, a charming neighborhood featuring unique shops, restaurants, and bars
- Attend a **Winnipeg Comedy Festival** performance and laugh out loud with local and international comedians
- Visit the **Leo Mol Sculpture Garden**, a beautiful garden featuring over 300 sculptures by the famous artist
- Take a **Street Art Tour** and explore the city's vibrant street art scene

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Visit **Grand Beach**, a scenic beach town located about an hour north of Winnipeg (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Explore **Lower Fort Garry**, a historic fur trading post located about 30 minutes north of Winnipeg (distance: 30 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Take a **Whiteshell Provincial Park** tour and explore the park's scenic lakes, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing opportunities (distance: 120 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Visit **Gimli**, a charming town located on the shores of Lake Winnipeg (distance: 80 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Explore the **Interlake Region**, a scenic region featuring scenic lakes, beaches, and historic sites (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
