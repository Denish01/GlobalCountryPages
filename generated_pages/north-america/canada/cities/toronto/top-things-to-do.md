# Top Things to Do in Toronto — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Toronto, you should not miss the iconic **CN Tower**, the historic **Kensington Market**, and the world-renowned **Royal Ontario Museum**, which offer a glimpse into the city's rich history, cultural diversity, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **stroll along the Waterfront Trail**, which offers scenic views of Lake Ontario and the city skyline
- Visit the **Graffiti Alley** in the Fashion District, a vibrant and ever-changing street art scene
- Explore the **historic Distillery District**, with its cobblestone streets and converted warehouses now home to trendy bars, restaurants, and boutiques
- Attend a **Toronto Blue Jays game** at Rogers Centre, a state-of-the-art stadium with a retractable roof
- Visit the **Canada's Wonderland**, a popular amusement park with thrill rides and family-friendly attractions
- Go on a **food tour** of **Chinatown** or **Little Italy**, sampling the diverse culinary delights of these vibrant neighborhoods

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **High Park**, a 400-acre green space with walking trails, picnic areas, and a famous cherry blossom festival
- **Scarborough Bluffs**, a scenic park with hiking trails, beaches, and stunning views of Lake Ontario
- **Rouge National Urban Park**, a unique blend of urban and natural environments with hiking and biking trails
- **Toronto Harbour**, a bustling waterfront area with boat tours, kayaking, and paddleboarding
- **Don Valley Brick Works Park**, a former industrial site turned nature reserve with hiking trails and a beautiful garden

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Royal Ontario Museum**, with its extensive collections of art, culture, and natural history
- **Art Gallery of Ontario**, featuring a diverse collection of Canadian and international art
- **Ontario Science Centre**, with interactive exhibits and hands-on activities for all ages
- **St. Lawrence Hall**, a historic venue hosting cultural events, concerts, and performances
- **Koerner Hall**, a state-of-the-art concert hall with a diverse range of musical performances
- **Bata Shoe Museum**, a unique museum showcasing a vast collection of footwear from around the world

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Power Plant**, a contemporary art gallery located in the Harbourfront Centre
- **Gibson House Museum**, a historic house museum showcasing life in 19th-century Toronto
- **Riverdale Farm**, a working farm in the heart of the city, offering a glimpse into rural life
- **Allan Gardens**, a beautiful conservatory with a stunning collection of plants and flowers
- **Campbell House Museum**, a historic house museum featuring a unique collection of artifacts and exhibits

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **Niagara Falls**, a scenic drive of approximately 1.5 hours, with stunning natural beauty and thrilling attractions
2. **Prince Edward County**, a 2.5-hour drive, with its picturesque countryside, wineries, and scenic coastal routes
3. **Bruce Peninsula**, a 4-hour drive, with its stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and scenic vistas
4. **Ottawa**, a 4.5-hour drive, with its rich history, cultural attractions, and scenic canal system
5. **Stratford**, a 2-hour drive, with its charming small-town atmosphere, scenic riverwalk, and world-renowned **Stratford Festival**
