British Columbia offers a diverse range of activities and experiences, from exploring the beautiful Stanley Park in Vancouver to hiking through the stunning landscapes of Garibaldi Provincial Park, making it a must-visit destination for outdoor enthusiasts and culture lovers alike.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a grizzly bear tour in Knight Inlet to witness these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat
  • Go storm watching in Tofino and experience the power of the Pacific Ocean
  • Visit a First Nations community and learn about the history and culture of the indigenous people of British Columbia
  • Take a hot spring tour in the Canadian Rockies and relax in the natural hot springs
  • Go helicopter skiing in the Chilcotin Mountains for a truly unique and exhilarating experience
  • Attend a Vancouver Canucks hockey game at Rogers Arena for a lively and entertaining experience
  • Take a wine tour in the Okanagan Valley and sample some of the region's renowned wines

Outdoor & Nature

  • Hike the West Coast Trail in Pacific Rim National Park for a challenging and scenic hike
  • Visit Garibaldi Provincial Park for stunning mountain scenery and hiking trails
  • Explore the Great Bear Rainforest and witness the diverse wildlife and ecosystems of the region
  • Go surfing in Tofino and ride the waves of the Pacific Ocean
  • Take a whale watching tour in Victoria and see orcas, humpback whales, and other marine life

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Museum of Anthropology at the University of British Columbia to learn about the indigenous cultures of the region
  • Attend a Vancouver Symphony Orchestra performance at the Orpheum Theatre for a night of classical music
  • Explore the Vancouver Art Gallery and see a wide range of contemporary and historical art
  • Take a First Nations cultural tour in Victoria and learn about the history and traditions of the indigenous people
  • Visit the Fort Langley National Historic Site and learn about the history of the fur trade and the establishment of British Columbia

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Harrison Hot Springs and relax in the natural hot springs
  • Explore the Cortes Island and see the stunning beaches and forests of the region
  • Take a kayaking tour in Desolation Sound and paddle through the stunning fjords and islands
  • Visit the Barkerville Historic Town and learn about the history of the gold rush era
  • Go stargazing in the Kootenay National Park and see the stunning night sky

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip from Vancouver to Whistler (120 km, 2 hours) and enjoy the stunning mountain scenery and outdoor activities
  • Visit Victoria from Vancouver (60 km, 1.5 hours by ferry) and explore the city's historic sites and cultural attractions
  • Go on a day trip from Kelowna to Big White Ski Resort (56 km, 1 hour) and enjoy the skiing and snowboarding
  • Take a day trip from Tofino to Pacific Rim National Park (30 km, 30 minutes) and explore the stunning beaches and forests
  • Visit Revelstoke from Vancouver (400 km, 4.5 hours) and enjoy the stunning mountain scenery and outdoor activities

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries:

3-day (highlights): Visit Stanley Park, Granville Island, and Grouse Mountain in Vancouver, and take a day trip to Whistler.

7-day (comprehensive): Explore Vancouver, Victoria, and Tofino, and visit Pacific Rim National Park and Garibaldi Provincial Park.

14-day (deep exploration): Visit all the major cities and attractions, and take time to explore the Canadian Rockies, Kootenay National Park, and the Great Bear Rainforest.

Key tips: book accommodations and popular attractions in advance, prioritize outdoor activities during good weather, and consider purchasing a BC Parks Pass for access to multiple national and provincial parks.
