# Top Things to Do in Ontario — Attractions & Experiences

Ontario offers a diverse array of activities and attractions that cater to all interests, from the iconic **CN Tower** in Toronto to the breathtaking natural beauty of **Algonquin Provincial Park**.

| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |

| CN Tower | Toronto | Observation Tower | For panoramic views of the city and Lake Ontario |
| Niagara Falls | Niagara Falls | Natural Wonder | To witness the majestic power and beauty of the falls |
| Royal Ontario Museum | Toronto | Museum | To explore a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits from around the world |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dog sled tour** through the winter landscape of **Muskoka** for an exhilarating adventure.
- Visit the **Elgin and Winter Garden Theatres** in Toronto for a guided tour of the last remaining double-decker theater in the world.
- Explore the **1000 Islands** along the St. Lawrence River by boat to see the stunning scenery and historic lighthouses.
- Experience the vibrant **Kensington Market** in Toronto, known for its eclectic shops, restaurants, and street art.
- Attend a **Toronto Blue Jays** baseball game at Rogers Centre for a fun day of sports and entertainment.
- Visit the **Shaw Festival** in Niagara-on-the-Lake to enjoy theatrical performances of classic plays.
- Go on a **polar bear safari** in **Coats Island** to see these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Bruce Trail**, which runs along the Niagara Escarpment, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
- Visit **Algonquin Provincial Park** for camping, hiking, and wildlife viewing, including moose and beavers.
- Explore the **1000 Islands National Park** for its beautiful islands, beaches, and outdoor recreational activities.
- Go **skiing** at **Blue Mountain Resort** in Collingwood for world-class slopes and facilities.
- Relax on the beaches of **Wasaga Beach**, the world's longest freshwater beach.
- Take a **kayaking** trip through the **Georgian Bay** to explore its many islands and inlets.

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Canadian Museum of History** in Ottawa to learn about the country's rich history and culture.
- Attend a performance at the **National Arts Centre** in Ottawa, which features a variety of music, theater, and dance productions.
- Explore the **Art Gallery of Ontario** in Toronto, which has a diverse collection of Canadian and international art.
- Take a tour of the **Casa Loma** in Toronto, a majestic castle with opulent decorations and beautiful gardens.
- Visit the **McMichael Canadian Art Collection** in Kleinburg, which showcases a wide range of Canadian art, including works by the Group of Seven.
- Attend a **Toronto Symphony Orchestra** concert at the Roy Thomson Hall for a night of classical music.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Wye Marsh Wildlife Centre** in Midland to see a variety of wildlife, including birds, turtles, and beavers.
- Explore the **Gibraltar Point Lighthouse** on the Toronto Islands, which offers stunning views of the city skyline.
- Take a tour of the **Steam Whistle Brewery** in Toronto, which produces a unique pilsner beer.
- Visit the **Royal Botanical Gardens** in Burlington, which features a beautiful collection of plants and flowers.
- Go to the **St. Lawrence Market** in Toronto, which offers a wide variety of food vendors and shops selling local products.

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Toronto to **Niagara Falls**, which is about 1.5 hours away by car, to see the stunning natural wonder and enjoy the surrounding attractions.
- Visit **Prince Edward County** from Toronto, which is about 2.5 hours away by car, to explore its beautiful beaches, wineries, and scenic countryside.
- Go on a day trip from Ottawa to **Mont Tremblant**, which is about 2 hours away by car, to enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and skiing.
- Take a day trip from Toronto to **Stratford**, which is about 2 hours away by car, to attend a performance at the **Stratford Festival** and explore the charming town.
- Visit **Muskoka** from Toronto, which is about 2 hours away by car, to enjoy the beautiful lakes, forests, and outdoor recreational activities.
