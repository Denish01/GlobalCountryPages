[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Canadian National Exhibition** is an annual fair that showcases Canadian culture, food, and entertainment, attracting millions of visitors each year.
- The **Art Gallery of Ontario** is a prominent art museum featuring a diverse collection of Canadian and international art, including works by the **Group of Seven**.
- The **Toronto Symphony Orchestra** is a world-class orchestra that performs at the **Roy Thomson Hall**, offering a range of classical music concerts and educational programs.
- The **Kawartha Lakes** region is home to a rich **Indigenous heritage**, with many historic sites and cultural events celebrating the traditional ways of the **Anishinaabe** people.
- The **Ottawa Valley** is known for its vibrant **folk music scene**, with numerous festivals and events showcasing traditional Canadian music and dance.
