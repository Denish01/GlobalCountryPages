# Top Things to Do in Quebec — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Quebec, you should not miss the opportunity to explore its rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural beauty, which can be experienced through its numerous top attractions, unique experiences, and outdoor activities.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dog sled tour** through the Quebec countryside for a thrilling winter adventure
- Visit a **sugar shack** to experience the traditional Quebecois practice of maple syrup production
- Explore the **Underground City** in Montreal, a network of tunnels and shops connecting buildings
- Attend a **Festival d'Été de Québec** concert or event, one of the largest outdoor music festivals in the world
- Go on a **whale watching tour** in the Saint Lawrence River to spot beluga whales and other marine life
- Visit the **Ice Hotel** in Valcartier, a hotel made entirely of ice and snow
- Take a **French cooking class** to learn the art of traditional Quebecois cuisine

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Jacques-Cartier National Park**, offering hiking trails, waterfalls, and scenic views
- Visit the **Mont-Tremblant National Park**, a popular destination for skiing, hiking, and wildlife watching
- Hike the **Sentier de la Gaspésie**, a scenic trail that winds through the Gaspé Peninsula
- Relax on the beaches of **Oka National Park**, a beautiful park with hiking trails and scenic views
- Go **skiing** at one of Quebec's many resorts, such as Mont-Tremblant or Le Massif

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Musée des Beaux-Arts de Montréal**, one of the largest and most respected museums in Canada
- Explore the **Pointe-à-Callière Museum**, a museum of history and archaeology in Montreal
- Attend a performance at the **Théâtre du Nouveau Monde**, a historic theater in Montreal
- Visit the **Notre-Dame Basilica**, a beautiful and historic church in Montreal
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Historic District of Old Quebec**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Musée de la Civilization**, a museum in Quebec City that explores the history and culture of Quebec
- Explore the **Quartier Petit Champlain**, a charming neighborhood in Quebec City with cobblestone streets and historic buildings
- Go to the **Marché de Noël**, a Christmas market in Quebec City with local vendors and festive atmosphere
- Visit the **Jardin Jeanne d'Arc**, a beautiful garden in Quebec City with stunning views of the Saint Lawrence River
- Take a **ferry ride** to the **Île d'Orléans**, a scenic island in the Saint Lawrence River with historic sites and beautiful views

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Quebec City to **Montmorency Falls** (30 minutes, 15 km) to hike and explore the surrounding area
- Visit **Mont-Tremblant** (2 hours, 130 km) from Montreal for a day of skiing or hiking
- Explore the **Eastern Townships** (2 hours, 120 km) from Montreal, a scenic region with charming towns and picturesque countryside
- Go to **Tadoussac** (3 hours, 200 km) from Quebec City, a small town at the confluence of the Saint Lawrence and Saguenay rivers, known for whale watching and scenic views
- Visit **Oka National Park** (1 hour, 60 km) from Montreal, a beautiful park with hiking trails, beaches, and scenic views
