# Cost of Living in Manitoba - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Manitoba is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $60-120 USD per day for backpackers, $120-250 USD per day for mid-range travelers, and $250-400 USD per day for those seeking a more comfortable experience.
