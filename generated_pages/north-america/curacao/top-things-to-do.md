# Top Things to Do in Curacao — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Curacao, be sure to explore the island's rich history, stunning natural beauty, and unique cultural experiences, which offer a mix of Dutch, African, and indigenous influences that set it apart from other Caribbean destinations.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dolphin-watching tour** in the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean Sea
- Visit the **Aloe Vera Plantation** and learn about the production of aloe vera products
- Explore the **Curacao Sea Aquarium** and see the diverse marine life of the island
- Go on a **rum-tasting tour** and sample the local rum at the Landhuis Chobolobo distillery
- Take a **Papiamento language class** and learn about the unique culture of the island
- Visit the **Curacao Ostrich Farm** and see the largest birds in the world up close
- Go on a **snorkeling or scuba diving trip** to explore the coral reefs and marine life of the island

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Christoffel National Park** and hike to the highest point on the island
- Explore the **Shete Boka National Park** and see the unique limestone formations and blowholes
- Relax on the beach at **Playa Kenepa** and enjoy the crystal-clear waters
- Go on a **hiking trip** through the **Rooi Katje** area and see the unique flora and fauna of the island
- Visit the **Curacao National Marine Park** and see the diverse marine life of the island

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museum Kura Hulanda** and learn about the history and culture of the island
- Explore the **Fort Amsterdam** and learn about the island's history and significance
- Attend a **local festival** or event and experience the vibrant culture of the island
- Visit the **National Archaeological Museum** and see artifacts from the indigenous people
- Take a **cooking class** and learn about the unique cuisine of the island
- Visit the **Landhuis Chobolobo** and learn about the production of rum and other local products

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ascension Hill** and see the unique rock formations and stunning views
- Explore the **Bullenbaai** area and see the unique flora and fauna of the island
- Relax on the beach at **Playa Lagun** and enjoy the crystal-clear waters
- Visit the **Curacao Liqueur Distillery** and sample the local liqueurs
- Go on a **bike tour** through the **Seroe Colorado** area and see the unique landscape and flora of the island

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Klein Curacao** and see the unique wildlife and stunning beaches of the island
- Visit the **Christoffel National Park** and hike to the highest point on the island
- Explore the **Shete Boka National Park** and see the unique limestone formations and blowholes
- Go on a **snorkeling or scuba diving trip** to the **Mushroom Forest** and see the diverse marine life of the island
- Take a day trip to **Willemstad** and explore the historic city center and visit the **Fort Amsterdam** and **Museum Kura Hulanda**
