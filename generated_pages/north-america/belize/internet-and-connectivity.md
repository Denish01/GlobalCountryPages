# Belize Internet & SIM Cards — Connectivity Guide

In Belize, you can expect a relatively good internet experience with average speeds and widespread mobile network coverage, making it easy to stay connected during your trip. 

[FACTBOX]
Average Internet Speed: 10 Mbps download
Mobile Network Coverage: Good
Main Carriers: Digicel, Smart, BTL
SIM Card Cost: 20 USD for tourist SIM
WiFi Availability: Common
5G Available: No
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] Internet Overview [/SECTION]
The internet in Belize is generally of good quality, with **average internet speeds** of 10 Mbps download, allowing for smooth browsing, emailing, and social media usage. However, there is a noticeable **urban vs rural divide**, with faster and more reliable internet connections available in urban areas like Belmopan and Belize City, while rural areas may experience slower speeds and more frequent outages. The **reliability of the internet** in Belize is also affected by the weather, with heavy rainfall and storms sometimes causing disruptions to internet services.

In terms of **infrastructure**, Belize has a relatively well-developed internet infrastructure, with a mix of **fiber-optic cables**, **cellular networks**, and **satellite connections** providing internet access to most parts of the country. However, the **cost of internet services** in Belize can be relatively high, especially for high-speed connections, which may be a challenge for some users. Despite these challenges, the internet has become an essential tool for many Belizeans, with **online services** and **e-commerce** becoming increasingly popular in recent years.

[SECTION] Getting a SIM Card [/SECTION]
To get a SIM card in Belize, you can visit one of the main carriers' stores, such as **Digicel**, **Smart**, or **BTL**, and purchase a tourist SIM card for around 20 USD. You will need to provide a valid **passport** and fill out a registration form to activate your SIM card. The recommended carriers for tourists are Digicel and Smart, which offer a range of **data plans** and **prepaid options**. It's also a good idea to check with your hotel or accommodation provider to see if they offer any **SIM card packages** or **internet promotions**.

[SECTION] WiFi Availability [/SECTION]
- Hotel WiFi quality in Belize is generally good, with many hotels and resorts offering **free WiFi** to their guests.
- Cafe WiFi culture is also popular in Belize, with many cafes and restaurants offering **free WiFi** to customers.
- Coworking spaces are available in major cities like Belize City and Belmopan, offering **high-speed internet** and other amenities.
- Public WiFi is available in some areas, such as **parks** and **public squares**, but the quality and reliability can vary.
- Many **tourist information centers** and **visitor centers** also offer **free WiFi** to tourists.

[SECTION] For Remote Workers [/SECTION]
- Coworking spaces like **Belize Coworking** and **The Hub** offer **high-speed internet**, **meeting rooms**, and other amenities for remote workers.
- Reliable cafe chains like **The Coffee Shop** and **Cafe Belize** offer **free WiFi** and a comfortable working environment.
- Backup internet options like **mobile hotspots** and **portable Wi-Fi routers** are available for purchase or rent.
- Some **co-living spaces** and **hostels** also offer **shared office spaces** and **high-speed internet** for remote workers.
- Using a **VPN** is recommended to ensure **online security** and **privacy** when working remotely in Belize.

[SECTION] Tips & Warnings [/SECTION]
- Internet **censorship** is not a major concern in Belize, but some **online services** may be blocked due to **copyright laws**.
- Using a **VPN** is recommended to access **geo-restricted content** and ensure **online security**.
- **Roaming alternatives** like **eSIMs** and **portable Wi-Fi routers** can be a cost-effective option for tourists.
- **Public WiFi** should be used with caution, as it can be **unsecured** and **vulnerable to hacking**.
- **Digicel** and **Smart** offer **international roaming** services, but the **rates** can be high, so it's recommended to check with your carrier before traveling.
- It's also a good idea to **check your device** for **compatibility** with Belize's **mobile networks** before purchasing a SIM card or using a **mobile hotspot**.
