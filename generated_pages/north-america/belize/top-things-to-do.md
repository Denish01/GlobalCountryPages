[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Garifuna drumming lesson** in the southern town of Dangriga to learn about the unique culture and history of the Garifuna people
- Go on a **manatee-watching tour** in the Placencia Lagoon to spot these gentle creatures in their natural habitat
- Explore the **Caves Branch River** on an inner tube or kayak to see the stunning rock formations and wildlife
- Visit a **Mayan village** to learn about traditional farming practices and daily life
- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to make traditional Belizean dishes such as rice and beans, stew chicken, and hudut
- Go on a **bioluminescent tour** in the nighttime to see the magical glow of the marine life in the waters of Belize

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Glover's Reef Atoll** for snorkeling and diving in the beautiful coral reefs
- **Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary** for hiking and spotting jaguars, ocelots, and other wildlife
- **Blue Creek** for swimming and hiking in the stunning natural surroundings
- **Victoria Peak** for hiking and taking in the breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside
- **Bacalar Chico National Park** for snorkeling, diving, and spotting dolphins and manatees

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Museum of Belize** in Belize City to learn about the history and culture of Belize
- **San Ignacio Market** to try local food and shop for handicrafts
- **Belize City House of Culture** to learn about the history and architecture of Belize
- **Lubaantun** to explore the ancient Mayan ruins and stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- **Garifuna Settlement** in Dangriga to learn about the history and culture of the Garifuna people

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Hopkins Village** for a relaxing beach vacation and chance to experience the local Garifuna culture
- **Turneffe Atoll** for snorkeling and diving in the beautiful coral reefs
- **Crooked Tree Wildlife Sanctuary** for spotting birds and other wildlife in the stunning natural surroundings
- **Tobacco Caye** for a secluded island getaway and chance to snorkel and dive in the beautiful waters

3. Take a day trip from **Placencia** to **Monkey River** (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 1 hour, highlights: wildlife spotting, stunning natural surroundings)
