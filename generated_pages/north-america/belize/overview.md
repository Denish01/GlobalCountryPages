[SECTION] What Is Belize? [/SECTION]
Belize is a **sovereign** country located in **North America**, bordered by **Mexico** to the north and **Guatemala** to the south and west. It has a diverse geography, with a long coastline along the **Caribbean Sea** and a mountainous interior. Belize is known for its rich cultural heritage, including a mix of **Mayan**, **British**, and **African** influences. The country has a relatively small population, with a mix of urban and rural communities.

Belize is a popular tourist destination, with a strong focus on **ecotourism** and **sustainable development**. The country is home to a wide range of flora and fauna, including **coral reefs**, **rainforests**, and **wildlife reserves**. Visitors can explore the country's many **islands**, **cayes**, and **atolls**, or experience the vibrant culture of its cities and towns. Belize is also a key player in regional organizations, including the **Central American Integration System** and the **Caribbean Community**.

The country's strategic location and natural resources have made it an important location for trade and commerce. Belize has a growing economy, with a mix of **agriculture**, **tourism**, and **manufacturing**. The country is also rich in natural resources, including **oil**, **gas**, and **minerals**. With its unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty, Belize is a fascinating country to explore.
