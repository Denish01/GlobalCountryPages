# Top Things to Do in Saint Kitts and Nevis — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Saint Kitts and Nevis, visit the Brimstone Hill Fortress, explore the scenic coastal roads, and indulge in the local cuisine, which showcases the unique blend of Caribbean, British, and African influences that define this twin-island nation.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **sugar plantation tour** to learn about the history of sugar production in Saint Kitts and Nevis
- Go **dolphin watching** in the waters around the islands, a unique and unforgettable experience
- Explore the **hot springs** on Nevis, a natural wonder with therapeutic properties
- Visit a **local market** to experience the vibrant culture and try some of the local cuisine

- Go **deep-sea fishing** in the waters around Saint Kitts and Nevis, known for their rich marine life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Nevis Peak National Park**, a protected area with stunning views and diverse wildlife
- Visit the **Belle Toute** beach on Saint Kitts, known for its picturesque scenery and tranquil atmosphere
- Take a **hike** through the **Ottley's Plantation** trail, a scenic route with stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Go **snorkeling** or **scuba diving** in the waters around the islands, known for their rich marine life and coral reefs
- Visit the **Lavallee Village** on Nevis, a picturesque village surrounded by lush greenery and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]

- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the local cuisine and try some of the traditional dishes

- Visit the **Fairview Great House and Botanical Gardens**, a beautifully restored great house with stunning gardens and views
- Learn about the **history of the sugar industry** at the Saint Kitts Sugar Factory, a significant historical landmark
