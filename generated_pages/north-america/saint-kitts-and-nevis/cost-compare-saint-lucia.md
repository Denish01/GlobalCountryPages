[SECTION] Overview: Saint Kitts and Nevis vs Saint Lucia [/SECTION]
The cost of living in Saint Kitts and Nevis is higher compared to Saint Lucia, mainly due to the former's stronger economy and higher standard of living. Saint Kitts and Nevis has a more developed tourism industry, which drives up prices for accommodations, food, and activities. On the other hand, Saint Lucia has a more laid-back atmosphere and a lower cost of living, making it an attractive option for expats and budget travelers. The overall cost of living difference between the two countries is around 15-20%, with Saint Lucia being the more affordable option.

The economic context of both countries also plays a significant role in determining the cost of living. Saint Kitts and Nevis has a more diversified economy, with a strong focus on tourism, finance, and manufacturing, which contributes to its higher cost of living. In contrast, Saint Lucia's economy is more reliant on agriculture and tourism, resulting in lower prices for goods and services. Despite the difference in cost of living, both countries offer a unique Caribbean experience, with stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and friendly locals.
