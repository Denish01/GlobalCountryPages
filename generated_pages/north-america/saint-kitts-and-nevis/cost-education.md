The cost of education in Saint Kitts and Nevis varies depending on the type of institution, with public schools being generally free or subsidized, while private and international schools can range from $2,000 to $10,000 per year.

The education system in Saint Kitts and Nevis is based on the British model, with **English** being the primary language of instruction.
