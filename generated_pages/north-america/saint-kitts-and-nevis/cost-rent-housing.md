# Rent & Housing Prices in Saint Kitts and Nevis

The cost of rent in Saint Kitts and Nevis can range from $400 to $1,200 USD per month for a one-bedroom apartment, depending on the location and quality of the accommodation.

[SECTION] Housing Market Overview [/SECTION]
The rental market in Saint Kitts and Nevis is relatively small, with a mix of apartments, houses, and villas available for rent. Typical lease terms range from 6 to 12 months, with a security deposit equivalent to one to two months' rent. Tenant rights are generally protected under the law, with provisions for fair rent, maintenance, and eviction procedures. Compared to regional neighbors, the housing costs in Saint Kitts and Nevis are relatively moderate, with prices similar to those in Antigua and Barbuda. However, the cost of living in Saint Kitts and Nevis is generally higher due to its smaller size and limited economy.

The housing market in Saint Kitts and Nevis is influenced by the tourism industry, with many properties being rented out to tourists and expats. This has driven up prices in popular areas, particularly in Basseterre and the surrounding coastal regions. However, there are still affordable options available, especially in the suburbs and rural areas. The government has implemented measures to control rent prices and protect tenants, but the market remains relatively unregulated. As a result, renters should be prepared to negotiate and carefully review their lease agreements before signing.
