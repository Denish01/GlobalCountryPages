Cultural Treasures

  • The Saint Kitts Carnival, also known as Sugar Mas, is a vibrant celebration of music, dance, and culture that takes place every year.
  • The Museum of Nevis History is a museum that showcases the history and culture of Nevis, with exhibits on the island's colonial past and its natural environment.
  • The Saint Kitts and Nevis Folklore is a rich and diverse tradition that includes stories, songs, and dances that have been passed down through generations.
  • The Nevisian Culture is a unique blend of African, European, and indigenous influences, reflected in the island's music, art, and traditions.
