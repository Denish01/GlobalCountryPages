Sint Maarten has a relatively small population, with approximately 41,349 people living on the island. The island has a high population density, with an average of 1,214 people per square kilometer. The population is expected to continue growing, albeit at a slow rate, due to a combination of natural increase and migration.

The population of Sint Maarten is characterized by a mix of indigenous, European, African, and Asian influences, reflecting the island's complex history and cultural heritage. The population is also relatively young, with a median age of 33.4 years.

The population of Sint Maarten is also influenced by migration patterns, with many people moving to the island for work or to join family members. The island has a relatively high level of migration, with many people coming from other Caribbean islands, as well as from Europe and North America.
