- The **Philipsburg Courthouse** is a historic landmark and a symbol of the island's Dutch heritage, featuring a mix of colonial and modern architectural styles.
- The **Sint Maarten Carnival Village** is a vibrant cultural hub, hosting music and dance performances, food festivals, and crafts exhibitions.
- The **Guavaberry Emporium** is a unique shop and cultural center, dedicated to the island's national fruit, the guavaberry, and its various products and traditions.
- The **Sint Maarten National Heritage Festival** celebrates the island's history, culture, and traditions, with music, dance, and food performances.
