[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure in Sint Maarten is generally well-developed, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and telecommunications services. However, costs can fluctuate depending on usage and seasonal variation, with higher demands during the peak tourist season from December to April. The **GDP per capita PPP** of $53,769 indicates a high standard of living, which can contribute to relatively higher utility costs.

The cost of utilities in Sint Maarten can be affected by various factors, including the size of the apartment, usage patterns, and the service providers chosen. It is essential to research and compare prices among different service providers to find the best deals and plans that suit individual needs.
