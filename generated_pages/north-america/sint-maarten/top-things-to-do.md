# Top Things to Do in Sint Maarten — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **sunset sailing trip** around the island to enjoy stunning views and snorkeling opportunities
- Go **zip lining** through the lush forests of Sint Maarten for an adrenaline-packed adventure
- Explore the island's **underwater world** with a guided **scuba diving** or **snorkeling** tour
- Visit the **Sint Maarten Zoo** to see a variety of animals, including monkeys, birds, and reptiles
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the island's unique cuisine and cooking techniques
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the island's scenic trails and beaches
- Explore the **island's rum distilleries** and learn about the production process

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike to the top of **Pic Paradis**, the island's highest point, for stunning views
- Explore the **Mullet Bay Pond**, a protected area for birds and other wildlife
- Visit the **Seaside Nature Park** for hiking, bird-watching, and scenic views
- Relax on **Simpson Bay Beach**, a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing
- Go **kayaking** or **paddleboarding** through the island's calm waters

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sint Maarten Museum** to learn about the island's history and culture
- Explore the **Philipsburg Courthouse**, a historic site with a museum and cultural center
- Attend a **local festival**, such as the Sint Maarten Carnival, to experience the island's vibrant culture
- Take a **dance class**, such as salsa or merengue, to learn about the island's music and dance traditions
- Visit the **Guavaberry Emporium**, a local shop selling traditional crafts and souvenirs

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Belair Beach**, a secluded spot with crystal-clear waters and stunning views
- Explore the **Sandy Ground**, a small village with a beautiful beach and scenic views
- Go to the **Cole Bay**, a quiet area with a beautiful beach and opportunities for water sports
- Take a **boat tour** to the nearby **Anguilla**, a small island with stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip to **Anguilla**, a small island with stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters (distance: 10 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
2. Visit **Saint Martin**, the French side of the island, to explore the capital city of Marigot and the scenic **Orient Bay** (distance: 15 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
3. Go to **Saba**, a small island with stunning natural beauty and opportunities for hiking and snorkeling (distance: 25 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
4. Take a day trip to **St. Eustatius**, a small island with a rich history and stunning natural beauty (distance: 30 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours)
