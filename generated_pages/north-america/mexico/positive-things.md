[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The ancient Mayan city of **Chichen Itza** is one of the most famous and mysterious archaeological sites in Mexico, and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- The **Day of the Dead** (Día de los Muertos) is a unique and colorful tradition that honors the deceased, and is now recognized by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity.
- The **Mexican muralism** art movement, led by artists such as Diego Rivera and Jose Clemente Orozco, is a testament to the country's rich cultural heritage and artistic expression.
- The **Historic Center of Mexico City** is a vibrant and culturally rich area that showcases the country's complex history, with a mix of Aztec ruins, colonial architecture, and modern landmarks.
- The ** mariachi music** is a traditional and iconic form of Mexican music, known for its lively rhythms and soulful melodies.
