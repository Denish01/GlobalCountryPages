Unique Experiences

  • Take a tequila tasting tour in the town of Tequila, the birthplace of Mexico's national spirit
  • Explore the charming town of San Pedro Tlaquepaque, known for its vibrant arts scene and traditional crafts
  • Visit a charro ranch to experience the traditional Mexican cowboy culture and horseback riding
  • Attend a lucha libre match in Guadalajara, a unique and exciting form of Mexican wrestling
  • Learn about the indigenous Wixarika culture and traditions at a local community center
  • Take a hot air balloon ride over the scenic countryside of Jalisco
  • Visit the vintage car museum in Guadalajara, featuring a collection of classic cars from around the world
  • Explore the historic center of Zapopan, a charming town with a rich history and cultural significance

Outdoor & Nature

  • Sierra de Quila national park, offering hiking, camping, and stunning natural scenery
  • Lake Chapala, perfect for boating, fishing, and relaxation
  • Puerto Vallarta beaches, such as Playa Olas Altas and Playa los Muertos
  • Sierra de Tapalpa, a scenic mountain range offering hiking, horseback riding, and stunning views
  • Cuale River, a scenic river that runs through the city of Puerto Vallarta and offers boat tours and water sports
  • Bosque de la Primavera, a forest reserve with hiking trails, waterfalls, and stunning natural beauty

  • Puerto Vallarta to Yelapa (30 km, 1 hour by boat), visit the charming coastal town of Yelapa and enjoy its stunning beaches and scenery

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (explore Guadalajara's historic center and visit the nearby town of Tlaquepaque), 7-day (visit the cities of Guadalajara, Puerto Vallarta, and Tapalpa, and explore the surrounding countryside), 14-day (deep exploration of Jalisco's culture, history, and natural beauty, including visits to the Sierra de Quila national park and the town of San Pedro Tlaquepaque). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and tours in advance, especially during peak travel seasons, and prioritize visits to popular attractions and experiences to avoid crowds and long wait times.
