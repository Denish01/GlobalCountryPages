[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Historic Center of Guadalajara** is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring stunning colonial architecture and historic landmarks like the Hospicio Cabañas.
- The **Tequila Trail** is a popular tourist route that takes visitors through the picturesque towns and distilleries of the tequila-producing region.
- The **Mariachi Festival** is an annual event that celebrates the region's rich musical heritage, featuring performances by local and international mariachi bands.
- The **Danza de los Viejitos** is a traditional folk dance that is an important part of Jalisco's cultural identity, performed during festivals and celebrations.
- The **Huichol Art** is a unique and colorful art form that is characteristic of the indigenous Huichol people, who live in the Sierra Madre mountains of Jalisco.
