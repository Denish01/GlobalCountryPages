Oaxaca's strategic location allows it to connect with the rest of Mexico and Central America, making it an important hub for trade and cultural exchange. The region's borders are also home to several important cities, including the capital city of Oaxaca de Juárez, which is a major cultural and economic center.

The region's location in the southeastern part of Mexico also makes it a key player in the country's **biodiversity**, with several important ecosystems, including tropical rainforests, dry forests, and coastal wetlands.
