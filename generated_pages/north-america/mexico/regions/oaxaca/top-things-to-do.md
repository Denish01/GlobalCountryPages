# Top Things to Do in Oaxaca — Attractions & Experiences

Oaxaca, a region in southern Mexico, is a treasure trove of rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and exciting experiences, offering a wide range of activities and attractions that cater to diverse interests and preferences.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional cooking class** to learn about Oaxacan cuisine and prepare local dishes like mole and tlayudas
- Participate in a **temazcal ceremony**, a pre-Hispanic ritual for purification and healing
- Visit a **mezcal distillery** to learn about the production process and sample different types of mezcal
- Explore the **Mercado de Artesanías**, a market dedicated to local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a **Guelaguetza**, a traditional Oaxacan celebration with music, dance, and food
- Learn about **natural dyeing** and **weaving** techniques at a local textile workshop
- Go on a **shamanic journey** with a local guide to explore the region's spiritual heritage

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike in the **Sierra Norte Mountains**, a stunning range with scenic valleys and traditional villages
- Visit the **Lagunas de Chacahua National Park**, a protected area with lagoons, mangroves, and diverse wildlife
- Explore the **Hierve el Agua** natural springs, a scenic spot with mineral water and stunning views
- Relax on the beaches of **Puerto Escondido**, a coastal town known for its vibrant nightlife and water sports
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Valle de Tule**, a region with a wide range of bird species and scenic landscapes

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo de las Culturas de Oaxaca**, a museum showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage
- Explore the **Templo de Santo Domingo de Guzmán**, a stunning church with intricate architecture and historic significance
- Attend a **performance** at the **Teatro Juárez**, a historic theater with a variety of cultural events
- Learn about **traditional pottery** at a local workshop in **Atzompa**
- Visit the **Centro de las Artes de San Agustín**, a cultural center with exhibitions, workshops, and performances

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Cueva del Molino**, a scenic cave with stunning rock formations and hiking trails
- Explore the **Pueblo Magico de Teotitlán del Valle**, a traditional village with vibrant culture and stunning scenery
- Go **hiking** in the **Cerro del Fortín**, a scenic hill with stunning views of Oaxaca City
- Relax in the **Baños de Zoogocho**, a natural hot spring with scenic surroundings and therapeutic properties
- Discover the **Museo de la Filatelia**, a unique museum dedicated to the history of philately

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Mitla** (45 km, 1 hour), a town with ancient Zapotec and Mixtec ruins and stunning mosaics
- Visit **Teotitlán del Valle** (30 km, 45 minutes), a traditional village with vibrant culture and stunning scenery
- Explore **Puerto Escondido** (230 km, 4 hours), a coastal town with beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife
- Go to **Hierve el Agua** (70 km, 2 hours), a natural spring with stunning scenery and mineral water
- Visit **Tule** (15 km, 30 minutes), a town with a ancient, gigantic cypress tree and scenic surroundings
