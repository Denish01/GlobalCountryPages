# Cost of Living in Oaxaca - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Oaxaca is generally considered to be affordable, with a daily budget of $30-50 USD per day for backpackers, making it an attractive destination for travelers and expats alike.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-50 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $50-80 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $80-120 USD
Currency: MXN
Tipping Custom: expected (10-15% in restaurants and bars)
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Oaxaca? [/SECTION]
Oaxaca is a region in southern Mexico known for its rich indigenous culture, delicious cuisine, and stunning natural beauty. Compared to other major cities in Mexico, Oaxaca is relatively affordable, with a cost of living that is lower than in cities like Mexico City or Cancun. However, prices can vary depending on the location, with the city of Oaxaca being more expensive than the surrounding rural areas. Overall, Oaxaca offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for travelers and expats.

In terms of global averages, Oaxaca is generally cheaper than many popular tourist destinations in North America and Europe. The cost of accommodation, food, and transportation is relatively low, making it an excellent choice for budget-conscious travelers. However, prices can increase during peak travel seasons, so it's essential to plan ahead and book accommodations and tours in advance.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel to save on accommodation costs
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls to experience authentic cuisine at a lower price
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to save on transport costs
- Avoid traveling during peak seasons (December to April) to save on accommodation and tour prices
- Learn some basic Spanish phrases to negotiate prices and interact with locals
- Consider purchasing a local SIM card or portable Wi-Fi hotspot to save on data costs

Overall, Oaxaca offers a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty at an affordable price, making it an excellent destination for travelers and expats looking to experience the best of Mexico without breaking the bank.
