[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Tulum National Park** is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most visited archaeological sites in Mexico, known for its stunning coastal scenery and well-preserved Mayan ruins.
- The **Coba Ruins** are a significant cultural treasure, featuring the **Nohoch Mul** pyramid, which is the tallest pyramid in the Yucatan Peninsula.
- The **Chichen Itza** archaeological site is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous Mayan sites in Mexico, known for its **El Castillo** pyramid and the **Ball Court**.
- The **Festival of Life and Death** is a traditional celebration in the town of Xcaret, which honors the Mayan ancestors and features music, dance, and cultural performances.
- The **Mayan Art** is a unique and vibrant art form that reflects the rich cultural heritage of the Mayan civilization, characterized by intricate ceramics, textiles, and sculptures.
