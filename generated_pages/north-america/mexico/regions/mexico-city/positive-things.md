[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Palacio de Bellas Artes** is a stunning example of Art Nouveau architecture and a major cultural center, hosting various exhibitions and performances throughout the year.
- The **Day of the Dead** is a traditional Mexican holiday that is celebrated in Mexico City with parades, altars, and other cultural events.
- The **Mexican National Museum of Anthropology** is one of the most important museums in the country, showcasing a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits on Mexico's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Zócalo** is the main square in the city and a historic landmark, surrounded by important buildings, including the **Cathedral** and the **Government Palace**.
- The ** Floating Gardens of Xochimilco** is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular tourist destination, offering a unique and relaxing experience.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
