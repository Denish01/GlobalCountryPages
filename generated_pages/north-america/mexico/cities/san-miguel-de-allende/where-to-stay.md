# Where to Stay in San Miguel de Allende — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

The best areas to stay in San Miguel de Allende are the historic center, Guadalupe, and Atotonilco, which offer a mix of colonial architecture, vibrant cultural scene, and easy access to amenities and attractions.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, here's what you can expect:
**Centro Historico** (San Miguel de Allende)
Best For: Luxury / Couples
Vibe: The historic center is the heart of San Miguel de Allende, with cobblestone streets, picturesque plazas, and stunning colonial architecture, offering a romantic and sophisticated atmosphere. The area is filled with upscale shops, restaurants, and bars, making it perfect for those looking for a luxurious experience.
Price Range: $150-$300/night
Pros: Central location, historic charm, high-end amenities
Cons: Can be noisy, limited parking options

**Guadalupe** (San Miguel de Allende)
Best For: Families / Backpackers
Vibe: Guadalupe is a charming neighborhood located just outside the historic center, offering a more laid-back and affordable atmosphere, with a mix of local shops, restaurants, and bars. The area is popular with families and backpackers, who appreciate its relaxed vibe and easy access to amenities.
Price Range: $30-$100/night
Pros: Affordable, local atmosphere, easy access to public transportation
Cons: Limited nightlife options, can be a bit far from the historic center

**Atotonilco** (San Miguel de Allende)
Best For: Luxury / Wellness
Vibe: Atotonilco is a picturesque neighborhood located just outside San Miguel de Allende, known for its natural hot springs, spas, and wellness retreats. The area offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere, perfect for those looking to relax and rejuvenate.
Price Range: $100-$250/night
Pros: Natural hot springs, spas, and wellness retreats, peaceful atmosphere
Cons: Limited dining options, can be a bit far from the historic center

**San Antonio** (San Miguel de Allende)
Best For: Families / Business
Vibe: San Antonio is a residential neighborhood located just outside the historic center, offering a more subdued and family-friendly atmosphere. The area is popular with families and business travelers, who appreciate its easy access to amenities and quiet streets.
Price Range: $80-$200/night
Pros: Family-friendly, easy access to amenities, quiet streets
Cons: Limited nightlife options, can be a bit far from the historic center

**Allende** (San Miguel de Allende)
Best For: Backpackers / Budget travelers
Vibe: Allende is a budget-friendly neighborhood located near the historic center, offering a lively and social atmosphere, with a mix of hostels, bars, and restaurants. The area is popular with backpackers and budget travelers, who appreciate its affordable prices and vibrant nightlife.
Price Range: $20-$50/night
Pros: Affordable, social atmosphere, easy access to public transportation
Cons: Can be noisy, limited amenities
