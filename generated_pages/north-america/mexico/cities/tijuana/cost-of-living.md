# Cost of Living in Tijuana - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Tijuana can vary depending on lifestyle and preferences, but overall, it is considered an affordable city with a daily budget range of $20-50 USD for backpackers, $50-100 USD for mid-range travelers, and $100-200 USD for comfort seekers. 

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $20-50 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $50-100 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $100-200 USD
Currency: MXN
Tipping Custom: 10-15% expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Tijuana? [/SECTION]
Tijuana is a major city in Mexico, located on the US-Mexico border, and its cost of living is relatively low compared to other major cities in North America. The city offers a unique blend of Mexican and American cultures, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike. Compared to its neighboring cities in the United States, Tijuana is significantly more affordable, with lower prices for accommodation, food, and transportation. However, compared to other cities in Mexico, Tijuana is slightly more expensive due to its proximity to the US border and the resulting higher demand for goods and services.

Overall, Tijuana is an excellent choice for travelers and expats looking for an affordable and culturally rich experience. The city's affordability, combined with its vibrant cultural scene and proximity to the US, make it an attractive destination for people from all over the world. With a little planning and research, visitors can enjoy all that Tijuana has to offer without breaking the bank.
