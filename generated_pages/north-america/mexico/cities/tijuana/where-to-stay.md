# Where to Stay in Tijuana — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Tijuana, the best areas to stay are those that offer a mix of safety, accessibility, and amenities, with top options including the Zona Centro, Playas de Tijuana, and Zona Río neighborhoods. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, consider the following:
**Zona Centro** (Tijuana)
Best For: Backpackers / Budget travelers
Vibe: Zona Centro is the heart of Tijuana, with a vibrant atmosphere and a wide range of dining and entertainment options. The area is lively and energetic, with a mix of old and new buildings.
Price Range: $20-$50/night
Pros: Central location, easy access to public transportation, and a variety of affordable accommodation options. The area is also home to many restaurants, bars, and shops.
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, especially at night.

**Playas de Tijuana** (Tijuana)
Best For: Families / Beach lovers
Vibe: Playas de Tijuana is a coastal neighborhood with a relaxed and laid-back atmosphere, perfect for those looking to enjoy the beach and outdoor activities. The area is known for its beautiful beaches, parks, and scenic views.
Price Range: $30-$70/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, scenic views, and a range of outdoor activities such as surfing and hiking. The area is also home to many restaurants and shops.
Cons: Can be windy and cool in the winter months.

**Zona Río** (Tijuana)
Best For: Business / Luxury travelers
Vibe: Zona Río is a upscale neighborhood with a modern and sophisticated atmosphere, known for its high-end shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment options. The area is clean and safe, with a mix of modern and traditional architecture.
Price Range: $50-$100/night
Pros: Modern and safe, with easy access to shopping and entertainment options. The area is also home to many restaurants and bars.
Cons: Can be expensive, and traffic in the area can be heavy.

**La Mesa** (Tijuana)
Best For: Families / Mid-range travelers
Vibe: La Mesa is a residential neighborhood with a quiet and peaceful atmosphere, known for its parks, restaurants, and shopping centers. The area is family-friendly and safe, with a mix of modern and traditional housing.
Price Range: $25-$50/night
Pros: Quiet and peaceful, with easy access to parks and outdoor activities. The area is also home to many restaurants and shops.
Cons: Can be far from the city center, and public transportation options may be limited.

**Avenida Revolución** (Tijuana)
Best For: Backpackers / Partygoers
Vibe: Avenida Revolución is a lively and energetic neighborhood, known for its bars, clubs, and entertainment options. The area is perfect for those looking to experience Tijuana's nightlife.
Price Range: $20-$40/night
Pros: Lively and energetic, with easy access to bars, clubs, and entertainment options. The area is also home to many restaurants and shops.
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, especially at night.
