# Cost of Living in Guadalajara - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Guadalajara is generally affordable, with a daily budget of $30-60 USD per person for a mid-range experience, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider living outside the city center to save on rent and utilities.
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls to save on food costs.
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to save on transport costs.
- Take advantage of free or low-cost cultural events and activities in the city.
- Learn some basic Spanish to negotiate prices and interact with locals.
- Avoid exchanging money at airports or tourist areas, and instead use ATMs or banks to get a better exchange rate.
