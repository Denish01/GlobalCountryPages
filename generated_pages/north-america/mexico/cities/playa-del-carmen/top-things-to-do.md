# Top Things to Do in Playa del Carmen — Attractions & Experiences

Playa del Carmen, a city located on the Caribbean coast of Mexico's Yucatan Peninsula, offers a wide range of activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring ancient Mayan ruins to snorkeling in the crystal-clear waters of the Mesoamerican Reef.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Temazcal** ceremony, a traditional Mayan purification ritual, at a local spa or wellness center
- Go on a **Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve** tour, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, to see dolphins, manatees, and tropical birds
- Visit the **Xochimilco Floating Gardens**, a network of canals and floating gardens, to see the local flora and fauna
- Take a **Cenote** tour, a natural swimming hole, to swim in the crystal-clear waters and see the stalactites and stalagmites
- Go on a **Swim with Dolphins** tour, to interact with these intelligent and social creatures
- Take a **Cooking Class**, to learn how to prepare traditional Mexican dishes, such as tacos and mole

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve**, a protected area, to see the diverse wildlife and ecosystems
- Go to the **Tulum National Park**, to see the ancient Mayan ruins and swim in the beach
- Take a **Snorkeling** tour, to see the coral reef and marine life of the Mesoamerican Reef
- Visit the **Xel-Ha Park**, to swim with tropical fish and see the natural aquarium
- Go on a **Hiking** tour, to explore the jungle and see the local flora and fauna

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo de la Cultura Maya**, to see the history and culture of the Mayan civilization
- Take a **Spanish Language Class**, to learn the local language and culture
- Go to the **Playa del Carmen Church**, to see the local architecture and attend a mass
- Visit the **Tulum Museum**, to see the history and culture of the ancient Mayan city
- Take a **Local Workshop**, to learn how to make traditional Mexican crafts, such as textiles and pottery

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Mahahual Beach**, a secluded beach, to relax and swim in the calm waters
- Take a **Ferry to Cozumel**, to explore the island and see the local culture
- Go to the **Punta Laguna**, a nature reserve, to see the local wildlife and ecosystems
- Visit the **Coba Village**, to see the local culture and traditions
- Take a **Bicycle Tour**, to explore the city and see the local architecture and culture

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Tulum**, 45 miles, 1 hour, to visit the ancient Mayan ruins and swim in the beach
- Go to **Coba**, 35 miles, 45 minutes, to explore the ancient Mayan city and climb the Nohoch Mul pyramid
- Visit **Akumal**, 20 miles, 30 minutes, to swim with sea turtles and snorkel in the crystal-clear waters
- Take a day trip to **Chichen Itza**, 120 miles, 2 hours, to visit the ancient Mayan city and see the Pyramid of Kukulkan
- Go to **Isla Mujeres**, 20 miles, 30 minutes, to explore the island and see the local culture
