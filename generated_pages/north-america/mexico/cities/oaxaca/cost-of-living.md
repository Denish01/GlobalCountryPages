# Cost of Living in Oaxaca - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Oaxaca is generally considered to be affordable, with a daily budget of $30-50 USD per day for a backpacker, $50-100 USD per day for a mid-range traveler, and $100-200 USD per day for a comfortable stay.

[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Oaxaca? [/SECTION]
Oaxaca is a city located in the southern part of Mexico, known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. Compared to other major cities in Mexico, Oaxaca is relatively affordable, with a cost of living that is lower than in cities like Mexico City or Cancun. However, prices can vary depending on the location, with the city center tend to be more expensive than the outskirts. Overall, Oaxaca offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike. In terms of global averages, Oaxaca is considered to be a relatively cheap destination, with prices comparable to those found in other parts of Latin America.

Oaxaca's affordability is due in part to its local economy, which is driven by agriculture, tourism, and small-scale industry. The city has a thriving market scene, with many vendors selling traditional goods and food at affordable prices. Additionally, the city's relatively low cost of living makes it an attractive destination for digital nomads and remote workers, who can enjoy a high quality of life without breaking the bank. However, it's worth noting that prices can vary depending on the time of year, with peak tourist season (December to April) tend to be more expensive than the off-season.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel, which can be a affordable and social way to meet other travelers.

- Use public transportation, such as buses or colectivos, which are cheap and convenient.
- Avoid eating at touristy restaurants, which tend to be more expensive than local eateries.
- Shop at local markets or tiendas, which offer a wide range of goods at affordable prices.
- Learn some basic Spanish phrases, which can help you navigate the city and interact with locals.
