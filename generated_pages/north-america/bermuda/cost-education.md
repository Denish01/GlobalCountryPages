# Education Costs in Bermuda

The cost of education in Bermuda varies widely, with public schools being generally free or subsidized, while private schools and international institutions can range from $5,000 to over $20,000 per year.

[SECTION] Education System Overview [/SECTION]
Bermuda's education system is considered to be of high quality, with a strong emphasis on **English language instruction**. The system is divided into primary, secondary, and tertiary levels, with 12 years of compulsory education. Public schools are generally well-regarded and free or subsidized for local students, while private schools and international institutions offer a range of options for expat families and those seeking alternative curricula. The language of instruction is English, and the academic year typically runs from September to June.

The quality of education in Bermuda is considered to be on par with other developed countries in the region, with a strong focus on **academic achievement** and **extracurricular activities**. International schools are available, offering curricula such as the **International Baccalaureate** (IB) and **Cambridge International Examinations** (CIE). These schools cater to expat families and those seeking a more global education, and often have higher tuition fees than local private schools.
