# Where to Stay in Saint Martin — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When it comes to deciding where to stay in Saint Martin, the best areas to consider are those that offer a mix of beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and convenient access to amenities and activities.

**Nettle Bay** (Marigot)
Best For: Luxury
Vibe: Nettle Bay is known for its upscale atmosphere and stunning views of the lagoon, making it perfect for those looking for a relaxing and sophisticated getaway. The area is surrounded by beautiful beaches and offers a range of water sports and activities.
Price Range: $200-500/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, upscale amenities, convenient access to Marigot
Cons: Can be quite expensive, limited budget options

**Sandy Ground** (Marigot)
Best For: Family
Vibe: Sandy Ground is a charming area with a laid-back atmosphere, featuring a beautiful beach and a range of restaurants and shops. The area is perfect for families, with plenty of activities and amenities to keep kids entertained.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: Beautiful beach, family-friendly amenities, convenient access to Marigot
Cons: Can be quite crowded during peak season

**Baie Orientale** (Orient Bay)
Best For: Backpacker
Vibe: Baie Orientale is a vibrant and lively area, known for its beautiful beach and range of water sports and activities. The area has a laid-back atmosphere, making it perfect for backpackers and young travelers.
