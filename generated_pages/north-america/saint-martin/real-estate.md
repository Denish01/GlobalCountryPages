- There are no restrictions on the type of property that can be bought, but **commercial properties** require a **special permit**.
- Foreign buyers can own property **freehold**, but they need to pay **annual property taxes**.
- Foreign buyers can also **lease** property for a period of up to 99 years, with the option to **renew** the lease.
- Foreign companies can also buy property in Saint Martin, but they need to **register** with the **French authorities** and obtain a **tax identification number**.
