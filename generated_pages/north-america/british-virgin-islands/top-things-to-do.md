# Top Things to Do in British Virgin Islands — Attractions & Experiences

The British Virgin Islands offer a diverse range of activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring the stunning natural beauty of **The Baths** on Virgin Gorda to snorkeling in the crystal-clear waters of **Trunk Bay** on Tortola, and discovering the rich history and culture of the islands at the **Virgin Islands Folk Museum** in Road Town.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **sailing trip** around the islands to explore the hidden coves and beaches
- Go on a **snorkeling or diving trip** to explore the underwater world of the British Virgin Islands
- Attend a **full moon party** on Jost Van Dyke for a night of music, dancing, and fun
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the local cuisine and how to prepare traditional dishes
- Go on a **kayaking trip** through the mangroves to explore the unique ecosystem
- Take a **yoga class** on the beach to relax and unwind in a beautiful setting
- Go on a **fishing trip** to catch some of the local seafood and enjoy a fresh meal

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Sage Mountain National Park** for hiking trails and stunning views
- Visit the **Gorda Peak National Park** for hiking trails and stunning views
- Relax on **Long Bay Beach** for excellent swimming and sunbathing opportunities
- Explore the **The Baths** on Virgin Gorda for unique granite formations and crystal-clear waters
- Visit the **Tortola Forest** for hiking trails and stunning views
- Go on a **whale watching trip** to see the humpback whales in their natural habitat

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Virgin Islands Folk Museum** to learn about the history and culture of the British Virgin Islands
- Attend a **local festival** to experience the music, dance, and food of the islands
- Take a **tour of the Callwood Rum Distillery** to learn about the history of rum production in the islands
- Visit the **Old Government House Museum** to learn about the history of the islands
- Take a **class on traditional island crafts** to learn about the local arts and crafts
- Attend a **church service** to experience the local culture and music

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Bubbly Pool** on Jost Van Dyke for a unique natural wonder
- Explore the **Copper Mine** on Virgin Gorda for a glimpse into the islands' mining history
- Relax on **Savannah Bay Beach** on Virgin Gorda for excellent swimming and sunbathing opportunities
- Visit the **North Sound** on Virgin Gorda for excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities
- Take a **hike to the top of Mount Sage** for stunning views of the surrounding islands

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip to **Jost Van Dyke** (30 minutes from Tortola) to explore the beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife
2. Visit **Peter Island** (1 hour from Tortola) for a luxury resort experience and excellent snorkeling opportunities
3. Take a day trip to **Norman Island** (30 minutes from Tortola) to explore the historic island and excellent snorkeling opportunities
4. Visit **Salt Island** (1 hour from Tortola) to explore the historic salt mines and excellent diving opportunities
5. Take a day trip to **Virgin Gorda** (1 hour from Tortola) to explore the unique granite formations and crystal-clear waters of The Baths
