# Where to Stay in Dominican Republic — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When deciding where to stay in the Dominican Republic, consider the popular areas of Punta Cana, Santo Domingo, and Puerto Plata, which offer a range of accommodations and activities for different types of travelers.

**Punta Cana** (Punta Cana)
Best For: Luxury / Family
Vibe: Known for its beautiful beaches and luxurious all-inclusive resorts, Punta Cana is a great destination for families and couples looking for a relaxing vacation. The area is also home to several golf courses and offers a range of outdoor activities.
Price Range: $200-$500/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, luxurious resorts, and a range of outdoor activities. The area is also relatively safe and has a well-developed tourist infrastructure.
Cons: Can be quite expensive, and some areas can feel overcrowded during peak season.

**Santo Domingo** (Santo Domingo)
Best For: Backpacker / Business
Vibe: As the capital city of the Dominican Republic, Santo Domingo is a bustling metropolis with a rich history and culture. The city is home to several historic landmarks, museums, and cultural attractions.
Price Range: $30-$150/night
Pros: Rich history and culture, affordable accommodations, and a range of dining and entertainment options. The city is also a great base for exploring the surrounding area.
Cons: Can be quite noisy and crowded, and some areas can be considered unsafe at night.

**Puerto Plata** (Puerto Plata)
Best For: Family / Adventure
Vibe: Located on the northern coast of the Dominican Republic, Puerto Plata is a popular destination for families and adventure seekers. The area is known for its beautiful beaches, water sports, and outdoor activities such as hiking and biking.
Price Range: $100-$300/night
Pros: Beautiful beaches, outdoor activities, and a range of accommodations. The area is also relatively affordable and has a more laid-back atmosphere.
Cons: Can be quite windy, and some areas can be affected by rain during the summer months.
