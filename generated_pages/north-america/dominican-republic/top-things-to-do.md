# Top Things to Do in Dominican Republic — Attractions & Experiences

The Dominican Republic offers a diverse range of activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring the oldest city in the New World, Santo Domingo, to relaxing on the beautiful beaches of Punta Cana, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking for a mix of history, culture, and outdoor adventure.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **cigar-rolling** class and learn about the country's famous tobacco industry
- Go on a **whale-watching** tour in Samaná Bay, a prime location for spotting humpback whales
- Visit a **coffee plantation** in the central highlands and learn about the production process
- Explore the **caves** of Los Haitises National Park, a unique and fascinating geological formation
- Attend a **merengue** dance class and learn about the country's vibrant music and dance culture
- Take a **safari** tour through the countryside and experience the local way of life
- Go on a **kiteboarding** lesson in Cabarete, a popular spot for water sports

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Del Este National Park**, a protected area with diverse wildlife and ecosystems
- Hike to the **Pico Duarte**, the highest peak in the Caribbean, and enjoy breathtaking views
- Visit the **Lago Enriquillo**, a saltwater lake and home to a wide range of bird species
- Relax on the **Bavaro Beach**, a stunning stretch of coastline with crystal-clear waters
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the countryside and enjoy the scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo del Arte Moderno**, a museum showcasing the country's modern art scene
- Explore the **Colonial Zone**, a historic neighborhood with a rich cultural heritage
- Attend a **traditional music** performance, such as merengue or bachata
- Take a **cooking class** and learn about the country's cuisine and ingredients
- Visit the **Santo Domingo Cathedral**, a stunning example of colonial architecture
- Explore the **Alcázar de Colón**, a historic palace and museum showcasing the country's history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Bayahibe**, a charming coastal town with a rich history and culture
- Explore the **Cueva de las Maravillas**, a cave system with stunning rock formations and ancient Taino petroglyphs
- Relax on the **Playa Grande**, a secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and powdery sand
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Lago Enriquillo**, a unique and fascinating ecosystem
- Visit the **Hacienda Santa Cruz**, a historic plantation with a rich history and stunning architecture

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Samaná**, a charming coastal town with a rich history and culture, located 115 km from Santo Domingo (approximately 2 hours by car)
- Visit the **Los Haitises National Park**, a protected area with diverse wildlife and ecosystems, located 120 km from Santo Domingo (approximately 2.5 hours by car)
- Explore the **town of La Romana**, a charming coastal town with a rich history and culture, located 100 km from Santo Domingo (approximately 1.5 hours by car)
- Take a day trip to **Cabarete**, a popular spot for water sports and outdoor activities, located 40 km from Puerto Plata (approximately 1 hour by car)
- Visit the **Damajaqua Waterfalls**, a stunning natural wonder with 27 cascades, located 20 km from Cabarete (approximately 30 minutes by car)
