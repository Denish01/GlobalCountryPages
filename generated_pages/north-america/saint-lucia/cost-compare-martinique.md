[SECTION] Overview: Saint Lucia vs Martinique [/SECTION]
The cost of living in Saint Lucia and Martinique can vary significantly, with Saint Lucia generally offering a more affordable lifestyle. The economic context of both countries plays a significant role in determining the cost of living, with Saint Lucia having a smaller economy and lower GDP per capita compared to Martinique. As a result, prices for goods and services in Saint Lucia tend to be lower, making it an attractive option for those looking for a more affordable Caribbean lifestyle. However, it's essential to note that Martinique, as an overseas department of France, has a more developed economy and infrastructure, which can impact the cost of living.

The overall cost of living difference between Saint Lucia and Martinique is around 15-20% in favor of Saint Lucia. This means that for every dollar spent in Martinique, you can expect to pay around 80-85 cents in Saint Lucia. This difference can be attributed to various factors, including the cost of housing, food, transportation, and utilities. While both countries offer a unique and desirable Caribbean lifestyle, the cost of living difference can be a significant factor for those deciding which country to visit or live in.
