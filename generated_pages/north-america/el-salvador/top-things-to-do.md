# Top Things to Do in El Salvador — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of El Salvador, visit the **Joyas de Cerén** archaeological site, explore the **Parque Nacional Los Volcanes**, and relax on the beaches of **El Tunco**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **surf lesson** in El Tunco, a popular surf spot with consistent waves
- Explore the **coffee plantations** along the Ruta de las Flores, and learn about the production process
- Visit a **local market**, such as the Mercado Central in San Salvador, to experience the vibrant culture and try traditional foods
- Go on a **volcano hike**, such as the Santa Ana Volcano, for stunning views and a challenging adventure
- Take a **boat tour** of Lake Coatepeque, a picturesque crater lake with stunning scenery
- Attend a **traditional festival**, such as the Fiestas de San Salvador, to experience the local music, dance, and food

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Parque Nacional Los Volcanes**: a national park featuring three major volcanoes, including the Santa Ana Volcano
- **Montecristo Cloud Forest**: a lush cloud forest with hiking trails, waterfalls, and stunning scenery
- **El Imposible National Park**: a national park with hiking trails, waterfalls, and a variety of wildlife
- **Lake Coatepeque**: a picturesque crater lake with stunning views, boat tours, and water sports
- **El Tunco**: a popular surf spot and beach town with a laid-back atmosphere

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Museo Nacional de Antropología**: a museum showcasing the history and culture of El Salvador, with a collection of artifacts and exhibits
- **Catedral Metropolitana**: a stunning cathedral in San Salvador, featuring beautiful architecture and historic significance
- **Teatro Nacional**: a historic theater in San Salvador, hosting a variety of performances and events
- **Instituto de Cultura**: a cultural center in San Salvador, offering workshops, classes, and exhibits
- **Feria Internacional de San Salvador**: an international fair featuring music, dance, food, and crafts from around the world

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Ataco**: a charming town along the Ruta de las Flores, known for its vibrant arts scene and stunning natural scenery
- **Nahuizalco**: a picturesque town in the mountains, featuring traditional crafts, stunning views, and a relaxed atmosphere
- **Playa El Zonte**: a secluded beach town with a laid-back atmosphere, popular with surfers and nature lovers
- **Cerro Verde National Park**: a national park featuring stunning scenery, hiking trails, and a variety of wildlife
- **Suchitoto**: a charming colonial town with a rich history, stunning architecture, and a vibrant arts scene
