[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **classic car tour** of Havana to experience the city's vibrant culture and stunning architecture
- Visit a **tobacco plantation** in Viñales to learn about the production of Cuba's famous cigars
- Enjoy a **salsa dance lesson** in Havana to learn the country's iconic dance style
- Go **scuba diving** in the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean to explore the island's stunning coral reefs
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Cuban cuisine and ingredients
- Attend a **baseball game** to experience the country's passion for the sport
- Visit a **rumba performance** to enjoy the energetic and vibrant music and dance

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Viñales Valley** national park to discover the unique landscape and tobacco plantations
- Relax on the beautiful **Varadero Beach** and enjoy the crystal-clear waters
- Visit the **Cienfuegos Botanical Garden** to see the stunning collection of tropical plants
- Go **hiking** in the **Sierra Maestra Mountains** to enjoy the stunning scenery and wildlife
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Zapata Swamp** to explore the unique ecosystem and wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo de la Revolución** to understand the country's revolutionary history
- Explore the **National Museum of Fine Arts** to see the stunning collection of Cuban art
- Attend a **performance** at the **Gran Teatro de La Habana** to enjoy the country's vibrant music and dance
- Visit the **Fusterlandia** community project to see the unique and colorful artwork
- Take a **tour** of the **Havana Cathedral** to learn about the city's rich history and architecture
- Visit the **Santiago de Cuba Carnival Museum** to learn about the city's famous carnival celebrations

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Fernando Ortiz African Cultural Museum** to learn about the country's African heritage
- Explore the **Jardines de la Reina** national park to discover the unique landscape and wildlife
- Take a **tour** of the **Havana Street Art** scene to see the vibrant and colorful murals
- Relax on the beautiful **Playa Ancón** beach in Trinidad
- Visit the **Callejon de Hamel** to see the unique and colorful artwork and street performers

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Havana to **Viñales** (180 km, 3 hours) to explore the stunning natural beauty and tobacco plantations
- Visit **Trinidad** (370 km, 5 hours) from Havana to explore the charming colonial streets and architecture
- Take a day trip from Varadero to **Havana** (140 km, 2 hours) to experience the city's vibrant culture and history
- Visit **Cienfuegos** (250 km, 4 hours) from Havana to see the stunning colonial architecture and beautiful botanical garden
- Take a day trip from Santiago de Cuba to **Baracoa** (150 km, 3 hours) to explore the stunning natural beauty and unique culture
