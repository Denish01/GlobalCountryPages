[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Viñales Valley** is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its stunning natural beauty and traditional tobacco farms.
- The **Havana Cathedral** is a beautiful example of Cuban architecture, with a mix of Baroque and Neoclassical styles.
- The **Tropicana Nightclub** is a world-famous venue, showcasing Cuban music and dance.
- The **Cuban National Ballet** is one of the most prestigious ballet companies in the world, with a rich history of producing talented dancers.
- The **Festival of New Latin American Cinema** is an annual event, showcasing the best of Latin American film.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Fidel Castro** was a key figure in Cuban history, leading the country through a period of significant social and economic change.
- **Celia Cruz** was a world-renowned singer, known for her powerful voice and energetic performances.
- The **Cuban diaspora** has made significant contributions to the arts, sciences, and politics in countries around the world.
- **José Martí** was a national hero, who played a key role in Cuba's struggle for independence from Spain.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **warm hospitality** of the Cuban people is often cited as one of the highlights of visiting the country.
- The **delicious cuisine** is a mix of Spanish, African, and Caribbean flavors, with popular dishes such as **ropa vieja** and **arroz con pollo**.
- The **stunning natural beauty** of Cuba, with its beautiful beaches, mountains, and valleys.
- The **vibrant music scene**, with live music performances available in many bars and clubs.
- The **rich history and culture**, with many museums, historical sites, and cultural events to explore.
- The **affordable cost of living**, making it a popular destination for budget-conscious travelers.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **town of Trinidad** is a hidden gem, with its well-preserved colonial architecture and vibrant arts scene.
- The **Cienfuegos Botanical Garden** is a beautiful and peaceful oasis, with a wide variety of plant species.
- The **Cuba Marine Park** is a protected area, with a stunning array of marine life and coral reefs.
- The **Callejón de Hamel** is a colorful and vibrant street in Havana, known for its street art and live music performances.
