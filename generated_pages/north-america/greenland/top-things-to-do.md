# Top Things to Do in Greenland — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Greenland, visit the **Ilulissat Icefjord**, explore the **Nuuk** city, and take a boat tour among **icebergs** in **Disko Bay**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dog sled tour** across the ice cap, a traditional mode of transportation in Greenland
- Go **whale watching** in Disko Bay, where you can see humpback, minke, and fin whales
- Experience the **Northern Lights** in the winter months, a spectacular display of colored lights in the sky
- Learn about **Inuit culture** and traditions at a local workshop or festival
- Take a **kayaking trip** among icebergs in Disko Bay, a unique and thrilling experience
- Go on a **hunting trip** with local guides, where you can hunt for musk oxen, reindeer, and other Arctic animals
- Attend a **traditional Inuit festival**, where you can experience the local music, dance, and food

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Sermermiut** valley, a scenic valley with stunning views of the icefjord
- Hike to the **Nordenskiöld Fjord**, a beautiful fjord with stunning scenery and wildlife
- Visit the **Kangerlussuaq National Park**, a vast park with stunning scenery and hiking opportunities
- Go **fishing** in the **Arctic Ocean**, where you can catch halibut, cod, and other fish species
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Sermitsiaq** island, a scenic island with stunning views and wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Greenland National Museum**, where you can learn about the history and culture of the Inuit people
- Attend a **traditional Inuit dance performance**, where you can experience the local music and dance
- Take a **cooking class**, where you can learn how to prepare traditional Inuit dishes such as **mattak** and **suaasat**
- Visit the **Nuuk Cathedral**, a beautiful wooden church and a symbol of Greenland's history
- Explore the **Sisimiut Museum**, where you can learn about the history and culture of the town
- Attend a **local festival**, where you can experience the local music, dance, and food

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tasiilaq** town, a charming town on the east coast with a rich cultural heritage
- Explore the **Nanortalik** island, a scenic island with stunning views and wildlife
- Take a **hike** to the **Flower Valley**, a scenic valley with stunning views and unique flora
- Go **fishing** in the **Igdlorssuit** lake, a lake filled with trout and other fish species
- Attend a **local workshop**, where you can learn about traditional Inuit crafts such as **knitting** and **carving**
