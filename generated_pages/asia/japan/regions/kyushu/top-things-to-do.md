# Top Things to Do in Kyushu — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Kyushu, visit its unique attractions, immerse in its cultural heritage, and explore its breathtaking natural landscapes, which showcase the region's distinct character and charm.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **stroll through the traditional Japanese gardens** of the Suizenji Jojuen Garden in Kumamoto
- Experience the **unique volcanic landscapes** of Sakurajima, including its black sand beaches and volcanic hiking trails
- Visit the **historic city of Nagasaki**, with its blend of Japanese, Chinese, and European influences
- Relax in the **natural hot springs** of the Yunessun hot spring resort in Yufuin
- Explore the **traditional crafts and artisans** of the Kyushu region, including pottery, textiles, and woodwork
- Attend a **traditional Japanese tea ceremony** at the Fukuoka Japanese Garden

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike through the **Aso-Kuju National Park**, with its diverse landscapes and scenic trails
- Visit the **pristine beaches and waterfalls** of Yakushima Island
- Explore the **volcanic landscapes** of Sakurajima, including its volcanic hiking trails and black sand beaches
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Sasebo Harbor**, with its scenic waterfront and mix of Japanese and Western architecture
- Visit the **Kuju Mountains**, with their scenic hiking trails and stunning natural beauty

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine**, dedicated to the god of learning and culture
- Explore the **Kumamoto Castle**, one of Japan's most impressive and historic castles
- Attend a **traditional Japanese festival**, such as the Hakata Gion Yamakasa festival in Fukuoka
- Visit the **Nagasaki Museum of History and Culture**, with its exhibits on the city's unique cultural heritage
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Fukuoka City Museum**, with its exhibits on the city's history and culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Yufuin town**, with its natural hot springs and scenic landscapes
- Explore the **Sakurajima Island**, with its unique volcanic landscapes and stunning natural beauty
- Take a **stroll through the traditional Japanese gardens** of the Suizenji Jojuen Garden in Kumamoto
- Visit the **Kurokawa Onsen**, a scenic hot spring town in the mountains of Kyushu
- Attend a **local festival**, such as the Sasebo Harbor Festival, with its unique cultural heritage and traditional performances

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Fukuoka to **Dazaifu**, with its historic shrine and traditional Japanese gardens (30 km, 30 minutes by train)
- Visit **Sasebo** from Fukuoka, with its scenic harbor and mix of Japanese and Western architecture (100 km, 1 hour by train)
- Explore **Kumamoto** from Fukuoka, with its historic castle and traditional Japanese gardens (200 km, 2 hours by train)
- Take a day trip from Kagoshima to **Sakurajima**, with its unique volcanic landscapes and stunning natural beauty (15 km, 15 minutes by ferry)
- Visit **Yakushima Island** from Kagoshima, with its pristine beaches and waterfalls (180 km, 2 hours by ferry)
