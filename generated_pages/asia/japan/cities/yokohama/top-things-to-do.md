[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]

- Visit the **Yokohama Hakkeijima Sea Paradise** for an aquarium and amusement park experience
- Explore the **Bashamichi** neighborhood, known for its historic architecture and trendy shops
- Sample **Yokohama's Chinatown cuisine**, including delicious Chinese dishes and desserts
- Attend a **Yokohama DeNA BayStars** baseball game at Yokohama Stadium

- Take a **Yokohama walking tour** to discover the city's hidden gems and historic landmarks
