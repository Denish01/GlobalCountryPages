[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local restaurants and try traditional Japanese cuisine, which is often cheaper than Western-style food.
- Use public transportation instead of taxis to get around the city.
- Buy a prepaid IC card, such as an ICOCA or SUICA card, to ride public transportation and make purchases at convenience stores and vending machines.
- Take advantage of free admission to many of Nara's temples and shrines on certain days of the month or year.
- Shop at local markets or supermarkets instead of convenience stores to save on food and other essentials.
