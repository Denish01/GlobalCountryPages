# Top Things to Do in Nara — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Deer Feeding**: Feed and interact with the friendly deer at Nara Park
- **Traditional Japanese Tea Ceremony**: Participate in a traditional Japanese tea ceremony at the Isuien Garden or other traditional tea houses
- **Kimono Wearing**: Wear a traditional Japanese kimono and stroll around the city
- **Japanese Calligraphy**: Learn the art of Japanese calligraphy at a local workshop
- **Nara Craft Tour**: Take a tour of local crafts and artisans, such as pottery and woodblock printing
- **Moon Viewing**: Participate in a traditional Japanese moon viewing event at a local temple or garden
