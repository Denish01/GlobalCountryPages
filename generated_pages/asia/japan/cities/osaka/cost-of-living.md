# Cost of Living in Osaka - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Osaka is generally considered to be moderate to expensive, with a daily budget for a backpacker ranging from $60-100 USD per day, while a mid-range traveler can expect to spend around $100-200 USD per day.

Daily Budget (Comfort): $200-300 USD
Currency: JPY

[/FACTBOX]
