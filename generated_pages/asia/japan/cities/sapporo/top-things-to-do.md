# Top Things to Do in Sapporo — Attractions & Experiences

Sapporo, the largest city on the northern Japanese island of Hokkaido, offers a wide range of exciting activities and experiences for visitors, from its stunning natural beauty to its unique cultural attractions.

[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sapporo Clock Tower | North 1 West 2, Chuo-ku | Historical Landmark | Iconic symbol of Sapporo and one of the oldest clock towers in Japan |
| Sapporo Snow Festival | Odori Site, Chuo-ku | Festival | Famous winter festival featuring intricate snow and ice sculptures |
| Sapporo Beer Museum | 9-1-1 Kita 7-jo Higashi, Higashi-ku | Museum | Learn about the history of beer in Japan and sample local brews |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a stroll through the beautiful **Odori Park**, which stretches through the heart of Sapporo and features beautiful gardens and fountains
- Sample some of the local **seafood** at the Sapporo Central Wholesale Market, including fresh crab and salmon
- Visit the **Sapporo Beer Factory** to learn about the brewing process and sample some of the local beers
- Explore the **Sapporo City Archives**, which features exhibits on the history and culture of Sapporo
- Relax in a **traditional Japanese onsen** (hot spring) at one of the many onsen resorts in the area
- Learn about the **Ainu culture**, the indigenous people of Hokkaido, at the Ainu Cultural Center
- Take a **skiing or snowboarding lesson** at one of the many ski resorts in the area, including Mount Teine and Sapporo Kokusai Ski Resort

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the beautiful **Shikotsu-Toya National Park**, which features stunning lakes, mountains, and forests
- Hike to the top of **Mount Teine**, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area
- Visit the **Hokkaido University Botanical Garden**, which features a wide range of plant species and a peaceful atmosphere
- Take a stroll along the **Toyohira River**, which runs through the heart of Sapporo and features beautiful parks and gardens
- Go **skiing or snowboarding** at one of the many ski resorts in the area, including Mount Teine and Sapporo Kokusai Ski Resort
- Explore the **Nopporo Forest Park**, which features beautiful forests and hiking trails

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sapporo Museum of Art**, which features a wide range of Japanese and international art
- Explore the **Historical Village of Hokkaido**, which features traditional Japanese buildings and exhibits on the history and culture of Hokkaido
- Attend a **traditional Japanese tea ceremony** at one of the many tea houses in the area
- Learn about the **Ainu culture** at the Ainu Cultural Center, which features exhibits and performances
- Visit the **Sapporo Clock Tower**, which is one of the oldest clock towers in Japan and features a museum and observation deck
- Explore the **Sapporo Central Wholesale Market**, which features fresh seafood and local produce, as well as a lively atmosphere

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sapporo City Archives**, which features exhibits on the history and culture of Sapporo
- Explore the **Nopporo Forest Park**, which features beautiful forests and hiking trails
- Take a stroll through the **Maruyama neighborhood**, which features beautiful gardens and traditional Japanese architecture
- Visit the **Sapporo Beer Factory**, which offers tours and tastings of local beers

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Otaru**, a charming coastal town with a rich history and culture, located about 30 minutes from Sapporo by train
- Visit **Furano**, a picturesque town in the heart of Hokkaido, known for its beautiful landscapes and outdoor activities, located about 2 hours from Sapporo by car
- Explore **Lake Toya**, a stunning lake with beautiful scenery and outdoor activities, located about 2.5 hours from Sapporo by car
- Take a day trip to **Asahikawa**, a city located about 2 hours from Sapporo by train, known for its beautiful gardens and traditional Japanese architecture
- Visit **Biei**, a picturesque town in the heart of Hokkaido, known for its beautiful landscapes and outdoor activities, located about 2.5 hours from Sapporo by car
