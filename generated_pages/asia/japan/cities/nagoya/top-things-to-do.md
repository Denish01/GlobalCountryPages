[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Nagoya Castle** tour at night to see the castle's beautiful illumination
- Visit the **Osu Shopping District** to experience the city's vibrant nightlife and try some local street food
- Attend a **traditional Japanese tea ceremony** at the Nagoya Japanese Garden
- Explore the **Nagoya City Art Museum** to see a collection of modern and contemporary Japanese art
- Take a **stroll along the Yamazaki River** to enjoy the city's natural beauty
- Visit the **Nagoya TV Tower** for panoramic views of the city

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]

- **Yamazaki River**: a river running through the city, popular for strolling and picnicking
- **Korankei Valley**: a scenic valley with hiking trails and beautiful fall foliage
