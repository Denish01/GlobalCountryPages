# Cost of Living in Fukuoka - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Fukuoka is generally considered moderate, with a daily budget of $60-120 USD per person for a mid-range traveler, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs

- Use public transportation instead of taxis to get around the city
- Take advantage of the city's many free or low-cost attractions, such as the Fukuoka Tower and the Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine
- Shop at local markets or supermarkets instead of convenience stores to save on food and other essentials
- Learn some basic Japanese phrases to help you navigate the city and interact with locals, which can also help you save money by avoiding tourist traps and overpriced services.
