# Top Things to Do in Fukuoka — Attractions & Experiences

Fukuoka, a city on the island of Kyushu in Japan, offers a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions, experiences, and cultural activities that make it a must-visit destination for travelers.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **tonkotsu ramen**-making class and learn the art of creating Fukuoka's famous noodle dish
- Visit the **Fukuoka Fish Market** at dawn to see the daily fish auction and sample the freshest sushi
- Explore the **Yatai** food stalls, a unique feature of Fukuoka's culinary scene
- Attend a **Hakata Gion Yamakasa** festival, a traditional event featuring colorful floats and traditional performances
- Visit the **Fukuoka Kimono** shop and learn about the traditional Japanese garment
- Take a **stroll along the Naka River** and enjoy the beautiful views and scenery

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit **Maizuru Park**, a scenic park featuring beautiful gardens and a historic castle
- Explore the **Genkai Sea**, a beautiful coastal area with stunning views and marine life
- Hike the **Sefuri Mountain**, a scenic mountain with beautiful trails and stunning views
- Relax at **Nokonoshima Island Park**, a peaceful park featuring beautiful gardens and a zoo
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Hakata Bay**, a beautiful bay with stunning views of the surrounding cityscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Fukuoka Museum of Art**, a museum featuring a collection of modern and contemporary art
- Explore the **Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine**, a historic shrine dedicated to the god of learning and culture
- Attend a **traditional tea ceremony** at the **Ohori Park**, a beautiful park featuring a traditional tea house
- Visit the **Kushida Shrine**, a historic shrine famous for its beautiful architecture and traditional festivals
- Take a **calligraphy class** and learn the art of traditional Japanese writing
- Visit the **Fukuoka Asian Art Museum**, a museum featuring a collection of modern and contemporary Asian art

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Fukuoka Castle Ruins**, a historic site featuring the remains of a 17th-century castle
- Explore the **Tenjin district**, a trendy area featuring unique shops and restaurants
- Take a **stroll through the Nakasu** district, a historic area featuring traditional architecture and scenic canals
- Visit the **Fukuoka Anpanman Children's Museum**, a unique museum featuring interactive exhibits and play areas
- Relax at the **Hakata Traditional Crafts Center**, a peaceful center featuring traditional crafts and workshops

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Sasebo**, a coastal city featuring a beautiful harbor and historic architecture (distance: 120 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit **Dazaifu**, a historic town featuring the famous Tenmangu Shrine (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Explore **Yanagawa**, a scenic town featuring canals and traditional architecture (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a day trip to **Kitakyushu**, a city featuring a beautiful harbor and historic architecture (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit **Munakata**, a scenic town featuring beautiful beaches and traditional architecture (distance: 30 km, travel time: 45 minutes)
