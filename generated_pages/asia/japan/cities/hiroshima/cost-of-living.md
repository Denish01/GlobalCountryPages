# Cost of Living in Hiroshima - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Hiroshima is generally moderate, with a daily budget for a backpacker ranging from $60-100 USD, mid-range traveler from $100-200 USD, and comfort seeker from $200-300 USD.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $60-100 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $100-200 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $200-300 USD
Currency: JPY
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Hiroshima? [/SECTION]
Hiroshima is a city in Japan with a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, offering a moderate cost of living compared to other major cities in Japan, such as Tokyo or Osaka. The city's prices are generally lower than those in Tokyo, but higher than in smaller Japanese cities. Compared to global averages, Hiroshima is considered to be a moderately expensive city, with prices for accommodation, food, and transportation falling somewhere in between those of Western Europe and North America. Overall, Hiroshima offers a great balance of quality of life and affordability, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

The cost of living in Hiroshima can vary depending on the individual's lifestyle and preferences. However, in general, the city offers a range of affordable options for accommodation, food, and entertainment, making it an excellent choice for travelers and expats looking for a unique Japanese experience without breaking the bank. From trying local street food to visiting historic landmarks, Hiroshima has something to offer for every budget and interest.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs
- Eat at local eateries or try street food to experience affordable and delicious Japanese cuisine
- Use public transportation instead of taxis to get around the city
- Visit the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park and Museum, which offers free admission
- Take a stroll along the Miyajima Island, which is free to visit and offers beautiful scenery
- Try to avoid visiting during peak travel seasons, such as cherry blossom season, when prices tend to be higher

Overall, Hiroshima is a great destination for travelers and expats looking for a unique Japanese experience without breaking the bank. With its moderate cost of living, rich culture, and historic landmarks, Hiroshima is an excellent choice for anyone interested in exploring Japan.
