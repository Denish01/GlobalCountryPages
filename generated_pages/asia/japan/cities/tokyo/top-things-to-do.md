# Top Things to Do in Tokyo — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Tokyo, visit iconic spots like Shibuya Crossing, Meiji Shrine, and Tsukiji Outer Market, and delve into unique experiences, cultural landmarks, and hidden gems that showcase the city's vibrant atmosphere and rich heritage.

[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Shibuya Crossing | Shibuya | Scenic Spot | Witness the busiest intersection in the world |
| Meiji Shrine | Yoyogi Park | Shinto Shrine | Dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken |
| Tsukiji Outer Market | Tsukiji | Market | Savor the freshest sushi and seafood in Tokyo |
| Tokyo Skytree | Sumida | Observation Deck | Enjoy panoramic views of the city from 634 meters high |

| Hamarikyu Gardens | Chuo | Gardens | Stroll through a former duck hunting ground for the Imperial family |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **sumo wrestling tournament** tour at the Ryogoku Kokugikan Arena
- Relax in an **onsen** (hot spring) at the Otemba Hot Spring or Spa LaQua
- Visit a **robot restaurant** in Shinjuku's Kabukicho district for a bizarre show
- Attend a **tea ceremony** at the Happo-en Garden or the Tea Ceremony Room
- Explore the **neon districts** of Shinjuku, Shibuya, and Golden Gai
- Visit the **teamLab Borderless** digital art museum in Odaiba
- Experience the **vending machine culture** by trying unique vending machine snacks and drinks

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden** for a mix of Japanese, French, and English landscaping
- Take a **hike** at the **Mount Takao** or **Mount Mitake** for scenic views of the city
- Relax at the **Imperial Palace East Garden** for a peaceful stroll
- Explore the **Hamamatsucho Park** for a beautiful cherry blossom viewing spot
- Visit the **Koishikawa Korakuen Garden** for a traditional Japanese garden experience

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Meiji Shrine** for a serene Shinto experience
- Explore the **Tokyo National Museum** for a comprehensive look at Japanese art and history
- Attend a **kabuki play** at the Kabuki-za Theatre
- Visit the **Ghibli Museum** for a unique look at Japanese animation
- Take a **calligraphy class** at the Tokyo Calligraphy School
- Explore the **Asakusa district** for a traditional Japanese atmosphere

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kappabashi Dougu Street** for a unique shopping experience
- Explore the **Neon Museum** in Shinjuku for a showcase of vintage neon signs
- Relax at the **Hamarikyu Gardens** for a peaceful afternoon
- Visit the **Daikanyama** district for a trendy shopping and dining experience
- Take a **stroll** along the **Sumida River** for scenic views of the city

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Nikko** (120 km, 2 hours) to visit the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Toshogu Shrine
- Visit **Kamakura** (50 km, 1 hour) to explore the Great Buddha and other historic temples
- Explore **Yokohama** (30 km, 30 minutes) for a visit to the Minato Mirai 21 district and the Yokohama Museum of Art
- Take a day trip to **Hakone** (100 km, 2 hours) for scenic views of Mt. Fuji and a ride on the Hakone Ropeway
- Visit **Kawasaki** (20 km, 30 minutes) to explore the Kawasaki Daishi Temple and the Kawasaki Museum

[SECTION] Planning Tips [/SECTION]
[CALLOUT] Suggested itineraries: 3-day (visit iconic spots like Shibuya Crossing, Meiji Shrine, and Tsukiji Outer Market), 7-day (explore different districts like Asakusa, Akihabara, and Harajuku), 14-day (deep dive into Tokyo's culture, history, and nature). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: plan ahead for popular attractions, purchase a Suica or Pasmo card for easy travel, and try to avoid rush hour. [/CALLOUT]
To make the most of your trip, consider visiting during the **cherry blossom season** (March-May) or the **autumn foliage** (November-December) for a unique experience. With careful planning and an open mind, you'll be able to experience the best of Tokyo and create unforgettable memories.
