Unique Experiences

  • Take a Naftalan oil bath, a traditional treatment that is said to have healing properties
  • Attend a performance of the Azerbaijan State Opera and Ballet Theater, which features a wide range of classical and contemporary productions
  • Visit a local tea house, where you can experience traditional Azerbaijani hospitality and try local delicacies
  • Take a cookery class, where you can learn how to make traditional Azerbaijani dishes such as plov and dolma
  • Go horse riding in the Caucasus Mountains, where you can experience the stunning natural beauty of the region
  • Attend a traditional Azerbaijani wedding, which is a unique and unforgettable experience
  • Take a tour of a local winery, where you can learn about the production of Azerbaijani wine and taste some of the best varieties

Outdoor & Nature

  • Shahdag National Park, which offers stunning mountain scenery and a wide range of outdoor activities
  • Gobustan National Park, which features ancient petroglyphs and stunning natural landscapes
  • Caspian Sea, where you can swim, sunbathe, and enjoy water sports
  • Caucasus Mountains, which offer a wide range of hiking and trekking trails
  • Qobustan Mud Volcanoes, which are a unique and fascinating natural phenomenon
  • Lankaran National Park, which is home to a wide range of flora and fauna, including the rare Persian leopard

Cultural Experiences

  • National Museum of History of Azerbaijan, which features a wide range of exhibits on the history and culture of Azerbaijan
  • Azerbaijan State Museum of Art, which features a collection of Azerbaijani and European art
  • Baku Museum of Miniature Books, which is home to a unique collection of miniature books from around the world
  • Sheki Museum of History, which features exhibits on the history and culture of the Sheki region
  • Ganja Museum of History, which features exhibits on the history and culture of the Ganja region
  • Ateshgah Museum, which is dedicated to the history and culture of the Ateshgah temple

Hidden Gems

  • Lahij village, which is a remote and picturesque village in the Caucasus Mountains
  • Qabala village, which is a charming village in the north of Azerbaijan that is known for its natural beauty and historic landmarks
  • Nakhchivan city, which is a historic city in the south of Azerbaijan that is known for its stunning architecture and cultural landmarks
  • Shaki city, which is a charming city in the north of Azerbaijan that is known for its historic landmarks and natural beauty
  • Gakh district, which is a remote and picturesque region in the north of Azerbaijan that is known for its natural beauty and historic landmarks

Day Trips

  • Gobustan and Absheron tour, which takes you to the ancient petroglyphs of Gobustan and the natural gas fire of Yanar Dag (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1 hour)
  • Sheki tour, which takes you to the historic city of Sheki and the stunning natural beauty of the surrounding countryside (distance: 300 km, travel time: 4 hours)
  • Qabala tour, which takes you to the charming village of Qabala and the stunning natural beauty of the surrounding countryside (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)
  • Lankaran tour, which takes you to the historic city of Lankaran and the stunning natural beauty of the surrounding countryside (distance: 300 km, travel time: 4 hours)
  • Shahdag tour, which takes you to the stunning natural beauty of the Shahdag National Park (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of Baku and the Absheron Peninsula), 7-day (comprehensive tour of Azerbaijan, including Sheki, Qabala, and Lankaran), 14-day (deep exploration of Azerbaijan, including the Caucasus Mountains and the historic city of Nakhchivan). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book your accommodations and tours in advance, especially during peak season, and prioritize the top attractions and experiences that you want to have. Consider visiting during the shoulder season (April-May or September-October) to avoid the crowds and enjoy mild weather.
