[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Naftalan oil bath**, a traditional treatment that is said to have healing properties
- Attend a performance of the **Azerbaijan State Opera and Ballet Theater**, which features a wide range of classical and contemporary productions
- Visit a **local tea house**, where you can experience traditional Azerbaijani hospitality and try local delicacies
- Take a **cookery class**, where you can learn how to make traditional Azerbaijani dishes such as plov and dolma
- Go **horse riding** in the Caucasus Mountains, where you can experience the stunning natural beauty of the region
- Attend a **traditional Azerbaijani wedding**, which is a unique and unforgettable experience
- Take a **tour of a local winery**, where you can learn about the production of Azerbaijani wine and taste some of the best varieties
