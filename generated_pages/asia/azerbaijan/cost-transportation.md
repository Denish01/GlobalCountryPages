[SECTION] Getting Around Overview [/SECTION]
Azerbaijan has a well-developed transportation network, with a range of options available for getting around, including buses, taxis, and ride-hailing services. The quality of public transit is generally good, with modern buses and a comprehensive network of routes. Most people in Azerbaijan get around by using public transportation, with buses and minibuses being the most popular options. The capital city, Baku, has a particularly well-developed public transportation system, with a metro system and a range of bus and minibus routes.

In addition to public transportation, many people in Azerbaijan also use taxis and ride-hailing services, which are widely available and relatively affordable. The country's roads are generally in good condition, making it easy to get around by car, and there are a number of car rental companies operating in the country. Overall, Azerbaijan has a well-developed transportation network that makes it easy to get around, whether you are using public transportation, taxis, or renting a car.

[SECTION] Transport Tips [/SECTION]
- To save money on transportation, consider using public transportation or ride-hailing services instead of taxis.
- Use **Bolt** or **Yandex.Taxi** for ride-hailing, as they are widely available and relatively affordable.
- Always negotiate the fare with taxi drivers before you start your journey, as they may not always use the meter.
- Be aware of traffic rules and regulations, and always wear a seatbelt when driving or riding in a car.
- Consider renting a car for longer trips or for traveling to rural areas, as public transportation may be limited.
- Download the **Azerbaijan Transportation** app for information on public transportation routes and schedules.
