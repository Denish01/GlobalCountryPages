# Utility & Internet Costs in Vietnam

The cost of utilities and internet in Vietnam can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $80 to $150 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Vietnam's utility infrastructure has undergone significant improvements in recent years, with a focus on increasing access to electricity, water, and sanitation. However, the quality of these services can still vary depending on the location, with urban areas generally having better infrastructure than rural areas. The cost of utilities in Vietnam is relatively stable, but can fluctuate depending on seasonal variations in demand. For example, electricity costs may be higher during the summer months when air conditioning usage is more prevalent.

The Vietnamese government has implemented measures to regulate the cost of utilities and ensure that they remain affordable for the majority of the population. However, the cost of utilities can still be a significant burden for low-income households, particularly in rural areas where access to modern amenities may be limited. Additionally, the cost of utilities can vary significantly depending on the location, with cities like Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City tend to have higher utility costs than smaller towns and rural areas.
