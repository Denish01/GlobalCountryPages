# Top Things to Do in Vietnam — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Vietnam, you should not miss visiting its stunning natural wonders, immersing yourself in its rich cultural heritage, and exploring its vibrant cities, which offer a mix of traditional and modern attractions.

[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ha Long Bay | Quang Ninh Province | Natural Wonder | A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring over 1,600 limestone islands and islets in various shapes and sizes |
| Imperial Citadel of Thang Long | Hanoi | Historical Site | A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the former imperial palace of the Ly, Tran, and Le dynasties |
| Mekong Delta | Southwestern Vietnam | Natural Wonder | A vast network of rivers, canals, and floating markets showcasing the region's unique culture and biodiversity |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **street food tour** in Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City to sample local delicacies like pho, banh mi, and spring rolls
- Attend a **water puppet show** in Hanoi to experience a unique and traditional form of Vietnamese entertainment
- Visit a **local market**, such as the Dong Xuan Market in Hanoi or the Ben Thanh Market in Ho Chi Minh City, to immerse yourself in the sights, sounds, and smells of Vietnamese commerce
- Explore the **cave systems** of Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park, featuring stunning limestone formations and underground rivers
- Take a **cooking class** in Hoi An to learn how to prepare traditional Vietnamese dishes like cao lau and white rose dumplings
- Go on a **motorbike tour** through the scenic countryside of northern Vietnam, offering breathtaking views of the region's natural beauty

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the stunning **Ha Long Bay**, featuring over 1,600 limestone islands and islets in various shapes and sizes
- Visit the **Mekong Delta**, a vast network of rivers, canals, and floating markets showcasing the region's unique culture and biodiversity
- Hike through the **Sapa Hills**, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys
- Relax on the beautiful beaches of **Nha Trang**, featuring clear waters, white sand, and a vibrant nightlife
- Explore the **Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring stunning caves, limestone karst mountains, and diverse wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Temple of Literature**, a historic temple dedicated to Confucius and Vietnam's first national university
- Explore the **Imperial Citadel of Thang Long**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the former imperial palace of the Ly, Tran, and Le dynasties
- Attend a **traditional Vietnamese music performance**, featuring instruments like the dan nguyet and the k'ni
- Visit the **Museum of Ethnology**, showcasing the diverse cultural heritage of Vietnam's 54 ethnic groups
- Take a **calligraphy class**, learning the traditional art of Vietnamese calligraphy and the cultural significance of written characters

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tam Coc**, a scenic area featuring limestone karst mountains, caves, and rivers, often referred to as "Ha Long Bay on land"
- Explore the **Con Dao Islands**, a remote archipelago featuring beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and a rich cultural heritage
- Take a **homestay** in a rural village, offering a unique glimpse into traditional Vietnamese life and culture
- Visit the **Hang Son Doong**, the world's largest cave, featuring stunning limestone formations and underground rivers
- Go on a **trekking tour** in the **Pu Luong Nature Reserve**, featuring stunning scenery, diverse wildlife, and traditional hill tribes

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Hanoi** to the **Ninh Binh Province**, featuring stunning natural scenery, ancient temples, and traditional Vietnamese villages (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Cu Chi Tunnels** from **Ho Chi Minh City**, offering a glimpse into the country's turbulent past and the resourcefulness of the Viet Cong (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Explore the **Mekong Delta** from **Ho Chi Minh City**, featuring floating markets, traditional villages, and stunning natural scenery (distance: 70 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a day trip from **Hoi An** to the **My Son Sanctuary**, featuring ancient Cham ruins and a glimpse into the region's spiritual and cultural past (distance: 40 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Sapa Hills** from **Hanoi**, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, as well as traditional hill tribes and markets (distance: 380 km, travel time: 6 hours)
