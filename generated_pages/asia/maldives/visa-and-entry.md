[SECTION] By Visitor Type [/SECTION]
**Tourists:** Tourists can enter the Maldives without a pre-arranged visa and can stay for a maximum of 30 days. The typical stay for tourists is 7-10 days, and extensions are possible for an additional 60 days. Tourists must hold a valid passport, a return ticket, and proof of sufficient funds.
**Business Travelers:** Business travelers must obtain a **Business** visa, which requires an invitation letter from a Maldivian company or organization. The invitation letter must be approved by the Maldivian government, and the business traveler must meet certain requirements, such as holding a valid passport and a return ticket.
**Students:** Students must obtain a **Student** visa, which requires proof of enrollment in a Maldivian educational institution. The student must also meet certain requirements, such as holding a valid passport and a return ticket, and must provide proof of sufficient funds.
**Workers:** Workers must obtain a **Work** visa, which requires sponsorship by a Maldivian employer. The worker must meet certain requirements, such as holding a valid passport and a return ticket, and must provide proof of qualifications and experience.
