# Utility & Internet Costs in Maldives

The cost of utilities and internet in Maldives can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure in Maldives is generally well-developed, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and gas. However, the costs can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher demand during the peak tourist season from December to April. The Maldivian government has implemented measures to reduce the country's reliance on fossil fuels and increase the use of renewable energy sources, which may impact utility costs in the future.

The quality of utility services in Maldives is generally high, with a stable supply of electricity and water. However, there may be occasional disruptions during severe weather conditions or maintenance work. The costs of utilities are generally stable, but may increase during peak season due to higher demand. It is essential to note that the costs of utilities can vary depending on the location, with higher costs in the capital city of Male compared to other areas.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
- To set up utilities, it is recommended to contact the provider directly and provide the necessary documentation, such as a copy of your passport and proof of address.
- Prepaid electricity plans are available, but postpaid plans may offer more flexibility and convenience.
- Dhiraagu and Ooredoo are the main internet and phone providers in Maldives, offering a range of plans and services.
- To save on utility bills, it is essential to monitor your usage and adjust your habits accordingly, such as turning off lights and appliances when not in use.
- Considering the high cost of utilities, it may be more cost-effective to opt for a bundled plan that includes internet, phone, and TV services.
