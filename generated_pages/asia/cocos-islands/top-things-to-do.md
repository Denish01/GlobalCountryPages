# Top Things to Do in Cocos Islands — Attractions & Experiences

The Cocos Islands offer a unique blend of Australian and Malay culture, stunning beaches, and exciting outdoor activities, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking for an off-the-beaten-path experience.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Malay cooking class** on Home Island to learn about local cuisine and culture
- Go on a ** turtle nesting tour** on Pulu Keeling to witness the nesting process of green turtles
- Experience the **Cocos Islands' unique fusion of Australian and Malay cultures** through local music and dance performances
- Learn about the **history of the Cocos Islands** through a guided tour of the Cocos Islands Museum
- Take a **fishing charter** to explore the surrounding waters and catch some of the island's famous seafood
- Participate in a **beach cleanup** to contribute to the conservation of the island's stunning beaches and marine life
- Go on a **snorkeling or diving trip** to explore the coral reefs and marine life of the Cocos Islands

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Pulu Keeling National Park** and its diverse marine life
- Visit the **Cocos Islands' scenic beaches**, such as those on Direction Island and Horsburgh Island
- Go **hiking** on West Island to take in the stunning views of the surrounding waters
- Take a **boat tour** to explore the surrounding islands and marine life
- Go **birdwatching** on Pulu Keeling to spot some of the island's unique bird species
- Visit the **Wildlife Conservation Area** to learn about the conservation efforts on the island

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Cocos Islands Museum** to learn about the island's history and culture
- Explore the **Traditional Malay Kampong** on Home Island to experience traditional Malay village life
- Attend a **local festival or event**, such as the Cocos Islands' annual cultural festival
- Take a **guided tour** of the Oceania House to learn about its historical significance
- Visit the **Cocos Islands' local markets** to try some of the island's unique cuisine and shop for local handicrafts
- Learn about the **history of the Cocos Islands' lighthouses**, such as the one on Horsburgh Island

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Cocos Islands' scenic lookout points**, such as Cannonball Point, to take in the stunning views
- Explore the **island's hidden coves and bays**, such as those on Direction Island
- Take a **sunrise or sunset tour** to experience the island's unique light and scenery
- Go on a **kayaking or paddleboarding trip** to explore the island's coastline and marine life
- Visit the **Cocos Islands' local art galleries** to see some of the island's unique artwork and handicrafts

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip to Christmas Island**, located just a short flight from the Cocos Islands, to experience its unique culture and scenery
- Visit the **Pulu Keeling National Park** for a day of snorkeling, diving, or simply relaxing on the beach
- Take a **fishing charter** to explore the surrounding waters and catch some of the island's famous seafood
- Go on a **boat tour** to explore the surrounding islands and marine life
- Visit the **Cocos Islands' neighboring islands**, such as Horsburgh Island and Direction Island, to experience their unique scenery and culture
