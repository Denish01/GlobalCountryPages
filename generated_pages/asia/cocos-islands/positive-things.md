[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Cocos Islands Museum** showcases the islands' rich cultural heritage, with exhibits on the history, traditions, and customs of the Cocos Malay people.
- The **West Island Mosque** is a beautiful example of Islamic architecture and a significant cultural landmark on the islands.
- The **Cocos Islands' traditional crafts**, such as woodcarving and weaving, are still practiced today, with many local artisans selling their wares at markets and shops.
- The **Cocos Islands' traditional cuisine**, which includes dishes such as **nasi lemak** and **gulai**, is a unique blend of Malay and Australian flavors.
- The **Cocos Islands' traditional festivals**, such as **Hari Raya Aidilfitri** and **Hari Raya Aidiladha**, are important cultural events that bring the community together.
