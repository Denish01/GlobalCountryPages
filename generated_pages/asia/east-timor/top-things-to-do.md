# Top Things to Do in East Timor — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of East Timor, visit the **Nino Konis Santana National Park**, explore the vibrant **Dili** market, and relax on the stunning **Jacó Beach**, which offer a mix of culture, nature, and adventure that define this unique Asian country.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional weaving workshop** in the village of **Gleno** to learn about local textiles and patterns
- Attend a **traditional Timorese dance performance** in **Dili** to experience the country's rich cultural heritage
- Go on a **whale watching tour** in the **Ombai Strait** to spot humpback whales and other marine life
- Visit a **local coffee plantation** in the **Ermera** district to learn about coffee production and taste some of the best coffee in Asia
- Take a **cooking class** in **Dili** to learn about traditional Timorese cuisine and ingredients
- Go on a **trek to the traditional village** of **Fatubessi** to experience rural life and stunning scenery
- Participate in a **traditional Timorese festival** such as the **Festa do Avante** to experience the country's vibrant culture

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Nino Konis Santana National Park** for its diverse wildlife and coral reefs
- Hike to the **Mount Ramelau** peak for breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
- Relax on the stunning **Jacó Beach** with its pristine white sand and crystal-clear waters
- Visit the **Tasi Tolu** lakes for scenic walking trails and picnic areas
- Go **snorkeling or diving** in the **Ombai Strait** to explore the coral reefs and marine life

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museum of East Timorese Resistance** to learn about the country's struggle for independence
- Explore the **Dili Market** for its vibrant stalls selling local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a **traditional Timorese dance performance** in **Dili** to experience the country's rich cultural heritage
- Visit the **Christo Rei** monument for its iconic statue of Christ and panoramic views of the city
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Maubisse Fortress** to learn about its history and significance

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Nino Konis Santana National Park** (120 km, 3 hours) for its diverse wildlife and coral reefs
- Visit the **Jacó Beach** (140 km, 4 hours) for its pristine white sand and crystal-clear waters
- Explore the **Maubisse Fortress** (70 km, 2 hours) for its 19th-century Portuguese fort and stunning views
- Go on a **whale watching tour** in the **Ombai Strait** (100 km, 3 hours) to spot humpback whales and other marine life
- Take a day trip to the **Atauro Island** (30 km, 1 hour by boat) for its stunning beaches and coral reefs
