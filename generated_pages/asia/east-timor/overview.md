[SECTION] What Is East Timor? [/SECTION]
East Timor, officially known as the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste, is a **sovereign** country located in **Asia**. East Timor is known for its beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and rugged mountains, making it a popular destination for tourists and adventure seekers.
