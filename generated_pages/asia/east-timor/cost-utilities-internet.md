# Utility & Internet Costs in East Timor

The cost of utilities and internet in East Timor can vary, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $250 USD, while internet plans with 60 Mbps speed can cost around $30 to $50 USD per month.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure in East Timor is still developing, with some areas experiencing frequent power outages and water shortages. However, the government has been investing in improving the infrastructure, and the quality of services is gradually increasing. The costs of utilities in East Timor can be stable, but they may fluctuate depending on the season. For example, during the dry season, water costs may increase due to scarcity, while electricity costs may rise during the hot summer months due to higher demand for air conditioning.

The overall cost of living in East Timor is relatively low, but the cost of utilities can still be a significant expense for many households. The inflation rate in East Timor is around 2.1%, which is relatively low compared to other countries in the region. However, the cost of utilities can still increase over time, and households may need to budget accordingly. It's also worth noting that some utilities, such as electricity and water, may be subsidized by the government, which can help keep costs down for low-income households.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
- To set up utilities in East Timor, you will typically need to provide identification and proof of address, and pay a deposit to the utility provider.
- Prepaid electricity meters are available, which can help households budget their electricity usage and avoid unexpected bills.
- Timor Telecom and Telemor are the main internet and phone providers in East Timor, and they offer a range of plans to suit different budgets and needs.
- To save on utility bills, households can take steps such as using energy-efficient appliances, turning off lights and electronics when not in use, and using water-efficient fixtures.
