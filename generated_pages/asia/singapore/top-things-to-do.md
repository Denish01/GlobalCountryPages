# Top Things to Do in Singapore — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Singapore, you should not miss the iconic Gardens by the Bay, the vibrant streets of Chinatown, and the stunning Marina Bay Sands, which offer a glimpse into the city-state's unique blend of culture, architecture, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Night Safari** at the Singapore Zoo to explore the park's nocturnal animals
- Explore the **Street Art** in the alleys of Little India and Chinatown
- Enjoy a **Food Tour** to sample the city-state's diverse culinary delights
- Visit the **Hawker Centers** to experience the local street food scene
- Take a **Cable Car Ride** to Sentosa Island for panoramic views of the city
- Explore the **ArtScience Museum** to discover innovative exhibitions
- Attend a **Formula 1** racing event at the Marina Bay Street Circuit

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **MacRitchie Reservoir Park** for hiking and nature trails
- Explore the **Bukit Timah Nature Reserve** for rainforest and wildlife
- Relax on the **Siloso Beach** on Sentosa Island
- Take a **River Cruise** along the Singapore River
- Visit the **Singapore Botanic Gardens** for a peaceful escape from the city
- Explore the **Pulau Ubin** island for a glimpse into rural Singapore

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Singapore** to learn about the city-state's history
- Explore the **Chinatown Heritage Centre** to discover the culture and traditions of the Chinese community
- Attend a **Performance** at the Esplanade Theatres on the Bay
- Visit the **Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple** to experience Hindu culture and architecture
- Take a **Cooking Class** to learn about Singaporean cuisine
- Visit the **Kampong Glam** district to explore the Malay heritage of Singapore

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tiong Bahru** neighborhood for its charming cafes and boutique shops
- Explore the **Joo Chiat** district for its Peranakan culture and architecture
- Take a stroll along the **Kallang River** for a peaceful escape from the city
- Visit the **Little India Arcade** for a glimpse into Indian culture and traditions
- Explore the **Gillman Barracks** for its contemporary art galleries and studios

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Johor Bahru** in Malaysia (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour) to explore the city's markets and cuisine
- Visit the **Bintan Island** in Indonesia (distance: 160 km, travel time: 2 hours by ferry) for its beautiful beaches and resorts
- Explore the **Pulau Tioman** island in Malaysia (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours by ferry) for its coral reefs and marine life
- Take a day trip to **Malacca** in Malaysia (distance: 240 km, travel time: 3.5 hours) to explore the city's historic sites and culture
- Visit the **Desaru** beach in Malaysia (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours) for its scenic coastline and water sports
