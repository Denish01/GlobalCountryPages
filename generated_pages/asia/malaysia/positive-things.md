[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Petronas Twin Towers** in Kuala Lumpur are an iconic example of modern Malaysian architecture and a symbol of the country's economic success.
- The **Batu Caves** temple complex in Kuala Lumpur is a sacred site for Hindus and a popular tourist destination.
- The **Malay Traditional House** is a classic example of Malaysian architecture, with its wooden walls and tiled roofs.
- The **Kuala Lumpur Performing Arts Centre** is a hub for traditional and contemporary Malaysian arts, including music, dance, and theater.
- The **Thaipusam festival** is a colorful and vibrant celebration of Hindu culture, with processions and ceremonies taking place throughout the country.
