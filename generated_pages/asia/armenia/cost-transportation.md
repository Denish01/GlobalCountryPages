[SECTION] Getting Around Overview [/SECTION]
Armenia has a relatively well-developed transportation system, with a variety of options available for getting around, including buses, minibuses, taxis, and ride-hailing services. The quality of public transit is generally good, with frequent and reliable services connecting major cities and towns. Most people in Armenia get around using public transportation, with buses and minibuses being the most popular options. The capital city, Yerevan, has a comprehensive public transportation system, including buses and a metro system, making it easy to get around without a car.

In addition to public transportation, many people in Armenia also use taxis and ride-hailing services, which are widely available and relatively affordable. The country's transportation network is also well-connected to neighboring countries, with regular bus and train services to Georgia, Azerbaijan, and Iran. However, it's worth noting that the transportation infrastructure in some rural areas can be limited, and the quality of roads can vary significantly depending on the region.
