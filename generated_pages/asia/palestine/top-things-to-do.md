# Top Things to Do in Palestine — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Palestine, visitors should explore its rich history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant culture by visiting iconic sites like the Church of the Nativity, hiking through the beautiful Wadi Qelt, and immersing themselves in the local traditions of cities like Ramallah and Hebron.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Palestinian cooking class** in Ramallah to learn about local cuisine and hospitality
- Attend a **Oud-making workshop** in Hebron to learn about the craft of making this traditional Middle Eastern instrument
- Explore the **ancient olive groves** of the West Bank, where some trees date back over 2,000 years
- Visit a **local keffiyeh factory** in Hebron to learn about the production of this iconic Palestinian scarf
- Join a **guided tour of the Separation Barrier** to understand the complexities of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict
- Participate in a **traditional Palestinian coffee ceremony** to experience the local culture and hospitality
- Visit a **local vineyard** in the Bethlehem area to taste some of the region's excellent wines

- Explore the **Ramallah Old City**, with its historic buildings, traditional markets, and vibrant cultural scene
