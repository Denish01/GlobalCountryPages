[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **heritage walk** in the old city to explore the historic **Peth** areas and experience the local culture
- Visit the **Tulsi Baug** market to shop for traditional **Marathi** clothes and handicrafts
- Attend a **Kathak** dance performance at the **Pune** cultural center
- Explore the **Osho** commune and participate in meditation and yoga sessions
- Go on a **food tour** to sample the local **Puneri** cuisine, including **missal pav** and **vada pav**
- Take a **day trip** to the nearby **Rajmachi** fort and enjoy trekking and nature walks
- Learn about **Ayurveda** and experience traditional Indian medicine at a local wellness center
