# Cost of Living in Pune - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Pune, India is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget of $20-50 USD for backpackers, $50-100 USD for mid-range travelers, and $100-200 USD for those seeking comfort.

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Pune? [/SECTION]
Pune is a city located in the state of Maharashtra, India, and its cost of living is relatively moderate compared to other major cities in the country. The city has a growing economy, a large student population, and a thriving IT industry, which has led to an increase in prices over the years. However, compared to global averages and other major cities in Asia, Pune is still considered to be relatively affordable. The cost of living in Pune is lower than in cities like Mumbai or Delhi, but higher than in smaller towns and cities in India.

In terms of neighbors, Pune is situated near several other major cities in Maharashtra, including Mumbai and Nashik. The cost of living in these cities can vary significantly, with Mumbai being one of the most expensive cities in India. However, Pune's proximity to these cities and its own growing economy make it an attractive option for people looking to live and work in a moderate-cost city.
