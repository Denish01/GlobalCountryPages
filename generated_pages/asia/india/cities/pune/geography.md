[SECTION] Location & Borders [/SECTION]
Pune is situated in the western part of India, in the state of Maharashtra, and is the second-largest city in the state after Mumbai. The city is nestled in the Sahyadri Mountain range of the Western Ghats and is surrounded by the Bhima and Mutha rivers. Pune is strategically located near the Deccan Plateau and is connected to several major cities in India, including Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad. The city's proximity to the Arabian Sea, which is approximately 150 kilometers to the west, has made it an important center for trade and commerce in the region.

Pune is bordered by several districts, including Ahmednagar to the east, Solapur to the southeast, Satara to the south, Raigad to the west, and Thane to the northwest. The city is also close to several major highways and rail lines, making it an important transportation hub in the region. The nearby cities of Mumbai and Bangalore are major economic and cultural centers, and Pune's proximity to these cities has contributed to its growth and development as a major urban center.

Pune's terrain is characterized by a mix of flat plains and rolling hills, with the Sahyadri Mountain range of the Western Ghats running along the western edge of the city. The city is situated in the valley of the Mutha River, which flows through the city and provides water for irrigation and drinking purposes. The surrounding hills and mountains are covered with dense forests, which are home to a wide range of flora and fauna. The city's terrain is also dotted with several lakes and reservoirs, including the Khadakwasla Lake and the Panshet Lake, which are popular tourist destinations.

The landscape of Pune is also marked by several valleys and plateaus, including the Bhima Valley and the Purandar Plateau. These valleys and plateaus are fertile and support a wide range of crops, including sugarcane, wheat, and grapes. The city's terrain is also home to several natural springs and waterfalls, including the Bhimashankar Waterfall and the Tamhini Waterfall, which are popular tourist destinations.
