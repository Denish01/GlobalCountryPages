# Where to Stay in Jaipur — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

To stay in Jaipur, consider the top neighborhoods of Bani Park, Sindhi Camp, and C Scheme, which offer a range of accommodations for different types of travelers, from budget-friendly options to luxury hotels.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas in Jaipur, the following characteristics can be noted:
**Bani Park** (Jaipur)
Best For: Luxury, Family
Vibe: Bani Park is a tranquil and upscale neighborhood with a mix of modern and traditional architecture, offering a serene atmosphere and easy access to local attractions. The area is known for its beautiful parks and gardens, making it a great spot for families and those seeking relaxation.
Price Range: $50-200/night
Pros: Close proximity to the city center, plenty of dining options, and a range of accommodation choices. The area is also known for its vibrant markets and shopping centers.
Cons: Traffic can be heavy during peak hours, and some areas can be quite crowded.

**Sindhi Camp** (Jaipur)
Best For: Backpacker, Budget
Vibe: Sindhi Camp is a bustling and vibrant neighborhood with a mix of street food stalls, local shops, and budget-friendly accommodations, offering a glimpse into the daily life of Jaipur's residents. The area is known for its lively atmosphere and affordable prices.
Price Range: $10-50/night
Pros: Affordable prices, plenty of street food options, and a lively atmosphere. The area is also close to the city's main bus station and railway station.
Cons: Can be noisy and crowded, and some areas may lack basic amenities.

**C Scheme** (Jaipur)
Best For: Business, Luxury
Vibe: C Scheme is a modern and upscale neighborhood with a mix of office buildings, shopping centers, and luxury hotels, offering a convenient and comfortable stay for business travelers. The area is known for its sleek architecture and modern amenities.
Price Range: $100-500/night
Pros: Close proximity to the city's business district, modern amenities, and a range of dining options. The area is also known for its vibrant nightlife and entertainment options.
Cons: Can be quite expensive, and some areas may lack a traditional Jaipur feel.

**Vaishali Nagar** (Jaipur)
Best For: Family, Mid-range
Vibe: Vaishali Nagar is a quiet and residential neighborhood with a mix of apartments, guesthouses, and mid-range hotels, offering a peaceful and comfortable stay for families and those seeking a local experience. The area is known for its beautiful parks and gardens, making it a great spot for relaxation.
Price Range: $30-100/night
Pros: Affordable prices, plenty of local amenities, and a peaceful atmosphere. The area is also close to the city's main attractions and shopping centers.
Cons: May lack a vibrant nightlife, and some areas can be quite far from the city center.

**Johari Bazaar** (Jaipur)
Best For: Luxury, Shopping
Vibe: Johari Bazaar is a historic and upscale neighborhood with a mix of traditional shops, street food stalls, and luxury hotels, offering a unique and authentic Jaipur experience. The area is known for its vibrant markets and shopping centers, making it a great spot for those seeking to explore the city's rich culture.
Price Range: $50-200/night
Pros: Close proximity to the city's main attractions, plenty of shopping options, and a range of accommodation choices. The area is also known for its beautiful architecture and historic landmarks.
Cons: Can be quite crowded, and some areas may lack modern amenities.

**Tonk Road** (Jaipur)
Best For: Business, Mid-range
Vibe: Tonk Road is a modern and commercial neighborhood with a mix of office buildings, shopping centers, and mid-range hotels, offering a convenient and comfortable stay for business travelers. The area is known for its sleek architecture and modern amenities.
Price Range: $30-100/night
Pros: Close proximity to the city's business district, modern amenities, and a range of dining options. The area is also close to the city's main attractions and shopping centers.
Cons: Can be quite expensive, and some areas may lack a traditional Jaipur feel.

**Amer** (Jaipur)
Best For: Luxury, History
Vibe: Amer is a historic and upscale neighborhood with a mix of traditional architecture, historic landmarks, and luxury hotels, offering a unique and authentic Jaipur experience. The area is known for its beautiful forts and palaces, making it a great spot for those seeking to explore the city's rich history.
Price Range: $50-200/night
Pros: Close proximity to the city's main attractions, plenty of historic landmarks, and a range of accommodation choices. The area is also known for its beautiful architecture and scenic views.
Cons: Can be quite crowded, and some areas may lack modern amenities.

[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]
- Book in advance, especially during peak season (October to March), to ensure availability and the best prices.
- Compare prices across different booking platforms, such as Booking.com, Agoda, and Hotels.com, to find the best deals.
- Consider negotiating prices, especially for longer stays or during the off-season.
- Be aware of any additional fees or taxes, such as the 28% GST (Goods and Services Tax) applicable to hotel bookings in India.
- Read reviews and check the hotel's location, amenities, and services before making a booking.
- Look for discounts and promotions, especially during the off-season or for last-minute bookings.
- Consider booking a package deal that includes accommodation, transportation, and activities for a hassle-free experience.
