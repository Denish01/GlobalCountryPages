[SECTION] What Is Jaipur? [/SECTION]
Jaipur is a city located in the northern part of India, in the state of Rajasthan. It is known for its stunning architecture, vibrant culture, and rich history. The city is often referred to as the **Pink City**, due to the distinctive color of its buildings, which were painted pink in 1876 to welcome the Prince of Wales. Jaipur is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors from all over the world with its stunning **forts**, **palaces**, and **temples**. The city is also a major commercial center, with a strong focus on **textiles**, **jewelry**, and **crafts**.

Geographically, Jaipur is situated in the **Aravalli Hills**, which provide a stunning backdrop to the city. The city is surrounded by several neighboring cities, including Delhi, Agra, and Udaipur. Jaipur is a major hub for **trade** and **commerce**, with a strong focus on **industries** such as **information technology**, **manufacturing**, and **tourism**. The city is also home to several **universities** and **research institutions**, making it a major center for **education** and **research**.

Jaipur is a city with a rich cultural heritage, with a strong focus on **music**, **dance**, and **art**. The city is home to several **festivals** and **events**, including the **Jaipur Literature Festival**, which attracts visitors from all over the world. The city is also known for its **cuisine**, which is a unique blend of **Rajasthani** and **Mughal** flavors. Jaipur is a city that seamlessly blends **tradition** and **modernization**, making it a fascinating destination for visitors.

3. 1947 - India gained independence from British rule, and Jaipur became part of the newly formed country.
4. 1956 - Jaipur became the capital of the Indian state of Rajasthan.
5. 1970s - Jaipur experienced rapid **urbanization** and **industrialization**, leading to significant economic growth.
6. 2000s - Jaipur became a major hub for **information technology** and **tourism**, with several major companies setting up operations in the city.
