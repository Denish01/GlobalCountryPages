# Top Things to Do in Jaipur — Attractions & Experiences

Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, offers a plethora of exciting experiences for visitors, from exploring magnificent forts and palaces to immersing in the city's rich cultural heritage.

| Hawa Mahal | Badi Chaupar, Jaipur | Palace | A unique palace with intricate latticework and stunning architecture, providing a glimpse into the lives of royal women |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take an **elephant safari** through the surrounding countryside, offering a unique perspective on the city's natural beauty
- Explore the **bazaars of Jaipur**, such as Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar, which offer a wide range of traditional handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a **cookery class**, where you can learn to prepare traditional Rajasthani dishes and gain insight into the local cuisine
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the city, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and architecture
- Visit a **local village**, where you can experience rural life and interact with the local community
- Go on a **photography tour**, where you can capture the city's stunning architecture and vibrant culture

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Sariska National Park**, a popular wildlife reserve that is home to a wide range of animals, including tigers, leopards, and crocodiles
- **Abhaneri Step Well**, a unique and ancient step well that is surrounded by beautiful countryside
- **Ramgarh Lake**, a large lake that offers boating and fishing opportunities, as well as stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- **Aravalli Hills**, a range of hills that offer hiking and trekking opportunities, as well as stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- **Galta Ji Temple**, a beautiful temple that is situated in a scenic valley and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Anokhi Museum of Hand Printing**, a unique museum that showcases the art of hand printing and offers workshops and demonstrations
- **Jawahar Kala Kendra**, a cultural center that hosts various art exhibitions, performances, and workshops
- **Hawa Mahal Museum**, a museum that is situated in the Hawa Mahal palace and offers a glimpse into the lives of royal women
- **City Palace Museum**, a museum that showcases the city's rich history and cultural heritage, featuring impressive artifacts and exhibits
- **Ravindra Manch**, a cultural center that hosts various performances, including music, dance, and theater

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Panna Meena Ka Kund**, a unique and ancient step well that is surrounded by beautiful countryside
- **Kanak Vrindavan**, a beautiful garden that is situated in a scenic valley and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- **Moti Dungri Ganesh Temple**, a beautiful temple that is situated on a hill and offers stunning views of the surrounding city
- **Chokhi Dhani**, a rural village that offers a glimpse into traditional Rajasthani life and culture

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Ajmer**, a historic city that is situated 130 km from Jaipur and offers a range of cultural and historical attractions, including the famous Dargah Sharif shrine (2 hours, 130 km)
- **Pushkar**, a sacred city that is situated 145 km from Jaipur and offers a range of cultural and historical attractions, including the famous Pushkar Lake (3 hours, 145 km)
- **Ranthambore National Park**, a popular wildlife reserve that is situated 180 km from Jaipur and offers a range of safari and wildlife viewing opportunities (4 hours, 180 km)
- **Bharatpur**, a historic city that is situated 180 km from Jaipur and offers a range of cultural and historical attractions, including the famous Keoladeo National Park (4 hours, 180 km)
- **Alwar**, a historic city that is situated 160 km from Jaipur and offers a range of cultural and historical attractions, including the famous Bala Qila fort (3 hours, 160 km)
