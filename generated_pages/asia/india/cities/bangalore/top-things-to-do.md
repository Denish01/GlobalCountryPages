# Top Things to Do in Bangalore — Attractions & Experiences

Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka state in India, offers a wide range of activities and attractions that cater to different interests and preferences, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **brewery tour** at one of the many craft breweries in Bangalore, such as Arbor Brewing Company or Microbrewery, to learn about the brewing process and sample local beers
- Explore the **street food scene** in Bangalore, with popular dishes like idlis, vadas, and dosas, at eateries like Vidyarthi Bhavan or MTR
- Attend a **classical music concert** at the Bangalore Gayana Samaja, a renowned cultural institution that promotes Indian classical music
- Visit the **Chokkanahalli Forest** for a **tree-planting** experience, where you can plant saplings and contribute to the city's green cover
- Take a **yoga class** at the Sivananda Yoga Vedanta Centre, which offers classes and workshops in traditional yoga and meditation
- Go on a **cycling tour** of the city, exploring its streets, parks, and lakes, with companies like Bangalore Cycling Experience or Namma Cycle

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Bannerghatta National Park**, a 25,000-acre park with a zoo, safari park, and butterfly garden, home to a wide range of flora and fauna
- **Nandi Hills**, a popular spot for hiking and trekking, with stunning views of the surrounding countryside
- **Hogenakkal Falls**, a beautiful waterfall on the Kaveri River, perfect for boating and swimming
- **Anthargange**, a scenic hill range with caves, temples, and trekking trails, ideal for adventure seekers
- **Skandagiri Hills**, a popular spot for trekking and hiking, with stunning views of the sunrise

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **National Gallery of Modern Art**, a museum showcasing modern and contemporary Indian art, with a collection of over 500 works
- **Kannada Sahitya Parishat**, a cultural institution that promotes Kannada literature and culture, with a museum and library
- **Dharmaraya Swamy Temple**, a historic temple dedicated to Lord Dharmaraya, with stunning architecture and a peaceful atmosphere
- **Kempegowda Museum**, a museum dedicated to the founder of Bangalore, with exhibits on the city's history and culture
- **Ragigudda Sri Prasanna Anjaneyaswamy Temple**, a popular temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, with stunning architecture and a peaceful atmosphere

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Heritage Centre and Aerospace Museum**, a museum showcasing the history of Indian aviation and aerospace, with interactive exhibits and displays
- **The Venkatappa Art Gallery**, a gallery showcasing the works of Indian artists, with a collection of over 600 works
- **The Jayachamarajendra Wadiyar Golf Club**, a golf course with stunning views of the surrounding countryside, perfect for golf enthusiasts
- **The Bangalore Fort**, a historic fort built in the 18th century, with stunning architecture and a peaceful atmosphere
- **The St. John's Church**, a beautiful church with stunning stained-glass windows and a peaceful atmosphere

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Mysore**, a city known for its palaces, gardens, and cultural heritage, located 150 km from Bangalore, 3-hour drive
- **Hassan**, a town known for its ancient temples and historical sites, located 180 km from Bangalore, 4-hour drive
- **Shravanabelagola**, a town known for its ancient Jain temples and historical sites, located 160 km from Bangalore, 3.5-hour drive
- **Nandi Hills**, a popular spot for hiking and trekking, located 60 km from Bangalore, 1.5-hour drive
- **Hogenakkal Falls**, a beautiful waterfall on the Kaveri River, located 140 km from Bangalore, 3-hour drive
