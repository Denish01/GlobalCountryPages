# Cost of Living in Varanasi - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Varanasi can vary depending on your lifestyle, but overall, it is considered an affordable city with a daily budget of $15-30 USD for backpackers, $30-60 USD for mid-range travelers, and $60-100 USD for those seeking comfort.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $15-30 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $60-100 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Varanasi? [/SECTION]
Varanasi is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, and its cost of living is relatively low compared to other major cities in India. The city's affordable prices make it an attractive destination for backpackers and budget travelers. However, prices can vary depending on the location, with areas near the Ganges River and popular tourist spots tend to be more expensive. Compared to its neighbors, Varanasi is generally cheaper than cities like Delhi and Mumbai, but more expensive than smaller towns in the region.

In general, Varanasi offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an excellent choice for travelers and expats looking to experience Indian culture without breaking the bank. The city's rich history, vibrant markets, and stunning riverfront make it a unique and fascinating place to explore, and its affordable prices make it an ideal destination for travelers of all budgets.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse outside the city center to save on accommodation costs
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls to enjoy affordable and delicious meals
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to save on transport costs
- Avoid traveling during peak season (December to February) to save on accommodation and tour prices
- Learn some basic Hindi phrases to negotiate prices and interact with locals
- Take a sunrise boat ride on the Ganges River to experience the city's beauty without breaking the bank

Overall, Varanasi is a city that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, and its affordable prices make it an attractive destination for travelers of all budgets. With some basic knowledge of the city's costs and a few money-saving tips, you can have a wonderful and memorable experience in Varanasi without breaking the bank.
