[SECTION] Location & Borders [/SECTION]

Varanasi is surrounded by the Indian states of Uttar Pradesh, with the cities of Allahabad to the west and Gorakhpur to the east. The city is also close to the Nepal border, which is located about 200 kilometers to the north.

The strategic location of Varanasi has made it an important center of trade and commerce in the region. The city is well-connected to other parts of India by road, rail, and air, with the nearest airport being the Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport, which is located about 25 kilometers from the city center. Varanasi is also an important pilgrimage center, with millions of devotees visiting the city every year to take a dip in the holy Ganges River and visit the numerous temples and ghats that line the riverbank.
