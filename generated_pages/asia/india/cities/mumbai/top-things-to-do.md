# Top Things to Do in Mumbai — Attractions & Experiences

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is a bustling metropolis with a rich cultural heritage, offering a wide range of exciting experiences, from historic landmarks and cultural attractions to unique activities and outdoor adventures, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Slum Tour** to explore the vibrant and resourceful communities of Dharavi, one of the largest slums in Asia
- Experience the **Dabbawala** system, a unique lunch delivery service that has been in operation for over a century
- Visit the **Chor Bazaar** at night, when the market transforms into a bustling hub of activity
- Take a **Street Food Tour** to sample the diverse and delicious cuisine of Mumbai, from vada pav to bhelpuri
- Attend a **Bollywood Dance Class**, where you can learn the iconic moves and choreography of Indian cinema
- Explore the **Kala Ghoda Art District**, a vibrant neighborhood filled with art galleries, museums, and cultural events
- Visit the **Dhobi Ghat**, an open-air laundry facility that has been in operation for over a century

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sanjay Gandhi National Park**, a protected area with a variety of flora and fauna, including lions, tigers, and crocodiles
- Explore the **Borivali National Park**, a smaller but equally beautiful park with hiking trails and scenic views
- Relax on **Juhu Beach**, a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and street food
- Take a **Ferry to Elephanta Island**, where you can explore the ancient caves and enjoy the scenic views of the Arabian Sea
- Visit the **Hanging Gardens**, a beautiful park with stunning views of the city and the sea

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya**, a museum showcasing Indian and Western art and culture
- Explore the **National Gallery of Modern Art**, a museum featuring an impressive collection of modern and contemporary Indian art
- Attend a **Classical Music Performance** at the **NCPA** (National Centre for the Performing Arts)
- Visit the **Siddhivinayak Temple**, one of the most revered temples in the city, dedicated to Lord Ganesha
- Take a **Cooking Class**, where you can learn the art of traditional Indian cuisine
- Visit the **Bhau Daji Lad Museum**, a museum showcasing the history and culture of Mumbai

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Mani Bhavan**, a museum dedicated to the life and legacy of Mahatma Gandhi
- Explore the **Khotachiwadi** neighborhood, a charming area filled with historic homes and narrow streets
- Take a **Walk along the **Worli Sea Face**, a scenic promenade with stunning views of the Arabian Sea
- Visit the **Girgaum Chowpatty**, a popular spot for street food and entertainment
- Attend a **Performance at the **Prithvi Theatre**, a renowned theater company showcasing Indian and international productions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Lonavala**, a hill station located about 100 km from Mumbai, known for its scenic views and trekking trails (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit **Alibag**, a coastal town located about 100 km from Mumbai, known for its beautiful beaches and historic fort (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Explore **Matheran**, a hill station located about 120 km from Mumbai, known for its scenic views and trekking trails (distance: 120 km, travel time: 3 hours)
- Take a day trip to **Khandala**, a hill station located about 80 km from Mumbai, known for its scenic views and trekking trails (distance: 80 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Visit **Karjat**, a town located about 60 km from Mumbai, known for its scenic views and trekking trails (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1 hour)
