# Where to Stay in Ahmedabad — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Ahmedabad, the best areas to stay are typically around the central business district, near major landmarks, or in areas with easy access to public transportation, offering a range of options for different types of travelers and budgets.

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas in Ahmedabad, considerations include the type of traveler, vibe, price range, pros, and cons.

**Navrangpura** (Ahmedabad)
Best For: Business travelers
Vibe: Located in the heart of Ahmedabad, Navrangpura offers a bustling and modern atmosphere, with easy access to shopping and dining options. The area is known for its vibrant nightlife and entertainment venues.
Price Range: $30-100/night
Pros: Close proximity to major business hubs, a wide range of dining and entertainment options, and excellent connectivity to other parts of the city.
Cons: Traffic congestion during peak hours, limited options for budget travelers.

**Vastrapur** (Ahmedabad)
Best For: Families and luxury travelers
Vibe: Vastrapur is a upscale neighborhood with a peaceful and serene atmosphere, surrounded by lush greenery and featuring some of the city's best restaurants and shopping centers. The area is known for its beautiful lakeside views and walking trails.
Price Range: $50-200/night
Pros: Scenic views, proximity to the famous Vastrapur Lake, and a variety of high-end dining and shopping options.
Cons: Limited budget-friendly options, and the area can be quite crowded on weekends.

**Ashram Road** (Ahmedabad)
Best For: Backpackers and budget travelers
Vibe: Ashram Road is a lively and bustling area, known for its vibrant street food scene, local markets, and affordable accommodation options. The area is close to the famous Sabarmati Ashram and offers a glimpse into the city's rich history and culture.
Price Range: $10-50/night
Pros: Affordable accommodation options, a wide range of street food and local markets, and proximity to major historical landmarks.
Cons: Can be quite noisy and crowded, and limited options for luxury travelers.

**Paldi** (Ahmedabad)
Best For: Mid-range travelers and families
Vibe: Paldi is a charming neighborhood with a mix of old and new Ahmedabad, featuring a variety of restaurants, shops, and entertainment options. The area is known for its beautiful parks and gardens, and offers a peaceful atmosphere.
Price Range: $20-80/night
Pros: Affordable accommodation options, a range of dining and entertainment options, and proximity to major parks and gardens.
Cons: Limited options for luxury travelers, and the area can be quite crowded during peak hours.

**S G Road** (Ahmedabad)
Best For: Business travelers and luxury travelers
Vibe: S G Road is a modern and upscale area, known for its high-end shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment options. The area is close to the city's major business hubs and offers excellent connectivity to other parts of the city.
Price Range: $50-250/night
Pros: Close proximity to major business hubs, a wide range of high-end dining and shopping options, and excellent connectivity to other parts of the city.
Cons: Limited options for budget travelers, and the area can be quite crowded during peak hours.

**Ellis Bridge** (Ahmedabad)
Best For: Families and mid-range travelers
Vibe: Ellis Bridge is a scenic neighborhood with a peaceful atmosphere, surrounded by lush greenery and featuring some of the city's best restaurants and shopping centers. The area is known for its beautiful riverside views and walking trails.
Price Range: $20-80/night
Pros: Scenic views, proximity to the famous Sabarmati River, and a variety of mid-range dining and shopping options.
Cons: Limited options for luxury travelers, and the area can be quite crowded on weekends.
