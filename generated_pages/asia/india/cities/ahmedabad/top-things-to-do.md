# Top Things to Do in Ahmedabad — Attractions & Experiences

Ahmedabad, a city in the state of Gujarat, India, offers a rich cultural heritage and a plethora of exciting experiences for visitors, making it a must-visit destination with its unique blend of history, architecture, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Explore the **old city** of Ahmedabad, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring narrow streets, historic monuments, and vibrant markets
- Take a **heritage walk** through the city's historic neighborhoods, such as the Pols and the Chowks, to experience the city's rich cultural heritage
- Visit the **Sabarmati Riverfront**, a recently developed promenade along the Sabarmati River, offering stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere
- Attend a **garba performance**, a traditional Gujarati folk dance, to experience the city's vibrant cultural scene
- Take a **cookery class** to learn about traditional Gujarati cuisine, featuring dishes such as dhokla, handvo, and undhiyu
- Explore the **street food** scene in Ahmedabad, featuring a wide range of traditional snacks and dishes, such as fafda, jalebi, and kachoris
- Visit the **Ahmedabad Haat**, a weekly market featuring local handicrafts, textiles, and souvenirs

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Nalsarovar Bird Sanctuary**, a protected area featuring a wide range of bird species, including migratory birds and waterfowl
- Explore the **Thol Bird Sanctuary**, a wetland area featuring a wide range of bird species, including migratory birds and waterfowl
- Take a **boat ride** on the **Sabarmati River**, offering stunning views of the city and the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Kankaria Lake**, a large lake featuring a wide range of recreational activities, including boating, fishing, and walking trails
- Explore the **Ahmedabad Botanical Garden**, a beautiful garden featuring a wide range of plant species, including flowers, trees, and herbs

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lalbhai Dalpatbhai Museum**, a museum featuring a wide range of Indian art and artifacts, including textiles, sculptures, and paintings
- Explore the **Gujarat Science City**, a science museum featuring interactive exhibits and displays on science and technology
- Attend a **traditional Gujarati festival**, such as the Navratri or the Diwali, to experience the city's vibrant cultural scene
- Visit the **Hutheesing Jain Temple**, a stunning Jain temple featuring intricate carvings and beautiful architecture
- Take a **classical music performance**, featuring traditional Indian instruments and vocals
- Explore the **Ahmedabad Museum**, a museum featuring a wide range of exhibits on the city's history, culture, and art

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sarkhej Roza**, a historic complex featuring a mosque, a tomb, and a palace, showcasing the city's rich cultural heritage
- Explore the **Dada Harir Stepwell**, a beautifully carved stepwell built in 1499, featuring intricate stone carvings and a unique architectural style
- Take a **walk through the Pols**, the historic neighborhoods of Ahmedabad, featuring narrow streets, historic monuments, and vibrant markets
- Visit the **Rani Rupavati Mosque**, a historic mosque built in 1430, featuring a unique blend of Islamic and Hindu architectural styles
- Explore the **Ahmedabad City Museum**, a museum featuring a wide range of exhibits on the city's history, culture, and art
