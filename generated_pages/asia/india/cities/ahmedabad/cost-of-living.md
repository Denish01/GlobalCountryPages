# Cost of Living in Ahmedabad - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Ahmedabad is generally affordable, with a daily budget of $10-30 USD for backpackers, $30-60 USD for mid-range travelers, and $60-100 USD for comfort seekers. 

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $10-30 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $60-100 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Ahmedabad? [/SECTION]
Ahmedabad is a city located in the state of Gujarat, India, and its cost of living is relatively low compared to other major cities in India. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, with a wide range of accommodation options, dining experiences, and transportation modes available at affordable prices. Compared to global averages, Ahmedabad is an affordable destination, with prices significantly lower than those in Western countries. However, prices in Ahmedabad are slightly higher than those in other smaller cities in India.

In terms of neighboring cities, Ahmedabad is more expensive than some of the smaller towns in Gujarat, but it is still relatively affordable compared to major cities like Mumbai or Delhi. The city's affordable cost of living makes it an attractive destination for tourists, business travelers, and expats alike. With its rich cultural heritage, historic landmarks, and modern infrastructure, Ahmedabad offers a great balance of tradition and modernity at a relatively low cost.
