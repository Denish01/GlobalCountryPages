# Cost of Living in Udaipur - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Udaipur is relatively affordable, with a daily budget of $15-30 USD for backpackers, $30-60 USD for mid-range travelers, and $60-100 USD for those seeking comfort.

Daily Budget (Comfort): $60-100 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: expected (5-10%)
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Udaipur? [/SECTION]
Udaipur, a city in the Indian state of Rajasthan, is known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. The cost of living in Udaipur is relatively low compared to other major cities in India, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern amenities, with a range of accommodation options, dining experiences, and transportation modes available at affordable prices. Compared to global averages, Udaipur is a very affordable city, with prices significantly lower than those in Western countries.

In terms of neighboring cities, Udaipur is more expensive than some of the smaller towns in Rajasthan, but less expensive than major cities like Delhi or Mumbai. The city's economy is driven by tourism, with many locals catering to the needs of visitors, which has led to a proliferation of affordable accommodation options, restaurants, and shops. Overall, Udaipur offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an excellent choice for those looking to experience the best of India without breaking the bank.
