[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **boat ride** on Lake Pichola and enjoy the stunning views of the city and the surrounding landscape
- Explore the **local markets** and try some of the traditional Rajasthani cuisine, such as dal baati churma and laal maans
- Visit the **Shilpgram**, a crafts village that showcases the traditional crafts and artisans of Rajasthan
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make some of the traditional Rajasthani dishes
- Go on a **camel safari** and explore the surrounding countryside
- Attend a **traditional folk performance**, such as a puppet show or a folk dance performance
- Take a **yoga class** and learn about the traditional Indian yoga practices
- Go on a **photography tour** and capture the stunning beauty of the city and its surroundings
