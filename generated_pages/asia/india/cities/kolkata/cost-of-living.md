# Cost of Living in Kolkata - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Kolkata is generally affordable, with a daily budget of $15-30 USD per person for backpackers, $30-60 USD for mid-range travelers, and $60-100 USD for those seeking comfort.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $15-30 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $60-100 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 5-10% in restaurants and bars is appreciated
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Kolkata? [/SECTION]
Kolkata, the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal, is a city with a rich cultural heritage and a relatively low cost of living compared to other major cities in India. The cost of living in Kolkata is lower than in cities like Mumbai or Delhi, but higher than in smaller towns and cities in India. Compared to global averages, Kolkata is an affordable city, with prices for accommodation, food, and transportation being relatively low. However, prices can vary depending on the location, with areas like Park Street and Camac Street being more expensive than other parts of the city.

Overall, Kolkata offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for tourists, expats, and locals alike. The city's affordable cost of living, combined with its rich cultural heritage and vibrant atmosphere, make it an ideal place to visit or live. Whether you're a backpacker on a tight budget or a traveler seeking comfort and luxury, Kolkata has something to offer.
