# Where to Stay in Kolkata — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Kolkata, the best areas to stay are those that offer a mix of convenience, comfort, and cultural experiences, with top neighborhoods including Park Street, Camac Street, and Ballygunge, among others. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, consider the following options:
**Park Street** (Kolkata)
Best For: Luxury, Business, Family
Vibe: Known for its upscale restaurants, bars, and shopping centers, Park Street is a vibrant and cosmopolitan area that offers a unique blend of colonial and modern architecture. The area is always bustling with activity, making it ideal for those who enjoy the city life.
Price Range: $50-200/night
Pros: Easy access to major attractions, plenty of dining and entertainment options, well-connected to other parts of the city.
Cons: Can be quite crowded and noisy, limited budget-friendly options.

**Camac Street** (Kolkata)
Best For: Luxury, Business
Vibe: This area is a hub for business and commerce, with many corporate offices and upscale hotels, and is known for its sleek, modern architecture and vibrant nightlife. The area is also home to several high-end shopping centers and restaurants.
Price Range: $40-150/night
Pros: Convenient for business travelers, plenty of high-end amenities, close to major attractions.
Cons: Limited budget-friendly options, can be quite expensive.

**Ballygunge** (Kolkata)
Best For: Family, Luxury
Vibe: This upscale neighborhood is known for its beautiful parks, lakes, and colonial-era architecture, making it a peaceful and serene oasis in the midst of the bustling city. The area is also home to several high-end shopping centers and restaurants.
Price Range: $30-100/night
Pros: Peaceful and scenic, plenty of green spaces, close to major attractions.
Cons: Limited budget-friendly options, can be quite expensive.

**Esplanade** (Kolkata)
Best For: Backpacker, Budget
Vibe: This historic area is known for its colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and vibrant street food scene, making it a great option for budget-conscious travelers who want to experience the city's rich cultural heritage. The area is always bustling with activity, making it ideal for those who enjoy the city life.
Price Range: $10-50/night
Pros: Affordable, plenty of street food options, close to major attractions.
Cons: Can be quite crowded and noisy, limited upscale amenities.

**Salt Lake** (Kolkata)
Best For: Business, Family
Vibe: This modern neighborhood is known for its sleek, contemporary architecture and vibrant nightlife, making it a great option for business travelers and families who want to experience the city's modern side. The area is also home to several high-end shopping centers and restaurants.
Price Range: $20-80/night
Pros: Convenient for business travelers, plenty of modern amenities, close to major attractions.
Cons: Limited budget-friendly options, can be quite expensive.

[SECTION] Booking Tips [/SECTION]
- Book in advance, especially during peak season (October to February), to ensure availability and the best rates.
- Use online booking platforms like Booking.com, Agoda, or MakeMyTrip to compare prices and find deals.
- Consider negotiating the price, especially when booking directly with a hotel or guesthouse.
- Look for seasonal discounts and promotions, especially during the off-season.
- Be sure to read reviews and check the hotel's location and amenities before booking.
- Use travel apps like TripIt or Hotel Tonight to find last-minute deals and discounts.
- Consider booking a package deal that includes accommodation, transportation, and sightseeing to save money and time.

[SECTION] Safety & Location Tips [/SECTION]
- Be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas and at night, and keep an eye on your belongings.
- Avoid carrying large amounts of cash and use reputable taxi services or ride-hailing apps instead of hailed taxis.
- Stay in well-lit and populated areas, especially at night, and avoid walking alone in dimly lit or deserted areas.
- Keep your hotel room door locked and secure, and use the hotel safe to store valuables.
- Be mindful of local customs and traditions, and dress modestly when visiting temples or other cultural sites.
- Consider staying in areas with easy access to public transportation, such as the metro or bus stations, to make it easier to get around the city.
