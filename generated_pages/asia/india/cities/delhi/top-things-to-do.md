# Top Things to Do in Delhi — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Delhi, visit the Red Fort, explore the street food scene in Chandni Chowk, and marvel at the stunning architecture of the Akshardham Temple, which are just a few of the many top attractions and unique experiences that this vibrant city has to offer.

| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Qutub Minar | Mehrauli | Historical Monument | Tallest minaret in India and symbol of Delhi's rich history |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **street food tour** in Chandni Chowk to sample the city's famous cuisine, including chaat, kebabs, and jalebi.
- Explore the **Sunder Nursery**, a beautiful park with stunning gardens and a tranquil atmosphere.
- Visit the **Dilli Haat**, a marketplace showcasing Indian handicrafts, textiles, and cuisine.
- Take a **cycle rickshaw tour** of Old Delhi to experience the city's vibrant streets and historic landmarks.
- Attend a **classical music performance** at the India Habitat Centre to experience the city's rich cultural heritage.
- Visit the **Hauz Khas Village**, a trendy neighborhood with boutique shops, restaurants, and bars.
- Explore the **National Crafts Museum**, which showcases India's rich craft heritage and offers workshops and demonstrations.

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lodhi Garden**, a beautiful park with stunning gardens, walking trails, and historic monuments.
- Explore the **Okhla Bird Sanctuary**, a haven for birdwatchers with over 300 species of birds.
- Take a **hike** in the **Aravalli Hills**, which offer stunning views of the city and surrounding countryside.
- Visit the **Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary**, a popular spot for birdwatching and nature walks.
- Explore the **Yamuna Biodiversity Park**, a unique park with a variety of plant and animal species.

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum**, which offers a comprehensive overview of Indian history, art, and culture.
- Explore the **Crafts Museum**, which showcases India's rich craft heritage and offers workshops and demonstrations.
- Attend a **performance** at the **Kamani Auditorium**, a popular venue for music, dance, and theater.
- Visit the **Sanskriti Museums**, which showcase Indian art, culture, and traditions.
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about Indian cuisine and cooking techniques.
- Explore the **Hauz Khas Complex**, a historic site with a mosque, tomb, and madrasa.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Shankar's International Dolls Museum**, a unique museum with a collection of dolls from around the world.
- Explore the **Khirki Masjid**, a historic mosque with stunning architecture and a peaceful atmosphere.
- Take a **walk** through the **Nizamuddin Basti**, a historic neighborhood with narrow streets and charming shops.
- Visit the **Ghalib Academy**, a museum dedicated to the life and works of the famous Urdu poet Mirza Ghalib.
- Explore the **Chhatarpur Temple**, a beautiful temple with stunning architecture and a peaceful atmosphere.

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **Agra** (230 km, 4 hours): Visit the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri.
2. **Jaipur** (280 km, 5 hours): Explore the Pink City, including the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal.
3. **Mathura** (180 km, 3 hours): Visit the birthplace of Lord Krishna and explore the city's historic temples and landmarks.
4. **Vrindavan** (190 km, 3.5 hours): Explore the city's historic temples and landmarks, including the Banke Bihari Temple and ISKCON Temple.
5. **Rishikesh** (230 km, 4.5 hours): Visit the yoga capital of India and explore the city's ashrams, temples, and scenic landscapes.
