# Top Things to Do in Agra — Attractions & Experiences

Agra, a city in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India, is home to some of the most iconic and culturally significant attractions in the world, and the top things to do here include visiting the Taj Mahal, exploring the Agra Fort, and discovering the city's rich history and heritage.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Taj Mahal | Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj | Monument | A stunning white marble mausoleum and one of the Seven Wonders of the World |
| Agra Fort | Agra Fort, Rakabganj | Fort | A historic fort that showcases the city's rich Mughal heritage and architecture |
| Fatehpur Sikri | Fatehpur Sikri, Uttar Pradesh | Historical Site | A abandoned city that was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and features stunning architecture |
| Itmad-Ud-Daulah's Tomb | Moti Bagh, Agra | Mausoleum | A beautiful tomb that is often referred to as the "Baby Taj" due to its stunning white marble and intricate inlays |
| Akbar's Mausoleum | Sikandra, Agra | Mausoleum | A historic mausoleum that is the final resting place of the great Mughal emperor Akbar |
| Jama Masjid | Kinari Bazar, Hing ki Mandi, Agra | Mosque | A beautiful mosque that features stunning architecture and is one of the largest in India |
| Mehtab Bagh | Nagla Devjit, Agra | Garden | A beautiful garden that offers stunning views of the Taj Mahal and is a great spot for sunset |
| Chini Ka Rauza | Chini Ka Rauza, Agra | Mausoleum | A historic mausoleum that is the final resting place of the poet and prime minister of the Mughal Empire, Afzal Khan |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Sunrise or Sunset Tour** of the Taj Mahal to experience the stunning monument in the golden light of dawn or dusk
- Explore the **Local Markets** of Agra, such as the Kinari Bazar and the Raja Ki Mandi, to experience the city's vibrant culture and shop for local handicrafts
- Take a **Cooking Class** to learn how to make traditional Indian cuisine, such as **Biryani** and **Tandoori Chicken**
- Go on a **Guided Tour** of the Agra Fort and learn about the history and significance of the monument
- Take a **Yoga Class** on the banks of the Yamuna River and experience the city's spiritual side
- Attend a **Classical Music Performance** at one of the city's many cultural venues and experience the rich musical heritage of India

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Yamuna River** and take a boat ride to experience the city's natural beauty
- Explore the **Keetham Lake** and enjoy the scenic views and birdwatching opportunities
- Go on a **Hiking Trip** to the nearby **Aravalli Hills** and experience the natural beauty of the region
- Visit the **Sur Sarovar Bird Sanctuary** and spot a variety of bird species, including the **Sarus Crane** and the **Indian Peafowl**
- Take a **Cycling Tour** of the city and explore the scenic countryside and rural villages

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Taj Museum** to learn about the history and significance of the Taj Mahal
- Explore the **Agra Archaeological Museum** to learn about the city's rich cultural heritage and history
- Attend a **Traditional Indian Dance Performance**, such as the **Kathak** or the **Bharatanatyam**
- Visit the **Kailash Temple** and experience the city's spiritual side
- Take a **Guided Tour** of the **Fatehpur Sikri** and learn about the history and significance of the abandoned city

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Chini Ka Rauza**, a historic mausoleum that is often overlooked by tourists
- Explore the **Kinari Bazar**, a vibrant market that offers a glimpse into the city's culture and daily life
- Take a **Walking Tour** of the city and discover the many **Hidden Temples** and **Mosques** that are scattered throughout the city
- Visit the **Ram Bagh**, a beautiful garden that offers stunning views of the Taj Mahal
- Attend a **Local Festival**, such as the **Taj Mahotsav**, to experience the city's vibrant culture and traditions

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Fatehpur Sikri**, a historic city that is located just 40 kilometers from Agra and offers a glimpse into the city's rich cultural heritage
- Visit the **Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary**, a scenic park that is located just 60 kilometers from Agra and offers a variety of birdwatching opportunities
- Take a day trip to **Mathura**, a historic city that is located just 60 kilometers from Agra and is known for its stunning temples and cultural significance
- Visit the **Vrindavan**, a scenic town that is located just 15 kilometers from Mathura and is known for its stunning temples and spiritual significance
- Take a day trip to **Jaipur**, a historic city that is located just 240 kilometers from Agra and is known for its stunning architecture and cultural significance
