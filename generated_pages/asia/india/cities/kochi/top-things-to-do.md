[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Kerala Ayurveda massage** to rejuvenate your body and mind at one of the many spas in the city
- Attend a **Kathakali performance**, a traditional dance form that originated in Kerala, to experience the rich cultural heritage of the region
- Go on a **spice tour** to learn about the different types of spices that are grown and traded in the region
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the traditional cuisine of Kerala and the different cooking techniques used in the region
- Explore the **street art scene** in Fort Kochi, which features a collection of murals and graffiti created by local and international artists
- Visit the **Kochi Biennale**, a contemporary art festival that showcases the work of local and international artists
- Go on a **houseboat cruise** through the backwaters of Kochi to experience the natural beauty of the region

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Thattekad Bird Sanctuary**, a protected area that is home to a wide variety of bird species
- Explore the **Kerala Backwaters**, a network of lakes, rivers, and canals that are home to a diverse range of flora and fauna
- Go on a **hiking trip** to the **Western Ghats**, a mountain range that is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna
- Visit the **Cherai Beach**, a popular beach that is known for its scenic views and water sports
- Take a **boat ride** through the **Periyar National Park**, a protected area that is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including elephants, tigers, and monkeys

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kerala Folklore Museum**, a museum that showcases the traditional art and culture of Kerala
- Explore the **Jew Town**, a historic neighborhood that is home to a wide variety of shops, restaurants, and cultural attractions
- Attend a **classical music performance**, a traditional form of music that originated in Kerala
- Visit the **Paradesi Synagogue**, a historic synagogue that is one of the oldest in India
- Take a **yoga class**, a traditional form of exercise that originated in India
- Visit the **Mattancherry Palace**, a historic palace that is home to a wide variety of artifacts and artwork

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
