# Cost of Living in Kochi - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Kochi is relatively affordable, with a daily budget of $15-30 USD for backpackers, $30-60 USD for mid-range travelers, and $60-100 USD for comfort seekers. 

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $15-30 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $60-100 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Kochi? [/SECTION]
Kochi is a city in the state of Kerala, India, and its cost of living is relatively low compared to other major cities in the country. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, with a rich history and natural beauty. Compared to global averages, Kochi is an affordable destination, with prices for accommodation, food, and transportation being relatively low. However, prices can vary depending on the location, with areas like Fort Kochi and MG Road tend to be more expensive than other parts of the city.

In comparison to its neighbors, Kochi has a similar cost of living to other cities in Kerala, such as Thiruvananthapuram and Kozhikode. However, it is more expensive than some of the smaller towns and villages in the state. Overall, Kochi offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in budget-friendly accommodations, such as hostels or guesthouses, to save on rent.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls, which offer delicious and affordable meals.
- Use public transportation, such as buses or autos, to get around the city.
- Avoid traveling during peak season, when prices for accommodations and transportation tend to be higher.
- Learn to haggle when shopping at local markets or from street vendors.
- Take advantage of the city's many free or low-cost attractions, such as the Fort Kochi beach or the Dutch Palace.
