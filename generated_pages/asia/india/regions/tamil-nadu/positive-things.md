[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Brihadeeswara Temple** in Thanjavur is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest temples in India, known for its intricate carvings and sculptures.
- The **Bharatanatyam** dance form is a classical art form that originated in Tamil Nadu and is known for its beauty and elegance.
- The **Pongal** festival is a traditional harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu, with decorations, music, and traditional foods like **pongal** and **vadai**.
- The **Chennai Music Season** is a annual festival that showcases traditional Carnatic music and dance performances by renowned artists.
- The **Tamil Nadu Museum** in Chennai is a repository of the state's rich cultural heritage, with a collection of artifacts, sculptures, and paintings.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **A.P.J. Abdul Kalam**, the former President of India, was born in Tamil Nadu and is known for his contributions to the country's space program.
- The **Tamil diaspora** has made significant contributions to the global economy, with many Tamil entrepreneurs and professionals holding key positions in multinational companies.
- **Ravi Shankar**, the renowned sitar player, was born in Tamil Nadu and is credited with popularizing Indian classical music globally.
- The **Tamil community** has a strong presence in countries like the United States, United Kingdom, and Singapore, with many Tamil associations and cultural organizations promoting the language and culture.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **hospitality** of the Tamil people, with visitors often commenting on the warm welcome and friendly nature of the locals.
- The **traditional Tamil cuisine**, with popular dishes like **idli**, **dosai**, and **vadai** being a favorite among visitors.
- The **scenic beauty** of the state, with the **Western Ghats** and **Eastern Ghats** mountain ranges offering stunning views and trekking opportunities.
- The **rich cultural heritage**, with many visitors attending traditional festivals and cultural events like the **Pongal** festival and **Chennai Music Season**.
- The **beautiful beaches** along the **Coromandel Coast**, with popular destinations like **Marina Beach** and **Kovalam Beach** being a favorite among tourists.
- The **spiritual significance** of the state, with many visitors visiting ancient temples like the **Brihadeeswara Temple** and **Meenakshi Amman Temple**.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Hogenakkal Falls** on the **Kaveri River** is a lesser-known tourist destination, with the falls offering a stunning view and a chance to take a **coracle ride**.
- The **Tamil Nadu Hill Stations** like **Ooty** and **Kodaikanal** offer a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life, with scenic views and trekking opportunities.
- The **Chettinad region** is known for its rich cultural heritage and traditional architecture, with many visitors attending **Chettinad festivals** and trying **Chettinad cuisine**.
- The **Pichavaram Mangrove Forest** is a unique ecosystem with a diverse range of flora and fauna, with visitors taking **boat rides** to explore the forest.
