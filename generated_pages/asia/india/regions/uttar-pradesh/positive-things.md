[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Taj Mahal**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, attracting millions of visitors every year.
- The **Kumbh Mela**, a major Hindu festival, is held in Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh, and is recognized by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity.
- The **Lucknow Chikan**, a traditional embroidery style, is a significant part of the region's cultural heritage and is known for its intricate designs and patterns.
- The **Ram Leela**, a traditional folk theater form, is an important part of Uttar Pradesh's cultural landscape, with many performances held during the Navratri festival.
- The **Varanasi Ghats**, a series of steps leading to the Ganges River, are a sacred site for Hindus and a significant cultural treasure of the region.
