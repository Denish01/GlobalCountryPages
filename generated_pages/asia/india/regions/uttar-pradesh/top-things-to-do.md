# Top Things to Do in Uttar Pradesh — Attractions & Experiences

Uttar Pradesh, a region in northern India, offers a diverse range of activities and attractions that cater to different interests and preferences, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Taj Mahal | Agra | Monument | A stunning white marble mausoleum and one of the Seven Wonders of the World |
| Agra Fort | Agra | Fort | A historic fort that showcases Mughal architecture and offers breathtaking views of the Taj Mahal |
| Varanasi Ghats | Varanasi | Ghats | A series of riverbank stairs along the Ganges River that offer a glimpse into the city's spiritual and cultural heritage |
| Fatehpur Sikri | Agra | Historical Site | A abandoned city that was once the capital of the Mughal Empire and features impressive architecture and ruins |
| Allahabad Fort | Prayagraj | Fort | A historic fort that dates back to the Mughal era and offers stunning views of the Ganges and Yamuna rivers |
| Lucknow Residency | Lucknow | Historical Site | A complex of buildings that showcases the region's rich history and cultural heritage |
| Sarnath | Varanasi | Archaeological Site | A historic site that marks the spot where Buddha delivered his first sermon and features several ruins and monuments |
| Ramnagar Fort | Varanasi | Fort | A historic fort that dates back to the 18th century and offers stunning views of the Ganges River |
[/TABLE]
