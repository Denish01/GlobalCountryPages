# Cost of Living in Maharashtra - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Maharashtra is generally moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $20-50 USD for backpackers to $100-200 USD for mid-range travelers and $250-500 USD for those seeking comfort.

Daily Budget (Comfort): $100-250 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: expected (5-10%)
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Maharashtra? [/SECTION]
Maharashtra, being one of the most populous and economically developed states in India, has a cost of living that is relatively moderate compared to other major cities in the world. However, prices can vary greatly depending on the location, with cities like Mumbai and Pune being more expensive than smaller towns and rural areas. Compared to its neighboring states, Maharashtra is generally more expensive, but still offers a great value for money for tourists and expats alike.

Maharashtra's economy is driven by a mix of industries, including technology, manufacturing, and services, which has led to a growth in the standard of living and a corresponding increase in prices. However, the state still offers a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, rich history, and natural beauty, making it an attractive destination for travelers. With some planning and research, visitors can find affordable options for accommodation, food, and transportation, making Maharashtra an excellent choice for travelers with a moderate budget.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in smaller towns or outside city centers to save on accommodation costs
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls to experience authentic cuisine at a lower cost
- Use public transport or walk/bike whenever possible to save on transportation costs
- Avoid traveling during peak season (December to February) to get better deals on flights and accommodation
- Learn some basic Hindi and Marathi phrases to negotiate prices and interact with locals
- Take advantage of the many free or low-cost cultural and historical attractions in Maharashtra, such as the Gateway of India and the Ellora Caves
