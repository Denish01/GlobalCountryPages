# Top Things to Do in Maharashtra — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Maharashtra, visit its iconic attractions, immerse in unique experiences, and explore its rich cultural heritage, vibrant cities, and breathtaking natural landscapes.

| Ajanta Caves | Aurangabad | Ancient Buddhist Caves | UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring intricate rock-cut architecture and paintings |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **street food tour** in Mumbai to sample the city's diverse culinary delights, from vada pav to bhelpuri
- Experience the **Ganesh Chaturthi festival** in Mumbai, a 10-day celebration of Lord Ganesha's birthday
- Visit a **traditional Maharashtrian village** to learn about rural life and culture
- Go on a **wine tasting tour** in Nashik, a major wine-producing region in India
- Attend a **classical music performance** at the National Centre for the Performing Arts in Mumbai
- Take a **yoga class** in Pune, a city known for its wellness and spiritual retreats

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Western Ghats**, a mountain range that stretches along the western coast of India and is home to numerous national parks and wildlife sanctuaries
- Visit the **Pench National Park**, known for its tiger reserve and diverse wildlife
- Relax on the **Juhu Beach** in Mumbai, a popular spot for swimming and sunset-watching
- Go **hiking** in the **Sahyadri Mountains**, a range that offers breathtaking views and challenging trails
- Take a **boat ride** on the **Tungabhadra River**, which flows through the city of Pune

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya**, a museum in Mumbai that showcases the city's history and culture
- Explore the **Buddhist caves** at Karla and Bhaja, ancient rock-cut temples that date back to the 2nd century BCE
- Attend a **traditional Maharashtrian dance performance**, such as the Lavani or Tamasha
- Visit the **Siddhivinayak Temple**, one of the most famous temples in India, dedicated to Lord Ganesha
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Maharashtrian cuisine and its unique flavors and ingredients

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Bund Garden** in Pune, a beautiful park that offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape
- Explore the **Kolad** region, known for its scenic valleys and picturesque villages
- Take a **trek** to the **Rajmachi Fort**, a historic fort that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills
- Visit the **Aga Khan Palace** in Pune, a historic palace that showcases the city's rich cultural heritage
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Mayani Bird Sanctuary**, a protected area that is home to numerous species of birds

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Mumbai to **Lonavala** (distance: 82 km, travel time: 2 hours), a hill station known for its scenic beauty and trekking trails
- Visit **Ellora Caves** (distance: 350 km, travel time: 6 hours) from Pune, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring ancient rock-cut temples
- Explore the **Alibag** region (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2.5 hours) from Mumbai, known for its scenic beaches and historic forts
- Go on a day trip from Pune to **Mahabaleshwar** (distance: 120 km, travel time: 3 hours), a hill station known for its scenic beauty and strawberry farms
- Visit the **Aurangabad** region (distance: 350 km, travel time: 6 hours) from Mumbai, known for its ancient caves and historical landmarks
