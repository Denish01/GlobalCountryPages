[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Padmanabhaswamy Temple** in Thiruvananthapuram is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the wealthiest temples in the world.
- The **Kathakali** dance form is a unique and intricate art form that tells stories from Hindu epics.
- The **Thrissur Pooram** festival is a vibrant and colorful celebration that showcases Kerala's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Chettikulangara Devi Temple** is a famous temple dedicated to the goddess Bhagavathi, known for its intricate carvings and beautiful architecture.
- The **Theyyam** ritual is an ancient and unique art form that combines music, dance, and theater to tell stories from Hindu mythology.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Narayana Murthy**, co-founder of Infosys, is a notable figure from Kerala who has made significant contributions to India's IT industry.
- The **Kerala diaspora** has made significant contributions to the global economy, with many Keralites working in the Middle East and other parts of the world.
- **Arundhati Roy**, a renowned author, is from Kerala and has won several awards for her literary works, including the Booker Prize.
- The **values of Kerala**, such as its strong emphasis on education and social welfare, have made it a model for other regions to follow.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **backwaters of Kerala** are a popular tourist destination, with many visitors enjoying boat rides and stays in traditional houseboats.
- The **cuisine of Kerala** is known for its unique flavors and spices, with popular dishes like **sadhya** and **idlis**.
- The **beaches of Kerala**, such as **Kovalam** and **Varkala**, are popular for their scenic beauty and water sports.
- The **hill stations of Kerala**, such as **Munnar** and **Wayanad**, are known for their scenic beauty and trekking trails.
- The **festivals of Kerala**, such as **Onam** and **Vishu**, are colorful and vibrant celebrations that showcase the state's rich cultural heritage.
- The **hospitality of Kerala** is renowned for its warmth and generosity, with many visitors praising the state's friendly and welcoming people.

- The **Edakkal caves** are a unique and fascinating destination that features ancient carvings and rock formations.
- The **Bekal fort** is a historic fort that is known for its beautiful architecture and scenic views.
- The **Kerala folklore museum** is a museum that showcases the state's rich cultural heritage, with a wide range of artifacts and exhibits on display.
