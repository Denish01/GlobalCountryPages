# Top Things to Do in Kerala — Attractions & Experiences

Kerala, a region in southwestern India, offers a diverse range of activities and experiences that cater to different interests, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to explore its rich culture, stunning natural beauty, and unique traditions. 

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Periyar National Park | Thekkady | Wildlife Sanctuary | To see **Indian elephants**, **tigers**, and other wildlife in their natural habitat |
| Backwaters of Alleppey | Alleppey | Natural Wonder | For a **houseboat cruise** and to experience the local way of life |
| Fort Kochi | Kochi | Historical Site | To explore the **Dutch**, **Portuguese**, and **British** colonial architecture and history |
| Padmanabhaswamy Temple | Thiruvananthapuram | Temple | To admire the **Dravidian architecture** and learn about the temple's **legendary treasures** |
| Munnar Tea Plantations | Munnar | Tea Estate | To learn about **tea production** and enjoy the scenic views of the tea gardens |
| Athirapally Falls | Thrissur | Waterfall | To marvel at the **80-foot waterfall** and hike in the surrounding forest |
| Bekal Fort | Kasaragod | Historical Site | To explore the **16th-century fort** and enjoy the stunning views of the Arabian Sea |
| Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary | Kumarakom | Bird Sanctuary | To spot **migratory birds** and enjoy a **boat ride** in the backwaters |
| Edakkal Caves | Wayanad | Cave | To explore the **prehistoric rock shelters** and learn about the **ancient civilizations** |
| Marari Beach | Alleppey | Beach | To relax and enjoy the **tranquil atmosphere** of this **pristine beach** |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Ayurvedic massage** at one of the many spas and wellness centers in Kerala
- Attend a **Kathakali performance**, a traditional dance-drama that tells stories from Indian mythology
- Learn about the **art of Kerala cuisine** and take a cooking class to prepare local dishes
- Go on a **spice plantation tour** and learn about the production of **pepper**, **cardamom**, and other spices
- Participate in a **yoga or meditation retreat** and rejuvenate your mind and body
- Take a **village tour** and interact with the local community to learn about their customs and traditions

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Periyar National Park** and spot **wildlife** such as elephants, tigers, and deer
- Hike to the **top of Anamudi**, the highest peak in southern India
- Visit the **Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary** and spot **migratory birds** such as the Siberian crane
- Relax on the **beaches of Kovalam** and enjoy the **water sports** such as surfing and kayaking
- Take a **trekking trip** in the **Western Ghats** and explore the **scenic valleys** and **mountains**

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Padmanabhaswamy Temple** and learn about the **history and legends** of the temple
- Explore the **Kerala Folklore Museum** and learn about the **traditional arts and crafts** of the state
- Attend a **traditional festival** such as the **Onam** or **Vishu** and experience the local culture
- Take a **visit to the Jewish Synagogue** in Kochi and learn about the **history of the Jewish community** in Kerala
- Visit the **Trichur Pooram** and watch the **elephant procession** and **traditional performances**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Aranmula village** and watch the **traditional mirror-making** process
- Explore the **Chinnakanal waterfalls** and enjoy the **scenic views** of the surrounding landscape
- Take a **boat ride** in the **Kuttanad backwaters** and experience the **local way of life**
- Visit the **Thiruvananthapuram zoo** and see the **rare and endangered species** of animals
- Explore the **Muziris heritage site** and learn about the **ancient history** of the region

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Kochi to **Athirapally Falls** (distance: 70 km, travel time: 2 hours) and enjoy the scenic views of the waterfall
- Visit the **Guruvayur Temple** from Thrissur (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour) and learn about the **history and legends** of the temple
- Take a day trip from Alleppey to **Marari Beach** (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes) and relax on the beach
- Visit the **Bekal Fort** from Kasaragod (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes) and explore the **historical site**
- Take a day trip from Munnar to **Top Station** (distance: 40 km, travel time: 1.5 hours) and enjoy the **scenic views** of the tea gardens
