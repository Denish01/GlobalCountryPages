# Cost of Living in Kerala - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Kerala is generally affordable, with a daily budget of $15-30 USD for backpackers, $30-60 USD for mid-range travelers, and $60-100 USD for comfort seekers.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $15-30 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $60-100 USD
Currency: INR
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 5-10% in high-end establishments
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Kerala? [/SECTION]
Kerala is a region in southern India known for its lush green landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and relatively low cost of living. Compared to other major cities in India, Kerala is an affordable destination, with prices lower than in cities like Mumbai or Delhi. However, compared to other states in southern India, Kerala is slightly more expensive, particularly when it comes to accommodation and food. Overall, Kerala offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

In terms of global averages, Kerala is significantly cheaper than many Western countries, but more expensive than some other Asian destinations. For example, a meal at a local eatery in Kerala might cost $3-5 USD, while a similar meal in the United States or Europe might cost $10-15 USD. However, Kerala's prices are comparable to those in other Indian states, making it a great value destination for travelers.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in budget-friendly accommodations like hostels or guesthouses, which can save you up to 50% on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls, which offer delicious and affordable meals.
- Use public transport like buses and auto-rickshaws, which are cheap and convenient.
- Avoid traveling during peak season (December to February), when prices for accommodation and transport tend to be higher.
- Try to negotiate prices when shopping at local markets or taking taxis, as this can help you save up to 10-20% on costs.
- Consider buying a local SIM card or portable Wi-Fi hotspot, which can help you save up to 50% on international roaming charges.

Overall, Kerala is an affordable destination that offers a great balance of quality and price. With some basic planning and research, you can have a comfortable and enjoyable trip to Kerala without breaking the bank.
