- Foreigners can only buy property in designated **freehold areas**, such as The Pearl-Qatar and Lusail.
- Foreigners can also buy property through a **leasehold agreement**, which allows them to use the property for a specified period of time.
- Foreign companies can buy property in Qatar, but they must be registered with the Qatari authorities.
- Foreigners must also obtain a **residence visa** to buy property in Qatar.
