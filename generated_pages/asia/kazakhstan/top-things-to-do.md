# Top Things to Do in Kazakhstan — Attractions & Experiences

Kazakhstan offers a diverse range of exciting activities and attractions for visitors to explore, from its stunning natural landscapes to its rich cultural heritage. 

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Bayterek Tower | Astana | Monument | Offers panoramic views of the city and is a symbol of Astana's modernization |
| Khan Shatyr Entertainment Center | Astana | Shopping and Entertainment | Features a large shopping mall, entertainment complex, and a beach club with an artificial beach |
| Turkestan | Turkestan | Historical Site | A city with a rich history, featuring the Mausoleum of Khawaja Ahmad Yasawi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Kazakh eagle hunting** lesson and learn about the ancient art of hunting with golden eagles
- Visit a **yurt camp** and experience the traditional nomadic lifestyle of the Kazakh people
- Attend a **Nauryz celebration**, a traditional Kazakh holiday marking the beginning of spring
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the stunning landscapes of the Altai Mountains
- Learn about **Kazakh cuisine** and take a cooking class to learn how to prepare traditional dishes such as beshbarmak and shashlik
- Take a **train ride** on the Turkestan-Siberia Railway, which offers breathtaking views of the Kazakh countryside
- Visit a **local bazaar** and try some of the traditional Kazakh foods and drinks, such as kuyrdak and shubat

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Altai Mountains**, a stunning mountain range featuring glaciers, lakes, and forests
- Visit **Kolsay Lakes**, a system of three lakes surrounded by beautiful mountains and forests
- Go **hiking** in the **Trans-Ili Alatau National Park**, which features stunning mountain scenery and a variety of wildlife
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Caspian Sea**, the largest inland body of water in the world
- Visit the **Aksu-Zhabagly Nature Reserve**, which is home to a variety of wildlife, including the rare snow leopard

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Kazakhstan**, which features a collection of exhibits on Kazakh history and culture
- Attend a **traditional Kazakh music performance**, featuring instruments such as the dombra and the kui
- Explore the **Turkestan Historical and Cultural Reserve**, which features a collection of historical buildings and monuments
- Take a **tour of the Almaty Mosque**, a beautiful example of Islamic architecture
- Visit the **Kasteyev State Museum of Arts**, which features a collection of Kazakh and Russian art
- Attend a **performance of the Kazakh State Opera and Ballet**, which features a variety of traditional and modern performances

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Saryarka Steppe and Lakes of Northern Kazakhstan**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring a unique landscape of steppes and lakes
- Explore the **Mangystau Region**, which features a unique landscape of canyons, mountains, and deserts
- Take a **tour of the Almaty TV Tower**, which offers panoramic views of the city
- Visit the **Atyrau Regional Museum**, which features a collection of exhibits on the history and culture of the region
- Go on a **trekking tour** of the **Tian Shan Mountains**, which features stunning mountain scenery and a variety of wildlife

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Almaty** to the **Turgen Gorge**, which features stunning mountain scenery and a variety of hiking trails (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Big Almaty Lake** from **Almaty**, which features stunning mountain scenery and a variety of hiking trails (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a day trip from **Astana** to the **Burabai National Park**, which features stunning natural scenery and a variety of outdoor activities (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)
- Visit the **Kolsay Lakes** from **Almaty**, which features stunning mountain scenery and a variety of hiking trails (distance: 300 km, travel time: 5 hours)
- Take a day trip from **Turkestan** to the **Sauran Ancient Settlement**, which features a collection of historical ruins and monuments (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour)
