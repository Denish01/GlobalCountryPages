[SECTION] What Is Sri Lanka? [/SECTION]
Sri Lanka is a **sovereign** island nation located in **Asia**, situated in the Indian Ocean, off the coast of India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and friendly people. The country has a long history dating back to ancient times, with many historical sites and landmarks that attract tourists from around the world. Sri Lanka is also famous for its delicious cuisine, which is a unique blend of Indian, Chinese, and Middle Eastern flavors.

Geographically, Sri Lanka is an island with a total area of approximately 65,610 square kilometers, making it a relatively small country. It has a diverse landscape, with mountains, forests, and beaches, and is home to a wide range of flora and fauna. The country has a tropical climate, with two main monsoon seasons that bring rain to different parts of the island at different times of the year.

Sri Lanka is a popular tourist destination, with many attractions and activities to offer visitors. From the ancient cities of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa to the beautiful beaches of Mirissa and Weligama, there is something for everyone in Sri Lanka. The country is also known for its vibrant culture, with many festivals and celebrations throughout the year, including the famous **Esala Perahera** festival in Kandy.

[SECTION] Key History [/SECTION]
1. 3000 BC - The first human settlements in Sri Lanka date back to this time, with the arrival of the **Vedda** people, who are believed to be the indigenous inhabitants of the island.
2. 550 BC - The **Buddhist** faith was introduced to Sri Lanka by **Arahath Mahinda**, a Buddhist monk who arrived on the island during the reign of King **Devanampiyatissa**.
3. 1215 AD - The **Polonnaruwa** kingdom was established, marking the beginning of a new era in Sri Lankan history, during which the island was unified under a single ruler.
4. 1505 AD - The **Portuguese** arrived in Sri Lanka, marking the beginning of European colonization, which would last for over four centuries.
5. 1948 AD - Sri Lanka gained **independence** from British colonial rule, becoming a sovereign nation.
6. 1983 AD - The **Sri Lankan Civil War** broke out, a conflict that would last for over two decades and have a profound impact on the country.
7. 2009 AD - The Sri Lankan government declared victory over the **Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE)**, bringing an end to the civil war.

- Sri Lanka is home to a wide range of **wildlife**, including elephants, leopards, and sloth bears, which can be found in the country's many national parks and wildlife reserves.
- The country has a unique **cuisine**, which is a blend of different flavors and influences, including Indian, Chinese, and Middle Eastern.
- Sri Lanka is famous for its **tea**, which is grown in the central highlands and is known for its high quality and distinct flavor.
