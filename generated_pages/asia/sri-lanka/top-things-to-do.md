# Top Things to Do in Sri Lanka — Attractions & Experiences

Sri Lanka offers a diverse range of activities and experiences for visitors, from exploring ancient temples and colonial cities to relaxing on beautiful beaches and taking part in vibrant cultural festivals.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Ayurvedic massage** at a local spa to relax and rejuvenate
- Learn how to cook **Sri Lankan cuisine** with a local cooking class
- Attend a **Kandyan dance performance** to experience the country's vibrant cultural heritage
- Go on a **whale watching tour** in Mirissa to see blue whales, sperm whales, and dolphins
- Take a **train ride** through the scenic hills and tea plantations of the **Hill Country**
- Experience the **Esala Perahera festival** in Kandy, a vibrant and colorful festival with music, dance, and elephant parades
- Visit a **tea plantation** to learn about the production process and taste some of the world's best tea
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the countryside for a unique and breathtaking perspective

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Knuckles Mountain Range** for hiking and trekking opportunities
- Visit **Udawalawe National Park** for elephant sightings and birdwatching
- Relax on the beach in **Trincomalee**, a beautiful coastal town with a rich history
- Go **surfing** in **Arugam Bay**, a popular spot for surfers
- Take a **boat tour** of **Bundala National Park**, a wetland sanctuary with a wide variety of birdlife
- Visit the **Horton Plains National Park**, a unique park with a mix of grasslands and cloud forests

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Colombo** to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Kandy Museum** to see a collection of artifacts and exhibits on Kandyan history and culture
- Attend a **Buddhist ceremony** at a local temple to experience the country's spiritual heritage
- Take a **tour of the Galle Fort** to learn about the city's rich history and cultural significance
- Visit the **Anuradhapura Museum** to see a collection of artifacts and exhibits on ancient Anuradhapuran history and culture
- Watch a **traditional Sri Lankan drumming performance** to experience the country's vibrant music and dance

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lankatilaka Temple**, a beautiful and historic temple with stunning views
- Explore the **Dambadeniya Ruins**, a historic site with a rich history and cultural significance
- Take a **scenic drive** through the **Hill Country** to see the stunning landscapes and tea plantations
- Visit the **Mihintale Temple**, a historic site with a rich history and cultural significance
- Relax on the beach in **Tangalle**, a beautiful and secluded coastal town

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Colombo** to **Kandy** (110 km, 3 hours), to visit the Temple of the Tooth and explore the city
- Visit **Galle** from **Colombo** (120 km, 2.5 hours), to explore the historic fort and city
- Take a day trip from **Kandy** to **Sigiriya** (90 km, 2 hours), to visit the rock fortress and explore the surrounding countryside
- Visit **Dambulla** from **Kandy** (70 km, 1.5 hours), to see the cave temple and explore the surrounding area
- Take a day trip from **Galle** to **Mirissa** (40 km, 1 hour), to relax on the beach and go whale watching
