[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Archery lessons**: Learn the traditional sport of Bhutan and experience the country's unique culture
- **Hot stone bath**: Relax and rejuvenate with a traditional hot stone bath, a unique and soothing experience
- **Bhutanese cooking class**: Learn how to prepare traditional Bhutanese dishes and experience the country's cuisine
- **Mask making**: Create your own traditional Bhutanese mask and learn about the country's rich cultural heritage
- **Festival attendance**: Attend one of Bhutan's vibrant festivals, such as the Paro Tshechu or Thimphu Tshechu, and experience the country's unique culture
- **Homestay experience**: Live with a local family and experience the traditional Bhutanese way of life
- **Yak trekking**: Explore Bhutan's stunning natural beauty on a yak trek, a unique and exhilarating experience

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Jigme Dorji National Park**: Explore the stunning natural beauty of Bhutan's largest national park, home to a wide range of flora and fauna
- **Phobjikha Valley trek**: Trek through the picturesque Phobjikha Valley, known for its natural beauty and conservation efforts
- **Punakha River rafting**: Experience the thrill of rafting on the Punakha River, a scenic and exciting adventure
- **Himalayan mountain biking**: Explore Bhutan's stunning natural beauty on a mountain bike, a unique and exhilarating experience
- **Bumthang Valley hiking**: Hike through the picturesque Bumthang Valley, known for its natural beauty, culture, and history
