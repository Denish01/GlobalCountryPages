# Cost of Living in UAE - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in or visiting the United Arab Emirates (UAE) can vary greatly depending on the location, lifestyle, and personal preferences, but overall, it is considered to be a **moderate** to **expensive** destination.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate / Expensive
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $80-120 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $150-250 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $300-500 USD
Currency: AED
Tipping Custom: expected (10% in restaurants and bars)
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is UAE? [/SECTION]
The UAE is a popular tourist destination and a hub for business and commerce, which can drive up prices for accommodations, food, and other living expenses. Compared to its neighbors, Saudi Arabia and Oman, the UAE is generally more expensive, especially in cities like Dubai and Abu Dhabi. However, the country offers a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, world-class infrastructure, and a wide range of amenities, making it an attractive destination for visitors and expatriates alike. The cost of living in the UAE can vary depending on the location, with cities like Dubai and Abu Dhabi tend to be more expensive than other parts of the country.

The UAE's economy is driven by its oil and gas industry, but it has also diversified into other sectors such as finance, tourism, and real estate. This diversification has led to an increase in the cost of living, particularly in the major cities. However, the government has implemented various initiatives to make the country more attractive to visitors and expatriates, such as introducing a value-added tax (VAT) and improving public transportation.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment with others to reduce rent costs
- Use public transportation instead of taxis or driving a car
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls instead of mid-range or fine dining restaurants
- Avoid shopping at malls and instead opt for local markets or souks
- Take advantage of happy hour deals or discounts at restaurants and bars
- Plan your trip during the off-season (May-September) to get better deals on accommodations and tourist attractions

Overall, the UAE can be a **moderate** to **expensive** destination, but with some planning and research, it is possible to have a great experience without breaking the bank. By being mindful of your expenses and taking advantage of money-saving tips, you can enjoy all that the UAE has to offer.
