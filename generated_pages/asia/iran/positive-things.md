[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Persepolis** archaeological site, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a testament to Iran's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Taq-e Bostan** monument, a collection of ancient rock reliefs, showcases the country's historical significance.
- The **Persian Carpet** is an iconic symbol of Iranian art and craftsmanship, with intricate designs and patterns.
- The **Nowruz** celebration, a traditional Persian New Year festival, is a vibrant and colorful expression of Iranian culture.
- The **Golestan Palace**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a stunning example of Iranian architecture and design.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]

- **Rumi**, a 13th-century Persian poet, has had a profound impact on world literature, with his poetry and teachings still widely studied and admired today.
- The **Baha'i faith**, founded in Iran in the 19th century, has spread to become a global religion, with a strong emphasis on unity, equality, and social justice.
