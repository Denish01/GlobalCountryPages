# Top Things to Do in Iran — Attractions & Experiences

Iran offers a diverse array of cultural, historical, and natural attractions that make it a unique and fascinating destination for travelers, with its rich heritage and stunning landscapes making it a must-visit country in Asia.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Persian tea** in a local teahouse, where you can experience the country's hospitality and culture
- Visit a **traditional Iranian bathhouse**, where you can relax and rejuvenate in a unique and historic setting
- Attend a **traditional Persian music performance**, where you can experience the country's rich cultural heritage and musical traditions
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Iranian dishes, such as kebabs and stews
- Go on a **desert trek** and experience the country's stunning natural beauty and unique landscapes
- Visit a **traditional Iranian village**, where you can experience the country's rural culture and way of life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Dasht-e Kavir** desert, one of the largest and most beautiful deserts in the world, with stunning sand dunes and unique landscapes
- Explore the **Hyrcanian forests**, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most beautiful and biodiverse forests in the world
- Go **hiking** in the **Alborz Mountains**, where you can experience stunning natural beauty and unique landscapes
- Visit the **Caspian Sea**, the largest inland body of water in the world, with stunning beaches and unique wildlife
- Go on a **wildlife tour** and see the country's unique and endangered species, such as the Persian leopard and the Asiatic cheetah

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Iran**, where you can learn about the country's rich history and cultural heritage
- Explore the **Tehran Museum of Contemporary Art**, which features a wide range of modern and contemporary Iranian art
- Attend a **traditional Persian festival**, such as the **Nowruz** celebrations, which mark the beginning of spring and the new year
- Visit the **Shiraz Literary Museum**, which showcases the country's rich literary heritage and cultural traditions
- Take a **calligraphy class** and learn about the country's unique and beautiful script

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kandovan village**, a unique and historic village that is built into the side of a mountain
- Explore the **Tabriz Bazaar**, one of the oldest and most historic bazaars in the Middle East
- Go to the **Hormuz Island**, a stunning island in the Persian Gulf with unique landscapes and natural beauty
- Visit the **Ramsar Palace**, a beautiful and historic palace that showcases the country's rich cultural heritage
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Anzali Lagoon**, a stunning and biodiverse wetland that is home to a wide range of wildlife

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Tehran** to the **Karaaj** city, which is known for its stunning natural beauty and unique landscapes (distance: 45 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Shiraz** city and its surroundings, including the **Persepolis** and **Pasargadae** ancient ruins (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Go on a day trip from **Isfahan** to the **Kashan** city, which is known for its stunning natural beauty and unique cultural heritage (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)
- Take a day trip from **Yazd** to the **Meybod** city, which is known for its stunning natural beauty and unique cultural heritage (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Qom** city and its surroundings, including the **Jamkaran Mosque** and the **Qom Bazaar** (distance: 150 km, travel time: 2 hours)
