# Top Things to Do in Lebanon — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Lebanon, you should not miss the opportunity to explore its rich history, stunning natural beauty, and vibrant culture, which can be experienced through its many top attractions, unique experiences, and cultural landmarks.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine tour** in the Bekaa Valley, where you can sample some of Lebanon's renowned wines and learn about the production process
- Experience the traditional **henna night** ceremony, a pre-wedding celebration that involves music, dance, and henna decorations
- Visit a **souk**, a traditional marketplace where you can find everything from spices and sweets to handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a **festival**, such as the Baalbek International Festival, which showcases music, dance, and theater performances
- Learn how to make traditional **Lebanese cuisine**, such as tabbouleh and kibbeh, in a cooking class
- Explore the **caves of Jeita**, where you can take a boat ride through the stunning underground limestone formations
- Take a **falconry** lesson, where you can learn about the ancient art of hunting with birds of prey
- Visit a **traditional Lebanese village**, where you can experience rural life and hospitality

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Cedars of God**, a forest of ancient cedar trees that is a symbol of Lebanon
- Hike in the **Mount Lebanon range**, where you can enjoy stunning views and picturesque villages
- Visit the **Palm Islands Nature Reserve**, a protected area that is home to a variety of flora and fauna
- Relax on the **beaches of Byblos**, where you can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and water sports
- Take a **boat trip** along the coast, where you can see the stunning rock formations and marine life
- Explore the **Bekaa Valley**, a fertile region that is home to vineyards, farms, and traditional villages

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Beirut**, which showcases Lebanon's history and culture
- Explore the **Sursock Museum**, which features a collection of modern and contemporary art
- Attend a **performance** at the Beirut Opera House, which hosts a variety of music, dance, and theater events
- Visit the **Mohammad Al-Amin Mosque**, a stunning mosque that is one of the largest in the world
- Take a **cooking class**, where you can learn how to make traditional Lebanese dishes
- Explore the **American University of Beirut Museum**, which features a collection of archaeological and cultural artifacts

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Jbeil**, a charming coastal town that is known for its historic architecture and stunning views
- Explore the **village of Deir el Qamar**, a picturesque village that is home to a variety of historic buildings and cultural landmarks
- Take a **hike** in the **Horsh Ehden Nature Reserve**, a protected area that is home to a variety of flora and fauna
- Visit the **Baalbek Museum**, which features a collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the history and culture of the region
- Explore the **old city of Saida**, a historic city that is home to a variety of cultural landmarks and traditional souks
