# Education Costs in Macau

The cost of education in Macau varies depending on the type of institution, with public schools being generally free or subsidized, while private and international schools can range from $2,000 to $15,000 per year.

[SECTION] Education System Overview [/SECTION]
The education system in Macau is divided into public and private schools, with both types of institutions offering a range of programs from primary to secondary education. Public schools in Macau are generally considered to be of high quality and are free or subsidized for local students, while private schools tend to cater to expatriate families and offer a range of curricula, including **International Baccalaureate** and **British GCSE**. The language of instruction in public schools is primarily **Cantonese**, while private schools often use **English** or **Portuguese**. Compulsory education in Macau lasts for 12 years, from the age of 5 to 17.

The quality of education in Macau is generally considered to be high, with a strong emphasis on **mathematics**, **science**, and **language**. International schools in Macau offer a range of curricula and are popular among expatriate families, with some schools offering **bilingual** programs that combine **English** and **Cantonese** instruction. The academic year in Macau typically runs from September to June, with a summer break in July and August. Overall, the education system in Macau is well-regarded and provides students with a strong foundation for future academic and professional success.
