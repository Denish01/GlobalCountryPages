[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **A-Ma Temple**, a historic temple dedicated to the goddess Mazu, is a prominent cultural landmark and a symbol of Macau's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Ruins of St. Paul's**, a 17th-century church complex, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a testament to Macau's history as a major trading center.
- The **Macao Museum** showcases the region's history, culture, and traditions, with a collection of artifacts and exhibits that highlight Macau's unique cultural identity.
- The **Cantonese Opera**, a traditional form of Chinese opera, is a vibrant and expressive art form that is still performed in Macau today.
- The **Macao's Gastronomy**, a unique blend of Chinese, Portuguese, and Macanese cuisine, is a reflection of the region's cultural diversity and exchange.
