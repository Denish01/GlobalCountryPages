Cultural Treasures

  • The Ruins of St. Paul's is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a iconic landmark in Macau, showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage.
  • The A-Ma Temple is a historic temple that dates back to the 15th century and is dedicated to the goddess Mazu, a significant figure in Chinese culture.
  • The Macao Museum showcases the history and culture of Macau, with a collection of artifacts and exhibits that highlight the region's unique blend of Chinese and Portuguese influences.
  • The Senado Square is a historic square that features a mix of Portuguese and Chinese architectural styles, reflecting the region's cultural diversity.
  • The Taipa Village is a traditional village that preserves the region's cultural heritage, with many historic buildings and landmarks.
