# Top Things to Do in Macau — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Macau, visit the iconic **Ruins of St. Paul's**, try your luck at the **Casino Lisboa**, and explore the historic **Senado Square**, which are just a few of the many attractions and activities that this special administrative region of China has to offer.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **gondola ride** through the canals of The Venetian Macao
- Try your luck at the **Casino Grand Lisboa**, one of the largest casinos in Macau
- Visit the **Macau Grand Prix Museum** to learn about the history of the Macau Grand Prix
- Take a **helicopter tour** of the city for a bird's-eye view
- Attend a **greyhound racing** event at the Macau Canidrome
- Learn about the **traditional Macanese cuisine** and try some local dishes like minchi and feijoada
- Visit the **Macau Fisherman's Wharf** for a taste of the city's seafood and a glimpse into its fishing industry
- Explore the **Street of Happiness**, a historic street lined with old shops and restaurants

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Coloane Park** for a picnic or a hike
- Explore the **Hac Sa Beach**, a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing
- Take a **hike** through the **Guia Hill**, which offers stunning views of the city
- Visit the **Seac Pai Van Park**, a large park with gardens, lakes, and walking trails
- Go **beachcombing** on the **Cheoc Van Beach**, a quiet beach with a beautiful view
- Explore the **Ka Ho Reservoir**, a scenic spot for hiking and bird-watching

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **A-Ma Temple**, one of the oldest temples in Macau
- Explore the **Museum of Macau**, which tells the story of the city's history and culture
- Attend a **traditional Macanese festival**, such as the Macau International Music Festival
- Visit the **Luís de Camões Garden**, a beautiful garden dedicated to the famous Portuguese poet
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Macanese cuisine
- Visit the **St. Dominic's Church**, a historic church that dates back to the 16th century

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Mandarin's House**, a historic house that showcases traditional Macanese architecture
- Explore the **Lilau Square**, a charming square with historic homes and shops
- Take a **walk** through the **St. Augustine's Square**, a historic square with a beautiful church and a fountain
- Visit the **Na Tcha Temple**, a small temple dedicated to the goddess Na Tcha
- Visit the **Rua do Cunha**, a street lined with traditional shops and restaurants

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip to Hong Kong**, which is just a short ferry ride away (distance: 64 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Zhuhai** city in mainland China, which is connected to Macau by a bridge (distance: 35 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Explore the **Guangzhou** city, which is a major cultural and economic center in southern China (distance: 145 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a **day trip to Shenzhen**, a modern city in mainland China known for its theme parks and shopping malls (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Visit the **Zhongshan** city, which is a historic city in Guangdong province with a rich cultural heritage (distance: 120 km, travel time: 2 hours)
