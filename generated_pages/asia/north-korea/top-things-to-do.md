# Top Things to Do in North Korea — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a **mass game** performance, a spectacular display of synchronized movements and music
- Visit a **local market**, such as the Pyongyang Department Store No. 1, to see the daily life of North Koreans
- Take a **stroll along the Taedong River**, which runs through the heart of Pyongyang
- Join a **guided tour** of the city's major landmarks, led by a knowledgeable local guide
- Experience the **Pyongyang Metro**, a unique and fascinating aspect of the city's infrastructure
- Visit the **Mansudae Art Studio**, where artists create stunning works of art, including the famous statues of the leaders
- Enjoy a **traditional Korean meal**, such as naengmyeon or bibimbap, at a local restaurant

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Myohyangsan** mountains, known for their scenic trails and waterfalls
- Visit the **Kumgangsan** mountains, a popular destination for hiking and outdoor activities
- Take a **boat ride** on the **Taedong River**, which offers a unique perspective on the city
- Relax on the **beaches of Wonsan**, a coastal city with stunning natural beauty
- Hike to the **top of Mount Kumgang**, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Demilitarized Zone (DMZ)**, a unique and fascinating aspect of the country's natural landscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Korean Revolution Museum**, which showcases the country's history and revolutionary spirit
- Explore the **Pyongyang Museum of Fine Arts**, which features a collection of traditional and modern Korean art
- Attend a **performance at the Pyongyang Grand Theatre**, which hosts a variety of cultural events
- Visit the **Mansudae Grand Monument**, a symbol of the country's pride and resilience
- Take a **tour of the Pyongyang Folklore Park**, which offers a glimpse into traditional Korean culture and crafts
- Visit the **Victorious Fatherland Liberation War Museum**, which provides insight into the country's perspective on the Korean War

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ryongwang Pavilion**, a historic building with stunning views of the city
- Explore the **Potonggang District**, a charming area with a mix of old and new architecture
- Take a **stroll through the **Pyongyang Botanical Garden**, a peaceful oasis in the heart of the city
- Visit the **Kaesong Folk Custom Museum**, which showcases the traditional customs and way of life of the Kaesong region
- Attend a **local festival**, such as the Pyongyang Spring Festival, which celebrates the arrival of spring
