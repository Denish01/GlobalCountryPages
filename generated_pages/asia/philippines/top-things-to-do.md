# Top Things to Do in Philippines — Attractions & Experiences

The Philippines offers a diverse array of exciting activities and experiences, from exploring historic sites and vibrant cities to relaxing on beautiful beaches and discovering unique wildlife.

| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Intramuros | Manila | Historical Site | Explore the walled city, a testament to the country's rich history and Spanish colonial past |
| Chocolate Hills | Bohol | Natural Wonder | Marvel at over 1,000 conical hills that turn brown during the dry season |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **jeepney** ride, a colorful and iconic mode of transportation in the Philippines
- Attend a **Fiesta**, a vibrant and lively celebration that showcases the country's culture and traditions
- Learn **Arnis**, the national martial art of the Philippines, which combines elements of karate and stick fighting
- Go on a **whale shark** watching tour in Donsol, Sorsogon, and swim alongside these gentle giants
- Experience the **MassKara Festival** in Bacolod, a celebration of music, dance, and costumes
- Take a **kalesa** ride, a traditional horse-drawn carriage, through the historic streets of Vigan
- Visit a **Taal Lake** fish farm and learn about the unique fishing practices in the area

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Puerto Princesa Subterranean River**, a stunning underground river and cave system
- Hike to the **Mount Pulag** summit, the third highest peak in the Philippines, and witness the breathtaking views
- Relax on the beautiful beaches of **El Nido**, Palawan, and take in the stunning limestone cliffs
- Visit the **Chocolate Hills** in Bohol and marvel at the unique geological formation
- Go **island hopping** in **Boracay** and explore the surrounding islands and beaches
- Trek through the **Ifugao Rice Terraces**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and learn about the history and culture of the area

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum** in Manila and learn about the country's history, art, and culture
- Explore the **Intramuros** historic walled city and visit the **Fort Santiago** and **San Agustin Church**
- Attend a **Cultural Show** at the **Cultural Center of the Philippines** and witness the traditional music, dance, and costumes
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Filipino dishes like **adobo** and **sinigang**
- Visit the **Vigan Heritage Village** and walk through the historic streets and buildings
- Learn about the **T'boli** tribe and their traditional weaving and crafts in **Lake Sebu**, South Cotabato

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar** in Bataan, a collection of restored Spanish colonial houses
- Explore the **Hundred Islands National Park** in Pangasinan and take a boat tour through the stunning islands and beaches
- Take a **food tour** of **Pampanga** and sample the local cuisine, including **sisig** and **lechon**
- Visit the **Dapitan Shrine** in Zamboanga del Norte, a historic site that commemorates the exile of **Jose Rizal**
- Go **birdwatching** in **Verde Island**, Batangas, and spot some of the unique bird species in the area

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Manila** to **Tagaytay** (55 km, 1 hour), and visit the **Taal Volcano** and **People's Park**
- Visit **Corregidor Island** (45 km, 1.5 hours from Manila), a historic island that played a significant role in World War II
- Take a day trip from **Cebu** to **Moalboal** (90 km, 2 hours), and go **snorkeling** or **diving** in the stunning waters
- Visit **Pagsanjan Falls** (100 km, 2.5 hours from Manila), a beautiful waterfall that can be reached by boat or hike
- Take a day trip from **Davao** to **Samal Island** (10 km, 30 minutes), and relax on the beautiful beaches and take a **zip line** tour
