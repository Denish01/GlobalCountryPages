Zhejiang, a region in eastern China, offers a diverse range of experiences, from ancient temples and traditional villages to stunning natural scenery and vibrant cities, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to explore the country's rich cultural heritage and breathtaking landscapes.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a Chinese tea-making class in the scenic hills of Anji County, where you can learn about the production process and sample some of the region's famous green teas
  • Explore the traditional villages of Xidi and Hongcun, which offer a glimpse into rural Chinese life and feature well-preserved ancient architecture
  • Attend a performance of Zhejiang opera, a unique and expressive form of traditional Chinese theater
  • Visit the Zhejiang Museum of Natural History, which features a wide range of exhibits on the region's natural history and wildlife
  • Take a bamboo rafting trip down the Xixi Wetlands, which offers a unique and relaxing way to explore the area's scenic waterways
  • Learn about Chinese calligraphy and painting at a local art studio, where you can try your hand at creating traditional Chinese art
  • Visit the Qingyuan Garden, a beautiful and historic garden that features a wide range of plants and flowers

Outdoor & Nature

  • West Lake: a stunning lake with numerous temples, gardens, and walking trails
  • Moganshan: a scenic mountain with hiking trails and stunning views
  • Thousand Islets Lake: a beautiful lake with over 1,000 islands, offering boat tours and hiking trails
  • Xixi National Wetland Park: a unique and diverse ecosystem, home to a wide range of plant and animal species
  • Tiantai Mountain: a sacred mountain in Chinese Buddhism, offering stunning views and hiking trails
  • Qiantang River: a major river that offers stunning views and a chance to see the famous Qiantang River tidal bore

Cultural Experiences

  • Lingyin Temple: one of China's most famous and revered Buddhist temples, featuring intricate carvings and paintings
  • Zhejiang Museum: a comprehensive museum that features a wide range of exhibits on the region's history, culture, and art
  • Nanxun Ancient Town: a well-preserved ancient town, featuring traditional architecture and canals
  • Qingyuan Garden: a beautiful and historic garden that features a wide range of plants and flowers
  • Tiantai Mountain Temple: a sacred temple in Chinese Buddhism, offering stunning views and a chance to learn about Buddhist culture
  • Xixi Ancient Town: a historic town that features traditional architecture and a wide range of cultural attractions

Hidden Gems

  • Shaoxing Ancient Town: a well-preserved ancient town that features traditional architecture and canals
  • Dongyang Woodcarving Museum: a unique museum that showcases the region's famous woodcarving traditions
  • Longmen Ancient Town: a historic town that features traditional architecture and a wide range of cultural attractions
  • Jiande Ancient Town: a well-preserved ancient town that features traditional architecture and canals
  • Yiwu Ancient Town: a historic town that features traditional architecture and a wide range of cultural attractions

Day Trips

  • Hangzhou to Moganshan: a scenic mountain town that offers hiking trails and stunning views (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)
  • Hangzhou to Xixi Wetlands: a unique and diverse ecosystem, home to a wide range of plant and animal species (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
  • Hangzhou to Thousand Islets Lake: a stunning lake with over 1,000 islands, offering boat tours and hiking trails (distance: 150 km, travel time: 2.5 hours)
  • Hangzhou to Nanxun Ancient Town: a well-preserved ancient town, featuring traditional architecture and canals (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
  • Hangzhou to Tiantai Mountain: a sacred mountain in Chinese Buddhism, offering stunning views and hiking trails (distance: 250 km, travel time: 4 hours)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries:

3-day itinerary: explore Hangzhou's West Lake and Lingyin Temple, visit the Xixi Wetlands and take a Chinese tea-making class

7-day itinerary: add visits to Moganshan, Thousand Islets Lake, and Nanxun Ancient Town, and attend a performance of Zhejiang opera

14-day itinerary: explore the region's many cultural and natural attractions, including Tiantai Mountain, Putuo Island, and the Zhejiang Museum of Natural History

Key tips: book accommodations and transportation in advance, especially during peak travel seasons, and try to avoid visiting during Chinese holidays and festivals, when popular attractions can be very crowded.
