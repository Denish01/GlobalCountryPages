Zhejiang is a region in China known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and impressive economic development, with numerous positive achievements and highlights that make it a unique and fascinating place to explore.

Cultural Treasures

  • The West Lake in Hangzhou is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of China's most famous natural attractions, known for its stunning scenery and cultural significance.
  • The Lingyin Temple in Hangzhou is one of China's most famous Buddhist temples and is known for its intricate carvings, paintings, and sculptures.
  • The Xixi National Wetland Park in Hangzhou is a unique and fascinating cultural attraction that showcases the region's traditional way of life and natural beauty.
  • The Zhejiang Traditional Instrumental Music is a unique and expressive form of music that is known for its use of traditional instruments such as the erhu and the pipa.
  • The Longjing Tea Plantations in Hangzhou are a popular destination for tea enthusiasts and are known for producing some of China's finest green tea.

People & Global Impact

  • Jack Ma, the founder of Alibaba Group, is one of Zhejiang's most famous sons and has become a global business leader and philanthropist.
  • Zhejiang's diaspora community has made significant contributions to the development of Chinese communities around the world, particularly in the United States and Europe.
  • Zhejiang's business leaders have become known for their entrepreneurial spirit and innovative approach to business, with many companies from the region becoming major players in the global economy.
  • Zhejiang's educators have made significant contributions to the development of education in China, with many of the region's universities and research institutions becoming major centers of learning and innovation.

What Visitors Love

  • The natural beauty of Zhejiang, including its mountains, lakes, and coastline, is a major draw for visitors from around the world.
  • The traditional cuisine of Zhejiang, including its famous dishes such as Dongpo pork and beggar's chicken, is known for its unique flavors and ingredients.
  • The rich cultural heritage of Zhejiang, including its traditional opera, music, and art, is a major attraction for visitors interested in history and culture.
  • The hospitality of the Zhejiang people, who are known for their warm welcomes and generous spirit, makes visitors feel at home.
  • The modern cities of Zhejiang, including Hangzhou and Ningbo, offer a unique blend of traditional and modern culture, with many attractions and activities to suit all interests.
  • The outdoor activities available in Zhejiang, including hiking, cycling, and water sports, make it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Hidden Gems

  • The Anji County in Zhejiang is a hidden gem that is known for its stunning natural beauty, including its bamboo forests and waterfalls.
  • The Tiantai County in Zhejiang is a unique and fascinating destination that is known for its traditional Buddhist culture and stunning scenery.
  • The Xiangshan County in Zhejiang is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with its stunning coastline and scenic hiking trails.
  • The Ningbo Museum in Zhejiang is a hidden gem that is known for its unique collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the region's history and culture.
