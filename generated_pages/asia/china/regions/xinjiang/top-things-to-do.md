# Top Things to Do in Xinjiang — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Xinjiang, visit the **Heavenly Lake**, explore the **Kashgar Old City**, and hike through the **Tianchi Lake Scenic Area**, which offer a glimpse into the region's stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and historic significance.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Kashgar Sunday Market**, a vibrant and bustling marketplace where you can experience the local culture and try traditional foods
- Take a **camel ride** through the **Taklamakan Desert**, one of the largest deserts in the world, and experience the unique landscape and wildlife
- Visit a **Kazakh village** and experience the traditional nomadic lifestyle, including trying local foods and drinks
- Attend a **Uyghur music and dance performance**, showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage and traditional arts
- Try **Xinjiang cuisine**, a unique blend of Chinese, Islamic, and Central Asian flavors and ingredients
- Take a **hike** through the **Pamir Mountains**, one of the most beautiful and rugged mountain ranges in the world
- Visit a **traditional Uyghur family** and experience the local customs and way of life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Tianchi Lake Scenic Area**: a stunning lake surrounded by mountains and forests, with opportunities for hiking and boating
- **Kanas Lake**: a pristine lake surrounded by mountains and forests, with opportunities for hiking and boating
- **Pamir Mountains**: a rugged and beautiful mountain range, with opportunities for hiking, trekking, and mountaineering
- **Taklamakan Desert**: one of the largest deserts in the world, with opportunities for camel rides and desert exploration
- **Altun Mountains**: a beautiful and rugged mountain range, with opportunities for hiking, trekking, and mountaineering
- **Ili Valley**: a scenic valley surrounded by mountains and forests, with opportunities for hiking, horseback riding, and rafting
