# Top Things to Do in Jiangsu — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Jiangsu, visit the iconic **Humble Administrator's Garden** in Suzhou, explore the ancient **City Wall of Nanjing**, and take a stroll along the picturesque **Grand Canal** in Wuxi, which are just a few of the many incredible attractions this region has to offer.

| City Wall of Nanjing | Nanjing | Historical Site | One of the best-preserved city walls in China, offering stunning views of the city |
| Grand Canal | Wuxi | Historical Waterway | The oldest and longest canal in the world, a testament to ancient Chinese engineering |
| Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum | Nanjing | Historical Site | The tomb of the first Ming dynasty emperor, a masterpiece of Chinese architecture |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Chinese painting class** in Suzhou to learn the techniques of this ancient art form
- Attend a **Kunqu Opera performance** in Nanjing, a traditional Chinese opera that dates back to the 16th century
- Explore the **canals of Zhouzhuang**, a picturesque water town that offers a glimpse into traditional Chinese life
- Visit a **silk factory** in Suzhou to learn about the production of this luxurious fabric
- Take a **calligraphy class** in Nanjing to learn the art of Chinese writing
- Go on a **hot spring tour** in Wuxi, where you can relax in natural hot springs and enjoy the scenic views
- Sample **local cuisine**, such as Suzhou-style dumplings and Nanjing salted duck, to experience the unique flavors of Jiangsu

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Mount Jiuhua**, a scenic mountain range that offers hiking trails, waterfalls, and stunning views
- **Taihu Lake**, a large freshwater lake that is perfect for boating, fishing, and birdwatching
- **Yancheng Coastal Wetlands**, a nature reserve that is home to a variety of bird species and offers stunning coastal views
- **Wuyi Mountains**, a scenic mountain range that offers hiking trails, waterfalls, and stunning views
- **Xishan Island**, a scenic island in the Yangtze River that offers stunning views, hiking trails, and historic sites

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Nanjing Museum**, a comprehensive museum that features a collection of Chinese art, history, and culture
- **Suzhou Silk Museum**, a museum that showcases the production of silk and the history of the silk industry
- **Qinhuai Lantern Fair**, a traditional lantern festival that takes place in Nanjing every year
- **Confucius Temple**, a historic temple in Nanjing that is dedicated to the philosopher Confucius
- **Linggu Temple**, a historic temple in Nanjing that features a stunning pagoda and scenic gardens

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The ancient town of Tongli**, a picturesque water town that is often overlooked by tourists
- **The **Mochou Lake Park**, a scenic park in Nanjing that offers stunning views, walking trails, and historic sites
- **The **Qixia Temple**, a historic temple in Nanjing that features stunning architecture and scenic gardens
- **The **Suzhou Amusement Land**, a theme park that offers a variety of rides, shows, and attractions
- **The **Wuxi Movie Studio**, a film studio that offers tours and behind-the-scenes looks at Chinese movie production

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Nanjing to **Hangzhou**, the capital of Zhejiang province, which is famous for its scenic **West Lake** (distance: 200 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a day trip from Suzhou to **Shanghai**, the largest city in China, which is famous for its **Bund** and **Oriental Pearl Tower** (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a day trip from Wuxi to **Yixing**, a city famous for its **ceramic production** and **natural scenery** (distance: 50 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Take a day trip from Nanjing to **Yangzhou**, a city famous for its **canals** and **traditional architecture** (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a day trip from Suzhou to **Zhouzhuang**, a picturesque water town that offers a glimpse into traditional Chinese life (distance: 50 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
