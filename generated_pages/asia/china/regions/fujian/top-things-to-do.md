- Visit a **earth tower** village to learn about the history and significance of these unique structures
- Take a **bamboo rafting** trip down the **Nine-Bend River** to experience the region's stunning natural scenery
- Participate in a **traditional folk festival** to experience the local culture and traditions
- Learn about the **history of maritime trade** in Quanzhou and its significance in the region's development
- Take a **calligraphy** class to learn about the traditional art of Chinese calligraphy
