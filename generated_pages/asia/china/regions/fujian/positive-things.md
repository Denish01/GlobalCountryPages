[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Gulangyu Island**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a charming island with a rich cultural heritage, featuring historic architecture, museums, and traditional music venues.
- The **Quanzhou Maritime Museum** showcases the city's maritime history and trade routes, with a collection of artifacts and exhibits on shipbuilding, navigation, and cultural exchange.
- The **Fujian Tulou**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a unique type of earthen building that has been preserved and protected, showcasing the region's traditional architecture and communal living.
- The **Nanyin Music**, a traditional form of Chinese music, is still performed and preserved in Fujian, with many local musicians and artists working to promote and develop this ancient art form.
- The **Fuzhou Lacquerware**, a traditional craft that dates back to the Song dynasty, is a highly prized and intricate form of decorative art, with many local artisans still practicing this traditional technique.
