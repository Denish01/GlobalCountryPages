# Top Things to Do in Sichuan — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Sichuan, visit the iconic **Leshan Giant Buddha**, explore the vibrant streets of **Jinli**, and sample the region's renowned **Sichuan cuisine**, which showcases the unique blend of spicy and numbing flavors that Sichuan is famous for.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Sichuan cooking class** to learn the secrets of traditional Sichuan cuisine, including the famous **Kung Pao chicken** and **Mapo tofu** dishes
- Visit a **traditional Sichuan tea house** to experience the local tea culture and sample some of the region's famous **Sichuan tea**
- Explore the **Chengdu silk road** and learn about the history of the silk trade in Sichuan
- Attend a **Sichuan opera performance**, which combines music, dance, and acrobatics
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the stunning scenery of **Jiuzhaigou National Park**
- Visit a **local market** to try some of the region's unique **street food**, such as **twice-cooked pork** and **dan dan noodles**
- Learn about **Sichuan's traditional medicine** and visit a **traditional Chinese medicine shop**
- Visit a **Sichuan wine brewery** to sample some of the region's famous **Sichuan wine**

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the stunning scenery of **Jiuzhaigou National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Hike to the summit of **Mount Emei**, one of the four sacred Buddhist mountains in China
- Visit the **Huanglong Scenic Area**, known for its breathtaking scenery and unique geological formations
- Take a boat ride on **Lugu Lake**, a beautiful and tranquil lake surrounded by stunning scenery
- Go **hiking** in the **Qingcheng Mountain** scenic area, the birthplace of Taoism
- Visit the **Wolong National Nature Reserve**, home to giant pandas, golden monkeys, and other rare species

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Wuhou Temple**, dedicated to Zhuge Liang, a famous strategist of the Three Kingdoms period
- Explore the **Sanxingdui Museum**, which showcases the mysterious Sanxingdui culture
- Attend a **traditional Sichuan music performance**, which combines traditional instruments and singing
- Visit the **Jinsha Site Museum**, which showcases the history and culture of the Jinsha Kingdom
- Take a **calligraphy class** to learn about traditional Chinese calligraphy
- Visit the **Sichuan Museum**, which showcases the history and culture of Sichuan province

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Guanghan Sanxingdui Ancient Town**, a lesser-known town with a rich history and culture
- Explore the **Danba County**, known for its stunning scenery and traditional Tibetan culture
- Take a **boat ride** on the **Min River**, which offers stunning scenery and a glimpse into local life
- Visit the **Langzhong Ancient Town**, a well-preserved ancient town with a rich history and culture
- Go **hiking** in the **Xiling Snow Mountain** scenic area, which offers stunning scenery and a range of outdoor activities

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Leshan City** (150 km, 2 hours) to visit the **Leshan Giant Buddha** and explore the city's historic center
- Visit **Mount Emei** (150 km, 2.5 hours) to hike to the summit and explore the scenic area
- Explore **Jiuzhaigou National Park** (400 km, 6 hours) to see the stunning scenery and unique geological formations
- Take a day trip to **Dujiangyan City** (60 km, 1 hour) to visit the **Dujiangyan Irrigation System** and explore the city's historic center
- Visit **Qingcheng Mountain** (70 km, 1.5 hours) to hike to the summit and explore the scenic area
