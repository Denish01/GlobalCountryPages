[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Giant Buddha of Leshan**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a 71-meter-tall stone statue carved into the side of a mountain, showcasing the region's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Sichuan Opera** is a traditional art form that combines music, dance, and acrobatics, featuring vibrant masks and elaborate costumes.
- The **Jinli Street** in Chengdu is a historic street filled with traditional architecture, shops, and restaurants, offering a glimpse into the region's past.
- The **Dujiangyan Irrigation System**, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an ancient irrigation system that has been in use for over 2,000 years, demonstrating the region's engineering prowess.
- The **Mount Emei Scenic Area**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the four sacred Buddhist mountains in China, is a popular destination for hiking and spiritual retreats.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Deng Xiaoping**, a former Chinese paramount leader, was born in Sichuan and played a crucial role in China's economic reform and opening-up policy.
- The **Sichuan diaspora** has made significant contributions to the global community, with many Sichuanese individuals excelling in fields such as business, science, and the arts.
- **Jiang Zemin**, a former Chinese president, was also born in Sichuan and served as the general secretary of the Communist Party of China.
- The **values of Sichuan people**, such as hospitality and resilience, have been shaped by the region's history and culture, and are highly regarded by visitors and locals alike.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **hospitality of the Sichuan people**, known for their warm and welcoming nature, makes visitors feel at home.
- The **Sichuan cuisine**, famous for its spicy dishes and unique flavors, is a major draw for foodies and adventurous eaters.
- The **scenic beauty of the region**, including the **Mount Emei Scenic Area** and the **Jiuzhaigou National Park**, offers breathtaking landscapes and opportunities for outdoor activities.
- The **vibrant city life of Chengdu**, with its modern infrastructure and rich cultural heritage, provides a unique blend of traditional and contemporary experiences.
- The **traditional festivals and events**, such as the **Sichuan Opera Festival** and the **Chengdu Lantern Festival**, showcase the region's rich cultural heritage and offer a glimpse into its history and customs.
- The **hot springs and spa resorts**, such as the **Chengdu Hot Spring Resort**, provide a relaxing and rejuvenating experience for visitors.
