[SECTION] How Henan Is Divided [/SECTION]
Henan is divided into 18 prefecture-level cities and 1 prefecture, with Zhengzhou as its capital. The administrative structure of Henan is a complex system, with each prefecture-level city having its own administrative divisions, including counties, districts, and townships. This system allows for efficient governance and management of the region's resources and population. The prefecture-level cities in Henan are further divided into districts, counties, and county-level cities, which are responsible for providing public services and implementing regional policies.

The division of Henan into smaller administrative units has enabled the region to develop its economy and culture in a more targeted and effective manner. Each prefecture-level city has its own unique character and strengths, with some specializing in industry, others in agriculture, and others in tourism. This diversity has contributed to Henan's overall economic growth and has made it an attractive destination for investors and tourists alike.
