# Top Things to Do in Henan — Attractions & Experiences

Henan is a treasure trove of history, culture, and natural beauty, offering a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that showcase its unique heritage, from the ancient **Longmen Grottoes** to the scenic **Songshan National Park**.

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Longmen Grottoes | Luoyang | UNESCO World Heritage Site | Features over 100,000 **Buddhist carvings** and statues |
| Shaolin Temple | Dengfeng | Buddhist Temple | Famous for its **martial arts** and **Kung Fu** performances |
| White Horse Temple | Luoyang | Buddhist Temple | Considered one of the oldest **Buddhist temples** in China |
| Guanlin Temple | Luoyang | Buddhist Temple | Dedicated to the **Guan Yu**, a legendary general and deity |

| Yellow River Scenic Area | Zhengzhou | Scenic Area | Provides stunning views of the **Yellow River** and its surroundings |
| Songshan National Park | Dengfeng | National Park | Features **hiking trails**, **waterfalls**, and **scenic valleys** |
| Luoyang Museum | Luoyang | Museum | Showcases a vast collection of **artifacts** and **exhibits** on Henan's history and culture |
| Zhengzhou Museum | Zhengzhou | Museum | Offers insights into the **history** and **culture** of the region |
