# Where to Stay in Suzhou — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Suzhou, the best areas to stay are generally located in the city center, near popular attractions and public transportation, with top options including the **Guanqian Street**, **Lion Grove Garden**, and **Jinchang District** neighborhoods.

**Guanqian Street** (Suzhou)
Best For: luxury / business
Vibe: Guanqian Street is a bustling commercial area with a mix of traditional and modern architecture, offering a unique blend of old and new Suzhou. The street is lined with shops, restaurants, and entertainment options, making it a great spot for those who want to experience the city's vibrant atmosphere.

**Lion Grove Garden** (Suzhou)
Best For: family / luxury
