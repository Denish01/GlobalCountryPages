Hangzhou, the capital city of Zhejiang Province in China, is a must-visit destination that offers a wide range of exciting activities and attractions, from its breathtaking natural scenery to its rich cultural heritage, with the top things to do including visiting the famous West Lake, exploring the historic Lingyin Temple, and taking a stroll along the picturesque Qinghefang Ancient Street.

Unique Experiences

  • Take a traditional Chinese tea ceremony at the Longjing Tea Plantation to learn about the history and cultural significance of tea in China
  • Cooking classes are available at various restaurants and cooking schools, where visitors can learn how to prepare traditional Hangzhou dishes
  • Silk production workshops offer a hands-on experience of silk production, from sericulture to weaving
  • Traditional Chinese medicine classes are available at various hospitals and clinics, where visitors can learn about the principles and practices of traditional Chinese medicine
  • Calligraphy classes are available at various art schools and studios, where visitors can learn the art of Chinese calligraphy
  • Qipao making classes are available at various fashion schools and studios, where visitors can learn how to make traditional Chinese qipao dresses
  • Chinese painting classes are available at various art schools and studios, where visitors can learn the techniques and styles of traditional Chinese painting

Outdoor & Nature

  • Qiantang River: take a boat cruise or stroll along the riverbank to enjoy the stunning views and scenery
  • Xixi National Wetland Park: explore the unique ecosystem and biodiversity of the wetland by boat or on foot
  • Longjing Tea Plantation: take a stroll through the scenic tea plantation and enjoy the stunning views
  • West Lake: take a boat ride or stroll along the lake's scenic walking paths to enjoy the breathtaking natural scenery
  • Jiuxi Scenic Area: a scenic area featuring numerous waterfalls, lakes, and hiking trails

Cultural Experiences

  • Lingyin Temple: visit one of China's most famous temples to learn about Buddhist history and culture
  • China National Silk Museum: learn about the history and production of silk in China
  • Hangzhou Museum: a museum showcasing the history and culture of Hangzhou
  • Qinghefang Ancient Street: explore the traditional shops, restaurants, and tea houses on this well-preserved ancient street
  • Xixi Wetland Museum: learn about the unique ecosystem and biodiversity of the Xixi Wetland

Hidden Gems

  • Meijiawu Village: a traditional village famous for its tea production and stunning natural scenery
  • Longjing Village: a traditional village famous for its green tea production and stunning natural scenery
  • Jade Emperor Hill: a scenic hill featuring numerous temples, gardens, and stunning views
  • Qingzhiwu Temple: a lesser-known temple featuring intricate carvings, paintings, and sculptures
  • Hangzhou Paradise: an amusement park featuring numerous rides, shows, and attractions

Day Trips

  • Wuzhen Water Town: a traditional water town located about 1 hour from Hangzhou, featuring numerous canals, bridges, and traditional architecture
  • Xitang Water Town: a traditional water town located about 1.5 hours from Hangzhou, featuring numerous canals, bridges, and traditional architecture
  • Ningbo City: a city located about 2 hours from Hangzhou, featuring numerous historical sites, museums, and cultural attractions
  • Putuo Island: an island located about 2.5 hours from Hangzhou, featuring numerous beaches, temples, and scenic views
  • Anji County: a county located about 1.5 hours from Hangzhou, featuring numerous natural scenic areas, temples, and traditional villages

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries:

- 3-day itinerary: visit West Lake, Lingyin Temple, and Qinghefang Ancient Street

- 7-day itinerary: explore the Xixi National Wetland Park, Longjing Tea Plantation, and Qiantang River, and visit the China National Silk Museum and Hangzhou Museum

- 14-day itinerary: take a deep exploration of Hangzhou's natural scenery, cultural heritage, and traditional villages, and visit nearby cities and towns such as Wuzhen and Ningbo

Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book accommodations and attractions in advance, especially during peak travel seasons, and prioritize the top attractions and experiences based on visitor ratings and cultural significance
