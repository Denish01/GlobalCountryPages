# Top Things to Do in Xi'an — Attractions & Experiences

Xi'an, a city steeped in history and culture, offers a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that showcase its rich heritage, from ancient **Terracotta Warriors** to vibrant **Muslim Quarter**.

[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Terracotta Warriors and Horses | Lintong District | Archaeological Site | Witness the life-sized army of terracotta soldiers that dates back to the Qin dynasty |
| Xi'an City Wall | Xi'an City | Historical Landmark | Explore the oldest and best-preserved city wall in China, offering stunning views of the city |
| Big Wild Goose Pagoda | Yanta District | Buddhist Temple | Admire the 7-story pagoda that has become a symbol of Xi'an and houses numerous Buddhist scriptures and artifacts |
| Muslim Quarter | Xi'an City | Cultural District | Discover the vibrant streets filled with Islamic cuisine, souvenirs, and architecture that reflect the city's diverse heritage |
| Shaanxi History Museum | Xi'an City | Museum | Learn about the region's rich history and culture through an extensive collection of artifacts and exhibits |

| Drum Tower | Xi'an City | Historical Landmark | Climb to the top of the tower for panoramic views of the city and learn about its significance in Chinese history |
| Little Wild Goose Pagoda | Xi'an City | Buddhist Temple | Explore the smaller but equally impressive pagoda that offers a glimpse into Xi'an's Buddhist heritage |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **calligraphy class** to learn the art of traditional Chinese writing
- Explore the **Xi'an Food Street** and sample local delicacies such as **liang pi** and **rou jia mo**
- Attend a **traditional Chinese music performance** at the Xi'an Concert Hall
- Visit a **local market** and try your hand at **haggling** for souvenirs
- Join a **Chinese cooking class** and learn how to prepare authentic Xi'an dishes
- Take a **bike tour** along the ancient city wall and explore the surrounding countryside

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike to the top of **Huashan Mountain**, one of China's five sacred mountains
- Explore the **Qinling Mountains**, a range that stretches across southern Shaanxi Province
- Visit the **Xi'an Botanical Garden**, home to over 4,000 plant species
- Take a **boat ride** along the **Wei River**, which flows through the heart of Xi'an
- Go **hiking** in the **Cuihua Mountain**, a scenic area with stunning views of the surrounding landscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Shaanxi Grand Opera House** and watch a performance of traditional Chinese opera
- Explore the **Xi'an Museum of Fine Arts**, which features a collection of Chinese art and artifacts
- Attend a **traditional Chinese tea ceremony** at a local tea house
- Visit the **Great Mosque of Xi'an**, one of the oldest and most famous mosques in China
- Take a **Chinese language class** and learn the basics of Mandarin

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Stele Forest**, a museum that houses a collection of ancient stone steles and inscriptions
- Explore the **Beilin Museum**, which features a collection of ancient artifacts and relics
- Take a **walk along the ancient city wall** at night, when it is beautifully lit up
- Visit the **Xi'an Folk Street**, a pedestrian street lined with shops, restaurants, and street performers
- Discover the **hidden temples** and **gardens** scattered throughout the city

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Terracotta Warriors and Horses** (30 km, 1 hour)
- Visit the **Huashan Mountain** (120 km, 2 hours)
- Explore the **Qinling Mountains** (100 km, 2 hours)
- Go to the **Hanyangling Mausoleum** (30 km, 1 hour), the tomb of the first Han dynasty emperor
- Take a day trip to the **Ancient City of Luoyang** (150 km, 2.5 hours)
