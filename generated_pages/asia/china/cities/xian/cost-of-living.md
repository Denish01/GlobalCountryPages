# Cost of Living in Xi'an - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Xi'an is relatively affordable, with a daily budget of $20-50 USD for backpackers, $50-100 USD for mid-range travelers, and $100-200 USD for those seeking a comfortable experience.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $20-50 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $50-100 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $100-200 USD
Currency: CNY
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Xi'an? [/SECTION]
Xi'an is a city located in the Shaanxi province of China, known for its rich history and cultural heritage. Compared to other major cities in China, such as Shanghai and Beijing, Xi'an is relatively affordable. The cost of living in Xi'an is lower than in these cities, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike. However, prices in Xi'an have been rising in recent years, especially in the city center, due to its growing popularity and urbanization.

In comparison to global averages, Xi'an is still considered an affordable city, with a cost of living index that is lower than many major cities in the world. The city's affordable prices, combined with its unique cultural and historical attractions, make it an excellent choice for travelers and expats looking for a high quality of life at a lower cost. The local currency, CNY, is the official currency used in Xi'an, and it is widely accepted throughout the city.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local eateries and street food stalls to enjoy affordable and delicious meals.
- Use public transportation, such as buses and metro, to get around the city.
- Avoid traveling during peak season (June to August) to save on accommodation and tourist attraction prices.
- Learn some basic Mandarin phrases to help you navigate the city and interact with locals.
- Take advantage of the city's many free or low-cost cultural attractions, such as the Xi'an City Wall and the Muslim Quarter.
