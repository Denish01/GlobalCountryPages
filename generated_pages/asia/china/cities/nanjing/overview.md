[SECTION] What Is Nanjing? [/SECTION]
Nanjing is a major city in China, located in the eastern part of the country, in the Yangtze River Delta region. It is the capital of Jiangsu Province and has a long history dating back to the Qin Dynasty. Nanjing has been an important cultural, economic, and political center in China for centuries, with a rich heritage of historical landmarks, cultural institutions, and natural beauty. The city is situated on the south bank of the Yangtze River, which has played a significant role in its development and growth.

Nanjing is also an important transportation hub, with a major international airport, a comprehensive highway network, and a well-developed public transportation system.
