# Top Things to Do in Nanjing — Attractions & Experiences

Nanjing, the capital of Jiangsu Province in China, offers a rich cultural heritage and a blend of traditional and modern attractions that make it a must-visit destination for travelers.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **calligraphy** class to learn about traditional Chinese writing and art
- Visit the **Qinhuai Lantern Festival** to experience the city's vibrant culture and traditions
- Explore the **Nanjing 1912** district, a popular spot for shopping, dining, and entertainment
- Attend a **traditional Chinese opera** performance at the Nanjing Opera House
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about Jiangsu cuisine and how to prepare traditional dishes
- Visit the **Nanjing Folk Customs Village** to learn about the city's folk customs and traditions
- Explore the **Nanjing Metro Museum** to learn about the history and development of the city's metro system

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Purple Mountain** National Park, a popular spot for hiking and scenic views
- **Xuanwu Lake**, a popular spot for boating and walking
- **Qinhuai River**, a historic river that offers scenic views and boat rides
- **Nanjing Zhongshan Botanical Garden**, a beautiful garden featuring a variety of plant species
- **Laoshan National Forest Park**, a scenic park featuring hiking trails, waterfalls, and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Nanjing Museum**, one of the largest and most comprehensive museums in China
- **Presidential Palace**, a museum showcasing the history of the Republic of China and the life of Sun Yat-sen
- **Confucius Temple**, a historic temple dedicated to the famous Chinese philosopher Confucius
- **Nanjing Folk Customs Village**, a village that showcases the city's folk customs and traditions
- **Jiangsu Provincial Art Museum**, a museum featuring a collection of traditional and modern Chinese art
- **Nanjing Library**, a modern library featuring a vast collection of books, documents, and cultural artifacts

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Nanjing City God Temple**, a historic temple dedicated to the city god of Nanjing
- **The Taicheng Street**, a historic street featuring traditional shops, restaurants, and architecture
- **The Nanjing Christian Church**, a historic church that dates back to the 19th century
- **The Jiangsu Cuisine Museum**, a museum showcasing the history and development of Jiangsu cuisine
- **The Nanjing Traditional Chinese Medicine Museum**, a museum featuring a collection of traditional Chinese medicine and artifacts

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]

- **Zhenjiang**, a city known for its historic sites, temples, and traditional architecture (distance: 60 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- **Wuxi**, a city known for its beautiful lakes, gardens, and traditional architecture (distance: 150 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- **Suzhou**, a city known for its beautiful gardens, canals, and traditional architecture (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)
- **Hangzhou**, a city known for its beautiful West Lake, gardens, and traditional architecture (distance: 300 km, travel time: 4 hours)
