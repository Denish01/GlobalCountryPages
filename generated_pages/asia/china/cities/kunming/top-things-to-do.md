# Top Things to Do in Kunming — Attractions & Experiences

Kunming, the capital of Yunnan Province in southwestern China, offers a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that showcase its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty, and some of the top things to do in Kunming include visiting the Stone Forest, exploring the Dian Lake, and discovering the vibrant streets of the city.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Stone Forest for a breathtaking bird's-eye view of the unique limestone formations
- Explore the **Kunming Flower Market**, one of the largest flower markets in China, and discover the vibrant colors and fragrances of the local flowers
- Visit the **Dongchuan Red Land**, a scenic area known for its stunning red soil and beautiful scenery
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Yunnan dishes, such as **crossing-the-bridge noodles** and **steam pot chicken**
- Attend a **traditional Yunnan opera** performance and experience the rich cultural heritage of the province
- Visit the **Kunming Bird and Flower Market**, a bustling market where you can find a wide range of birds, flowers, and other local products
- Explore the **Jinma Biji Historic District**, a historic area with well-preserved traditional architecture and rich cultural heritage
