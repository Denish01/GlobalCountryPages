# Cost of Living in Chongqing - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Chongqing is generally **affordable**, with a daily budget of $20-50 USD for backpackers, $50-100 USD for mid-range travelers, and $100-200 USD for comfort seekers.

Currency: CNY

[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a hotel to save on accommodation costs.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls to enjoy delicious and affordable meals.
- Use public transportation, such as buses and metro, to get around the city instead of taxis.
- Avoid eating at touristy restaurants, which tend to be more expensive than local eateries.
- Shop at local markets or supermarkets for groceries and souvenirs instead of touristy shops.
- Take advantage of free walking tours and cultural events to explore the city without breaking the bank.
