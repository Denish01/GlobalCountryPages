[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Sichuan Cuisine Cooking Class** and learn how to make traditional dishes like mapo tofu and Kung Pao chicken
- Visit a **Traditional Sichuan Opera** performance and experience the unique music, dance, and acrobatics
- Explore the **Jinli Street Night Market** and try local street food and drinks
- Attend a **Giant Panda Volunteer Program** and help with panda conservation and research
- Learn about **Sichuan Tea Culture** and participate in a traditional tea ceremony
- Take a **Calligraphy Class** and learn about the art of Chinese calligraphy
- Visit a **Local Market** and try your hand at bargaining for souvenirs and local goods
- Experience the **Chengdu Hot Pot** and try the spicy and flavorful local dish

- Explore the **Huanglongxi Ancient Town** and enjoy the scenic canals and traditional architecture
