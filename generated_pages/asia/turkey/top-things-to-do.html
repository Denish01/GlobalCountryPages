Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries:

- 3-day itinerary: visit Istanbul's top attractions, such as Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque

- 7-day itinerary: explore Istanbul, visit the ancient city of Ephesus, and relax on the Aegean coast
