# Utility & Internet Costs in Kyrgyzstan

The cost of utilities and internet in Kyrgyzstan can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $50 to $150 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure in Kyrgyzstan is relatively underdeveloped, with frequent power outages and water shortages, especially in rural areas. The costs of utilities are generally stable, but can fluctuate depending on the season, with higher prices for electricity and heating during the winter months. The country has a **high inflation rate**, which can also impact the cost of utilities, with an inflation rate of 10.8% in 2023.

The utility costs in Kyrgyzstan can also vary depending on the location, with higher costs in urban areas such as Bishkek. The **GDP per capita** in Kyrgyzstan is $8,012, which is relatively low, and this can impact the affordability of utilities for many households. Additionally, the **out-of-pocket health spend** is 42.3%, which can also affect the household budget for utilities.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
- To set up utilities, it is recommended to contact the local provider and provide identification and proof of address.
