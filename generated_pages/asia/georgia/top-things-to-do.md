# Top Things to Do in Georgia — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine tasting tour** in the Kakheti region to sample some of the world's oldest wines
- Attend a **traditional Georgian supra** (feast) to experience the local culture and hospitality
- Learn about the ancient **Georgian alphabet** and calligraphy at a local workshop
- Visit a **local market** to sample traditional foods and drinks, such as khinkali and churchkhela
- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to prepare traditional Georgian dishes, such as khachapuri and lobio
- Go **horseback riding** through the stunning Svaneti Mountains and traditional villages
- Attend a **traditional Georgian music and dance performance** to experience the local culture and traditions

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the stunning **Svaneti Mountains** and traditional villages
- Hike and trek through **Kazbegi National Park** and scenic valleys
- Visit **Uplistsikhe Cave Town** and explore the ancient cave city
- Relax on the **Black Sea coast** and enjoy the city's vibrant atmosphere in Batumi
- Go **rafting or kayaking** on the Rioni River and enjoy the stunning scenery
- Explore the **Vardzia Cave City** and stunning rock-cut architecture

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Georgia** in Tbilisi to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Tbilisi State Opera and Ballet Theatre** and attend a performance
- Attend a **traditional Georgian festival**, such as the Tbilisi International Festival
- Visit the **Gelati Monastery** and marvel at the stunning medieval architecture and frescoes
- Take a **guided tour** of the ancient city of Mtskheta and learn about its history and significance
- Visit the **Sighnaghi Museum** and learn about the history and culture of the region

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **David Gareja Monastery** and explore the stunning cave city and rock-cut architecture
- Explore the **Tusheti National Park** and traditional villages

- Visit the **Ananuri Fortress** and learn about the history and significance of this medieval fortress
- Take a **hiking tour** of the stunning **Kazbegi Mountains** and scenic valleys

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip from Tbilisi to **Mtskheta** (approx. 1 hour, 30 minutes) and explore the ancient city and UNESCO World Heritage Site
2. Visit **Kazbegi National Park** (approx. 3 hours from Tbilisi) and hike and trek through the stunning mountain landscapes and scenic valleys
3. Take a day trip from Batumi to **Gonio Fortress** (approx. 30 minutes) and explore the ancient fortress and stunning views
4. Visit **Svaneti** (approx. 5 hours from Tbilisi) and explore the stunning mountain landscapes and traditional villages
5. Take a day trip from Kutaisi to **Gelati Monastery** (approx. 30 minutes) and marvel at the stunning medieval architecture and frescoes
