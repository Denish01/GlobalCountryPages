# Top Things to Do in Mongolia — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Mongolia, visit the **Genghis Khan Square** in Ulaanbaatar, explore the **Gobi Desert**, and immerse yourself in the traditional **Naadam Festival**.

[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Genghis Khan Square | Ulaanbaatar | Historical Landmark | Central meeting point and symbol of Mongolian history |
| National Museum of Mongolia | Ulaanbaatar | Museum | Comprehensive collection of Mongolian history, culture, and art |
| Gandantegchinlen Monastery | Ulaanbaatar | Buddhist Monastery | One of the most important Buddhist sites in Mongolia |
| Orkhon Valley | Central Mongolia | Cultural Landscape | UNESCO World Heritage Site and historic heartland of Mongolian culture |

| Khustai Nuruu National Park | Central Mongolia | National Park | Home to the endangered **Takhi** wild horses |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a traditional **Naadam Festival** to experience Mongolian sports, music, and dance
- Take a **horse-riding tour** across the Mongolian steppe to explore the vast landscape
- Visit a **nomadic family** to learn about their traditional lifestyle and customs
- Try **Mongolian cuisine**, including **boortsog** (fried dough) and **buuz** (steamed dumplings)
- Learn about **Mongolian throat singing** and try to sing like a local
- Stay in a **ger camp** to experience traditional Mongolian accommodation
- Participate in a **traditional Eagle Hunting** demonstration to see the skill of Mongolian hunters

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Gobi Desert**, one of the most unique and fragile ecosystems in the world
- Hike in the **Altai Mountains**, a remote and scenic range with stunning views
- Visit **Lake Khovsgol**, a pristine freshwater lake with crystal-clear waters
- Go **fishing** in the **Selenge River**, home to a variety of fish species
- Take a **camel ride** across the **Gobi Desert** to experience the vast landscape

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Mongolia** to learn about Mongolian history, culture, and art
- Explore the **Gandantegchinlen Monastery**, one of the most important Buddhist sites in Mongolia
- Attend a **traditional music and dance performance** to experience Mongolian culture
- Take a **calligraphy class** to learn about Mongolian script and writing
- Visit the **Zanabazar Museum of Fine Arts** to see a collection of Mongolian art and artifacts
- Learn about **Mongolian Buddhism** and visit the **Amarbayasgalant Monastery**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kharkhorin** town, the ancient capital of Mongolia and home to the **Erdene Zuu Monastery**
- Explore the **Khangai Mountains**, a scenic range with stunning views and traditional nomadic families
- Take a **boat tour** on **Lake Khovsgol** to see the surrounding mountains and wildlife
- Visit the **Tsonjin Boldog** statue, a giant statue of Genghis Khan on horseback
- Attend a **local festival**, such as the **Ice Festival** or the **Summer Festival**, to experience Mongolian culture

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Ulaanbaatar** to **Genghis Khan Statue** (54 km, 1 hour) to see the giant statue and learn about Mongolian history
- Visit the **Terelj National Park** (80 km, 2 hours) to explore the scenic park and unique rock formations
- Take a day trip from **Ulaanbaatar** to **Khustai Nuruu National Park** (100 km, 2.5 hours) to see the endangered **Takhi** wild horses
- Visit the **Amarbayasgalant Monastery** (360 km, 6 hours) to learn about Mongolian Buddhism and see the stunning architecture
- Take a day trip from **Ulaanbaatar** to **Tuvkhun Hermitage** (120 km, 3 hours) to explore the remote and scenic hermitage
