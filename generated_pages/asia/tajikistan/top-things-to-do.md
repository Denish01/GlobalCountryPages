# Top Things to Do in Tajikistan — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Tajikistan, visit the stunning **Fann Mountains**, explore the historic city of **Penjikent**, and discover the vibrant culture of **Dushanbe**, the capital city, which offers a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Wakhan Corridor**, a narrow strip of land that connects Tajikistan to China, and experience the unique culture of the **Wakhi people**.
- Attend a traditional **Tajik wedding**, known for its vibrant music, dance, and food.
- Visit a **local bazaar**, such as the Green Bazaar in Dushanbe, to experience the sights, sounds, and smells of traditional Tajik markets.
- Take a **homestay** in a rural village, such as **Sarytag** or **Artuch**, to experience traditional Tajik hospitality and cuisine.
- Learn about the production of **Tajik silk**, a traditional craft that has been passed down for generations.
- Visit a **Tajik tea house**, such as the **Chaykhana** in Dushanbe, to experience the traditional Tajik tea culture.
- Explore the **Fann Mountains** on horseback, a traditional mode of transportation in Tajikistan.
