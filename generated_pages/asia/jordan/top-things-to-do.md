# Top Things to Do in Jordan — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Jordan, the best things to do include exploring the ancient city of Petra, floating in the Dead Sea, and experiencing the vibrant culture of Amman, with a wide range of unique attractions and activities to suit all interests and travel styles.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Bedouin camp** tour in Wadi Rum and experience the traditional hospitality and culture of the Bedouin people
- Go on a **camel trek** through the desert landscapes of Wadi Rum and enjoy the stunning scenery
- Visit a **local market** in Amman and try some of the traditional Jordanian cuisine, such as **mansaf** and **kebabs**
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Jordanian dishes
- Go **scuba diving** or **snorkeling** in the Red Sea and explore the coral reefs and marine life
- Attend a **traditional Arabic music** performance and experience the vibrant culture of Jordan
- Take a **hot air balloon** ride over Wadi Rum and enjoy the breathtaking views of the desert landscape

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Dana Biosphere Reserve** and discover the unique flora and fauna of Jordan
- Hike the **Jordan Trail** and enjoy the stunning scenery and diverse landscapes
- Visit the **Dead Sea** and float in the salty waters
- Go **hiking** or **rock climbing** in Wadi Rum and enjoy the stunning desert landscapes
- Relax on the **beaches** of Aqaba and enjoy the warm waters of the Red Sea
- Take a **desert safari** and explore the dunes and canyons of Wadi Rum

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Jordan Museum** in Amman and learn about the history and culture of Jordan
- Explore the **Roman Theatre** in Amman and discover the historic significance of the site
- Attend a **traditional Jordanian wedding** and experience the vibrant culture and customs
- Take a **calligraphy class** and learn about the traditional Arabic script
- Visit the **Mosque of King Abdullah** in Amman and learn about the history and significance of the site
- Explore the **historic town** of Madaba and discover the ancient mosaics and historic buildings

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]

- Visit the **Hammamat Ma'in** hot springs and relax in the warm waters

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Jerash** from Amman (45 km, 1 hour) and explore the ancient Roman ruins
- Visit **Dead Sea** from Amman (60 km, 1.5 hours) and float in the salty waters
- Explore **Wadi Rum** from Aqaba (60 km, 1 hour) and enjoy the stunning desert landscapes
- Take a day trip to **Petra** from Wadi Musa (10 km, 30 minutes) and explore the ancient city
- Visit **Mount Nebo** from Amman (40 km, 1 hour) and enjoy the stunning views of the Dead Sea
