**Business Travelers:** Business travelers may need to provide additional documentation, such as an invitation letter from a Kuwaiti company or a letter from their employer. They may also need to apply for a different type of visa, such as a business visa or a work visa.
**Students:** Students will need to provide proof of enrollment in a Kuwaiti educational institution, as well as a letter of acceptance from the institution. They may also need to apply for a student visa, which can be valid for up to one year.
**Workers:** Workers will need to have a sponsorship from a Kuwaiti employer, and will need to apply for a work visa. The process for obtaining a work visa can be complex and time-consuming, and workers may need to provide additional documentation, such as a medical certificate and a police clearance certificate.
