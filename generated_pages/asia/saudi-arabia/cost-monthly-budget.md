# Monthly Budget Guide for Saudi Arabia

To live in Saudi Arabia, you would need around $2,500-$4,000 per month for a mid-range lifestyle, depending on your personal preferences and the city you choose to live in.

[SECTION] Cost of Living Overview [/SECTION]
Saudi Arabia is a country with a high standard of living, and its cost of living reflects this. With a GDP per capita of $71,375, it is one of the wealthiest countries in the world. The cost of living in Saudi Arabia can vary significantly depending on the city and lifestyle. For example, living in a smaller city like Jeddah or Dammam can be more affordable than living in the capital city of Riyadh. Overall, Saudi Arabia can be a relatively expensive country to live in, but it also offers many opportunities for employment and a high standard of living.

[SECTION] Budget Tier Lifestyles [/SECTION]
At the budget level of $1,800 per month, daily life in Saudi Arabia can be relatively basic but still comfortable. You would likely need to share an apartment with roommates and cook most of your meals at home. You could afford to take public transportation and occasionally take a taxi. You would need to be careful with your expenses and prioritize your spending, but you could still enjoy some entertainment and social activities, such as going to the movies or playing sports. However, you might not be able to afford many luxuries, such as eating out frequently or traveling.

At the mid-range level of $2,800 per month, daily life in Saudi Arabia can be more comfortable and enjoyable. You could afford to rent a one-bedroom apartment in a central location and cook some meals at home while also eating out occasionally. You could take a mix of public transportation and taxis, and occasionally rent a car for the weekend. You could also afford to join a gym or take a class, and enjoy more entertainment and social activities, such as going to restaurants or attending events. However, you might still need to be mindful of your expenses and prioritize your spending.

At the comfortable level of $4,500 per month, daily life in Saudi Arabia can be very enjoyable and luxurious. You could afford to rent a large apartment or house in a desirable location, and eat out frequently at high-end restaurants. You could take taxis or rent a car whenever you want, and enjoy many entertainment and social activities, such as traveling or attending expensive events. You could also afford to join a private club or take private classes, and enjoy many luxuries, such as a personal trainer or a maid. At this level, you would have a lot of freedom to pursue your interests and enjoy your life in Saudi Arabia.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Strategies [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment with roommates to reduce rent costs
- Cook at home instead of eating out to save on food costs
- Use public transportation instead of taxis or renting a car to save on transportation costs
- Take advantage of free or low-cost entertainment and social activities, such as visiting parks or attending community events
- Shop at local markets or discount stores to save on clothing and personal items
- Consider purchasing a used car instead of renting or buying a new one
- Take advantage of discounts or promotions offered by restaurants, gyms, or other businesses
- Use online resources or apps to find deals or discounts on everyday items
