- The **Hejaz Railway** is a historic railway that showcases the country's rich transportation history.
- The **Najd Village** in Riyadh is a cultural heritage site that preserves traditional Saudi architecture.
- The **Janadriyah Cultural Festival** is an annual festival that celebrates Saudi culture, music, and art.
- The **Al-'Ula** archaeological site is a historic city that dates back to the 6th century BC and features ancient rock-cut tombs.
