# Top Things to Do in Saudi Arabia — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Saudi Arabia, visit the **Al-Masjid al-Haram** in Mecca, explore the ancient city of **Mada'in Saleh**, and discover the vibrant culture of **Riyadh**, the capital city, which offers a unique blend of traditional and modern attractions.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **desert safari** in the **Rub' al Khali**, the largest sand desert in the world, and enjoy thrilling dune bashing, camel riding, and stargazing
- Explore the **traditional souks** of **Riyadh** and **Jeddah**, where you can find unique handicrafts, spices, and local delicacies
- Attend a **traditional Saudi wedding**, a vibrant and colorful celebration of music, dance, and feasting
- Visit a **Bedouin camp** and experience the hospitality of the nomadic tribes, learning about their customs and way of life
- Go **falconry** in the **desert**, a traditional Saudi sport that involves hunting with trained falcons
- Take a **cookery class** and learn how to prepare traditional Saudi dishes, such as **kabsa** and **shawarma**
- Explore the **street art** scene in **Riyadh**, which features vibrant murals and graffiti that reflect the city's modern culture

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Asir National Park**, which features stunning mountain scenery, hiking trails, and diverse wildlife
- Visit the **Farasan Islands**, a marine reserve with pristine beaches, coral reefs, and diverse marine life
- Take a **hike** in the **Jebel Sawda** mountains, which offer breathtaking views and challenging trails
- Go **scuba diving** in the **Red Sea**, which features stunning coral reefs and diverse marine life
- Visit the **Wadi Hanifa**, a scenic valley with stunning natural beauty and diverse wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Saudi Arabia**, which offers insights into Saudi history, culture, and natural heritage
- Explore the **Al-Balad Historical Area** in **Jeddah**, which features well-preserved Ottoman-era architecture and vibrant souks
- Attend a **traditional Saudi dance** performance, such as the **ardha**, which features colorful costumes and energetic music
- Visit the **Masmak Fortress**, a historic fort that played a significant role in the unification of Saudi Arabia
- Take a **calligraphy class** and learn about the art of Arabic calligraphy, which is an important part of Saudi culture
- Explore the **King Abdulaziz Historical Centre**, which features exhibits on Saudi history, culture, and natural heritage

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Al-'Ula** oasis, which features stunning natural beauty and ancient archaeological sites
- Explore the **Rijal Almaa** village, which is known for its traditional architecture and stunning natural scenery
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Farasan Islands**, which offers stunning views of the marine life and coral reefs
- Visit the **Jeddah Corniche**, a scenic waterfront area with stunning views of the Red Sea
- Explore the **Taif** region, which is known for its stunning natural beauty, traditional architecture, and vibrant culture

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Riyadh** to the **Edge of the World**, a scenic viewpoint with stunning views of the Acacia Valley (distance: 90 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Visit the **Mada'in Saleh** archaeological site from **Al-'Ula** (distance: 20 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Explore the **Al-Balad Historical Area** in **Jeddah** from **Riyadh** (distance: 950 km, travel time: 10 hours)
- Take a day trip from **Jeddah** to the **Taif** region, which is known for its stunning natural beauty and traditional architecture (distance: 170 km, travel time: 2.5 hours)
- Visit the **Abha** region from **Riyadh**, which is known for its stunning natural beauty and traditional architecture (distance: 750 km, travel time: 8 hours)
