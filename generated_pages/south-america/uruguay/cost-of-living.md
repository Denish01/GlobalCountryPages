# Cost of Living in Uruguay - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Uruguay is generally moderate, with daily budgets ranging from $30-60 USD for backpackers to $100-200 USD for mid-range travelers and $250-400 USD for comfort seekers. 

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $100-200 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $250-400 USD
Currency: UYU
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Uruguay? [/SECTION]
Uruguay is considered one of the more expensive countries in South America, but still offers a relatively affordable cost of living compared to many Western countries. The country's economy is relatively stable, and the cost of living is influenced by its location, with neighboring countries Argentina and Brazil having a significant impact on prices. Overall, Uruguay is a great destination for travelers and expats looking for a mix of urban and rural experiences, with a moderate cost of living that allows for a comfortable lifestyle.

In comparison to its neighbors, Uruguay is more expensive than Argentina but less expensive than Brazil. The country's unique blend of European and Latin American influences is reflected in its prices, with high-quality food, accommodations, and services available at a relatively affordable cost. However, prices can vary depending on the location, with the capital city Montevideo tend to be more expensive than other parts of the country.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in apartments or guesthouses outside of the city center for lower rent costs.
- Eat at local markets or street food stalls for affordable and delicious meals.
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to save on transport costs.
- Take advantage of free or low-cost cultural events and activities, such as visiting museums or attending festivals.
- Shop at local markets or supermarkets for groceries and other essentials.
- Learn some Spanish to negotiate prices and interact with locals, which can help you save money and have a more authentic experience.
