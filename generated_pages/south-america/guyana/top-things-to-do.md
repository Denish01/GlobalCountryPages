# Top Things to Do in Guyana — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Guyana, you should not miss visiting the stunning **Kaieteur Falls**, exploring the vibrant city of **Georgetown**, and immersing yourself in the unique cultural heritage of the indigenous communities.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Rupununi River** boat tour to spot giant otters, caimans, and over 400 species of birds
- Visit an **indigenous community** to learn about their traditional way of life and customs
- Go on a **guided hike** to the top of **Kaieteur Falls** for a breathtaking view
- Experience the **Georgetown Carnival** with its vibrant costumes and music
- Take a **sunset cruise** on the **Essequibo River** to see the city from a different perspective
- Attend a **traditional Amerindian festival** to learn about the country's rich cultural heritage
- Go on a **birdwatching tour** to spot some of the over 800 species of birds found in Guyana

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Kaieteur National Park** with its stunning waterfalls and diverse wildlife
- Hike to the top of **Mount Roraima** for breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Iwokrama Canopy Walkway** for a unique perspective on the rainforest
- Go on a **safari** in the **Rupununi Savannah** to spot wildlife such as jaguars and pumas
- Relax on the **Shell Beach** and spot turtles and birds in their natural habitat
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Essequibo River** to see the city from a different perspective

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Guyana National Museum** to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **St. George's Cathedral** and its stunning wooden architecture
- Attend a **traditional music performance** to experience the country's vibrant cultural scene
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the local cuisine and ingredients
- Visit the **Umana Yana** to learn about the indigenous culture and traditions
- Go on a **guided tour** of the **Georgetown** city to learn about its history and architecture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Barima-Waini** region to experience the unique culture of the **Arawak** people
- Explore the **Mazaruni** region to see the stunning **waterfalls** and **hiking trails**
- Go on a **fishing tour** in the **Essequibo River** to catch some of the local species
- Take a **tour** of the **Demerara Rum Distillery** to learn about the production process
- Visit the **Kanuku Mountains** to spot some of the unique wildlife found in the area
