# Utility & Internet Costs in Guyana

The cost of utilities and internet in Guyana can range from $150 to $300 per month for a small to medium-sized apartment, with internet prices starting at around $20 per month for a basic plan.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Guyana's utility infrastructure is relatively underdeveloped compared to other countries in the region, with frequent power outages and water shortages reported in some areas. However, the government has been investing in upgrades to the grid and distribution systems, which should lead to more stable and reliable services in the future. The cost of utilities in Guyana can be subject to fluctuation due to seasonal variation, with higher demand for electricity and water during the dry season (September to April) leading to potential increases in prices.

The overall quality of utility services in Guyana is improving, with efforts to increase access to clean water and sanitation, as well as to upgrade the electricity grid to reduce outages and improve efficiency. However, there are still challenges to be addressed, particularly in rural areas where access to basic services can be limited. Despite these challenges, the cost of utilities in Guyana remains relatively affordable, especially when compared to other countries in the region.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
- To set up utilities in Guyana, it is recommended to contact the relevant providers (GT&T for electricity and internet, Guyana Water Incorporated for water) and schedule an appointment for connection.
- Prepaid electricity options are available in Guyana, which can be a convenient option for those who want to control their energy usage and costs.
- The best internet providers in Guyana are GT&T and Digicel, which offer a range of plans and speeds to suit different needs and budgets.
- To save on utility bills, it is recommended to use energy-efficient appliances, turn off lights and electronics when not in use, and take advantage of prepaid options for electricity and internet.
- It is also a good idea to research and compare prices for different utility providers to find the best deals and promotions available.
