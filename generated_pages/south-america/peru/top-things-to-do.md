# Top Things to Do in Peru — Attractions & Experiences

Peru is a treasure trove of rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural beauty, offering countless opportunities for adventure, exploration, and discovery, with the top things to do including visiting the ancient Inca city of Machu Picchu, exploring the vibrant city of Lima, and experiencing the unique traditions of the Andean people.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional Andean textile workshop** in the Sacred Valley, where you can learn about the ancient techniques and symbolism behind these beautiful fabrics
- Experience the **Festival of the Sun**, a vibrant and colorful celebration of Andean culture, music, and dance
- Visit a **local market** in Cuzco or Lima, where you can sample traditional foods, try local drinks, and browse handmade crafts
- Take a **cooking class** in Lima, where you can learn about the unique flavors and ingredients of Peruvian cuisine
- Go **sandboarding** in the Huacachina Desert, a thrilling adventure sport that's perfect for adrenaline junkies
- Attend a **traditional music and dance performance** in the Andean highlands, where you can experience the rich cultural heritage of the region
- Take a **shamanic ceremony** in the Amazon rainforest, where you can learn about the traditional healing practices and spiritual beliefs of the indigenous people

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Manu National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most biodiverse places on the planet
- Hike the **Inca Trail**, a four-day trek that takes you through stunning Andean landscapes and ends at the Sun Gate of Machu Picchu
- Visit the **Colca Canyon**, where you can see condors soaring overhead and experience the stunning natural beauty of one of the deepest canyons in the world
- Take a **boat tour** on **Lake Titicaca**, where you can visit traditional Andean villages and experience the unique culture of the region
- Go **rafting** or **kayaking** on the **Urubamba River**, which offers stunning scenery and exciting white-water rapids
- Explore the **Amazon rainforest**, where you can see exotic wildlife, stay in an eco-lodge, and experience the incredible biodiversity of the region

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Anthropology**, which showcases the rich cultural heritage of Peru and features an impressive collection of artifacts and exhibits
- Explore the **Lima Cathedral**, a stunning example of colonial architecture and a significant cultural landmark
- Take a **tour of the Palacio de Gobierno**, the official residence of the President of Peru and a beautiful example of neoclassical architecture
- Visit the **Museo de la Nacion**, which features exhibits on Peruvian history, culture, and art
- Attend a **traditional Andean festival**, such as the **Festival of the Sun** or the **Inti Raymi**, which showcase the rich cultural heritage of the region
- Take a **Spanish language course** in Lima or Cuzco, where you can learn about the language, culture, and history of Peru

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Mancora**, a charming coastal town on the north coast of Peru, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and water sports
- Explore the **Cordillera Blanca**, a stunning mountain range in northern Peru, which offers breathtaking scenery, hiking opportunities, and traditional Andean villages
- Take a **tour of the coffee plantations** in the Andean highlands, where you can learn about the production of coffee and experience the unique culture of the region
- Visit the ** ancient city of Kuelap**, a fascinating archaeological site that's often referred to as the "Machu Picchu of the north"
- Go **birdwatching** in the **Pacaya-Samiria National Reserve**, which is home to over 1,000 species of birds and offers stunning scenery and wildlife viewing opportunities

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip to the Sacred Valley** from Cuzco, which includes visits to traditional Andean villages, stunning archaeological sites, and breathtaking natural scenery (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Ballestas Islands** from Lima, which are known for their stunning natural beauty, diverse wildlife, and vibrant marine life (distance: 250 km, travel time: 4 hours)
- Explore the **Colonial City of Trujillo** from Lima, which features stunning colonial architecture, vibrant cultural festivals, and traditional Andean music and dance (distance: 560 km, travel time: 8 hours)
- Take a **day trip to the Amazon rainforest** from Iquitos, which includes visits to traditional indigenous villages, stunning natural scenery, and exotic wildlife viewing opportunities (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Lake Titicaca** from Puno, which includes visits to traditional Andean villages, stunning natural scenery, and unique cultural experiences (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
