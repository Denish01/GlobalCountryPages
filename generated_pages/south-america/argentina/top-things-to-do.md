# Top Things to Do in Argentina — Attractions & Experiences

Argentina offers a diverse range of activities and experiences for visitors, from vibrant cities and rich cultural heritage to breathtaking natural landscapes and exciting outdoor adventures. 

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Iguazu Falls | Misiones Province | Natural Wonder | A breathtaking spectacle of 275 waterfalls on the border of Argentina and Brazil |
| Plaza de Mayo | Buenos Aires | Historical Landmark | The heart of Buenos Aires, surrounded by important government buildings and historical sites |
| Perito Moreno Glacier | Los Glaciares National Park | Natural Wonder | A stunning glacier that is still growing, with frequent ice calving and hiking opportunities |
| Teatro Colon | Buenos Aires | Performing Arts | One of the world's most famous opera houses, with guided tours and performances |
| Recoleta Cemetery | Buenos Aires | Historical Site | A unique and fascinating cemetery, final resting place of Eva Peron and other notable Argentines |
| Tierra del Fuego National Park | Tierra del Fuego Province | National Park | A beautiful park with hiking trails, mountains, and stunning coastal scenery |
| Museo Nacional de Bellas Artes | Buenos Aires | Museum | A world-class art museum with a collection of European and Argentine art |
| Caminito | Buenos Aires | Street Museum | A colorful and vibrant street in the La Boca neighborhood, filled with street art and live music |
| Aconcagua | Mendoza Province | Mountain | The highest peak in both the Western Hemisphere and the Southern Hemisphere, popular for climbing and hiking |
| La Boca Neighborhood | Buenos Aires | Neighborhood | A vibrant and colorful neighborhood, known for its street art, tango music, and lively atmosphere |
[/TABLE]

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **tango lesson** in Buenos Aires, the birthplace of this sensual and dramatic dance
- Sample some of the world's best **Malbec wine** in Mendoza, the heart of Argentina's wine country
- Experience the thrill of **gaucho culture** at an estancia, a traditional Argentine ranch
- Visit a **peña folklorica**, a traditional Argentine music and dance venue
- Take a **street art tour** in Buenos Aires, to explore the city's vibrant and eclectic street art scene
- Attend a **soccer match** at a stadium in Buenos Aires, to experience the passion and excitement of Argentine football

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike the **Inca Trail** in Salta Province, a challenging but rewarding trek through stunning mountain scenery
- Explore the **Ibera Wetlands**, a vast and diverse nature reserve in Corrientes Province
- Visit **Peninsula Valdes**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best places in the world to see **whales**, **sea lions**, and **penguins**
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Beagle Channel**, to see stunning glaciers and mountains in Tierra del Fuego National Park
- Go **skiing** or **snowboarding** in the **Andes**, with world-class resorts like Bariloche and Las Leñas

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo de Arte Latinoamericano de Buenos Aires (MALBA)**, a world-class museum of Latin American art
- Explore the **Feria de San Telmo**, a bustling and colorful market in Buenos Aires, filled with antiques, crafts, and street food
- Attend a **performance** at the **Teatro Colon**, one of the world's most famous opera houses
- Take a **cooking class**, to learn about traditional Argentine cuisine and how to make dishes like **asado** and **empanadas**
- Visit the **Catedral Metropolitana**, a beautiful and historic cathedral in Buenos Aires

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museo Evita**, a small but fascinating museum dedicated to the life and legacy of Eva Peron
- Explore the **Jardin Botanico**, a beautiful and peaceful botanical garden in Buenos Aires
- Take a **day trip** to the **Delta del Tigre**, a scenic and relaxing destination just outside of Buenos Aires
- Visit the **Iglesia de San Ignacio**, a beautiful and historic church in Buenos Aires
- Go **hiking** in the **Hornocal Mountains**, a scenic and off-the-beaten-path destination in Jujuy Province

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip** to **Colonia del Sacramento**, a charming and historic town in Uruguay, just a short ferry ride from Buenos Aires (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Lujan Basilica**, a beautiful and historic church just outside of Buenos Aires (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Explore the **Delta del Paraná**, a scenic and relaxing destination just outside of Buenos Aires (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Take a **day trip** to **Mendoza**, a beautiful and vibrant city in the heart of Argentina's wine country (distance: 1000 km, travel time: 2 hours by plane)
- Visit the **Sierras Chicas**, a scenic and mountainous region just outside of Cordoba (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
