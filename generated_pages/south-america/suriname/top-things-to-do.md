[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Marowijne River** boat tour and spot dolphins and other marine life
- Visit a **Saramaccan village** and learn about the traditional way of life of the Saramaccan people
- Go on a **kayaking** trip through the **Saramacca River** and explore the surrounding rainforest
- Attend a **Winti** prayer ceremony and learn about the traditional Afro-Surinamese religion
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Surinamese dishes
- Go on a **hiking** trip to the **Kasikasima** mountain and enjoy the stunning views
- Visit a **local market** and try traditional Surinamese snacks and drinks
