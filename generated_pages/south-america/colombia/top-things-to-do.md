[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]

- Explore the vibrant **street art** scene in Medellin and discover the city's unique cultural expression
- Attend a **salsa class** in Cali and learn the traditional dances of Colombia
- Visit a **traditional village** in the Amazonas region and experience the unique culture of the indigenous communities
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Coffee Region for breathtaking views of the stunning natural landscapes
