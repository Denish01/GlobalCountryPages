[SECTION] What Is Colombia? [/SECTION]
Colombia is a **sovereign** country located in the northwestern part of **South America**. It shares borders with several countries, including Venezuela, Brazil, Peru, Ecuador, and Panama. Colombia is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and vibrant cities. The country has a diverse geography, ranging from tropical rainforests to snow-capped mountains, and from beautiful beaches to vast deserts. Colombia is also home to a wide range of flora and fauna, including many **endemic species** that can be found nowhere else in the world.

Colombia has a long and complex history, with evidence of human habitation dating back to the **Pre-Columbian era**. The country was colonized by Spain in the 16th century, and it gained independence in 1810. Today, Colombia is a **unitary presidential constitutional republic**, with a democratically elected government and a diverse economy. The country is a major producer of **coffee**, **coal**, and **oil**, and it has a growing tourism industry.

Despite its many challenges, including a history of conflict and violence, Colombia is a country with a strong sense of resilience and hope. The country has made significant progress in recent years, with a growing economy, improved security, and a renewed focus on **sustainable development**. Visitors to Colombia can experience the country's vibrant culture, stunning natural beauty, and warm hospitality, making it a popular destination for travelers from around the world.

[SECTION] Key History [/SECTION]
1. 1810 - Colombia gains independence from Spain, with Simón Bolívar as one of the key leaders of the independence movement.
2. 1830 - The **Gran Colombia** republic is dissolved, and Colombia becomes a separate country.
3. 1948 - The **Bogotazo** riots take place, marking the beginning of a period of violence and instability in the country.
4. 1964 - The **FARC** guerrilla group is founded, starting a long and complex conflict that would last for decades.
5. 2016 - The Colombian government and the FARC sign a **peace agreement**, marking a major milestone in the country's journey towards peace and reconciliation.
6. 2019 - Colombia experiences a wave of **protests and demonstrations**, as citizens demand reforms and improvements in areas such as education, healthcare, and the economy.
7. 2020 - Colombia is affected by the **COVID-19 pandemic**, with significant economic and social impacts on the country.
