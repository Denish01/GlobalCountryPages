# Top Things to Do in Chile — Attractions & Experiences

Chile offers a diverse range of activities and experiences for travelers, from visiting the unique **Moai** statues on Easter Island to exploring the scenic beauty of **Torres del Paine National Park**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **wine tour** in the **Maipo Valley** and learn about Chile's famous wine production
- Visit a **traditional Mapuche community** and learn about the indigenous culture of Chile
- Go **stargazing** in the **Atacama Desert**, one of the best places in the world for astronomy
- Take a **gastronomic tour** of **Santiago** and try the local cuisine, including **pastel de choclo** and **chupe de mariscos**
- Go **hiking** in the **Cordillera de los Andes** and enjoy the scenic views of the mountains
- Attend a **traditional Chilean rodeo** and see the skilled **huasos** in action
- Visit the **Chilean Museum of Pre-Columbian Art** in **Santiago** and learn about the country's rich cultural heritage

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Torres del Paine National Park**: hike and camp in one of the most beautiful parks in South America
- **Conguillío National Park**: hike and ski in the **Andes Mountains**
- **La Campana National Park**: hike and camp in the **Coastal Range**
- **Chiloé National Park**: explore the unique culture and wildlife of **Chiloé Island**
- **Easter Island**: visit the **Rano Raraku** crater and see the **Moai** statues up close
- **Puerto Montt**: take a **boat tour** of the **Reloncaví Estuary** and see the **Andes Mountains** from the sea

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Museo Chileno de Arte Precolombino**: learn about the country's rich cultural heritage in **Santiago**
- **Palacio de La Moneda**: take a tour of the presidential palace and learn about Chile's history and government
- **Iglesia de San Francisco**: visit the historic church in **Santiago** and see the beautiful **Chilean colonial architecture**
- **Mercado Central**: visit the bustling marketplace in **Santiago** and try the local cuisine
- **Teatro Municipal**: attend a performance at the historic theater in **Santiago** and see the beautiful **Chilean architecture**
- **Museo Histórico Nacional**: learn about Chile's history and culture at the national museum in **Santiago**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Valle de Elqui**: visit the beautiful valley and see the **Andes Mountains** from a unique perspective
- **Isla Magdalena**: take a **boat tour** of the island and see the **Magellanic penguins** up close
- **Cajón del Maipo**: hike and camp in the **Andes Mountains** and enjoy the scenic views
- **Pichilemu**: visit the charming coastal town and enjoy the beautiful **beaches**
- **Curicó Valley**: take a **wine tour** of the valley and learn about the local wine production

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- **Valparaíso**: take a day trip from **Santiago** (120 km, 1.5 hours) and explore the historic city and its **colorful hills**
- **Viña del Mar**: take a day trip from **Santiago** (120 km, 1.5 hours) and enjoy the beautiful **beaches** and **garden city**
- **La Campana National Park**: take a day trip from **Santiago** (100 km, 1.5 hours) and hike in the **Coastal Range**
- **Isla Negra**: take a day trip from **Santiago** (100 km, 1.5 hours) and visit the **former home of Pablo Neruda**
- **Cajón del Maipo**: take a day trip from **Santiago** (70 km, 1 hour) and hike in the **Andes Mountains**
