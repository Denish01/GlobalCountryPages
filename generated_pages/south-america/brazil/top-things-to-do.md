# Top Things to Do in Brazil — Attractions & Experiences

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **samba** lesson in Rio de Janeiro and experience the vibrant rhythm of Brazilian music
- Go on a **capoeira** tour in Salvador and learn about the history and techniques of this unique martial art
- Attend a **Carnival** parade in Rio or Salvador and experience the colorful costumes and energetic atmosphere
- Take a **cooking class** in the Amazon and learn about the local ingredients and traditional dishes of the region
- Go on a **jaguar spotting** tour in the Pantanal region and see these majestic animals in their natural habitat
- Experience the **Afro-Brazilian culture** in Salvador and learn about the history and traditions of the African diaspora in Brazil
- Take a **street art tour** in Rio and see the vibrant murals and graffiti that cover the city's walls
- Go on a **coffee farm tour** in the southeastern states and learn about the production and harvesting of Brazilian coffee

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Amazon Rainforest** and see the diverse wildlife and indigenous communities that call it home
- Hike to the top of **Pedra da Gávea** in Rio de Janeiro and enjoy the stunning views of the city and the ocean
- Visit the **Lençóis Maranhenses National Park** and see the unique landscape of sand dunes and freshwater lagoons
- Go **surfing** in the northeastern states and ride the waves on the stunning beaches of the coast
- Take a **boat tour** of the **Iguazu Falls** and see the spectacular waterfall from a unique perspective
- Explore the **Tijuca Forest** in Rio de Janeiro and see the lush tropical vegetation and stunning views of the city

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museu Nacional de Belas Artes** in Rio de Janeiro and see the stunning collection of Brazilian art
- Explore the **Historic Center of Salvador** and see the rich cultural heritage and stunning colonial architecture
- Attend a **performance** at the **Theatro Municipal** in Rio de Janeiro and see the stunning architecture and vibrant cultural scene
- Take a **tour** of the **Palácio do Planalto** in Brasilia and learn about the history and architecture of the presidential palace
- Visit the **Museu Afro-Brasileiro** in Salvador and learn about the history and culture of the African diaspora in Brazil
- Explore the **Santa Teresa neighborhood** in Rio de Janeiro and see the stunning colonial architecture and vibrant cultural scene

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Paraty** in the southeastern states and see the stunning colonial architecture and vibrant cultural scene
- Explore the **Chapada Diamantina National Park** in the northeastern states and see the unique landscape and diverse wildlife
- Take a **tour** of the **street art** in the **Vila Madalena neighborhood** in São Paulo and see the vibrant murals and graffiti
- Go on a **hike** in the **Serra do Cipó National Park** and see the stunning scenery and diverse wildlife
- Visit the **island of Fernando de Noronha** and see the unspoiled beaches and diverse marine life

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip** from Rio de Janeiro to the **town of Búzios** and see the stunning beaches and vibrant cultural scene (distance: 105 miles, travel time: 2 hours)
- Go on a **day trip** from Salvador to the **town of Cachoeira** and see the stunning colonial architecture and vibrant cultural scene (distance: 70 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Take a **day trip** from São Paulo to the **town of Santos** and see the stunning beaches and vibrant cultural scene (distance: 45 miles, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Iguazu Falls** on a **day trip** from Foz do Iguaçu and see the spectacular waterfall (distance: 15 miles, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Go on a **day trip** from Manaus to the **Meeting of the Waters** and see the unique landscape where the dark waters of the Rio Negro meet the light waters of the Amazon River (distance: 10 miles, travel time: 20 minutes)
