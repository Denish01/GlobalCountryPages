# Cost of Living in Rio Grande do Sul - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Rio Grande do Sul is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget for backpackers ranging from $30-50 USD, mid-range travelers from $60-100 USD, and comfort seekers from $120-200 USD.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-50 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-100 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $120-200 USD
Currency: BRL
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 10% in restaurants and bars is common
[/FACTBOX]
