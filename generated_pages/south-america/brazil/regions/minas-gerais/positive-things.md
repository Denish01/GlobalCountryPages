[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]

- The **Sanctuary of Bom Jesus do Matosinhos** is a famous pilgrimage site and one of the most important examples of Brazilian Baroque art.
- The **Museu de Arte da Pampulha** in Belo Horizonte features a collection of modern and contemporary art, including works by famous Brazilian artists like Oscar Niemeyer and Portinari.
- The **Festa do Divino Espírito Santo** is a traditional festival celebrated in many towns and cities across Minas Gerais, featuring music, dance, and traditional food.
- The **Cuisine of Minas Gerais** is known for its hearty dishes, such as **feijoada** and **frango ao molho**, which reflect the region's European and African influences.
