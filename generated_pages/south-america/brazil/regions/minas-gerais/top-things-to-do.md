# Top Things to Do in Minas Gerais — Attractions & Experiences

Minas Gerais, a region in southeastern Brazil, offers a wide range of activities and experiences that cater to different interests, from exploring historic towns and cities like Ouro Preto and Diamantina, to visiting natural wonders such as the Serra do Cipó National Park and the **Cascata do Tabuleiro** waterfall.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit a **gold mine** in Ouro Preto to learn about the region's mining history
- Take a **train ride** through the historic town of São João del-Rei
- Explore the **diamond mines** of Diamantina and learn about the town's history
- Attend a **traditional festival** in one of the historic cities, such as the Festa do Divino in São João del-Rei
- Visit the **Inhotim art museum** and explore its unique blend of art and nature
- Go **hiking** in the Serra do Cipó National Park and explore its diverse flora and fauna
- Take a **cooking class** and learn about traditional Minas Gerais cuisine

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Serra do Cipó National Park**, with its diverse flora and fauna, hiking trails, and waterfalls
- Visit the **Cascata do Tabuleiro**, a stunning waterfall in a beautiful natural setting
- Go **hiking** in the **Pico da Bandeira National Park**, with its stunning views and diverse wildlife
- Take a **boat tour** on the **São Francisco River**, which forms the border between Minas Gerais and Bahia
- Visit the **Caparaó National Park**, with its stunning natural beauty and diverse flora and fauna

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museu da Inconfidência** in Ouro Preto, which tells the story of the Inconfidência Mineira, a rebellion against Portuguese rule
- Explore the **historic city** of Diamantina, with its charm and character of a 18th-century diamond mining town
- Attend a **traditional music performance** in one of the historic cities, such as a concert of traditional Minas Gerais music
- Visit the **São Francisco de Assis Church** in Ouro Preto, a beautiful example of colonial architecture
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Casa de Trombone** museum in São João del-Rei, dedicated to the history and development of the trombone

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **town of Prados**, with its historic architecture and natural surroundings
- Explore the **Serra da Canastra National Park**, with its diverse flora and fauna, hiking trails, and waterfalls
- Take a **tour of a traditional farm** in the countryside, to learn about rural life and traditional agriculture
- Visit the **museum of the Inconfidência Mineira** in São João del-Rei, which tells the story of the rebellion against Portuguese rule
- Go **hiking** in the **Serra do Espinhaço**, with its stunning natural beauty and diverse wildlife

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a **day trip** from Ouro Preto to the nearby town of Mariana, with its historic significance and beautiful architecture (distance: 12 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Visit the **Serra do Cipó National Park** from the town of São Roque de Minas (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Explore the **historic city** of Diamantina from the town of Curvelo (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Take a **day trip** from São João del-Rei to the nearby town of Tiradentes, with its picturesque streets and colonial architecture (distance: 15 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
