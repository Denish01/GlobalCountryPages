[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in neighborhoods outside of the city center, such as Santa Teresa or Lapa, which offer more affordable accommodation options.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls, which offer delicious and affordable meals.
- Use public transportation, such as buses or metro, instead of taxis or rental cars.
- Avoid traveling during peak season (December to March) when prices for accommodation and tourist services tend to be higher.
- Take advantage of free or low-cost activities, such as visiting the beach or hiking in the nearby mountains.
- Learn some basic Portuguese phrases, which can help you navigate and negotiate prices more effectively.
