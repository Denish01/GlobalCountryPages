# Top Things to Do in Sao Paulo State — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Sao Paulo State, visit popular attractions like the **Ibirapuera Park** in Sao Paulo City, explore unique experiences such as the **Sao Paulo Coffee Farm** tour, and take in the stunning natural beauty of **Ilhabela** island.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Sao Paulo Coffee Farm** tour to learn about coffee production and taste local varieties
- Visit the **Inhotim Institute** in Brumadinho to explore a contemporary art museum and botanical garden
- Explore the **Sao Paulo Street Art** scene in the Vila Madalena neighborhood
- Go on a **Sao Paulo Food Tour** to sample local cuisine and drinks
- Attend a **Sao Paulo Soccer Match** at the Allianz Parque stadium
- Take a **Hot Air Balloon Ride** over the countryside to see the state's scenic landscapes
- Visit the **Sao Jose dos Campos Air Show** to see aerial performances and aircraft displays
- Go on a **Wine Tasting Tour** in the Sao Roque region to sample local wines

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike in the **Serra do Mar State Park** to see the Atlantic Forest and scenic views
- Visit the **Ilhabela State Park** to explore beaches, waterfalls, and hiking trails
- Go **Rafting** in the Brotas region to experience the rapids of the Jacare-Guacu River
- Relax on the **Guarujá Beach** and enjoy the vibrant nightlife
- Explore the **Cantareira State Park** to see the scenic views and hiking trails
- Visit the **Anhangabaú Valley** to see the scenic valley and waterfalls

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sao Paulo Museum of Art** to see a collection of Western art
- Explore the **Embu das Artes** historic town to see the charming architecture and artisan shops
- Attend a performance at the **Sao Paulo Municipal Theater**
- Visit the **Inhotim Institute** to explore a contemporary art museum and botanical garden
- Take a **Samba Class** in Sao Paulo City to learn about Brazilian music and dance
- Visit the **Museum of the Portuguese Language** to learn about the history of the Portuguese language

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Sao Bento Monastery** in Sao Paulo City to see the historic monastery and stunning views
- Explore the **Vila Madalena** neighborhood to see the street art and vibrant nightlife
- Go to the **Parque da Independencia** to see the historic park and scenic views
- Take a **Boat Tour** of the **Billings Reservoir** to see the scenic views and wildlife
- Visit the **Taubate Historic Center** to see the charming architecture and historic landmarks

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Sao Paulo City to **Guarujá Beach** (distance: 97 km, travel time: 1.5 hours) to relax on the beach and enjoy the nightlife
- Visit the **Embu das Artes** historic town (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour) to see the charming architecture and artisan shops
- Go on a day trip from Campinas to **Anhangabaú Valley** (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours) to see the scenic valley and waterfalls
- Take a day trip from Sao Jose dos Campos to **Serra do Mar State Park** (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours) to hike and see the scenic views
