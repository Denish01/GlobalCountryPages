[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Sao Paulo Museum of Art** is one of the most important art museums in Brazil, featuring a collection of over 10,000 works of art.
- The **Sao Paulo Carnival** is a major cultural event in the state, attracting millions of visitors each year with its vibrant parades and street parties.
- The **Historic Center of Sao Paulo** is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring a mix of colonial and modern architecture and showcasing the city's rich cultural heritage.
- The **Sao Paulo State Symphony Orchestra** is one of the most renowned orchestras in Brazil, performing a wide range of classical music repertoire.
- The **Festa do Divino Espirito Santo** is a traditional festival in Sao Paulo State, celebrating the descent of the Holy Spirit with music, dance, and traditional foods.
