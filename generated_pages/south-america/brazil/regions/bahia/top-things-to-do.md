[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a **Capoeira** performance, a unique blend of martial arts, dance, and music that originated in Bahia
- Take a **Cooking Class** to learn about traditional Bahian cuisine, including dishes like **Moqueca** and **Abará**
- Visit a **Candomblé** temple to learn about the Afro-Brazilian religion and its significance in Bahian culture
- Explore the **Festa do Divino** in the town of Paraty, a colorful celebration of music, dance, and food
- Go on a **Whale Watching** tour in the Abrolhos Marine National Park, a protected area with an incredible array of marine life
- Learn about the **History of Chocolate** in the town of Ilhéus, where you can visit chocolate plantations and taste local products
- Take a **Samba** lesson to learn about the traditional Bahian music and dance
- Visit a **Local Market** to experience the vibrant colors and sounds of Bahian culture

- **Lençóis Maranhenses National Park**, a unique park with sand dunes and freshwater lagoons
- **Serra do Teixeira**, a mountain range with hiking trails, waterfalls, and stunning scenery
