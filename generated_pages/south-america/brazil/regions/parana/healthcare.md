However, the quality of care and access to specialized services can vary significantly depending on the location and type of facility. In urban areas, such as Curitiba, the capital of Parana, there are many high-quality private hospitals and clinics that offer specialized care, while in rural areas, access to healthcare services can be limited. However, there can be significant disparities in access to healthcare services between urban and rural areas, with rural areas often having limited access to specialized care and modern facilities. Despite these challenges, the healthcare system in Parana is generally well-regarded and provides a good level of care to those in need.
