# Cost of Living in Parana - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Parana, a region in Brazil, can vary depending on lifestyle and location, but overall it is considered to be moderate, with daily expenses ranging from $30-100 USD per day for a backpacker to $200-500 USD per day for a comfort-seeker.
