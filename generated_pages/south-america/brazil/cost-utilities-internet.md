# Utility & Internet Costs in Brazil

The cost of utilities and internet in Brazil can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Tips [/SECTION]
- To set up utilities, it's often necessary to provide identification and proof of address, and some providers may require a deposit or contract.
- Prepaid electricity plans can be a good option for those who want to control their spending, but postpaid plans may offer more flexibility and convenience.
- When choosing an internet provider, consider factors like speed, data allowance, and customer support, and look for promotions or discounts that can save you money.
- To save on utility bills, consider using energy-efficient appliances, turning off lights and electronics when not in use, and adjusting your thermostat to use less energy for heating and cooling.
- It's also a good idea to monitor your usage and adjust your plans accordingly to avoid excess charges or penalties.
