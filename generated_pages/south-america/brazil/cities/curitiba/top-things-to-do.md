# Top Things to Do in Curitiba — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Curitiba, visit the iconic **Jardim Botânico**, explore the vibrant **Oscar Niemeyer Museum**, and stroll through the historic **Largo da Ordem**, which offer a glimpse into the city's unique blend of culture, architecture, and natural beauty.
