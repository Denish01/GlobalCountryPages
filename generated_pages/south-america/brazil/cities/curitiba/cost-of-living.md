# Cost of Living in Curitiba - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Curitiba is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget of $30-60 USD for backpackers, $60-120 USD for mid-range travelers, and $120-250 USD for those seeking a more comfortable experience.

Overall, Curitiba offers a great balance between quality of life and affordability, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or budget hotel, which can be a more affordable option than mid-range or luxury hotels.
- Eat at local eateries or street food stalls, which offer delicious and affordable meals.
- Use public transportation, such as buses or metro, instead of taxis or ride-hailing services.
- Take advantage of free or low-cost attractions, such as parks, museums, and cultural events.
- Shop at local markets or grocery stores, which often have lower prices than supermarkets or convenience stores.
- Learn some basic Portuguese phrases, which can help you navigate the city and interact with locals, potentially leading to better deals or recommendations.
