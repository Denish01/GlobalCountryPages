# Top Things to Do in Natal — Attractions & Experiences

Natal, the capital of Rio Grande do Norte state in northeastern Brazil, offers a unique blend of stunning beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture, making it an exciting destination to explore, with top attractions including Fortaleza dos Reis Magos, Ponta Negra, and the Natal Historic Center.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **dune buggy tour** across the stunning dunes of Parque das Dunas, offering an adrenaline-pumping experience and breathtaking views of the coastline
- Visit the **Ponta Negra Night Market**, a bustling market filled with local vendors, food stalls, and live music, offering a unique glimpse into the city's vibrant nightlife
- Go **kitesurfing** in the waters of Praia de Pipa, a popular spot for water sports and adventure activities
- Explore the **historic neighborhood of Cidade Alta**, with its charming streets, colonial architecture, and lively cultural attractions
- Take a **sunset stroll** along the beach of Ponta Negra, offering breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean and surrounding landscape
- Attend a **traditional forró dance performance**, a unique and energetic dance style originating from the northeastern region of Brazil
