# Top Things to Do in Belo Horizonte — Attractions & Experiences

Belo Horizonte, the capital of Minas Gerais state in Brazil, offers a wide range of exciting activities and attractions that showcase its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and vibrant city life, making it a must-visit destination for any traveler.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Street Art** scene in the Savassi neighborhood, known for its vibrant murals and graffiti
- Take a **Food Tour** to sample traditional Minas Gerais cuisine, such as feijoada and pão de queijo
- Visit the **Feira de Artesanato** marketplace to shop for local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a **Football Match** at the Estádio Mineirão, one of the largest stadiums in Brazil
- Go on a **Bike Tour** through the city's scenic bike paths and parks
- Learn about the **Cachaça** production process at a local distillery and sample some of the famous Brazilian spirit

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Serra do Cipó National Park**: a stunning national park with hiking trails, waterfalls, and diverse wildlife
- **Lagoa da Pampulha**: a scenic lake with walking trails, picnic areas, and beautiful views
- **Parque da Serra do Curral**: a nature reserve with hiking trails, stunning views, and diverse flora and fauna
- **Mata do Jardim Botânico**: a lush forest with walking trails, waterfalls, and diverse plant species
- **Circuito de Lazer**: a scenic bike path and walking trail that surrounds the city
- **São Francisco River**: a river that offers scenic views, boat tours, and fishing opportunities

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Museu Histórico Abílio Barreto**: a museum showcasing the history and culture of Belo Horizonte
- **Teatro Municipal**: a historic theater that hosts various cultural events and performances
- **Centro Cultural Banco do Brasil**: a cultural center with exhibits, concerts, and workshops
- **Igreja Nossa Senhora do Rosário**: a historic church with stunning architecture and beautiful gardens
- **Museu de Ciências Naturais**: a natural history museum with interactive exhibits and a planetarium
- **Instituto Moreira Salles**: a cultural institution with exhibits, concerts, and workshops

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **Bairro da Savassi**: a charming neighborhood with street art, boutique shops, and restaurants
- **Praça do Papa**: a scenic square with beautiful views, street performers, and food vendors
- **Museu de Arte Sacra**: a museum showcasing a collection of sacred art and artifacts
- **Jardim Japonês**: a beautiful Japanese garden with tranquil atmosphere and stunning views
- **Café com Arte**: a cozy café with live music, art exhibits, and delicious coffee
