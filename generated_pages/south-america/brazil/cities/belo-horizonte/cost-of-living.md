# Cost of Living in Belo Horizonte - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Belo Horizonte is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $30-60 USD for a backpacker, $60-120 USD for a mid-range traveler, and $150-250 USD for a comfortable stay.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $150-250 USD
Currency: BRL
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 10% in restaurants and bars is appreciated
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Belo Horizonte? [/SECTION]
Belo Horizonte, the capital of Minas Gerais state in Brazil, is a city with a rich cultural scene and a relatively low cost of living compared to other major cities in Brazil, such as Rio de Janeiro or São Paulo. The cost of living in Belo Horizonte is generally in line with the national average, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike. However, prices can vary depending on the neighborhood, with areas like Savassi and Lourdes tend to be more expensive than others.

In comparison to other cities in South America, Belo Horizonte is generally more affordable than cities like Buenos Aires or Santiago, but more expensive than cities like Lima or Quito. The city's economy is driven by a mix of industries, including mining, steel production, and services, which contributes to its relatively stable cost of living. Overall, Belo Horizonte offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an excellent choice for travelers and expats looking to experience the best of Brazil without breaking the bank.
