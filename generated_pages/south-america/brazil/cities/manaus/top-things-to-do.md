# Top Things to Do in Manaus — Attractions & Experiences

Manaus, the capital of the Amazonas state in Brazil, offers a diverse range of activities and attractions for visitors to explore, from its rich cultural heritage to its stunning natural surroundings.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **canoe tour** through the Amazon rainforest and spot exotic wildlife
- Visit a **rubber plantation** and learn about the history of the rubber industry in the region
- Attend a **Capoeira performance**, a unique blend of martial arts and dance
- Go on a **piranha fishing** trip and try your hand at catching these infamous fish
- Take a **sunrise or sunset tour** of the Meeting of the Waters and witness the stunning colors of the sky
- Learn about the **traditional medicine** of the indigenous people and how they use plants to heal
- Visit a **local community** and learn about their customs and way of life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Juma Sustainable Development Reserve**, a protected area of rainforest
- Hike through the **Serra do Acari Mountains** and enjoy the stunning views
- Visit the **Ponta Negra Ecological Park**, a beautiful area of forest and beach
- Take a **boat tour** of the Rio Negro and spot dolphins and other wildlife
- Go on a **jungle trek** and spot exotic birds and animals
- Visit the **Anavilhanas Archipelago**, a unique area of forest and river

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museu de Artesanato**, a museum showcasing the traditional crafts of the region
- Attend a **performance at the Amazon Theatre**, a stunning example of Victorian architecture
- Explore the **Centro Cultural dos Povos da Amazônia**, a cultural center showcasing the traditions of the Amazon people
- Learn about the **history of the Amazonas state** at the **Museu do Amazonas**
- Visit the **Igreja Matriz**, a beautiful church in the heart of the city
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to make traditional Amazonian dishes

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Largo de São Sebastião**, a charming square in the heart of the city
- Explore the **Feira da Manaus Moderna**, a modern market selling local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Take a **stroll along the Rio Negro waterfront**, a beautiful area with stunning views
- Visit the **Museu Numismático**, a museum showcasing a collection of coins and banknotes
- Go to the **Praça do Relógio**, a charming square with a beautiful clock tower

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Presidente Figueiredo Waterfalls**, a stunning area of natural beauty (distance: 100km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Rio Preto da Eva**, a charming town on the banks of the Rio Negro (distance: 50km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Go on a **boat tour of the Anavilhanas Archipelago**, a unique area of forest and river (distance: 200km, travel time: 4 hours)
- Explore the **São Gabriel da Cachoeira**, a charming town on the banks of the Rio Negro (distance: 300km, travel time: 5 hours)
- Take a day trip to the **Tefé**, a charming town on the banks of the Rio Solimões (distance: 400km, travel time: 6 hours)
