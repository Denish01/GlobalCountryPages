# Cost of Living in Fortaleza - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Fortaleza, Brazil, is generally considered to be affordable, with a daily budget for a backpacker ranging from $30-50 USD, making it an attractive destination for travelers and expats alike.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Affordable
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-50 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $60-100 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $120-200 USD
Currency: BRL
Tipping Custom: not expected, but 10% in restaurants and bars is appreciated
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Fortaleza? [/SECTION]
Fortaleza is a city located in the northeastern part of Brazil, and its cost of living is relatively low compared to other major cities in the country, such as Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo. The city has a rich cultural scene, beautiful beaches, and a vibrant nightlife, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats. Compared to global averages, Fortaleza is considered to be an affordable city, with a cost of living index that is lower than many other major cities in South America. However, prices can vary depending on the neighborhood, with areas like Praia de Iracema and Meireles tend to be more expensive than others.

In terms of neighbors, Fortaleza is surrounded by other cities in the northeastern region of Brazil, such as Natal and Recife, which have similar costs of living. However, Fortaleza is generally considered to be one of the more affordable cities in the region, making it a popular destination for tourists and expats. The city's economy is driven by industries such as tourism, agriculture, and manufacturing, which contributes to its relatively low cost of living.

- Take advantage of the city's many free attractions, such as the beaches and parks, which can be a great way to save money and experience the local culture.
