# Top Things to Do in Brasilia — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Brasilia, the best things to do include exploring the city's stunning modernist architecture, experiencing its unique cultural events, and taking in the breathtaking natural beauty of the surrounding region.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a guided tour of the **Oscar Niemeyer** buildings to learn about the architect's vision and legacy
- Attend a performance at the **National Theatre** to experience the city's vibrant cultural scene
- Visit the **Feira de Artesanato** to browse local handicrafts and try traditional Brazilian cuisine
- Explore the **Eixo Monumental** at night to see the city's landmarks illuminated
- Take a **helicopter tour** over the city to get a bird's-eye view of Brasilia's unique urban planning
- Visit the **Centro de Convivência** to learn about the city's history and culture through interactive exhibits
- Attend a **festival or event** at the **Estádio Nacional de Brasília** to experience the city's energetic atmosphere

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike at the **Parque Olhos d'Água**, a scenic park with waterfalls and natural pools
- Visit the **Jardim Botânico**, a beautiful botanical garden featuring a variety of plant species
- Explore the **Lago Norte**, a large lake with opportunities for boating and fishing
- Take a **bike ride** along the **Eixo Rodoviário**, a scenic route with views of the city and surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Cerrado**, a unique ecosystem with diverse flora and fauna

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Museu Nacional da República** to learn about Brazilian history and culture
- Explore the **Instituto Brasileiro de Museus** to discover a wide range of art and artifacts
- Attend a **performance** at the **Teatro Nacional de Brasília** to experience the city's vibrant cultural scene
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Brazilian cuisine
- Visit the **Biblioteca Nacional de Brasília** to browse a vast collection of books and documents
- Attend a **workshop** at the **Centro Cultural Banco do Brasil** to learn about local arts and crafts

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Capela Nossa Senhora de Fátima**, a small chapel with stunning architecture and beautiful views
- Explore the **Vila Planalto**, a historic neighborhood with charming streets and shops
- Take a **stroll** along the **Lago Sul**, a scenic lake with views of the city and surrounding landscape
- Visit the **Museu de Arte de Brasília**, a small museum featuring a collection of modern and contemporary art
- Attend a **concert** at the **Auditório do IBAM**, a unique venue with a diverse range of performances

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **Pirenópolis**: a historic town with colonial architecture and stunning natural scenery (distance: 150 km, travel time: 2 hours)
2. **Goiânia**: the capital of Goiás state, known for its vibrant cultural scene and historic landmarks (distance: 200 km, travel time: 3 hours)
3. **Chapada dos Veadeiros**: a national park with stunning natural scenery and diverse wildlife (distance: 250 km, travel time: 4 hours)
4. **Caldas Novas**: a popular tourist destination known for its hot springs and scenic views (distance: 300 km, travel time: 5 hours)
5. **Planaltina**: a small town with a rich cultural heritage and stunning natural scenery (distance: 100 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
