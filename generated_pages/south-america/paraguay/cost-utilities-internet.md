# Utility & Internet Costs in Paraguay

The cost of utilities and internet in Paraguay can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure in Paraguay is generally well-developed, with most households having access to electricity, water, and gas. However, the costs of these utilities can fluctuate depending on the season and usage. During the summer months, electricity costs can increase due to higher demand for air conditioning, while water costs can rise during the dry season due to reduced supply. The Paraguayan government has implemented measures to stabilize utility costs, but prices can still vary depending on the provider and location.

The quality of utility services in Paraguay is generally good, with most providers offering reliable and efficient services. However, some areas may experience occasional power outages or water shortages, especially during periods of high demand. To mitigate these issues, the government has invested in upgrading the utility infrastructure, including the expansion of renewable energy sources and the improvement of water treatment facilities.
