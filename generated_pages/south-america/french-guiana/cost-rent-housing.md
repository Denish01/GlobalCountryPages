# Rent & Housing Prices in French Guiana

The cost of rent in French Guiana can range from $400 to $1,200 USD per month for a one-bedroom apartment, depending on the location and quality of the accommodation.

[SECTION] Housing Market Overview [/SECTION]
The rental market in French Guiana is relatively small, with a high demand for housing, particularly in the capital city of Cayenne. Typical lease terms range from 6 to 12 months, with a security deposit equivalent to one or two months' rent. Tenants have certain rights, such as the right to a written lease and protection from unfair eviction. In comparison to regional neighbors, French Guiana's housing costs are relatively high, particularly in the city center, due to the territory's strong economy and high standard of living.

The housing market in French Guiana is also influenced by the presence of a large number of **expatriates** and **foreign workers**, who are attracted by the territory's **mining** and **space industries**. As a result, there is a high demand for housing, particularly in the city center, which can drive up prices. However, outside of the city center, there are more affordable options available, including apartments and houses in the **suburbs** and **rural areas**.
