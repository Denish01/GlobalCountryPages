# Top Things to Do in French Guiana — Attractions & Experiences

French Guiana offers a unique blend of French and South American cultures, with a wide range of activities and attractions to explore, from the capital city of Cayenne to the lush rainforests and coastal beaches.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **pirogue** trip up the Maroni River to experience the traditional transportation method of the indigenous people
- Visit a **cacao farm** to learn about the production of chocolate and taste some of the local products
- Go on a **jaguar spotting** tour in the lush rainforest of French Guiana
- Attend a **traditional creole festival** to experience the vibrant culture and music of the region
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the unique fusion of French and South American cuisine
- Go on a **gold panning** adventure in the Approuague River

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Guiana Amazonian Park**, a vast protected area of rainforest and diverse wildlife
- Hike to the summit of **Montagne de Kaw**, the highest point in French Guiana
- Visit the **Tresor Nature Reserve**, a protected area of rainforest and home to many exotic species
- Relax on the beach at **Plage des Roches**, a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing
- Take a boat trip to the **Îles du Salut**, a chain of islands with unspoiled beaches and crystal-clear waters

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Cayenne Museum**, a museum showcasing the history and culture of French Guiana
- Explore the **Place des Palmistes**, a historic square in the heart of Cayenne
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, such as the **Léwoz** or **Kaliña**
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Kourou Space Centre**, a working spaceport with a rich history
- Visit the **Guiana Space Centre Museum**, a museum showcasing the history of space exploration in French Guiana

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **village of Awala-Yalimapo**, a small village on the coast with a beautiful beach and traditional way of life
- Explore the **Crique Fouilloux**, a small creek with a beautiful waterfall and diverse wildlife
- Take a **boat trip** to the **Island of Gonave**, a small island with a beautiful beach and crystal-clear waters
- Visit the **Jardin Botanique**, a beautiful botanical garden with a wide range of plant species

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip to the **Îles du Salut**, a chain of islands with unspoiled beaches and crystal-clear waters (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes by boat)
2. Visit the **Kourou Space Centre**, a working spaceport with a rich history (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1 hour by car)
3. Explore the **Guiana Amazonian Park**, a vast protected area of rainforest and diverse wildlife (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours by car)
4. Take a day trip to the **village of Maripasoula**, a small village on the border with Brazil (distance: 200 km, travel time: 4 hours by car)
5. Visit the **Tresor Nature Reserve**, a protected area of rainforest and home to many exotic species (distance: 50 km, travel time: 1 hour by car)
