[SECTION] Overview: Ecuador vs Peru [/SECTION]
Ecuador and Peru are two neighboring countries in South America that offer a rich cultural experience and a relatively low cost of living. The cost of living in Ecuador is around 10-20% lower than in Peru, making it an attractive destination for expats, students, and retirees. The economic context of both countries is also worth considering. However, Ecuador's use of the US dollar as its official currency makes it an attractive destination for foreigners, as it eliminates the need to worry about exchange rates. Overall, Ecuador's lower cost of living and stable economy make it a great option for those looking to live in South America without breaking the bank.
