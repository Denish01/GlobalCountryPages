# Utility & Internet Costs in Ecuador

The cost of utilities and internet in Ecuador can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $100 to $200 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Ecuador's utility infrastructure is generally well-developed, with most households having access to basic services like electricity, water, and gas. However, the quality of these services can vary depending on the location, with urban areas tend to have more reliable and efficient services. The costs of utilities in Ecuador have been relatively stable in recent years, with some seasonal variation. For example, electricity costs may be higher during the dry season (June to November) due to increased demand for air conditioning.

The overall cost of living in Ecuador is relatively low compared to other countries in South America, and the cost of utilities is no exception. However, the cost of utilities can still vary significantly depending on the location, with cities like Quito and Guayaquil tend to have higher costs than smaller towns and rural areas. Additionally, the cost of utilities can also depend on the type of accommodation, with apartments in modern buildings tend to have higher costs than houses or apartments in older buildings.
