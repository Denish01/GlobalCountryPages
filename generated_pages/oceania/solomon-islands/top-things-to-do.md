# Top Things to Do in Solomon Islands — Attractions & Experiences

The Solomon Islands offer a diverse range of exciting activities and experiences, from exploring the islands' rich history and culture to enjoying the stunning natural beauty of the Pacific Ocean, including visiting the **National Parliament** in Honiara, snorkeling in the **Marovo Lagoon**, and trekking to the **Mt. Popomanaseu** peak.
