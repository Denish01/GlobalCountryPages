# Top Things to Do in Fiji — Attractions & Experiences

Fiji offers a diverse range of activities and experiences, from exploring the crystal-clear waters and coral reefs of the Mamanuca and Yasawa Islands to immersing oneself in the local culture and traditions of the Fijian people.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Kava Ceremony** tour to learn about the traditional Fijian custom of drinking **kava**, a plant-based beverage
- Go on a **Pearl Farm** tour to learn about the process of pearl farming and see the beautiful **pearls** up close
- Experience the **Meke** traditional dance, a storytelling dance that is an important part of Fijian culture
- Take a **Village Tour** to learn about the daily life and customs of the Fijian people
- Go on a **Snorkeling** or **Diving** trip to explore the coral reefs and marine life of Fiji
- Attend a **Church Service** to experience the strong Christian faith of the Fijian people

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Sigatoka Sand Dunes** National Park, a unique ecosystem with sand dunes, forests, and wetlands
- Visit the **Kula National Park**, a protected area with a variety of plant and animal species, including the **Kula Lorikeet**
- Go hiking in the **Taveuni Island** rainforest, with its stunning waterfalls and scenic views
- Relax on the beaches of the **Mamanuca Islands**, with their crystal-clear waters and coral reefs
- Take a boat tour to see the **Fiji Dolphins**, a popular tourist attraction
- Explore the **Navua River**, with its scenic views and opportunities for fishing and kayaking

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Fiji Museum**, with its collection of artifacts and exhibits showcasing the history and culture of Fiji
- Attend a **Traditional Village** tour, to learn about the daily life and customs of the Fijian people
- Take a **Cooking Class**, to learn about the traditional Fijian cuisine and cooking methods
- Explore the **Sri Siva Subramaniya Temple**, a beautiful Hindu temple with stunning architecture
- Visit the **Thurston Gardens**, a peaceful oasis in the heart of Suva
- Attend a **Meke** performance, a traditional dance that is an important part of Fijian culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Taveuni Island**, a lesser-known island with stunning natural beauty and a variety of outdoor activities
- Explore the **Pacific Harbour**, a marina town with a variety of water sports and activities
- Take a tour of the **Navala Village**, a traditional village with thatched-roof **bures** and stunning views
- Go hiking in the **Colo-I-Suva Forest Reserve**, a protected area with scenic views and a variety of plant and animal species
- Visit the **Kula Wild Adventure Park**, a conservation park with a variety of Fijian wildlife

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Mamanuca Islands**, with its crystal-clear waters and coral reefs (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour)
- Visit the **Garden of the Sleeping Giant**, a beautiful orchid garden with stunning views (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
- Explore the **Sigatoka Sand Dunes** National Park, a unique ecosystem with sand dunes, forests, and wetlands (distance: 60 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)
- Take a day trip to the **Yasawa Islands**, with its clear waters and luxury resorts (distance: 80 km, travel time: 2 hours)
- Visit the **Thurston Gardens**, a peaceful oasis in the heart of Suva (distance: 5 km, travel time: 15 minutes)
