# Utility & Internet Costs in Fiji

The cost of utilities and internet in Fiji can vary depending on the location, usage, and provider, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $150 to $300 USD.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
Fiji's utility infrastructure is relatively well-developed, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and telecommunications services. However, the costs of these services can fluctuate due to factors such as fuel prices, seasonal demand, and government subsidies. For example, electricity costs can increase during the summer months when air conditioning usage is higher. Additionally, the Fijian government has implemented measures to reduce the cost of utilities, such as subsidies for low-income households and investments in renewable energy sources.

The quality of utility services in Fiji is generally good, with a high level of access to electricity, water, and sanitation. However, there can be occasional disruptions to services, particularly during natural disasters such as cyclones. The cost of utilities can also vary depending on the location, with urban areas tend to have higher costs than rural areas. Overall, the cost of utilities in Fiji is relatively stable, but it's essential to factor in seasonal variations and potential fluctuations in the cost of fuel and other factors.
