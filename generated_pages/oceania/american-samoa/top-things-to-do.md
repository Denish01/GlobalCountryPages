- Go on a **whale watching tour** in the waters around American Samoa to spot humpback whales, pilot whales, and other marine life
- Learn about Samoan **tattooing** and its cultural significance at a local tattoo shop or workshop
- Attend a **Samoan cultural festival** to experience the music, dance, and food of the island
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about traditional Samoan cuisine and cooking techniques
- Go on a **fishing charter** to catch local fish species and enjoy the stunning ocean views
- Explore the **ancient Samoan archaeological sites** to learn about the history and culture of the island
