[SECTION] Money-Saving Strategies [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment with roommates to reduce rent costs
- Cook at home instead of dining out, and take advantage of local markets and grocery stores
- Use public transportation instead of taxis or cars, and walk or bike whenever possible
- Take advantage of free or low-cost activities, such as visiting the beach or exploring local parks and trails
- Shop at local markets or stores instead of relying on imported goods
- Consider purchasing a used car or scooter instead of renting a vehicle
- Look for discounts or promotions at local restaurants and businesses
- Use energy-efficient appliances and turn off lights and electronics when not in use to reduce utility costs
