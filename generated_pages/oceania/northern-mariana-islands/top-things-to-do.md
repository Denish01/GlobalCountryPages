# Top Things to Do in Northern Mariana Islands — Attractions & Experiences

The Northern Mariana Islands offer a diverse range of exciting activities and experiences, from exploring historic sites and natural wonders to immersing in the local culture and trying unique adventures, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking for a mix of relaxation, education, and excitement.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Chamorro** dance lesson to learn about the traditional dances of the Northern Mariana Islands
- Go on a **turtle-watching** tour to see these amazing creatures up close
- Try **Carolynian** cuisine, a unique fusion of Asian and Pacific flavors
- Learn about the **WWII history** of the islands through a guided tour
- Attend a **traditional festival**, such as the Tinian Hot Pepper Festival or the Saipan Music Festival
- Take a **sunset sailing** tour to see the beautiful islands from a different perspective
- Go on a **fishing charter** to catch some of the local seafood

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **American Memorial Park**, which features hiking trails, a museum, and a memorial to the Marianas Campaign
- Visit the **Managaha Island Marine Sanctuary**, a protected area for marine life
- Go **snorkeling** or **scuba diving** at the **Grotto**, a stunning underwater cave
- Hike to the top of **Mount Tapochau**, the highest point on Saipan
- Take a **kayaking** tour to explore the islands' coastline and marine life
- Visit the **Saipan Botanical Garden**, a beautiful garden featuring local flora

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Saipan Museum** to learn about the history and culture of the Northern Mariana Islands
- Attend a **traditional dance performance**, such as the Chamorro or Carolinian dance
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about local cuisine and cooking techniques
- Visit the **San Jose Church**, a historic church that dates back to the 19th century
- Explore the **American Memorial Park**, which features a museum and a memorial to the Marianas Campaign
- Attend a **local festival**, such as the Tinian Hot Pepper Festival or the Saipan Music Festival

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Laolao Bay**, a secluded beach with crystal-clear waters
- Explore the **Kalabera Cave**, a unique cave with stunning rock formations
- Take a **horseback riding** tour to explore the island's countryside
- Visit the **Tinian Island**, a smaller island with a rich history and culture
- Go on a **bird-watching** tour to see some of the islands' unique bird species

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Tinian Island**, which features historic sites, beaches, and a unique culture (distance: 10 miles, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: Tinian Island, North Field)
- Visit **Rota Island**, which features historic sites, beaches, and a unique culture (distance: 40 miles, travel time: 1 hour, highlights: Rota Island, Taga Beach)
- Explore **Pagan Island**, a remote island with stunning natural beauty (distance: 100 miles, travel time: 2 hours, highlights: Pagan Island, Mount Pagan)
- Take a day trip to **Anatahan Island**, a small island with a unique culture and history (distance: 60 miles, travel time: 1.5 hours, highlights: Anatahan Island, Lake Anatahan)
- Visit **Sarigan Island**, a small island with stunning natural beauty and a unique culture (distance: 80 miles, travel time: 2 hours, highlights: Sarigan Island, Mount Sarigan)
