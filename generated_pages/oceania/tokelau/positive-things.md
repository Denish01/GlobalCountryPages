[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Fakaofo Fortress**, a historic site that showcases the country's rich history and cultural heritage, is a popular tourist attraction.
- The **Tokelauan Language** is a unique and important part of the country's cultural identity, with efforts being made to preserve and promote its use.
- The **Siapo** is a traditional Tokelauan art form that involves creating intricate designs on **tapa cloth** using natural dyes and pigments.
- The **Fatele** is a traditional Tokelauan dance that is performed on special occasions, such as weddings and festivals.
- The **Tuvalu-Tokelau Language Dictionary** is a valuable resource that documents the unique language and dialects of the region.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Sir Colin Meads**, a New Zealand rugby player, has Tokelauan ancestry and has been recognized for his contributions to the sport.
- The **Tokelauan diaspora** has made significant contributions to the economies of **New Zealand** and **Australia**, with many Tokelauans working in the **healthcare** and **education** sectors.
- **Ulu-o-Tokelau**, the traditional Tokelauan system of governance, emphasizes the importance of **community** and **cooperation**.
- The **Tokelauan values** of **respect**, **hospitality**, and **reciprocity** are highly valued and practiced by the community.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **warm hospitality** of the Tokelauan people, who are known for their friendly and welcoming nature.
- The **pristine beaches** and **crystal-clear waters** that surround the islands, making them ideal for **swimming**, **snorkeling**, and **fishing**.
- The **unique cuisine**, which includes dishes such as **fa'ausi** (steamed dumplings) and **palusami** (steamed taro and coconut cream).
- The **opportunities for outdoor adventure**, including **hiking**, **birdwatching**, and **cycling**.
- The **rich cultural heritage**, which is evident in the traditional **dances**, **music**, and **art**.
- The **relaxed and peaceful atmosphere**, which makes Tokelau an ideal destination for those seeking a tranquil and secluded getaway.

- The **Tokelauan traditional medicine**, which uses **natural remedies** and **herbal treatments** to promote health and wellbeing.
