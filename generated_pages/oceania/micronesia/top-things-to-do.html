Unique Experiences

  • Take a traditional canoe trip through the mangroves of Pohnpei and learn about the island's history and culture
  • Learn about the ancient sacred sites of Kosrae and their significance to the local people
  • Participate in a traditional Micronesian dance performance and learn about the history and cultural significance of the dances
  • Attend a traditional Micronesian feast and experience the local cuisine and hospitality
  • Visit a local village and learn about the daily life and customs of the Micronesian people
  • Take a fishing trip with local fishermen and learn about their techniques and traditions
  • Explore the WWII shipwrecks of Chuuk Lagoon and learn about the history of the war in the Pacific

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the Nan Madol ruins and its surrounding mangroves and learn about the history and ecology of the area
  • Hike to the top of Sokehs Rock and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding waters and islands
  • Visit the Utwe-Walung Marine Park and snorkel or dive in its pristine waters
  • Take a boat trip to Ant Atoll and marvel at the untouched beauty of this protected atoll
  • Explore the waterfalls and forests of Kosrae and discover its unique flora and fauna

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the Kosrae State Museum and learn about the history and culture of Kosrae and its people
  • Attend a traditional Micronesian dance performance and learn about the history and cultural significance of the dances
  • Participate in a local workshop and learn about traditional Micronesian crafts and customs
  • Visit the Pohnpei Harbor and watch the sunset over the water and enjoy the local seafood
  • Explore the historic town of Kolonia and learn about the history and culture of Micronesia
  • Attend a traditional Micronesian feast and experience the local cuisine and hospitality

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Lelu Island and explore its ancient ruins and historic sites
  • Take a boat trip to Mokil Atoll and discover its unique flora and fauna
  • Explore the waterfalls and forests of Pohnpei and discover its unique flora and fauna
  • Visit the Chuuk International Airport and experience the unique culture of Chuuk and its people

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip from Pohnpei to Ant Atoll (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1 hour, highlights: snorkeling, diving, and exploring the atoll)
  • Visit Kosrae from Pohnpei (distance: 300 km, travel time: 2 hours by plane, highlights: exploring the island, visiting the Kosrae State Museum, and snorkeling in the Utwe-Walung Marine Park)
  • Explore Chuuk Lagoon from Weno (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: diving, snorkeling, and exploring the lagoon)
  • Take a day trip from Kolonia to Sokehs Rock (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes, highlights: hiking, exploring the rock formation, and enjoying the views)
  • Visit Mokil Atoll from Pohnpei (distance: 100 km, travel time: 2 hours by boat, highlights: snorkeling, diving, and exploring the atoll)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights: Nan Madol, Chuuk Lagoon, and Pohnpei Harbor), 7-day (comprehensive: explore Pohnpei, Kosrae, and Chuuk), 14-day (deep exploration: visit all four states, attend a traditional feast, and take a boat trip to Ant Atoll). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: book your flights and accommodations in advance, prioritize your activities based on your interests, and consider hiring a local guide to get the most out of your trip.
