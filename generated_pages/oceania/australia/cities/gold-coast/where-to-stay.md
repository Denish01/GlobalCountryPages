# Where to Stay in Gold Coast — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting the Gold Coast, the best areas to stay include Surfers Paradise, Main Beach, Broadbeach, Burleigh Heads, and Coolangatta, each offering a unique experience for different types of travelers. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, here's what you need to know:
**Surfers Paradise** (Gold Coast)
Best For: Backpackers, families, and luxury travelers
Vibe: Surfers Paradise is the heart of the Gold Coast, known for its vibrant atmosphere, beautiful beaches, and lively nightlife. It's an area that caters to all, from budget-friendly options to high-end resorts.
Price Range: $80-$250/night
Pros: Central location, variety of accommodation options, and easy access to beaches and attractions.
Cons: Can be crowded and noisy, especially at night.

**Main Beach** (Gold Coast)
Best For: Luxury travelers and families
Vibe: Main Beach is an upscale area with a relaxed atmosphere, beautiful parks, and a stunning beach. It's perfect for those looking for a more laid-back experience.
Price Range: $150-$400/night
Pros: Close proximity to the beach, high-end amenities, and a variety of dining options.
Cons: Limited budget-friendly options.

**Broadbeach** (Gold Coast)
Best For: Families, business travelers, and luxury travelers
Vibe: Broadbeach is a popular area known for its beautiful beach, shopping centers, and restaurants. It's an ideal location for those looking for a mix of relaxation and entertainment.
Price Range: $100-$300/night
Pros: Variety of accommodation options, close proximity to the beach, and a range of dining and shopping options.
Cons: Can be busy, especially during peak season.

**Burleigh Heads** (Gold Coast)
Best For: Backpackers, surfers, and families
Vibe: Burleigh Heads is a laid-back area with a strong surf culture, beautiful beaches, and a national park. It's perfect for those looking for a more relaxed atmosphere.
Price Range: $60-$200/night
Pros: Budget-friendly options, stunning natural beauty, and a variety of outdoor activities.
Cons: Limited high-end amenities.

**Coolangatta** (Gold Coast)
Best For: Families, surfers, and budget travelers
Vibe: Coolangatta is a charming area with a relaxed atmosphere, beautiful beaches, and a range of outdoor activities. It's an ideal location for those looking for a more laid-back experience.
Price Range: $80-$250/night
Pros: Affordable accommodation options, stunning natural beauty, and a variety of outdoor activities.
Cons: Limited high-end amenities and nightlife options.
