# Top Things to Do in Gold Coast — Attractions & Experiences

The Gold Coast is a popular tourist destination in Australia, offering a wide range of activities and experiences that cater to different interests, from theme parks and beaches to cultural attractions and outdoor adventures. 

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dreamworld | Coomera | Theme Park | For its various thrill rides and family-friendly attractions |
| Sea World | Main Beach | Marine Park | To see dolphins, whales, and other marine life up close |
| Warner Bros. Movie World | Oxenford | Theme Park | For its movie-themed rides and attractions |
| Surfers Paradise Beach | Surfers Paradise | Beach | For its vibrant atmosphere and scenic views |
| Lamington National Park | Green Mountains | National Park | For its lush rainforests and hiking trails |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon** ride over the Gold Coast for a breathtaking view of the city and its surroundings
- Go on a **whale watching** tour to see humpback whales in their natural habitat
- Visit the **Gold Coast Hinterland** to explore its scenic villages and wineries
- Take a **surfing lesson** at one of the many surf schools along the coast
- Go on a **helicopter tour** to see the Gold Coast from a unique perspective
- Attend a **theme park event**, such as the Dreamworld Christmas party or the Sea World Carnivale

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Lamington National Park** and its many hiking trails and scenic lookouts
- Visit the **Burleigh Heads National Park** for its beautiful beaches and coastal walks
- Go **surfing** or **swimming** at one of the many beaches along the Gold Coast
- Take a **wildlife tour** to see native Australian animals in their natural habitat
- Visit the **Tamborine National Park** for its scenic views and hiking trails

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Gold Coast Art Gallery** to see a collection of local and international art
- Attend a **performance** at the **Home of the Arts** (HOTA) in Surfers Paradise
- Explore the **Ripley's Believe It or Not!** museum in Surfers Paradise
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about Australian cuisine and cooking techniques
- Visit the **Tamborine Mountain** to see its many art galleries and studios

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Miami Marketta** in Miami for its street food and live music
- Explore the **Varsity Lakes** area and its many parks and waterways
- Take a **walk** along the **Gold Coast Oceanway**, a network of pedestrian and cycle paths
- Visit the **Gallery of Modern Art** in Robina to see a collection of contemporary art
- Go to the **NightQuarter** in Helensvale for its night markets and live music

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip to **Byron Bay** (170 km, 2 hours) to visit its beautiful beaches and coastal towns
2. Visit the **Tamborine Mountain** (40 km, 1 hour) to see its many art galleries and studios
3. Go to the **Lamington National Park** (50 km, 1.5 hours) to explore its many hiking trails and scenic lookouts
4. Take a day trip to **Brisbane** (70 km, 1 hour) to visit its many cultural attractions and historic sites
5. Visit the **Sunshine Coast** (200 km, 3 hours) to see its beautiful beaches and theme parks

[SECTION] Planning Tips [/SECTION]
