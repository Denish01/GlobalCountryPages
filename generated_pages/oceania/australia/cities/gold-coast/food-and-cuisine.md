# Gold Coast Food Guide — Dishes, Street Food & Dining

When visiting the Gold Coast, be sure to try the city's fresh seafood, unique fusion dishes, and traditional Australian cuisine, which are heavily influenced by its coastal location and cultural diversity.

[FACTBOX]
Staple Foods: seafood, beef, lamb, avocados
National Dish: Pavlova, a meringue-based dessert topped with whipped cream and fresh fruits
Meal Times: 7-10 am for breakfast, 12-2 pm for lunch, 6-9 pm for dinner
Tipping at Restaurants: 5-10% in fine dining establishments, not expected in casual eateries
Dietary Notes: vegetarian-friendly, gluten-free options available, halal and kosher options limited
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] Food Culture Overview [/SECTION]
The Gold Coast's cuisine is defined by its coastal location, with a strong emphasis on fresh seafood, meats, and locally-grown produce. The city's cultural diversity, with influences from Asian and European cuisines, has resulted in a unique fusion of flavors and dishes. The Gold Coast is also known for its high-quality restaurants, cafes, and food establishments, which cater to a wide range of tastes and dietary requirements. From fine dining to casual eateries, the city offers a vibrant and exciting food scene that showcases the best of Australian cuisine.

The Gold Coast's food culture is also shaped by its tourist industry, with many restaurants and cafes catering to visitors from around the world. As a result, the city offers a wide range of international cuisines, including Chinese, Japanese, Italian, and Indian. However, traditional Australian cuisine, such as **barbecued meats** and **seafood**, remains a staple of the city's food scene. The Gold Coast is also home to a number of **farmers' markets**, which showcase the region's fresh produce and artisanal products.

The city's food scene is also influenced by its **beach culture**, with many restaurants and cafes offering outdoor dining and take-away options. The Gold Coast is also home to a number of **food festivals**, which celebrate the city's culinary diversity and showcase the best of local and international cuisine. From seafood festivals to food and wine events, the Gold Coast offers a wide range of culinary experiences that cater to all tastes and preferences.

[SECTION] Dining Customs [/SECTION]
- **Tipping**: 5-10% in fine dining establishments, not expected in casual eateries
- **Reservation**: recommended for fine dining establishments, not necessary for casual eateries
- **Dress Code**: smart casual for fine dining establishments, casual for eateries and cafes
- **Eating Etiquette**: use utensils, such as knives and forks, for formal meals, and hands for casual snacks
- **Table Manners**: say please and thank you, and wait for the host to start eating before beginning your meal
- **Food Allergies**: inform your server of any food allergies or dietary requirements

[SECTION] Where to Eat [/SECTION]
- **Restaurants**: fine dining establishments, such as The Fish House and The Bushfood Restaurant
- **Cafes**: casual eateries, such as The Coffee Shop and The Cafe
- **Food Courts**: indoor and outdoor food courts, such as the Surfers Paradise Food Court
- **Markets**: outdoor markets, such as the Burleigh Heads Markets and the Surfers Paradise Markets
- **Food Trucks**: mobile food vendors, often serving street food and snacks

[SECTION] Food Markets & Food Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Burleigh Heads Markets**: an outdoor market showcasing local produce, artisanal products, and street food, located at Burleigh Heads
- **Surfers Paradise Markets**: an outdoor market offering a variety of food, souvenirs, and local products, located at Surfers Paradise
- **The Fisherman's Wharf**: a seafood market and restaurant, offering fresh seafood and waterfront dining, located at Main Beach
- **Tamborine Mountain Distillery**: a distillery offering wine and spirit tastings, as well as a restaurant and cafe, located at Tamborine Mountain
- **The Gold Coast Food and Wine Festival**: an annual festival celebrating the city's culinary diversity, located at various venues across the Gold Coast
