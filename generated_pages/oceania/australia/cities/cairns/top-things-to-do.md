# Top Things to Do in Cairns — Attractions & Experiences

The best things to do in Cairns include exploring the Great Barrier Reef, visiting the Daintree Rainforest, and taking a scenic train ride to Kuranda, with a wide range of attractions and activities that cater to all interests and ages.

| Great Barrier Reef | Off the coast of Cairns | Natural Wonder | As the world's largest coral reef system, it's a must-visit for snorkeling and scuba diving |
| Daintree Rainforest | North of Cairns | National Park | To experience the oldest continuously surviving rainforest on earth |
| Kuranda Scenic Railway | Cairns to Kuranda | Train Ride | For a historic and scenic journey through the Barron Gorge |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon** ride over the Atherton Tablelands for a breathtaking view of the landscape
- Go on a **guided snorkeling or diving tour** to explore the Great Barrier Reef and its incredible marine life
- Learn about **Aboriginal rock art** and the history of the local Indigenous people on a guided tour
- Take a **sunset sailing trip** to watch the sunset over the Coral Sea
- Go **zorbing** down a hill in the rainforest for an adrenaline-pumping adventure
- Experience the **local food scene** on a guided food tour, sampling the best of Tropical North Queensland's cuisine

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Daintree River** for a wildlife cruise and to spot crocodiles, birds, and other wildlife
- Explore the **Wooroonooran National Park** for hiking, camping, and swimming in the natural pools
- Go **white-water rafting** on the Tully River for an exhilarating adventure
- Take a **guided hike** through the **Mossman Gorge** to see the stunning waterfalls and tropical rainforest
- Visit the **Cairns Botanic Gardens** to see a wide range of tropical plants and flowers

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tjapaltjarri Aboriginal Culture Centre** to learn about the history and traditions of the local Indigenous people
- Attend a **didgeridoo performance** to experience the unique sound of this traditional Aboriginal instrument
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about the local cuisine and how to prepare traditional dishes
- Visit the **Cairns Museum** to learn about the history and development of the city
- Explore the **Cairns Night Markets** to shop for local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a **traditional Aboriginal dance performance** to experience the vibrant culture of the local Indigenous people

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Crystal Cascades** for a scenic walk and swim in the natural pools
- Explore the **Kuranda Heritage Village** to see a collection of historic buildings and learn about the local history
- Take a **guided tour of the Cairns Botanic Gardens** to learn about the unique plants and flowers
- Go **fishing** in the Trinity Inlet or surrounding waters to catch some of the local seafood
- Visit the **Tolga Woodworks** to see local artisans at work and buy some handmade crafts

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Daintree Rainforest** (120 km, 2 hours) to explore the oldest continuously surviving rainforest on earth
- Visit the **Kuranda Village** (25 km, 30 minutes) to see the historic train station, markets, and scenic views
- Go on a **Great Barrier Reef day trip** (off the coast of Cairns) to snorkel or dive the world's largest coral reef system
- Take a day trip to the **Atherton Tablelands** (100 km, 2 hours) to see the stunning landscapes, waterfalls, and wildlife
- Visit the **Green Island** (45 km, 1 hour by boat) for a day of snorkeling, swimming, and relaxing on the beach
