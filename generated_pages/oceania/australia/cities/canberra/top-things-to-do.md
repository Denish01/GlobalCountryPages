[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over Canberra to see the city from a unique perspective
- Visit the **Old Bus Depot Markets** to browse local handicrafts and souvenirs
- Attend a performance at the **Canberra Theatre Centre**, which hosts a variety of shows and events
- Go on a **winery tour** in the surrounding regions, such as the **Murrumbateman** wine district
- Take a **stargazing tour** at the **Mount Stromlo Observatory** to explore the night sky
- Visit the **Canberra Glassworks** to see live glassblowing demonstrations and create your own artwork
- Explore the **street art scene** in the **Braddon** and **NewActon** precincts

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Namadgi National Park** for hiking, camping, and wildlife spotting
- Explore the **Tidbinbilla Nature Reserve**, which features native animals, bushwalking trails, and scenic views
- Take a stroll around **Lake Ginninderra**, which offers walking paths, picnic areas, and water activities
- Go **hiking** in the **Brindabella Ranges**, which offer scenic views and diverse flora and fauna
- Visit the **Australian National Botanic Gardens**, which features a wide range of native plants and scenic views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Australia** to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Australian War Memorial**, which features a museum, shrine, and memorial grounds
- Attend a performance at the **Canberra Theatre Centre**, which hosts a variety of shows and events
- Visit the **National Gallery of Australia**, which features an extensive collection of Australian and international art
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Parliament House** to learn about the country's government and history
- Visit the **Museum of Australian Democracy**, which explores the country's democratic history and system

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lanyon Homestead**, a historic property that features a museum, gardens, and scenic views
- Explore the **Canberra Walk-in Aviary**, which features a wide range of native and exotic birds
- Take a stroll through the **Glebe Park**, which offers scenic views, walking paths, and picnic areas
- Visit the **Canberra Museum and Gallery**, which features a collection of local art and historical artifacts
- Go to the **Old Parliament House**, which now serves as a museum and offers guided tours
