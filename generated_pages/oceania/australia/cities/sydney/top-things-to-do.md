# Top Things to Do in Sydney — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Sydney, visit iconic attractions like the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge, explore unique experiences like climbing the Harbour Bridge, and discover the city's vibrant cultural and natural scenes.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Sydney Harbour Bridge Climb**: Scale the iconic bridge for breathtaking views of the city and harbour
- **Whale Watching**: Take a guided tour to spot humpback whales, southern right whales, and other marine life
- **Sydney Food Tours**: Explore the city's culinary scene with guided tours of restaurants, cafes, and markets
- **Surfing at Bondi Beach**: Learn to surf or simply enjoy the sun, sand, and surf at one of Australia's most iconic beaches
- **New Year's Eve Fireworks**: Experience the world-famous fireworks display over Sydney Harbour
- **Vivid Sydney**: Witness the city come alive with light, music, and ideas during this annual festival
- **Aboriginal Cultural Tours**: Discover the history and traditions of Sydney's indigenous people with guided tours and workshops

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- **Blue Mountains National Park**: Explore the scenic trails, waterfalls, and villages of this stunning national park
- **Manly Beach**: Enjoy the sun, sand, and surf at this popular beachside suburb
- **Royal National Park**: Discover the diverse flora and fauna of this vast and beautiful national park
- **Pittwater**: Explore the tranquil waters and scenic coastline of this picturesque area
- **Sydney Harbour National Park**: Take a guided tour or simply enjoy a picnic in this beautiful park
- **Barrenjoey Lighthouse**: Visit the historic lighthouse and enjoy stunning views of the coastline

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- **Australian Museum**: Discover the natural history and culture of Australia with extensive exhibits and collections
- **Sydney Opera House Tour**: Take a guided tour of the iconic building and learn about its history and architecture
- **Museum of Applied Arts and Sciences**: Explore the innovative and interactive exhibitions on science, technology, and design
- **Art Gallery of New South Wales**: Visit the extensive collection of Australian and international art
- **State Library of New South Wales**: Discover the rich literary and cultural heritage of New South Wales with exhibitions and events
- **Sydney Jewish Museum**: Learn about the history and culture of the Jewish community in Australia

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- **The Grounds of Alexandria**: Explore the charming gardens, cafes, and shops of this former industrial site
- **Carriageworks**: Visit the historic railway yards and enjoy the vibrant arts and cultural scene
- **Marrickville**: Discover the eclectic shops, cafes, and restaurants of this vibrant inner-city suburb
- **Barangaroo**: Explore the stunning waterfront precinct and enjoy the shopping, dining, and entertainment options
- **Glebe Markets**: Browse the stalls and shops of this bustling marketplace and enjoy the eclectic atmosphere

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. **Blue Mountains**: Take a day trip to the Blue Mountains, 100 km and a 2-hour drive from Sydney, and enjoy the scenic views, hiking trails, and picturesque villages
2. **Hunter Valley**: Visit the Hunter Valley, 160 km and a 2.5-hour drive from Sydney, and enjoy the wine tastings, gourmet restaurants, and scenic countryside
3. **Palm Beach**: Take a day trip to Palm Beach, 45 km and a 1-hour drive from Sydney, and enjoy the stunning beaches, scenic coastal walks, and picturesque views
4. **Ku-ring-gai Chase National Park**: Explore the Ku-ring-gai Chase National Park, 30 km and a 45-minute drive from Sydney, and enjoy the scenic views, hiking trails, and Aboriginal rock art
5. **Port Stephens**: Visit Port Stephens, 200 km and a 3-hour drive from Sydney, and enjoy the dolphin watching, whale watching, and scenic coastal views
