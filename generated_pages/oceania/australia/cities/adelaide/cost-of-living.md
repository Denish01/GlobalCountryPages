# Cost of Living in Adelaide - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Adelaide is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $60-120 USD per day for a backpacker, $120-250 USD per day for a mid-range traveler, and $250-400 USD per day for a comfort-seeker.
