[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Brisbane Greeters** tour, a free service that offers personalized guided tours of the city
- Visit the **Jan Powers Farmers Markets** to sample local produce and artisanal goods
- Explore the **Street Art** scene in the **Woolloongabba** and **Fortitude Valley** neighborhoods
- Attend a performance at the **Queensland Performing Arts Centre**
- Go on a **Brisbane River** cruise to see the city from a different perspective
- Visit the **Eat Street Markets** for a unique dining experience with a wide range of food options
- Take a **Brisbane Bike Tour** to explore the city's scenic bike paths and attractions

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **D'Aguilar National Park** for hiking and wildlife spotting
- Explore the **Moreton Bay** area for beautiful beaches and marine life
- Go hiking in the **Lamington National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Relax on the beach at **Sandgate**, a charming coastal suburb
- Take a guided tour to see the **humpback whales** that migrate through the area between June and November
- Visit the **Karawatha Forest** for a peaceful walk and opportunity to spot local wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Queensland Museum** to learn about the state's history and culture
- Explore the **Institute of Modern Art** to discover innovative and thought-provoking exhibitions
- Attend a performance at the **Brisbane Powerhouse**, a hub for arts and culture
- Take a **Brisbane Street Art** tour to learn about the city's vibrant street art scene
- Visit the **State Library of Queensland** to explore the extensive collection of books and resources
- Attend a **Brisbane Festival** event, a celebration of music, theater, and dance

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **New Farm Park** for a peaceful picnic and stunning views of the Brisbane River
- Explore the **Teneriffe** neighborhood, known for its charming shops and cafes
- Take a stroll along the **Bulimba** waterfront, a picturesque and relaxing area
- Visit the **Paddington** neighborhood, famous for its antique shops and historic architecture
- Go to the **West End** neighborhood, a vibrant and eclectic area with a wide range of restaurants and shops

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Gold Coast**, approximately 70 km south of Brisbane, to visit the theme parks and beaches
- Visit the **Sunshine Coast**, approximately 100 km north of Brisbane, to explore the beautiful beaches and national parks
- Go to the **Moreton Island**, approximately 40 km east of Brisbane, to see the beautiful beaches and marine life
- Take a day trip to the **Scenic Rim**, approximately 100 km southwest of Brisbane, to explore the stunning natural beauty of the region
- Visit the **Tamborine Mountain**, approximately 80 km south of Brisbane, to see the beautiful waterfalls and national parks
