# Cost of Living in Brisbane - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Brisbane is generally considered to be moderate, with a daily budget ranging from $80-150 USD per day for a mid-range traveler, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

[FACTBOX]

Daily Budget (Comfort): $120-180 USD
Currency: AUD
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Brisbane? [/SECTION]
Brisbane is the third-largest city in Australia and is known for its high standard of living, cultural attractions, and outdoor recreational activities. Compared to other major cities in Australia, such as Sydney and Melbourne, Brisbane is generally considered to be more affordable. However, compared to other cities in the Asia-Pacific region, Brisbane is still considered to be a relatively expensive city. The cost of living in Brisbane is influenced by factors such as housing, food, transportation, and utilities, which can vary depending on lifestyle and personal choices.

In terms of global averages, Brisbane is more expensive than many cities in Asia and South America, but less expensive than many cities in North America and Europe. For example, the cost of a meal at a mid-range restaurant in Brisbane is around $15-25 USD per person, which is comparable to cities in the United States, but more expensive than cities in Southeast Asia. Overall, Brisbane offers a great balance of quality of life and affordability, making it an attractive destination for tourists, students, and expats.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider sharing an apartment or house with others to reduce rent costs
- Take advantage of Brisbane's public transportation system, which is efficient and affordable
- Eat at local eateries or food trucks, which offer affordable and delicious meals
- Visit the city's many parks and gardens, which are free or low-cost
- Avoid eating at restaurants in tourist areas, which tend to be more expensive
- Use the city's bike-share scheme or walk when possible, to reduce transportation costs

Overall, Brisbane offers a great balance of quality of life and affordability, making it an attractive destination for tourists, students, and expats. With some planning and research, it is possible to live in Brisbane on a moderate budget and enjoy all that the city has to offer.
