- The **Tiwi Islands** are home to a unique and vibrant Indigenous culture, known for their traditional **Tiwi art** and **music**.
- The **Darwin Festival** is an annual celebration of music, theater, and dance, showcasing the region's diverse cultural heritage.
- The **National Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Art Award** is a prestigious award that recognizes and celebrates the work of Indigenous artists from across Australia.
- The **Araluen Cultural Precinct** in Alice Springs is a hub for Indigenous art, culture, and performance, featuring a range of exhibitions and events throughout the year.
