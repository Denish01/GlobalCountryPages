# Top Things to Do in Western Australia — Attractions & Experiences

Western Australia offers a diverse range of exciting activities and experiences, from exploring the stunning natural beauty of the **Pinnacles Desert** and **Margaret River** region to discovering the vibrant culture of **Perth** and its surroundings.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **camel ride** along the beach in Broome
- Go **stargazing** at the **Pinnacles Observatory** in Nambung National Park
- Experience the **Didgeridoo** at a local Aboriginal cultural center
- Visit a **pearl farm** in Broome to learn about the industry
- Enjoy a **sunset** over the **Indian Ocean** at **Cable Beach** in Broome
- Explore the **street art** scene in **Fremantle**
- Take a **whale watching** tour from Perth or Exmouth

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Kakadu-like** scenery of **Karijini National Park**
- Hike the **Cape to Cape Track** in Margaret River
- Visit the **Turquoise Bay** in Exmouth for snorkeling and swimming
- Go **surfing** at **Surfers Point** in Margaret River
- Take a **wildlife tour** at **Rottnest Island** to spot quokkas and seals

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Western Australian Museum** in Perth to learn about the state's history and culture
- Explore the **Fremantle Markets** for local handicrafts and food
- Attend a **performance** at the **Perth Festival**
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about **bush tucker** and indigenous cuisine
- Visit the **New Norcia** town to experience **Benedictine** history and culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Lancelin** town for its **sandboarding** and **dune buggy** adventures
- Explore the **Georgiana Molloy** bushwalk in Margaret River
- Take a **ferry** to **Penguin Island** to spot **penguins** and **seals**
- Go **hot air ballooning** over the **Avon Valley**
- Visit the **York** town for its **historic** architecture and **museums**

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from Perth to **Rottnest Island** (19 km, 1 hour by ferry) to spot quokkas and enjoy the beach
- Visit **Fremantle** (19 km, 30 minutes from Perth) for its markets, prison, and street art
- Explore the **Margaret River** region (277 km, 3 hours from Perth) for its wine, surf, and natural beauty
- Go to **Pinnacles Desert** (200 km, 2.5 hours from Perth) to see the unique limestone formations
- Take a day trip from Broome to **Cape Leveque** (210 km, 3 hours) for its stunning beaches and **Aboriginal** culture

[SECTION] Planning Tips [/SECTION]

Book accommodations and tours in advance, especially during peak season. Prioritize activities based on your interests and the time of year you visit. Be sure to try some of the local **seafood** and **wine**. [/CALLOUT]
