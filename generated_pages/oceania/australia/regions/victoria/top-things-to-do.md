# Top Things to Do in Victoria — Attractions & Experiences

Victoria is a diverse and culturally rich region in southeastern Australia, offering a wide range of exciting activities and experiences that cater to all interests and preferences, from exploring world-class museums and galleries to enjoying the beautiful beaches and scenic landscapes of the Great Ocean Road.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **street art tour** in Melbourne's laneways to discover the city's vibrant urban art scene
- Visit the **Queen Victoria Market** to sample local foods and shop for unique souvenirs
- Go on a **hot air balloon ride** over the Yarra Valley to see the scenic countryside from above
- Take a **wine tour** in the Mornington Peninsula to sample some of the region's best wines
- Attend a **sports event** at the Melbourne Cricket Ground or other stadiums
- Visit the **Dandenong Ranges** to see the beautiful gardens and villages of this scenic region
- Go **skydiving** in Melbourne for a thrilling adventure experience
- Take a **cooking class** to learn about Australian cuisine and cooking techniques

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Grampians National Park** to hike and explore the rugged landscape and scenic lookouts
- Explore the **Great Ocean Road** to see the famous Twelve Apostles limestone stacks and other rock formations
- Go **surfing** in Torquay or other coastal towns to ride the waves
- Visit the **Wilson's Promontory National Park** to see the beautiful beaches and wildlife of this scenic park
- Take a **hike** in the **Yarra Ranges** to see the beautiful forests and waterfalls of this region
- Go **kayaking** on the **Yarra River** to see Melbourne from a different perspective

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Gallery of Victoria** to see the collection of Australian and international art
- Explore the **Australian Centre for the Moving Image** to learn about film and television
- Attend a **performance** at the **Melbourne Theatre Company** or other theaters
- Visit the **Melbourne Museum** to learn about the history and culture of Victoria
- Take a **tour** of the **Parliament House** to learn about the history and politics of the state
- Visit the **Heide Museum of Modern Art** to see the collection of Australian modern art

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Hosier Lane** to see the street art and vibrant atmosphere of this laneway
- Explore the **Collins Street** to see the historic buildings and upscale shopping
- Go to the **St Kilda Esplanade** to see the historic pier and amusement park
- Visit the **Brighton Bathing Boxes** to see the colorful beach huts and scenic beach
- Take a **walk** along the **Yarra River** to see the city from a different perspective

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to the **Yarra Valley** (60 km, 1 hour) to visit wineries and see the scenic countryside
- Visit the **Great Ocean Road** (100 km, 2 hours) to see the famous rock formations and scenic coastal drive
- Go to the **Phillip Island** (120 km, 2 hours) to see the Penguin Parade and other wildlife
- Take a day trip to the **Dandenong Ranges** (50 km, 1 hour) to see the beautiful gardens and villages
- Visit the **Mornington Peninsula** (70 km, 1.5 hours) to see the scenic coastline and wineries
