# Cost of Living in Victoria - Prices & Budget Guide

Living in or visiting Victoria can be moderately expensive, with daily budgets ranging from $60-150 USD for backpackers to $200-400 USD for mid-range travelers.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $60-120 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $120-250 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $250-400 USD
Currency: AUD
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Victoria? [/SECTION]
Victoria, a region in southeastern Australia, is known for its high standard of living and stunning natural beauty. The cost of living in Victoria is relatively high compared to other parts of the world, but it is still considered moderate compared to other major cities in Australia. The region's strong economy, driven by industries such as finance, technology, and tourism, contributes to its higher cost of living. However, visitors and residents can still find affordable options for accommodation, food, and transportation, making it an attractive destination for travelers and expats alike.

The cost of living in Victoria is influenced by its proximity to Melbourne, the state's capital city, which is one of the most expensive cities in Australia. However, outside of Melbourne, the cost of living in Victoria can be significantly lower, making it an attractive option for those looking for a more affordable lifestyle. Overall, Victoria offers a great balance of quality of life, natural beauty, and affordability, making it a popular destination for tourists and expats.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying outside of Melbourne city center to save on accommodation costs
- Take advantage of Victoria's public transportation system, which is efficient and affordable
- Eat at local eateries and cafes, which offer a range of affordable and delicious food options
- Visit local markets and grocery stores to buy fresh produce and staples at lower prices
- Look for free or low-cost attractions and activities, such as visiting local parks and beaches, or taking a self-guided tour of the city
- Consider buying a **Myki** card, which can be used to travel on public transportation throughout Victoria, and can be topped up at a lower cost than buying individual tickets.
