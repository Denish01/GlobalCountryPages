[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Great Barrier Reef** is one of the most biologically diverse ecosystems on the planet and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- The **Yarrabah Peninsula** is home to a thriving Indigenous community, with a rich cultural heritage and traditional practices.
- The **Queensland Art Gallery** features a collection of Australian and international art, including works by renowned artists such as Sidney Nolan and Brett Whiteley.
- The **Brisbane Riverwalk** is a popular pedestrian and cycling path that runs along the Brisbane River, offering stunning views of the city and its surroundings.
- The **Woodford Folk Festival** is an annual celebration of music, art, and culture, featuring performances and workshops by local and international artists.

[SECTION] People & Global Impact [/SECTION]
- **Steve Irwin**, the famous wildlife conservationist and television personality, was from Queensland and founded the Australia Zoo in Beerwah.
- **David Malouf**, a renowned Australian author and poet, was born in Brisbane and has written extensively about the city and its culture.
- **The Queensland diaspora** has made significant contributions to the global community, with many Queenslanders working in fields such as medicine, education, and international development.
- **Indigenous Queenslanders** have a rich and diverse cultural heritage, with many communities continuing to practice traditional customs and languages.

[SECTION] What Visitors Love [/SECTION]
- The **Gold Coast** is a popular tourist destination, known for its beautiful beaches, theme parks, and vibrant nightlife.
- The **Daintree Rainforest** is the oldest continuously surviving rainforest on earth, and a must-visit destination for nature lovers and hikers.
- **Queensland cuisine** is a unique blend of Australian and international flavors, with popular dishes such as seafood, steak, and tropical fruits.
- The **Brisbane River** offers stunning views and a range of activities, including kayaking, fishing, and ferry rides.
- The **Sunshine Coast** is a scenic region with beautiful beaches, national parks, and a relaxed, coastal lifestyle.
- The **Great Keppel Island** is a tropical paradise with crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and a range of outdoor activities such as snorkeling and diving.

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- The **Lamington National Park** is a lesser-known but equally stunning destination, with beautiful rainforests, waterfalls, and hiking trails.
- The **Toowoomba Carnival of Flowers** is a popular event that showcases the city's beautiful gardens and floral displays.
- The **Fraser Island** is the largest sand island in the world, with a unique ecosystem and a range of outdoor activities such as four-wheel driving and camping.
- The **Townsville Maritime Museum** is a small but fascinating museum that showcases the city's rich maritime history and heritage.
