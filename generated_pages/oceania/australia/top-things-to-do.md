# Top Things to Do in Australia — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Australia, the best things to do include exploring the country's iconic landmarks, immersing in unique experiences, and discovering its diverse natural beauty, vibrant culture, and hidden gems.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **surfing lesson** at Bondi Beach in Sydney, one of the world's most iconic surf spots
- Go on a **kangaroo safari** in the Outback, where you can see these amazing animals in their natural habitat
- Experience the **didgeridoo**, a traditional Aboriginal wind instrument, at a cultural workshop
- Take a **hot air balloon ride** over the Whitsunday Islands, offering breathtaking views of the Great Barrier Reef
- Learn about **bush tucker**, the native Australian ingredients used in traditional Aboriginal cuisine
- Attend a **Vivid Sydney** festival, a spectacular display of light, music, and ideas
- Go **snorkeling or diving** with **sharks and rays** at the Ningaloo Reef in Western Australia
- Take a **camel ride** along the beach at sunset on Cable Beach in Broome

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Daintree Rainforest**, the oldest continuously surviving rainforest on earth
- Hike the **Larapinta Trail**, a 231km trail that takes you through the stunning West MacDonnell Ranges
- Visit **Whitehaven Beach**, one of the most beautiful beaches in the world, with its pristine white sand and crystal-clear waters
- Go **whale watching** in **Hervey Bay**, a popular spot for humpback whale sightings
- Discover the **Pinnacles Desert**, a unique landscape of limestone pillars in Western Australia
- Take a **sunset cruise** on **Sydney Harbour**, offering stunning views of the city skyline

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Australia**, which tells the story of Australia's history, culture, and people
- Explore the **Art Gallery of New South Wales**, which features a diverse collection of Australian, European, and Asian art
- Attend a **performance at the Sydney Opera House**, one of the world's most famous performing arts centers
- Learn about **Aboriginal culture** at the **Tjapaltjarri Cultural Centre** in Alice Springs
- Visit the **Melbourne Museum**, which showcases the natural history, culture, and society of Victoria
- Take a **guided tour of the Parliament of Australia**, the country's federal parliament

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Cape Range National Park**, a lesser-known park with stunning scenery and wildlife
- Explore the **Margaret River region**, known for its beautiful beaches, wineries, and gourmet food
- Take a **scenic flight over the Kimberley**, a remote and rugged region in Western Australia
- Discover the **Flinders Ranges**, a mountain range in South Australia with stunning scenery and hiking trails
- Visit the **Thursday Island**, a small island in the Torres Strait with a rich history and culture

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip from **Sydney to the Blue Mountains**, a stunning national park with scenic views and hiking trails (100km, 2 hours)
- Visit the **Great Ocean Road** from **Melbourne**, a scenic drive with breathtaking coastline and rock formations (200km, 3 hours)
- Explore the **Whitsunday Islands** from **Airlie Beach**, a group of islands with stunning beaches and snorkeling opportunities (30km, 1 hour)
- Take a day trip from **Cairns to the Daintree Rainforest**, the oldest continuously surviving rainforest on earth (120km, 2.5 hours)
- Visit the **Kakadu National Park** from **Darwin**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning natural beauty and wildlife (200km, 3 hours)
