[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]

- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to prepare traditional Niuean dishes
- Go on a **fishing charter** to catch some of the island's famous seafood

- Attend a **traditional Niuean dance performance** to experience the local music and dance
