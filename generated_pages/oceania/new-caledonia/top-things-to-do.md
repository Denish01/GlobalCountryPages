# Top Things to Do in New Caledonia — Attractions & Experiences

New Caledonia offers a diverse range of activities and experiences, from visiting the iconic **L'Ilôt Canard** to exploring the stunning **Parc de la Rivière Bleue**, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking for a mix of culture, nature, and adventure.

[SECTION] Top Attractions [/SECTION]
[TABLE]
| Attraction | Location | Type | Why Visit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| L'Ilôt Canard | Noumea | Island | Snorkel or dive to see the coral reef and marine life |
| Parc de la Rivière Bleue | Yaté | National Park | Hike to the **Drowned Forest** and see the native **kauri** trees |
| Tjibaou Cultural Centre | Noumea | Museum | Learn about the **Kanak** culture and history |
| Isle of Pines | Isle of Pines | Island | Relax on the stunning **Kuto Bay** and **Kanumera Bay** beaches |
| Hienghène | Hienghène | Village | See the unique **Lindéralique** rock formations and **Turtle Bay** |
| Bourail | Bourail | Town | Visit the **Bourail Museum** and see the **New Caledonia Devil** |
| Noumea Market | Noumea | Market | Buy local handicrafts and try traditional **Kanak** cuisine |
| Amedee Lighthouse | Noumea | Lighthouse | Take a ferry to the island and climb to the top for panoramic views |
| Blue River Provincial Park | Yaté | National Park | See the **madeleines** and **waterfalls** |
| Mount Panié | Poya | Mountain | Hike to the highest point in New Caledonia for stunning views |
[/TABLE]
