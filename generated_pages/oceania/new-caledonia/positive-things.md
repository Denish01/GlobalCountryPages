[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Jean-Marie Tjibaou Cultural Centre** is a world-renowned institution that showcases the art, culture, and traditions of the Kanak people.
- The **Kanak Wood Carvings** are a unique and expressive form of traditional art that reflects the history and mythology of the indigenous people.
- The **Noumea Cathedral** is a beautiful example of French colonial architecture and a prominent landmark in the capital city.
- The **Yenghen Beach** is a stunning stretch of coastline that is known for its crystal-clear waters, white sand, and picturesque scenery.
- The **Hienghene Valley** is a scenic and culturally significant region that is home to many traditional Kanak villages and archaeological sites.

- The **French colonial architecture** of Noumea, the capital city, is a charming and picturesque aspect of the territory's cultural landscape.
