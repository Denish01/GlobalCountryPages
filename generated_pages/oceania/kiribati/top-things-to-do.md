# Top Things to Do in Kiribati — Attractions & Experiences

When visiting Kiribati, the best things to do include exploring the stunning beaches, coral reefs, and unique cultural heritage of this island nation, with top attractions like the **Tarawa Lagoon**, **Abaiang Island**, and **Phoenix Islands Protected Area**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **traditional fishing trip** with local fishermen to learn about their techniques and catch fresh seafood
- Attend a **Gilbertese dance performance** to experience the vibrant culture and traditions of Kiribati
- Learn about **coconut tree climbing** and try it yourself with the help of a local guide
- Visit a **local pearl farm** to see how pearls are cultivated and harvested
- Go on a **birdwatching tour** to spot some of the unique and rare bird species found in Kiribati
- Take a **sailing trip** around the islands to explore the stunning coastline and marine life

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Phoenix Islands Protected Area**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest marine protected areas in the world
- Visit the **Tarawa Lagoon** for swimming, snorkeling, and diving in crystal-clear waters
- Go **hiking** on **Butaritari Island** to discover the island's unique wildlife and scenic landscapes
- Relax on the stunning **beaches of Abaiang Island**, known for their white sand and turquoise waters
- Take a **boat tour** to spot **dolphins** and **whales** in their natural habitat

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kiribati National Museum** to learn about the history and culture of Kiribati
- Attend a **traditional Gilbertese wedding** to experience the local customs and traditions
- Take a **cooking class** to learn how to prepare traditional Kiribati dishes like **babai** and **te buaka**
- Explore the **Tarawa markets** to see local handicrafts and try traditional foods
- Visit the **British Colonial Administration** site on **Abemama Island** to learn about Kiribati's colonial history

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Kuria Island** to discover its unique wildlife and scenic landscapes
- Explore the **Line Islands**, a remote and untouched island chain with stunning beaches and marine life
- Take a **trip to the outer islands** to experience the local way of life and see the unspoiled beauty of Kiribati
- Go **fishing** in the **Tarawa Lagoon** to catch fresh seafood and see the local fishermen at work
- Visit the **Abaiang Island** to see the traditional villages and unspoiled beaches

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Abaiang Island** (30 km, 1 hour) to explore the traditional villages and unspoiled beaches
- Visit **Butaritari Island** (100 km, 2 hours) to go hiking and see the unique wildlife and scenic landscapes
- Go on a day trip to **Kuria Island** (50 km, 1.5 hours) to discover its unique wildlife and scenic landscapes
- Take a day trip to **North Tarawa** (10 km, 30 minutes) to explore the capital's history, culture, and markets
- Visit the **Phoenix Islands** (300 km, 6 hours) to explore the stunning coastline and marine life
