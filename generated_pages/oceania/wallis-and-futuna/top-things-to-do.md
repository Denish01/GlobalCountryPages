[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a traditional **Kava Ceremony**, a unique and important part of Wallisian and Futunian culture
- Take a **Turtle Watching Tour**, to see these incredible creatures in their natural habitat
- Learn about the islands' unique **Polynesian Navigation** techniques, used by ancient sailors to navigate the Pacific
- Experience the **Futunian Traditional Dance**, a vibrant and colorful performance that showcases the islands' rich cultural heritage
- Visit a **Local Market**, to sample traditional foods and drinks, and browse local handicrafts
- Take a **Sunset Cruise**, to see the stunning islands from a different perspective, and enjoy the breathtaking sunsets

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Faioa River**, which flows through the heart of Wallis Island, and offers stunning scenery and wildlife viewing opportunities
- Visit the **Lalolalo Forest Reserve**, a protected area of lush tropical forest, home to a wide range of unique and endemic species
- Relax on the beautiful **Halalo Beach**, a stunning white-sand beach, perfect for swimming and sunbathing
- Take a **Hike to the Top of Mount Puke**, the highest peak on Wallis Island, offering panoramic views of the surrounding waters
- Explore the **Caves of Futuna**, a network of limestone caves, featuring stunning rock formations and unique wildlife

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Wallis and Futuna Museum**, to learn about the islands' rich history and culture
- Attend a **Traditional Wedding Ceremony**, a unique and colorful event that showcases the islands' cultural heritage
- Take a **Cooking Class**, to learn about traditional Wallisian and Futunian cuisine, and sample local delicacies
- Explore the **Talietumu Fort**, a well-preserved ancient fort, showcasing the islands' rich history
- Visit the **St. Joseph's Cathedral**, a beautiful Catholic cathedral, featuring stunning architecture and historic significance

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ahoa Village**, a picturesque village, known for its beautiful beaches and historic sites
- Explore the **Futuna Island's East Coast**, a rugged and scenic coastline, featuring stunning rock formations and unique wildlife
- Take a **Boat Tour of the Lagoon**, to see the stunning islands from a different perspective, and enjoy the breathtaking scenery
- Relax on the beautiful **Alofi Beach**, a stunning white-sand beach, perfect for relaxation and swimming

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
