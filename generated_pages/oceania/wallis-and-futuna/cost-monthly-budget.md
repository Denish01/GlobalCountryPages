[SECTION] Budget Tier Lifestyles [/SECTION]
At the budget level of $800 per month, daily life in Wallis and Futuna is simple and modest. You could afford to eat out occasionally, but entertainment and social activities would be limited. For example, you might spend your weekends exploring the island's beautiful beaches and hiking trails, but you wouldn't be able to afford to take a taxi or rent a car. You would also have to be mindful of your expenses and make sacrifices in order to stay within your budget.

However, you would still need to be mindful of your expenses and make some sacrifices in order to stay within your budget. At the comfortable level of $4,000 per month, life in Wallis and Futuna is luxurious.
