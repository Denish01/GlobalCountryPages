# Top Things to Do in Tuvalu — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Tuvalu, visit the **Nanumaga Island** for its stunning beaches, explore the **Funafuti Conservation Area** for its unique wildlife, and attend a traditional **Tuvaluan dance** performance to immerse yourself in the local culture.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Attend a traditional **Tuvaluan dance** performance to experience the local culture
- Take a **sunset cruise** around the Funafuti Lagoon to see the island from a different perspective
- Learn about **traditional Tuvaluan fishing** methods and techniques
- Participate in a **local handicraft workshop** to learn about Tuvaluan crafts and art
- Go on a **whale watching tour** to see humpback whales in their natural habitat
- Experience a **traditional Tuvaluan feast** with local food and drinks
- Take a **guided tour** of the Funafuti Conservation Area to learn about the unique wildlife

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Funafuti Conservation Area** for its unique wildlife and scenic views
- Visit the **Nanumaga Island** for its pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters
- Go **snorkeling** or **diving** in the Funafuti Lagoon to see the coral reefs and marine life
- Take a **hike** on the **Niutao Island** to see the historic sites and traditional villages
- Relax on the **Vaitupu Island** beaches and enjoy the unspoiled coral reefs
- Go **fishing** in the waters around **Nukufetau Island** to catch fresh seafood

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Tuvalu National Museum** to learn about Tuvaluan history and culture
- Attend a **traditional Tuvaluan dance** performance to experience the local culture
- Explore the **Nui Village** to experience traditional Tuvaluan life
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Funafuti Conservation Area** to learn about the unique wildlife
- Visit the **Funafuti Fish Market** to see the local seafood and atmosphere
- Participate in a **local handicraft workshop** to learn about Tuvaluan crafts and art

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]

- Go **swimming** in the **Nanumaga Island** crystal-clear waters
- Attend a **local church service** to experience the Tuvaluan community
