[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take part in a traditional **Samoan Ava Ceremony**, where you can learn about the island's customs and culture
- Go on a **Turtle Watching Tour** and see these majestic creatures up close in their natural habitat
- Experience the **Samoan Tattooing** tradition and learn about the history and significance of these intricate designs
- Attend a **Church Service** and experience the island's strong Christian heritage
- Take a **Coconut Tree Climbing** lesson and learn how to harvest coconuts like a local
- Go on a **Kava Tasting** tour and learn about the traditional Samoan beverage made from the roots of the kava plant
- Participate in a **Traditional Samoan Dance** workshop and learn about the island's vibrant culture

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Hike to the top of **Mount Vaea**, which offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and ocean
- Explore the **O Le Pupu-Pue National Park**, which features lush tropical forests, waterfalls, and hiking trails
- Visit the **Falealupo Beach**, which is known for its stunning sunsets and crystal-clear waters
- Go **Snorkeling** or **Scuba Diving** in the **Apolima Strait**, which is home to a diverse array of marine life
- Take a **Whale Watching** tour and see these majestic creatures in their natural habitat
- Explore the **Savai'i Island**, which features pristine beaches, waterfalls, and traditional villages
