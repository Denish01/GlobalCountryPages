# Top Things to Do in Tonga — Attractions & Experiences

Tonga offers a unique blend of traditional Polynesian culture, stunning natural beauty, and exciting activities, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to experience the authentic Pacific Islands.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Tongan traditional dance** lesson and learn the history and significance of the dance
- Attend a **Kava ceremony**, an important part of Tongan culture and tradition
- Go on a **whale watching** tour and see humpback whales in their natural habitat
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Tongan dishes
- Visit a **Tongan village** and experience the local way of life
- Go on a **horseback ride** through the countryside and enjoy the stunning scenery
- Learn about **Tongan tattooing**, a traditional art form that is still practiced today

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **'Eua National Park**, which offers stunning scenery, hiking trails, and a variety of flora and fauna
- Visit the **Ha'apai Islands**, which are known for their beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and crystal-clear waters
- Go **snorkeling** or **diving** in the crystal-clear waters of the **Vava'u Islands**
- Take a **hike** through the **Kao Islands**, which offer stunning views of the surrounding islands and ocean
- Visit the **Fafa Island Resort**, which offers beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and a variety of outdoor activities
