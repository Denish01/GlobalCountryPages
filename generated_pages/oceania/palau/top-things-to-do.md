# Top Things to Do in Palau — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Palau, visit its stunning rock islands, explore its unique culture, and enjoy its breathtaking natural beauty, which includes famous attractions like the **Jellyfish Lake** and the **Rock Islands**.

| Rock Islands | Koror | Island Group | Iconic limestone islands with crystal-clear waters and diverse marine life |

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Snorkel or dive with **manta rays** and **whale sharks** in the crystal-clear waters of the Rock Islands
- Take a **traditional Palauan canoe** trip through the stunning rock islands and marine lakes
- Attend a **traditional Palauan dance** performance and learn about the local culture
- Visit a **local village** and experience the daily life of the Palauan people
- Go on a **fishing trip** with local fishermen and learn about their traditional fishing methods
- Take a **sunrise or sunset tour** of the Rock Islands and enjoy the breathtaking views
- Learn about **Palauan traditional medicine** and the local plants used for healing

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Ngarchelong State Forest** and discover the unique flora and fauna of Palau
- Hike to the **Ngardmau Waterfall** and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding jungle
- Visit the **Palau National Marine Sanctuary** and learn about the conservation efforts to protect the marine ecosystem
- Go **snorkeling or diving** in the Rock Islands and discover the diverse marine life
- Relax on the stunning **Long Beach** in Peleliu and enjoy the crystal-clear waters
- Take a **kayaking trip** through the Rock Islands and enjoy the breathtaking views

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Belau National Museum** and learn about Palau's history, culture, and traditions
- Attend a **traditional Palauan festival** and experience the local culture and customs
- Take a **cooking class** and learn how to prepare traditional Palauan dishes
- Visit the **Etpison Museum** and discover Palau's natural history, culture, and art
- Go on a **guided tour** of the **Uet era Ongel** historical site and learn about the country's history
- Attend a **local workshop** and learn about traditional Palauan crafts and skills

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Ongel Island** and explore the stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters
- Take a **hiking trip** to the **Ngarchelong State Forest** and discover the unique flora and fauna of Palau
- Go **fishing** in the **Malakal Harbor** and enjoy the local seafood
- Explore the **Koror State Forest** and discover the stunning views of the surrounding islands
- Attend a **local market** and experience the daily life of the Palauan people

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
1. Take a day trip to **Peleliu Island** (distance: 40 km, travel time: 1 hour) and explore the stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters
2. Visit the **Rock Islands** (distance: 30 km, travel time: 45 minutes) and enjoy snorkeling or diving in the crystal-clear waters
3. Go on a day trip to **Kayangel Island** (distance: 60 km, travel time: 2 hours) and experience the local culture and enjoy the beautiful beaches
4. Take a day trip to **Ngardmau** (distance: 20 km, travel time: 30 minutes) and hike to the stunning waterfall
5. Visit the **Ngarchelong State Forest** (distance: 15 km, travel time: 20 minutes) and discover the unique flora and fauna of Palau
