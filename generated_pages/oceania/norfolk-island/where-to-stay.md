# Where to Stay in Norfolk Island — Neighborhoods & Accommodation

When visiting Norfolk Island, the best areas to stay are in and around the capital city of Kingston, as well as the nearby town of Burnt Pine, which offer a range of accommodations and easy access to the island's main attractions. 

[SECTION] Best Areas to Stay [/SECTION]
For each of the top areas, consider the following:
**Kingston** (Kingston)
Best For: history buffs, luxury travelers
Vibe: Kingston is the historic capital of Norfolk Island, offering a unique blend of colonial architecture and modern amenities, with a relaxed and peaceful atmosphere.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: close proximity to historic sites, range of accommodation options, easy access to dining and shopping
Cons: limited nightlife options, can be quite quiet in the evenings

**Burnt Pine** (Burnt Pine)
Best For: families, budget travelers
Vibe: Burnt Pine is the commercial center of Norfolk Island, with a lively and bustling atmosphere, featuring a range of shops, restaurants, and cafes.
Price Range: $80-200/night
Pros: affordable accommodation options, range of dining and shopping choices, convenient location
Cons: can be noisy, limited historic charm

**Middlegate** (Kingston)
Best For: nature lovers, couples
Vibe: Middlegate is a peaceful and scenic area, surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills, with a romantic and secluded atmosphere.
Price Range: $100-250/night
Pros: stunning natural beauty, range of outdoor activities, peaceful and quiet
Cons: limited amenities, can be difficult to access without a car

**Stevenson Street** (Kingston)
Best For: business travelers, luxury seekers

Price Range: $200-400/night

**Bumbora** (Kingston)
Best For: families, adventure seekers
Vibe: Bumbora is a scenic and peaceful area, surrounded by natural beauty, with a range of outdoor activities and a relaxed atmosphere.
Price Range: $80-200/night
Pros: affordable accommodation options, range of outdoor activities, stunning natural beauty
Cons: limited amenities, can be difficult to access without a car

**Newport** (Kingston)
Best For: couples, luxury travelers
Vibe: Newport is a picturesque and peaceful area, featuring a range of high-end accommodations and stunning natural beauty, with a romantic and secluded atmosphere.
Price Range: $150-300/night
Pros: luxurious accommodations, stunning natural beauty, peaceful and quiet
Cons: limited amenities, can be difficult to access without a car
