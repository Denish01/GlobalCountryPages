The cost of utilities and internet in Norfolk Island can vary, but on average, a monthly utility bill for an 85 sqm apartment can range from $200 to $400 USD, while internet plans with 60 Mbps speed can cost between $50 to $100 USD per month.

[SECTION] Utility Costs Overview [/SECTION]
The utility infrastructure on Norfolk Island is relatively well-maintained, with a reliable supply of electricity, water, and garbage collection services. However, the costs of these services can fluctuate due to the island's remote location and limited resources. The prices of utilities are generally stable throughout the year, but there may be some seasonal variation in electricity costs, with higher rates during the summer months when air conditioning usage is more prevalent. The island's utility providers, such as the Norfolk Island Electricity Authority, strive to maintain a balance between providing reliable services and keeping costs affordable for residents.

The cost of utilities on Norfolk Island is also influenced by the island's dependence on imported fuels and goods. The prices of these imports can be subject to fluctuations in global markets, which can, in turn, affect the cost of utilities on the island. Additionally, the island's small population and limited economy can make it challenging to achieve economies of scale in utility provision, which can also impact costs. Despite these challenges, the island's utility providers are working to improve the efficiency and sustainability of their services, including the use of renewable energy sources and energy-efficient technologies.
