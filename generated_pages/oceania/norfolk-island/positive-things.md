[SECTION] Cultural Treasures [/SECTION]
- The **Norfolk Island Museum** showcases the island's rich history and cultural heritage, with exhibits on the island's convict past, the Bounty mutiny, and the Pitcairn settlers.
- The **Norfolk Island Cemetery** is a historic site that reflects the island's complex history, with graves dating back to the 18th century.
- The **Pitcairn Settlers Village** is a living museum that recreates the lives of the Pitcairn settlers, who arrived on the island in 1856.
- The **Norfolk Island Cultural Centre** promotes the island's unique culture, with exhibits on traditional crafts, music, and dance.
- The **Kingston Pier** is a historic landmark that serves as a reminder of the island's maritime history and its importance as a trading post.
