# Top Things to Do in Norfolk Island — Attractions & Experiences

Norfolk Island offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to explore a hidden gem in the Pacific Ocean, with top attractions including the **Norfolk Island National Park**, **Kingston**, and **Emily Bay**.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **glass-bottom boat tour** to explore the island's coral reefs and marine life without getting wet
- Learn about the island's history and culture through a **guided tour** with a local expert
- Attend a **Norfuk language class** to learn the unique language of the island
- Participate in a **traditional island feast** with local food and entertainment
- Go on a **whale-watching tour** during the winter months to spot humpback whales
- Take a **fishing charter** to catch some of the island's famous seafood
- Explore the island's **World War II history** through a guided tour of the island's military sites

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Norfolk Island National Park** with its unique flora and fauna
- Hike to the **Mount Bates** summit for panoramic views of the island
- Visit **Emily Bay** for snorkeling, swimming, and relaxing on the beach
- Take a **boat tour** to **Pine Island** to explore its coral reefs and marine life
- Go **birdwatching** to spot some of the island's unique and endemic species
- Visit **Anson Bay** for a secluded beach experience with crystal-clear waters

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Norfolk Island Museum** to learn about the island's history and culture
- Attend a **Sunday service** at the **St. Barnabas Chapel** for a unique cultural experience
- Take a **guided tour** of the **Kingston** historic site to learn about the island's penal colony history
- Visit the **Bounty Museum** to learn about the **Mutiny on the Bounty** and its connection to the island
- Participate in a **traditional island craft workshop** to learn about local crafts and skills
- Attend a **local festival** or event to experience the island's vibrant culture

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Norfolk Island Botanic Garden** to explore the island's unique flora
- Take a **sunrise or sunset tour** to experience the island's stunning natural beauty
- Explore the **Kingston** historic site at night for a unique and eerie experience
- Visit the **Anson Bay** cemetery to learn about the island's history and cultural heritage
- Take a **self-guided tour** of the island's **World War II** military sites
