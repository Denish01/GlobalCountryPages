Papua New Guinea offers a diverse array of exciting experiences, from exploring the famous Kokoda Track to discovering the unique culture of the Trobriand Islands, that showcase its rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural beauty.

Unique Experiences

  • Attend a traditional Sing-Sing festival, where locals showcase their music, dance, and costumes
  • Take a Sepik River cruise to experience the region's unique culture and wildlife
  • Visit a Trobriand Islands village to learn about the islanders' traditional way of life
  • Go on a birdwatching tour to spot some of Papua New Guinea's unique and exotic bird species
  • Learn about traditional woodworking and carving techniques from local artisans
  • Participate in a Kina Shell money ceremony, an important part of Papua New Guinea's cultural heritage
  • Take a cooking class to learn about local ingredients and traditional cooking methods

Outdoor & Nature

  • Explore the McAdam National Park, known for its stunning natural beauty and diverse wildlife
  • Hike the Kokoda Track, a challenging and historic trail that offers breathtaking views
  • Visit the Baiyer River Sanctuary, home to birds of paradise and other unique wildlife
  • Relax on the beautiful beaches of Kavieng and enjoy the town's diving opportunities
  • Take a diving or snorkeling trip to explore the stunning coral reefs and marine life of Kimbe Bay
  • Go on a wildlife tour to spot some of Papua New Guinea's unique and exotic animal species

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit the National Museum and Art Gallery to learn about Papua New Guinea's history and culture
  • Attend a traditional dance performance to experience the country's vibrant music and dance
  • Explore the Port Moresby Craft Market, where local artisans sell their traditional crafts and souvenirs
  • Take a guided tour of a traditional village to learn about the local way of life
  • Visit the Tavurvur Volcano to learn about its history and cultural significance
  • Learn about traditional medicine and healing practices from local experts

Hidden Gems

  • Visit the Lae War Cemetery, a historic site that honors the country's WWII history
  • Explore the Salisbury Plains, a unique and beautiful region known for its stunning natural beauty
  • Take a boat tour of the Duke of York Islands, a group of islands with stunning beaches and diving opportunities
  • Go on a hiking trip to the Mount Giluwe, the second-highest peak in Papua New Guinea
  • Visit the Goroka Market, a bustling marketplace where locals sell their traditional crafts and souvenirs

Day Trips

  • Take a day trip from Port Moresby to the Sogeri Plateau, a scenic region with stunning views and hiking opportunities (distance: 45 km, travel time: 1 hour)
  • Visit the Tavurvur Volcano from Rabaul, a historic site with stunning views and cultural significance (distance: 10 km, travel time: 30 minutes)
  • Explore the Kokoda Track from Owen Stanley Range, a challenging and historic trail with breathtaking views (distance: 60 km, travel time: 2 hours)
  • Take a day trip from Madang to the Baiyer River Sanctuary, a unique and beautiful region known for its wildlife and natural beauty (distance: 20 km, travel time: 1 hour)
  • Visit the Kimbe Bay from West New Britain, a stunning region with beautiful beaches and diving opportunities (distance: 30 km, travel time: 1.5 hours)

Planning Tips

Suggested itineraries: 3-day (highlights of Port Moresby and surrounding areas), 7-day (comprehensive tour of the country's main attractions), 14-day (deep exploration of Papua New Guinea's culture and natural beauty). Key tips for booking and prioritizing: plan ahead, research local customs and traditions, and consider working with a local tour operator to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip.
