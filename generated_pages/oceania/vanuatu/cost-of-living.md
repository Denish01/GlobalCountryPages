# Cost of Living in Vanuatu - Prices & Budget Guide

The cost of living in Vanuatu can range from affordable to moderate, with daily budgets varying from $30-60 USD for backpackers to $150-300 USD for comfort-seekers.

[FACTBOX]
Cost Level: Moderate
Daily Budget (Backpacker): $30-60 USD
Daily Budget (Mid-Range): $80-150 USD
Daily Budget (Comfort): $150-300 USD
Currency: VUV
Tipping Custom: not expected
[/FACTBOX]

[SECTION] How Expensive Is Vanuatu? [/SECTION]
Vanuatu is considered a moderately priced destination compared to other countries in the Pacific region. The cost of living in Vanuatu is relatively lower than in neighboring Fiji and New Caledonia, but higher than in the Solomon Islands. The country's economy is primarily driven by agriculture, tourism, and fishing, which contributes to its moderate cost of living. Overall, Vanuatu offers a great balance of affordability and quality of life, making it an attractive destination for tourists and expats alike.

In comparison to global averages, Vanuatu's cost of living is relatively high due to its remote location and limited access to certain goods and services. However, the country's stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and friendly locals make it a unique and rewarding destination. Visitors can expect to pay a premium for certain imported goods and services, but local produce and traditional restaurants offer affordable and delicious alternatives.

[SECTION] Money-Saving Tips [/SECTION]
- Consider staying in a hostel or guesthouse instead of a luxury resort to save on accommodation costs
- Eat at local markets or street food stalls to experience traditional cuisine at affordable prices
- Use public transportation or walk/bike whenever possible to reduce transport costs
- Buy local produce and products to avoid high prices for imported goods
- Negotiate prices when shopping at markets or with local vendors
- Learn some basic Bislama phrases to interact with locals and get a more authentic experience

By following these tips and being mindful of expenses, visitors can enjoy a wonderful and affordable experience in Vanuatu. With its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and friendly people, Vanuatu is a destination that has something to offer for every budget and interest.
