# Top Things to Do in Vanuatu — Attractions & Experiences

To experience the best of Vanuatu, visit the stunning **Port Vila Markets**, explore the historic **Ekasup Cultural Village**, and relax on the beautiful **Iririki Island**, which offer a glimpse into the country's rich culture, history, and natural beauty.

[SECTION] Unique Experiences [/SECTION]
- Take a **Kastom** dance lesson to learn about Vanuatu's traditional dance and music
- Visit a **Nakamal** to experience the local **Kava** culture and traditions
- Go on a **Tanna Island** volcano tour to witness the stunning eruptions of **Mount Yasur**
- Participate in a **Sand Drawing** workshop to learn about the traditional art form
- Take a **Survivor**-style adventure tour to experience the island's rugged terrain and beautiful landscapes
- Learn about Vanuatu's **WWII** history on a guided tour of the **Millennium Cave** and **SS President Coolidge**
- Experience the local **Food** and **Drinks** on a culinary tour of **Port Vila**

[SECTION] Outdoor & Nature [/SECTION]
- Explore the **Mele Cascades** and swim in the natural pools
- Hike to the **Mount Yasur** volcano on **Tanna Island**
- Relax on the beautiful **Pele Island** beaches
- Visit the **Havannah Harbour** for sailing, fishing, and water sports
- Go on a **Dive** or **Snorkel** trip to explore the **SS President Coolidge** shipwreck
- Take a **Wildlife** tour to spot **Dolphins**, **Turtles**, and **Birds** in their natural habitats

[SECTION] Cultural Experiences [/SECTION]
- Visit the **National Museum of Vanuatu** to learn about the country's history and culture
- Explore the **Ekasup Cultural Village** to experience the traditional **Kastom** way of life
- Attend a **Traditional Dance** performance to witness the local **Kastom** dances
- Take a **Woodcarving** workshop to learn about the traditional art form
- Visit the **Port Vila Markets** to experience the local culture and cuisine
- Learn about Vanuatu's **WWII** history at the **Millennium Cave** and **SS President Coolidge**

[SECTION] Hidden Gems [/SECTION]
- Visit the **Rimatuk** village on **Pele Island** to experience the local village life
- Explore the **Lakatoro** town on **Malekula Island** to learn about the local culture and history
- Relax on the beautiful **Uluveo Island** beaches
- Take a **Horse Riding** tour to explore the island's rugged terrain and beautiful landscapes
- Visit the **Vanuatu Handicraft** market to buy local handicrafts and souvenirs

[SECTION] Day Trips [/SECTION]
- Take a day trip to **Pele Island** (30 minutes from Port Vila) to experience the local village life and stunning beaches
- Visit **Mele Cascades** (20 minutes from Port Vila) to swim in the natural pools and enjoy the scenic surroundings
- Explore **Tanna Island** (1 hour from Port Vila) to witness the stunning eruptions of **Mount Yasur**
- Go on a day trip to **Aore Island** (30 minutes from Luganville) to relax in the beautiful island resort and enjoy water activities
- Take a day trip to **Emaotfer** (1 hour from Port Vila) to experience the local culture and stunning beaches
